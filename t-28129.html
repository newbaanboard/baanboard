Hi,

The AFS functions run perfectly on BaanIV c2. To make it work, we did need to upgrade the Portingset as well as we applied all available Tools solutions.

We did this approx. 2 years ago, and we currnetly have a number of high volume customizations based on AFS running live ...and they have been doing so for a long period. Lately we upgraded the standard program again due to problems (...limitations) with one specific new AFS based program. Again everything worked without problems after the upgrade.

For your information the site in question is an extremely customized site - hundreds of standard Baan programs are customized and even more are added. So the reasons for NOT experiencing problems is definitely not that we're having a "clean system".

Unless you have a very special system, I wouldn't be too considered about upgrading the Portingset/Tools - but of course you'll need to test it...
Go ahead, it's neither very complicated or risky as I see it.
