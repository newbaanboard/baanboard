I looked at the form and as Maria suggested the field is display only on the form. So the way I see things you have two options. The first is to change the session and its forms, so the srnb field is part of the find. I admit I do not know how Baan has two different find keys for the two forms. I have never attempted or tried this, so I am not sure how they did that.

The second option is to use the stpapi.change.view using the orno and pono fields. Then use the stpapi.browse.set to find the correct srnb record you are looking for.
