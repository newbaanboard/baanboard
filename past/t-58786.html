Infor Acquires Qurius' ERP LN Operations (http://www.infor.com/company/news/pressroom/pressreleases/infor-qurius/)

Acquisition Further Strengthens Infor's ERP LN Business and Enables Company to Expand Market Share in Europe
ATLANTA - Jul 01, 2010

Infor, the leading provider of business software for mid-market companies, today announced it has acquired Qurius' ERP LN operations. Qurius is an Infor implementation partner which provides architecture, realization and systems management for business and IT solutions, including infrastructures. As part of the terms of the deal, Infor will assume approximately 60 Qurius staff members who will continue to service Infor's ERP LN customers in Italy, Germany and Spain moving forward to ensure continuity.

News

* Qurius will divest its Infor business. However, it will continue to own and operate the rest of its publicly-traded business.
* Acquisition helps Infor continue to build its ERP LN business, which is a strategic priority for the company.
* As part of the acquisition, Infor will inherit approximately 60 staff members from Qurius' offices in Italy, Germany and Spain, expanding its market share throughout the region.
* Customers who are currently using Qurius as their technology partner to manage their ERP LN implementations will be able to continue to work with the same professional services team as well as have direct access to Infor's comprehensive suite of world-class business software applications moving forward.
* Infor ERP LN helps manufacturers reduce complexity, increase agility, and better serve their customers.

What We Say

"A strong channel partner program is critical to maintaining the close collaborative relationships that have enabled us to become the leading provider of business software for mid-market customers," said Dennis Michalis, corporate senior vice president, Infor. "Infor's acquisition of Qurius' ERP LN operations does not represent a shift in this strategy, we will continue to invest heavily in our channel partner program and recruit new partners across the globe to benefit our customers."
