What are the (browser) requirements to view Baanboard.com?

Browsers

Baanboard will work fine with most recent and (reasonably) standards compliant (HTML/CSS) web browsers such as:

Opera 9+
Firefox 2.x
IE 7.0+ Do not use any older versions of Internet Explorer such as 6.0, 5.5 etc, as these browsers will not render the navigation panel correctly.

Screen sizes

Baanboard recommends to view its site on a resolution of 1024x768 pixels or higher. We no longer support any lower resolutions as the vast majority of our visitors do use a higher resolution nowadays (see: Forum Statistics (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/psistats.php?do=display) > Display colors & Resolutions)
