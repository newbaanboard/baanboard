Merino signs strategic alliance with ESA to extend its reach in APAC and EMEA 

http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/u14567/Merino.jpg
This can be one of the most exciting news for the Infor ERP LN/ Baan community in APAC and EMEA. Merino as part of its strategic alliance with Enterprise Solutions Agility (ESA) extends its market reach to various countries in APAC and EMEA. This relationship is aimed and bringing the experience, Success and Strengths of Merino in Infor ERP LN /Baan to customers and prospects in various countries in APAC and EMEA. Merino Services Limited is one of the strategic and most successful Infor Partners with direct presence in North America, India and other Asian countries.
“ESA compliments Merino’s strong delivery capabilities with More than 3 decades of all round IT marketing and sales exposure in Europe, Australia and ME Region with extensive customer and channel contacts across the Region. Merino’s consider it as a very strategic move to extend its services in many regions/ countries with in APAC, Europe and Middle East” said Rishi Bansal, Vice President – Global Business, Merino.
