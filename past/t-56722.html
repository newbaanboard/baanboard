This month NAZDAQ (http://www.nazdaq-it.com/) celebrates 10 years of serving the Baan and ERP Ln community. On this occasion, we would like to share with you some of our news.
 
New Brochure
 
We have released a new 24 page full-color brochure (http://nazdaq-it.com/demo/cata.pdf) with the latest information about our unique products.
