Savio India Signs Merino to stabalize Infor ERPLN

http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/u19155/merino_logo.jpg (http://www.merinoservices.com)Merino to ensure improved financial processes within the organization


November 11, 2010 : One of the leading partners to Infor for ERP LN/ Baan, Merino Services Ltd.( MSL), announced signing Savio India Ltd, the leading manufacturer of textile machineries has chosen Merino as Partner to Progress to stabilize already implemented Infor ERP LN. Savio India had implemented Infor ERP LN and a view to further expand and improve utilization of financials they chose Merino to be their partner for the entire project.

The scope of this project will include deployment of a team of financial experts on to their premises. The team would ensure sound functioning of the entire financial and accounting processes replacing the legacy system Tally and seamless integration with other modules for accurate information flow to finance within the system.
