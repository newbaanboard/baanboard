Indian Subsidiary of China Based Meita Group Selects Merino Services to Implement Infor ERP LN

http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/u1/merino_consulting_logo.jpg
Merino Services has been selected as Partner to Progress to implement Infor ERP LN solution at Green Tech Industries, a subsidiary MEITA Group of China

January 12, 2011 - Infor's largest partner for ERP LN/ Baan, Merino Services announced, Green Tech Industries Ltd , one of the subsidiaries of the leading automotive manufacturers MEITA group has decided to implement Infor ERP LN at their organization.

The decision to implement ERP was an extension to the strategic IT initiative of the organization. GTI wanted their ERP to be the back bone of their entire organization. The implementation of ERP LN is expected to streamline all the business functions and help them reduce their total cost of operation gradually. The plan is to integrate ERP LN with the Visual Quality Management system of Infor.
