Posting a new thread (question)
If you would like to post a new question then follow these steps:
1. Navigate to the forum using the left-hand navigation panel:
http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/u1/icons/16x16/nav_down_right_yellow.gif Main > Forum

2. Click on the name of the forum in which you wish to make your post:
http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/new_thread_01.png

3. Then click on the New Thread button:
http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/new_thread_02.png

4. Enter the title and body of your post/message. Then click on Submit Thread:
http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/new_thread_03.png
