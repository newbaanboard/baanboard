Bry-Air goes live on latest version of Infor ERPLN

http://www.baanboard.com/system/files/u19155/MSL.jpg (http://www.merinoservices.com)Merino Services Ltd helped Bry-Air to migrate from Infor ERP LN SP0 to LN F2

November 16, 2010: Merino Services Ltd, Infor's largest partner for ERP LN/ Baan announces that Bry-Air, the flagship company of Pahwa Group, one the leading business houses in India offering end-to-end energy-efficient environmental control solutions has gone live on ERP LN FP2. Bry- Air had been using Infor ERP LN SP0 for several years and this year they planned to migrate to ERP LN FP2.

The scope of this project included migration of modules like manufacturing, finance, purchase, sales, warehousing, quality and services with specific reports that were required according to the Bry Air officials. The project was rolled out in 4 different subsidiaries /companies spread across India.

Gautam Dutta, Infor Practice Head, Merino Services Ltd said, "The key requirement of Bry- Air was to fit the entire business process in the standard Infor LN FP 2 with no functional customizations. This was a challenge for our MSL team involved in project but we mapped client's requirements 100% within the standard system without any hassles or failure on the time lines."
