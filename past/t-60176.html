Sales Analysis is always a challenge to do from standard ERP systems. Most of the senior Sales Managers, Head of Sales would like to generate actionable intelligence through maze of Sales Data provided by Baan/Infor ERP LN.

Senior Sales people would like to see following:
- Ability to drill down on regions, countries and cities
- Ability to extract information time (year/ month/ week)
- Ability to extract information Product Families/Product

There would be lot of such dimensions possible for analyzing information available in Baan/Infor systems.

The webinar on "Sales Analysis in Baan/ Infor ERP" will provide insights to above areas and will cover topics such as:
- Understanding sales trends, customer buying patterns using multi-dimensional analysis
- Building Interactive Reports on the fly
- Sample Reports for Sales Analysis
