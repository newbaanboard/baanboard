I assume you're still in the implementation phase (since there's no way that ALL production orders in live companies are closed unless the company is closing down all its factories, e.g. in case of insolvency). It will be rather tedious to cross check, but there shouldn't have been too many records to check yet in your sim 2 company. There are few things to check:
Have all integration transactions (especially the manufacturing ones, but all kinds to be safe) been posted?
Does anybody ever make any manual journal to the WIP account?
Check whether the WIP transactions are balanced out in Integration Transactions by Project/Order
Check the mapping. Could there be multiple WIP accounts referred to wrongly for different production activities? This could lead to WIP accumulation/decumulation
Last check: track each and every journal entry to the WIP account to see why there was accumulation/decumulation there
