Hi,

There is no restriction on the number of periods in Baan. I know customers who have defined each week as a period.

For you to have period 14, this change will have to be done in Company parameters. GTM would have to be done to change it from 13 to 14

Then from Maintain periods session, you will have to add period 14 and then maintain the status of the same.
