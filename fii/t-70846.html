Best approach would be to make use of Electronic Bank Statements handling in LN, instead of uploading a CSV file which is still manually filled with G/L accounts.

Bank statements are possible in all kinds of formats.
Native LN supports MT940 (Structured) and ASCII fixed position bank statement formats. The MT940 is a 'Bank Relation' option and for other formats I refer to (help text of) tfcmg5202m000 session.
Other formats (e.g. XML or CSV) are not supported by native LN.

From LN 10.3 and onwards these kind of formats (ISO20022/CAMT xml messages, BTRS/BAI2, etc.) are supported in LN with the 'Infor Local.ly - Banking Services' addition, which makes use of ION (11.1 or better 12.x).
Please have a look at Infor Xtreme for more information about this solution (https://www.inforxtreme.com/espublic/EN/AnswerLinkDotNet/SoHo/Solutions/SoHoViewSolution.aspx?SolutionID=1926258)
