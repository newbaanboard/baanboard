In some countries, ledger account numbers are prescribed by law. However, if you do not want or are unable to use these legal ledger account numbers (for instance, in connection with the reporting requirements of a holding company), you can define the chart of accounts to your requirements and enter the legal ledger account number here. You will then be able in "BAAN Finance", at lists of balances and transaction reports, to select whether to print out using the "private" or the legal account number.
