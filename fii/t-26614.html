In the Maintain Project Transaction Accounts session we have set up to Debit COGS and to credit Work-in-Process accounts. In our case we have three different COGS accounts (Direct Material, Direct Labor, Overhead Expense) and three WIP accounts (WIP Material, WIP Labor and WIP Overhead). We determine the proper account based on the cost price component used.
