Hi,

The problem is resolved now, while maintaining the Assets record Assets (tffam1500m000) session in FAM Module, the value has to be kept 0 "Zero" which not mandatory field, with this the Asset number is generated without value and then through Accounts Payable Module, the aforesaid asset record to the ledger wtih option "Investments" "Purchase Invoice Transactions"tfacp1120s000 session, the amount is updated in the FAM Module. In this manner the recording of double amount and current quantity is avoided manually.
