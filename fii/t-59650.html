Anticipated statuses come based on the no. of steps you want to carry out for a payment. Refer to Form 2 in Maintain payment Method sessions. If you issue a cheque to a supplier and wait for a confirmation from the bank to see if it has been realized or not till that stage it remains as an Anticipated Payment.

Advance is as usual payment without reference to an invoice.
