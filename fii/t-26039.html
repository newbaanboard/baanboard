Hi MRF,

Are you dealing with different legal entities, or are you thinking about different (Baan) Finance-Companies within 1 legal entity?

Usually, in a Finance Multisite Environment every Finance-Company represent a different legal entity. Therefore, in principle it is possible, to book transactions from one company to another within the same "Group of Companies". But there is no standard functionality to "condense" and transfer transactions to another company. Also, if you have different legal entities, usually you are not allowed to book the same issue twice, even the second booking is a "condensed" one.
