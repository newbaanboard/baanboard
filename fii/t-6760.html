In Baan IVc:
In addition to identifiying and correcting the problem. If you have a non-integration transaction that causes this problem. You will need to delete the error log for the transaction as well, otherwise the system still thinks the error exists. You may already know this but the first time it happened to me I spent nearly two hours tearing my hair out trying to work out why a transaction would not finalise after sorting out the problem.
