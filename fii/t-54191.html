Hi hdquestions,
it depends on what your requirements are.
Scanning in Baan is not possible as per standard.
If you just want to link the (externally) scanned invoice to a dataset in Baan then the ODM package could be an option (don't know if it's available in Baan IV).
Try a search for ODM on the board.

If you wan't to do a complete 'automated processing of incoming invoices' then there are several solutions out there which integrate whith Baan.
Ask INFOR for partners or have a look here:
