Hello CR,

The Archive Company setup should be a mirror image of operational company i.e., it should be exactly same with all the required data to perform the activities. If we do not maintain any of the data required for a company setup, it will create problems during archiving process. So we need to maintain even company(tcemm1170m000) and Enterprise unit(tcemm0130m000) to smooth flow of archiving process.
