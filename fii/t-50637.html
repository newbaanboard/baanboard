Yes, for customized items this is not possible.

Also delivering a produced customized item (on a sales order) does not result in a financial booking?

Delivering a customized item will trigger PCS/Cost of Goods Sold integration transactions, however, dependent on some parameter settings, these may not have a 1:1-relationship with the item sold.
