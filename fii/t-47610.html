The invalid value in tfcap200.balc occurs due to a program error leading to faulty calculations in this field. Programs updating the balc field are invoicings , corrections , payments , allocations , currency differences , payment differences etc . U need to monitor such invoices for further occurrences in future.

U can correct the error in SQL server through SQL query . Also tfacp2245m000 can probably correct this .
