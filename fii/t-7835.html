Dear Armstrong,

Is your man/machine ratio=1? This can happen for example when man occupation is 3 and machine occupation is also 3. The thing is, B5 calculates costs this way:

machine cost=machine hour*machine rate
labor cost=machine hour*man occ/machine occ*labor rate

Hence, if man/machine ratio=1 then machine cost=labor cost. It's a bit funny, but that's what we observed
