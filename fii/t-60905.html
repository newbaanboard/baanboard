Hi There,
use this way:
1. Depreciation method is straight line.
2. Put in the asset's life is 13 months.
3. Put in the service date is 01/12 2010.
4. Put in the accumulated depreciation is 500
5. put in the last depreciation period is 31/12/2010
6. Capitalized the asset (starting from service date), with suppres journal entries option enabled (and put in the asset journal on 2010 using journal voucher, not directly comes from asset module).
7. Depreciating the assets for year 2011. Depreciation cost will be 41,67 per month.

Good luck. I always use this way to solving this kind of fixed asset issue.
