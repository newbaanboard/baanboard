just to share the information :D

A) Transaction Origin: Warehousing (Transfer)
Finance Transaction: Valaution Result

An inventory transfer between two warehouses can face a valuation difference when the valuation method is Fixed Transfer Price (FTP). The inventory will be issued according the origin valuation price using the transaction Issue and be received at the destination valuation price by using the transaction Receipt.

B) Transaction Origin: Warehousing (Valuation)
Finance TRansactiion: Revaluation

In case the inventory valuation method is based on the actual costing methodology the inventory value will not need to be revised frequently or even never, but in case Fixed Transfer Price (FTP) is the ongoing valuation method revaluation are inevitable. A value revaluation will occur when a FTP price of an item changes
