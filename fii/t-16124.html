This is standard functionality in BAAN IV.
The order series of warehouse orders are fix.
You have to archive the warehouse orders (or delete them, this is a new functionality, if you are up-to-date with the Service Packs/Solutions) or
you have to enlarge the logistical order numbers (this possibility is part of SP 15) from 6 digits to 7 or 8 digits.
