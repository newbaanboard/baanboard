No, if you do a full update it always does the whole file. Why not archive - you can still print reports from the archive company if you need to? You can deactivate sorts, but then reports that use them will not work - but you can always reactivate them later and that will take a long time if the tables are big.

Do you really have to do a full update? Perhaps you should also have a look at your server processor, memory, and disk performance.
