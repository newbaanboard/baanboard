yes, you can
in standard Baan -report you can suppres the accountnumber by excluding the accountnumber in the detail.section. ( 1 and <> accounttnumber). This is only advisable if total credit and debit are zero at all times. Total of ledger is affected in the way that this still contains the posted debit and credit ammounts of the excluded accountnumber. You can put the same here ( 1 and <> accounttnumber) and the accoundnumer is excluded here also.

If you have made own reports in the financial reportgenerator you simply not define the accountnumber in the report.
