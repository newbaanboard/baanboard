1) Open Maintain Options By Integration Element {tfgld4121m000}
2) Select the 4th Dimension (Region in your case) and click on Relations button.
3) In the resulting session Maintain Simple Integration Relations by Element {tfgld4123s000}, select the country which is not listed("SLV" in your case) and assign an appropriate dimension code to it.
4) Save and exit.
