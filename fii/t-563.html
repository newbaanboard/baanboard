Hi,

You need to do the following.

Finance Implemented checked in Company Data
Enterprise Units defined in Orgware
Warehouses and Departments defined
Company Data set up in EMM
Company Parameters defined
Group Company Parameters defined
Logistic companies by financial company
Financial Companies by Group Co.
