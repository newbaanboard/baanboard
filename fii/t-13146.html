The dimension and ledger balances are always updated simultaneously for 0 level ledgers and 0 level dimension .

You may run tfgld3203m000 which brings back opening balances if year 2002 is finally closed . Else run tfgld6201m000 (year close provisional) to bring back balances (including opening balances) .

The ledger rebuild sessions do erase any opening balance that is provisional. If the previous year has been closed properly then the rebuilds should be ok, if the previous year has not been closed properly (yet) then you'll have to do a provisional year end after each ledger rebuild.
