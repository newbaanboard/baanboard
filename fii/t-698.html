4th January 2002, 12:05
Hi Phil,

Unfortunately that is what you have to do. If you need to enter data into a previous year that should be the current year in Finance.

Then once you have gone through the year end close process that will update the current year in company parameters. Also please note that the current year is only important in the year end close process anyway and does not have any bearing on the day to day workings of Baan Finance, so it shouldn't really matter that it is pointing to last year.

Hope it helps!

Phil,

I forgot to say that you do not have to wait to load live data. It can be loaded at the same time as the historical stuff, Baan allows multiple periods to be worked on at any time, just ensure you use the correct fiscal periods when loading the data.

You simply will not have the opening balance to the new year until you go through a year end process, but this can be done whenever!
