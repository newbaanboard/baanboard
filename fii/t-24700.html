The transaction type for purchase invoices has a field "Match with purchase orders". There could be two types of trans types based on that, one with match purchase orders and the other without. For example we take with matching = P03, wthout matching = P02.

When you enter a P02 invoice in the session, the status of the P02 dcument becomes "Transactions Entered" and will continue to remain like that. This is b'cos in this case there is no need to match with any PO.

When you enter a P03 invoice in the session, the status of the P03 document becomes "Registered". Later when it is matched with PO the status becomes "Matched" and when approved, the status becomes approved.

Based on parameter setting, only approved P03 invoices can be paid. For P02, they can be paid in cash management, right after finalisation.

Hope this helps.
