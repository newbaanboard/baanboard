You can maintain as much bank accounts as you want. Each must be pre-designated by a transaction type and a contra GL account that's all. This should be fairly simple and easy. For each bank and payment method you can define posting data and other related data. I don't foresee any big issue on this.

We are having more than 9 accounts in our set-up like
Hsbc sgd acct
Hsbc usd acct
Citi sgd acct
Citi usd acct etc...
