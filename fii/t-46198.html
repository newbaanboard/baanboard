Rebuild ledger history - whether its tfgld3203m000 or tfgld3201 or tfgld3206 do not show any report. You can try executing these in non job mode and check for yourself. No report gets printed. However on the session you can notice the progress that its making.

There is no way that you can make out what has changed after rebuild.

Alternatively you take print of the Trial balance before rebuild and compare it with the TB after rebuild- you will get to know the differences or what changes occured after rebuild.
