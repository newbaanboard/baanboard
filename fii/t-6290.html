Hi Anthony -

Scott is correct - the integration transaction that posts cycle counts is the combination Warehouse Order/Inventory Adjustment. You can tell the difference between ones generated via cycle count and ones generated from the maintain inventory transactions session by the order number - cycle count orders carry their own series.
