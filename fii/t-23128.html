It is a manual process.
1) You dump the transactions for your period into Excel or some other tool, where you can aggregate transactions by ledger and dimensions (pivot table is good) and you
2) make a journal voucher, that takes the sum of your transactions out of the existing period (reversal), and then you
3) make a new journal voucher that puts the sum of your transactions back into the correct period.

Consider wether you need all your balance account transactions in the correct period, since your end balances, will be the same anyway. That may save you from some difficulties. E.g. some of the balance accounts you are not allowed to make manual postings.

Good luck.
