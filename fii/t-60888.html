Possible, but it is a pretty intense custom report. PPV can come from multiple places, but will ultimately end up with a finalized transaction in your PPV account. From there (tfgld106), you have to determine if the transction was a manual posting to PPV or via integrations. If manual, your reference data will come from tfgld106. If integrations, you can find the source integrations (tfgld418, tfgld410, tfgld417) to determine if the PPV came from a purchase receipt and/or result. The integration will provide your link back to the original purchase order and receipt (tdpur041/tdpur045).
