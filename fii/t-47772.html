Hello,
when it is just a question of integration transactions... what about to set the integration parameters tfgld4150m000 in that way?
You can define that the integration transactions should be posted real time to the finance (session "Post integration transactions to finance transactions" not necessary) and / or if you want "Immediate start of finalization process" to Yes. But normally I would prefer a selection of a range of batches like sukesh suggested.
If you have too much integration batches, then you can perhaps change the settings for the compression of the transactions.
