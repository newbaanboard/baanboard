You could do a general issue(as in Baan4;"Enter Inventory Transaction: Production Issue") and record your consumption of consumables.

These maybe not be used in the production of an item but they sure plays a role in production and a percentage of it could be added to the cost of production of an item as an indirect rawmaterial surcharge.
