Revised legislation for the Construction Industry Scheme (CIS) is due to come into force in the UK from 6 April 2006. If any UK Baan customers are also requiring this functionality please can they reply to this thread or private message me.

See the following links for information:

http://www.new-cis.co.uk/
http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/new-cis/cis341.pdf (provides a good summary)

From a brief reading the following functionality seems to be missing from Baan IV:


Ability to store these details for each sub-contractor:

Unique Taxpayer reference (UTR)
National Insurance Number
The subcontractors status as verified by HMRC, as one of:

Gross payment
Payment under deduction
Unregistered (deduction must be made at a higher rate)

Verification reference provided by HMRC


Ability to store the standard rate of deduction, and the higher (unregistered) rate of deduction.

Storage of the value of the material component of a subcontractors invoice during the registration of a purchase invoice. The CIS deduction must be made on the value of the invoice less any material costs.

Produce statements for sub-contractors detailing payments and deductions made, on either a monthly basis or with payment. The verification reference provided by HMRC must be on these statements.

Provide reporting for monthly return to HMRC of payments made to sub-contractors, with details of the subcontractor, subcontractors reference, gross payments and any deductions made.
