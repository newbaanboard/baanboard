Well, these kind of differences are very common in Baan IV.
You have lot´s of chances to see such differences.

First check, if you have all Integration transactions processed to finance and also finalized these bookings in Finance! (No integration errors, etc!)

Please be aware, Baan Logistic doesn´t take care on Fiscal Periods, so this can also may result in a Difference to Baan Finance.

Of course, the most common reason for differences (beside "simple" Software-Bugs) is the integration Setup.

On the first run, you can check the balance of your inventory account against the Inventory per Item or Warehouse report in Logistics.
