Look in the tfgld106,i index9 to the correction document number in combination with the purchase approval transaction type.
For every new purchase approval document number for the related transaction there is a approval/unapproval done. Number 1,3, 5 etc (uneven) are approvals, and the equal ones the unapprovals.

Of just print tfacp1450m000 Postings by purchase invoice and count the numbers of the purchase approvals.
