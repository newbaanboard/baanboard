hi, cucucu,

please have a look at tfgld0108m000 Maintain Chart of Accounts, form2.
There you can enter (if you want to) 1 or 2 units for each gl-account.

If you do that, you can not only making entries with amounts e.g 1000,- euros on this account, but you can also say these 1000,- euros are 50 pieces or 100 kg.

In tfgld0110m000 Maintain Dimension you can choose, either if you want these units (of the ledger account) beeing entered on the dimension or not.

(we don´t use this functionality. i´ve heard that there could be sometimes some problems with the correctness of the units if entered through integration... but i´ve no own practical experience with that))
