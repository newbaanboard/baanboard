The set up is not specific to the service module but to tax in general. Baan does have an installation guide available from the knowledge base.

To calculate tax on a service invoice the customer has to be set up as taxable and the items issued must have a tax code. The key to the correct taxability is to map items or item groups to a Vertex product category. This is done in the Maintain Product Tax Category Matrix. The important step is to ensure that the Vertex TDM is complete.

I have found a couple of problems with the service module. First cost items may not tax correctly because you cannot specify a warehouse. This can cause jurisdiction issues, particular in Texas which is an origin state.

Also had a problem with New Mexico. Confirmed with Vertex that this was an issue with issolated to Vertex 1.3 and is fixed in the 2.0 version.
