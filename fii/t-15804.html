The only reason I can think of to use anything other than standard Baan (e.g. Multi-Currency extension) is if you need to financially report (that is, accounting reports) in more than 1 currency in than 1 financial group.
If you are just purchasing or selling in more than 1 currency then as pfsilva says just define another currency in Maintain Currencies.
