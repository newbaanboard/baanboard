Dear Babu,

Looking at this problem from distribution point of view, you can easily solve it by creating a report based on tdsls401 (SO Lines). From this table, you find all the lines which has been delivered (delivered qty not zero or tdsls401.dqua<>0) but hasn't been invoiced yet (no invoice number or tdsls401.ncmp=0 and tdsls401.ntyp="" and tdsls401.ninv=0, if I'm not mistaken. I don't have a B5 sys in front of me now). Then you sum up the amount for all these lines. Voila! You got the delivered but not yet invoiced amount (from distribution side)
