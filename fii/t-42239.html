Dear 8aaniv,

just 2 ideas:

(1) Charset of the user. We sometimes had to change the charset in maintain users from "iso-bin" to "8859-1" (as far as i remember) for specific finance sessions, error did not appaer any more but vice versa with other print sessions.

(2) Check $BSE/temp -> the sorting of the report should result in big temp files, maybe space problem (file to big / temp full )...

In General: We found a major bug in SP 18:
Text management. There was an error in the oracle db driver, fixed with portingset mid/end november 2005, a commit was missing, therefore an roll back happens (as far as i remember).
It was not stable to copy existings texts (tttxt ... e.g. item texts), or to create new texts. Existing texts was deleted, the textnumbers were chaotic ... Not sure if baan fixed it with the portingset delivered with SP 18.
