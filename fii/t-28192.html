jimbacon
20th September 2006, 01:29
You are not missing anything in your set up. When you have an asset which you are depreciating, you debit depreciation expense and credit accumulated depreciation. Your balance sheet shows the assets less accumulated depreciation for a net fixed asset balance. When you dispose of the asset, the accumulated depreciation is reversed because you no longer own the asset.
