Hai Arch,

In mho the easiest option is to redefine the reports (invoices) to be printed without company data in the header.

Then, based on the sales invoice print to 1 out of 3 printers each filled with logo printing paper (the 3 different brands).

This may sound too easy, but I think the only other option is programming (which you don't want as you stated).
