Hi,
In this case you can use item type-"Cost" & item group- "Cost to be Specified" in item.

Once you confirm receipt(Purhcase) system will trigger two integration document type codes in integration transaction-
Purchase Order/Receipt
Interim transit A/c Dr
Invoice Accrual A/c Cr

Purchase Order/Cost to be Specified
Interim Costs A/c Dr
Interim transit A/c Cr

While doing matching & approval system will ask you to put Expense Gl a/c.
After approval system will reverse Interim Cost & Invoice Accrual A/c.
& left final transaction -Expense A/c Dr. & BP Control A/c Cr.
