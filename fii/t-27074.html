Hi,

The solution which i am give is not a correct one but a work around and it works perfectly.

When u receive the invoice for the material match this invoice to the order partially even if the quantity and amount is matching and approve the invoice. When you receive the second invoice enter the invoice in Mainatin Transaction. In the PO field enter the Po number and save. The system will give you a warning saying that the order is on different supplier. AFter clicking "OK" it will display the Po and here u match again which was partially matched earlier. and approve the invoice. Repeat the steps of second invoice as many times as possible. see to it that when the last invoice is book the matching must be done completely.
