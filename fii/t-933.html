If you changed the current fiscal year using GTM ( i agree with erik! )...
You would not be able to run the close year process for the previous year. More explicitly,
1. you would not be able to balance the P&L accounts and have your closing transactions reflected in the GL.
2. You could not create your opening balance transactions and have them reflected in the GL.
The reason...
Baan closing processes will check the 'current fiscal year' field in the tfgld004! This means they will run for the year you have GTM'd into this field. You can see where this could get ugly rather quickly?
