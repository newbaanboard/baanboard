Indeed, the GTM is the only way to reopen the period. Be aware that this should only be done in the current year, in case you accidentally finally closed a period.

If you do so for already closed fiscal years and you make some changes, the closing balances will alter and thus no longer be the same as the opening balance of the next year... unless you change that as well. Baan does not support that in any way.

Did you check your trial balance? When doing this mutiple times, Baan will create an opening balance mutiple times too.

So, every time you will do ' Close Year ' (tfgld6203m000) an opening balance will be created. So, your opening balance amounts will exist double, triple, etc.

This means, that you should change your opening balance by ttaad before finalizing. Only the amounts added to the reopened year should be processed.
