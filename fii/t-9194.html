Hi!

In session tffst1102m000 in the field Var or Fixed Year/period Range select "Variable" as the option. After this in period shift correction in the filed "From" assign the value as -12 and in "To" 0. This will calcualte the YTD for both Debit and Credit column reference transactions of your P & L stmt.
