This sound like an old bug to me. Please check whether you have updated the component otfgld1210-otfgld1213. The table tfgld109 was updated last in the finalization process.

I assume that you use "Finalization" for your transaction type.
Even after you correct the status of the finalization run, you have to confirm whether the GL was updated correctly. Print the finalization report to check whether all the transaction lines were there. Print the trial balance to check whether the GL balance was updated.
