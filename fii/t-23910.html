The 'calculate currency differences' session is used for accounts in the GL that are in a different currency than the Company's Home Currency. We don't use this session at my company. All accounts are in the Company's HC.
You will want to use 'Exchange Gain/Loss' to calculate the difference between currency at purchase to invoicing.
