Hello,

you can pay the invoices with a cash run. This means that you select your invoices for payment (with session tfcmg1220m000). Thereby you get an payment advice containing all invoices you want to pay. When you process the payment advice the postings are made in the GL and a clearing file can be created for upload to the Bank.
