Maria,

Not sure how to rebuild tfgld410, but have come across one of the causes for missing records.

At a company I worked at, they had a customisation that allowed them to change the standard cost price roll up date on the item master file. The date in tiitm001 then did not have a corresponding entry in ticpr (200 or 300 I think).

When a transaction is created at the point in time when there is no corresponding date in ticpr, Baan simply drops the transaction from tfgld410.
