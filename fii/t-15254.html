Don't do that!! You don't have the possibility anymore to run the control account checklist to check if your subledger is in line with the trial balance. Make a sublevel in your trialbalance and you have the same effect. In case everything is running smooth you don't have a problem, in case of troubles you don't have the possibility to sort it out anymore.
