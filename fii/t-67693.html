The outcome of cycle counting is similar to inventory adjustment, so if your counting results in an increased amount of items, your stock will increase, and you will get a positive result in your P/L. (this is basic knowledge, and will work this way in most - if not all - ERP systems)
I think you are free to decide how often you want to count, but that question belongs in the distribution forum.
