Hi Sumit,
Before you proceed with calculating the currency differences, you first define the monetary items by setting the "Currency Analysis = Required, Calc.Curr.Diff." for accounts which represent monetary items (and, vv, set their account's "Currency Analysis" to something else for non-monetary items).
