Hi,
This kind of error can be very tricky. First of all you must determine where is the root cause of the problem.
By your summary, apparently you finalized a wrong transaction in a control account. If this is the case, maybe whith the help of Infor support you can correct it with a journal entry, but is not a common practice.
The other approach to do this is to create a complementary account where you account staff can register the journal entry.

By the other way, if you have a different balance on the control account versus the finalized transactions for that account, the first approach to solve this is to rebuild the account history. This is a standard process in Baan.

If you provide more detail the other members of the board can help you more.

Hope this helps.
