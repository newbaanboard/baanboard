Hi -

Have you taken a look at the Popup Help that is available in the session? It provides a good deal of info, including suggested debit and credit accounts to use.

To access this help, open the tipcs8150m000 session. Click on the help button (the question mark) and then click in the Transaction Origin Field. This will give you the first Popup Help screen. In this screen, click on the underlined "Integration PCS with BaaN Finance". This will take you into another screen that lists the various transaction combinations that can be set up in the tipcs8150m000 session. Click on each one of these to get a definition of the transaction and the suggested debit and credit account numbers. I referred to this help frequently when I set up our company's PCS project integrations.

Hope this helps!
