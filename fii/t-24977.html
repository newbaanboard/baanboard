Hi,
I have got this from Baan help..
----
BaanERP always posts an integration transaction to the same fiscal period for the companies related to the debit transaction and credit transaction. If a transaction with a logistical origin must be posted to two different financial companies, the transaction is posted to the same fiscal period in both companies. The same applies to reporting periods. This situation occurs if:

The debit transaction and credit transaction have a different company
The period statuses in the different companies differ during the posting of integration transactions

BaanERP determines the fiscal period and reporting period as follows:

1.Determine the period based on the transaction date

2.Based on this period, determine the first open period for the from and to company

3.Compare the determined open periods of the credit company and debit company:

a. periods are equal, goto step 6
b. periods differ, goto step 4

4.Select the latest period and check whether the period is open for the other company:

a. period is open, goto step 6
b. period is not open, goto step 5

5.Log an error
6.The period is determined

Example
A transaction is created in January, and the transaction must be posted to two financial companies. The status of the fiscal period January is open in company one. However, in company two, January is already closed. BaanERP will post the transaction to the fiscal period
February in both financial companies.
-----
This also is applicable if the posting is to one financial company...

In session tfgld4150s000 Integration parameteres U can allow posting to next open period or not.
