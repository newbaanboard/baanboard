If you are using BaaN IVc4, click on help from any screen in BaaN, and click on index. It will list down all the topics you will find #Integration PCS with Finance. This will give you the list of all combination of transactions (Transaction Origin and Financial Transaction) and also suggest the entry that will be generated for the selected combination. It will also tell you when that combination of transactions get triggered in BaaN.
