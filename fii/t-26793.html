Here is the information on the different fields in the EBS record.

Bank The bank relation of the bank which has sent the electronic bank statements (EBS) file.
Record Type for Bank Statement The record identifier in the EBS file.
Enter the record type which identifies each record in the EBS file.
Start Position During conversion, SSA ERP recognizes the beginning of a record by a specific sign in the electronic bank statements (EBS)) file.
Enter the start position of the location for the record identifier in the EBS file.
Related topics
Start/end positions

End Position During conversion, SSA ERP recognizes the beginning of a record by a specific sign in the electronic bank statements (EBS)) file.
Enter the end position of the location for the record identifier in the EBS file.
Related topics
Start/end positions

Record Type Value During conversion, SSA ERP recognizes the beginning of a record of an Electronic Bank Statement (EBS) file by a specific sign.
Here, you can enter that sign.
Note
The value of the sign must fit between the specified start and end positions.
Example
Start position 10
End position 12

The record type value cannot have more than three characters or digits.
