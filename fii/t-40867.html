It is certainly more complicated than just a journal voucher but it can be done. Is this just a one time import to load bunch of legacy system invoices or something you plan on doing frequently?

As you may know when you do a JV exchange import it is to temporary gld tables and then the Baan session 'Import JV batches by ID Number' takes that data and converts it into a financial batch, assigns document numbers, etc. Since there are no like tables for importing purchase invoices your exchange must accomplish these tasks. This is not easy.
