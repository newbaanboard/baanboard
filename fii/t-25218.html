U may copy the report to customized vrc and in the report u may use the cf$ function to re-initialize the $nama like

nama = cf$(1) & strip$(nama) & cf$(0)

Please note this way if the nama variable has no space left at the end , the complete letter behind nama will be bold . So take care of the same with len function and trimming of last few characters.
