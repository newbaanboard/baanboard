In BaaN IVb, there is no setup of CMS/Reservations from CMS in session tdinv8150m000. The ledger account numbers defined in session tdcms2101m000 Maintain Commission/Rebate Transaction Accounts.

The system will automatically post the accrual reversal for CMS/Purchase Invoice.
