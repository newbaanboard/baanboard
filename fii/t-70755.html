The 'Domestic Bank ID' is an attribute of the 'Bank Branch' field (tccom125.brch) on the 'Bank Account by Pay-to BP' session. And this field is referring to Bank Branches session (tfcmg0511m000, table tfcmg011), so the 'Domestic Bank ID' is the field tfcmg011.bnid.

Note, session tccom4125s000 is accessible as a form command in the details pay-to BP role session (tccom4124s000).
