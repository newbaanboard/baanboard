Hi,

It is not necessary to map all the IDT, but it is mandatory to map all those IDTs that have integration transactions created by non-financial packages. Otherwise, you could not close your fiancial periods (Fiscal, report, tax) in the point of system view. Furthermore, your financial information will not be entired.
