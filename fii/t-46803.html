Hi there,

Using your mapping scheme from FP2 should be possible, because you can also install on your FP2 environment the FP3 environment and use the service pack transaction scripts to upgrade. So if you would make a copy of your existing FP2 enviroment, install FP3 and follow the service pack transaction procedure, then all your data should have been converted and ready to use in the FP3 enviroment. There are already customers who have upgraded from FP2 to FP3 without any serious problems, so this should be possible.
Please note that regarding the financial mapping scheme, that after a service pack transition on LN 6.1 you should also initialize from tcfin the mapping scheme again. You should then copy your current active mapping scheme to a new version, check it and make that one active. Only after this procedure you will have a good working FP3 mapping scheme.
