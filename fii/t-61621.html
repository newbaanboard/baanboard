Inventory unit quantity gets posted as quantity1 in ledger . If all items getting posted to the ledger have same inventory unit and logistic configurations like surcharges , cost price components etc are configured suitably , you can have the quanity 1 in ledger coming from logistics . Please test it properly before putting to use.
