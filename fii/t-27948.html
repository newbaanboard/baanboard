Hi Gautam,

What do you mean by Intercompany Processing?

Standard BaanIV has no Intercompany sales or purchase.

BaanIV has only financial transactions as banktransactions and journal entries where Intercompany is possible.

I have done 2 implementations with some customizations to make Intercompany Sales and Purchase more automatically.
It is not a full automatic process, but you can already save a lot of time depending on the number of Intercompany transactions.
