Hi,
To make mihaelagr's point more clear: Opening balances won't come automatically. What is the status of your previous year. If you have not yet closed or at least provisionally closed your previous year, obviously you wont get your opening balance for current year. So, check the previous year. First you need to CLOSE YEAR PROVISIONALLY. Then you will get the opening balance in the current year.

Please note: If you have already PROVISIONAL CLOSED the year, and still you are not etting the opening balance, it means somebody would have run "tfgld3203m000: Rebuild Opening Balance/History from Transaction" This session resets the opening balance to zero, if the year is not yet Finally closed. Nothing to worry, go back and PROVISIONAL CLOSE the year again, and you will get your balances right :)
