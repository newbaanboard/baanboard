While making payment using payment advice, it is not possible to process the negative payments in Baan IV. I think from LN there is one option in the payment method to allow negative payments. In Baan IV audit will block the negative payments. One work around could be to settle that with some positive payment you will be making to the same supplier if there is any. Means if you want to make negative payment for 100 to resolve the issue, this payment should be done in combination with any other positive payment which is more than 100. The total composed amount for that supplier should be positive. Then only system will allow you to process the payments.
