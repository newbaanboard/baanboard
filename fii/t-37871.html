Hi Old Man,
i checked in our System. I think you have to define a record for year. This one can have a value of 0.0.
Try the following:
In budgets per year (tffbs0505m000/tffbs0105s000) insert a record for the Year/budget combination and leave the field budget distribution empty.
Afterwards insert a record in Budget Amounts and Quantities per Year (tffbs1500m000) for your desired ledger account/dimension combination (amount 0.0).
From there you can start session Budget Amounts and Quantities per Period where you can insert your values per period.
The values are automaticly accumulated in tffbs1500m000.

Hope this helps,
