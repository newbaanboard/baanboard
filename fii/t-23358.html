Hi Mohamed alsadr,

Actual depreciation every month should be booked as
Dr Depreciation expenses
Cr Accumulated depreciation

The depreciation absorption per production order should be booked as
Dr WIP
Cr FOH Control
This will later be closed into finished good inventory on conclusion of production
Later, when the goods are sold, it will be closed to COGS

Hence, there won't be any duplication
