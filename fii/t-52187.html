I believe you want the following setup:

910 = logistical and financial company, meant to be master data comp. as well.

911 = financial company

902 = group company

If that is so then both 910 + 911 have to have 902 in tfgld004 as group company, an entry of all companies in tfgld001 and in tfgld003 as base company.

In addition to that all have to have the same ledger accounts, period types, Ref-Currency and dimension types. If that all is the case then you should easily manage to link the acp tables.
