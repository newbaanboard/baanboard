Hi alex,

This is from the baan Help..


Purchase result :A purchase result only occurs when a valuation method of Fixed Transfer Price (FTP) is used. A purchase result is the difference between the FTP of an item and the order price. The purchase result is posted at the time that the goods are received.

Prive variance : You can change a purchase-order price after the goods are received.
You must generate a financial transaction and post the variance to BaanERP Warehousing.
