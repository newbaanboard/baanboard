Recommended table sharing is well documented in Baan help . Mainly master such as client,supplier , company data , ledger , dimension , mcs data and ledger history table need to be shared . For more specifics baan documentation is quite helpful.

If multi-company setup creates / introduces any difficulty in your desired functionality, cosnolidation can still be done in reporting very easily and is not a big problem.
