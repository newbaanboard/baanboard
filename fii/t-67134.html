Hello,

The purchase receipt transaction should have also created a transaction on the intercompany account in the related company. So if you consolidate the trial balance of both the companies the intercompany account balances should total to zero.

You can not pass manual entries on an inter company accounts. The credit balance in the intercompany account in the current company indicates that amount is payable to the related company.

Hemant
