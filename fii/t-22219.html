Hi tinkerbell,
i had the the same results when i tested the late payment surcharge some months ago.
Had a discussion with support about that. They told me that this is standard functionality :confused: and they implemented it as a special requirement for the dutch market :confused: :confused: :confused: .
This is how it works:
The late payment surcharge is calculated and is shown on the invoice independently from the due date.
Suppose you have invoice of 100 and surcharge of 5% after 30 days the invoice shows a sum of 105. Very clear for Customer :eek: .
When you are booking payment in tfacr then the surcharge is booked back if the payment is done before the 30 days.
So all transactions are correct and with a little customization on the invoice report you should be able to use this functionality.
Nevertheless we decided not to use it.
