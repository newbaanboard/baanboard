If I understand your question correctly. THe background process gets locked and the status is not updated. The process would have been partially processed so the batch gets locked with info that background did not process.

You can use the session invoke background session to release the lock (baan will take care of resetting the status internally)

Incase the system is unable to release this lock, you may have to reset the status in different tables, (I think its tfgld100, tfgld101 which you need to check the affected tables)

Hope I it has been directed to what you were questioning.
