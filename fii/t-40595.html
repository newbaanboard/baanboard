Hello,
I would have a look to the settings of "Maintain Payment Methods" tfcmg0140m000 for the cheque-payments.
There (on form 2) you can decide wether the payments should be combined by Baan or if each invoice should be payed with a separate cheque.
May that be a way?

Hi,
You are right. The field which does this combining is "Composing Limit" on Form 2 of Maintain Payment Method. It had a value of 1 which was changed to 99 and it started combining the transaction...
