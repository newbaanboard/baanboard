Apart from these tables tfgld200 series of tables are also updated to store balancesin GL. Also, there may be supplier open balance also affected.

Only tables tfgld018 100, 101, 102, 107 and tfacr200, 500 (tfacp200 are needed for the upload. All other tables will be updated automatically when running LN standard sessions for rebuilding history tables.
