Hi Mohamed_alsadr,
i had a quick search on my Baan Documents. The key element combination 'reason' and 'sales office' is not applicable for Integration Scheme Sales Invoicing (sales)/revenues analysis.
You can use 'Enterprise Unit' (of sales office) in combination with 'reason' or 'warehouse' in combination with 'reason' if these key fields are different for return orders in your setup.

Hope this helps,
