Hi,

I think alex is using Baan IV not Baan 5.0c, as there is no such session as this in Baan 5.0c..

Anyway Ale, you can change the due data from the open entries tfacr2520m000, select your invoice and double click and you can change the due date...

This will affect your aging analysis and your reports but you can't reprint the sales invoice with the new data...

Actually in Baan5.0c you can't reprint a sales invoice "with a new data "once it is composed, it will always be printed with the old data.
