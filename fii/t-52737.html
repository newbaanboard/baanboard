Hello,

I have experience with czech localization and some of changes in czech localization are needed by the law. The rest of it was made because most users wanted to.
As far as I know there are some customers running on standard but in this case you have to customize the system. (changes in intrastat, taxes - reporting, mandatory reports, gld, reports in GLD,SLS,PUR)
It depends what version of Baan you want to use. It seems to me localization on Baan IVc4 was bigger than on LN 6.1.

And dont forget the translation to czech language. I can hardly imagine the system running in Czech rep. without proper translation into native language.
