Vikash,
Regarding a report on advance Payment :
You can use the Report Print Invoice from Business Partner Open entriestfacp2421m000 with the flag ON Unallocated/ Advance payment. Keep other flags OFF. You can use TEXT flag on in case if you put your PO number at the time of making payment as text. You can put flag ON Fully Paid Invoice if you want to see all advance / unallocated Payment already linked to Invoices. You can use the option Anticipated payment if this suits you.
