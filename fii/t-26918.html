The due date has to be calculated the moment the invoice is entered.

What you want is that after matching to the receipt(s) the due date on the invoice is being recalculated and that is functionality, which is not present.
Also here you can have problems, because if an invoice is matched to multiple receipts, what receipt date has to be taken then, the oldest receipt I guess?

This recalculation of due date after matching is something that definitely not all users would want, so it should be parameter driven. Anyway it is not present (yet).
