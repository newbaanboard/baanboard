Alex

I am assuming you are using Baan 5B if so you set-up the link by opening the Business Partner session (tccom4500m000) and select the role "invoice from"(tccom4122s000).

Move to the invoicing tab and you will see the field Fin Supplier Group.

This is where you link the Supplier to the Group.
