Though I'm very new to Finance Module of LN... Out of curiosity about what Salvage Value means, I found the following information-
1. There is a field "Salvage Value" in session Asset Books (tffam1510m000); Here you can specify the amount that is expected to be recaptured when a fixed asset is disposed at the end of its useful life.
2. You can link the "Depreciation Code" also in this session to specify which Depreciation Method, LN should use for a particular Asset.
