Hi,
You should to update company code in tffst121.
For example, you copied your P/L report from company A, so in tffst121, t$cmpf, t$cmpt still keep "A", even you copied your report to company "B".
so, what you need to do is : update table tffst121, set t$cmpf, t$cmpt to new company code.
You can do it via tfgld9000m000, or you can do it via ttaad4100.
Coz it is just a minor change, you better do it via ttaad4100.
