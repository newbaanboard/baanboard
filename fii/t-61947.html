In LN, it is possible to modify the dimension codes on the AR or AP control account side of a cash transaction. The bank ledger account and dimensions, as specified on the Transaction Type, is not maintainable in the Transactions session.

As noted in the previous replies, please review your requirements and see if they can be met by dimension accounting on the BP control accounts, cash flow reasons or a combination of these.

If you can provide an example of what you wish to achieve, there might be other suggestions.
