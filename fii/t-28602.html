If you simply a delete, this may cause other problems along the way since many other tables (open items AP/AR, finalized transactions, integrations, etc.) depend on this table.

Best thing to do is archive (+purge), which will ensure that tables are handled in the right order so reference errors do not occur. Session tfgld6205m000 (archive/purge finance transaction) removes the 'tfgld018' records once others that depend on it are archived and removed. You should be at sp10 for "4c" (minimum) since additions were made around the time of service pack 10 for adding proper handling of 'tfgld018' in this session.
