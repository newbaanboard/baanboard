Hello,
Yes, it is correct. You will sitill have 100 USD but, lets say exchange rate 11.00 MXP per USD. Then in MXP the system will register 1100 MXP. This is due to multicurrency environment. The system will mantain both values.

If at the end of the month you reevaluate your bank, and the exchange rate is 12.00 MXP per USD, on USD you still have 100 USD but in MXP the system will adjust the MXP value doing a credit of 100 MXP to the bank and debt for 100 MXP to the LOST&GAIN Exchange Account.

If you donÂ´t reevaluate, the old values will remain.
