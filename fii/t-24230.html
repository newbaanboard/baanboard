As I wrote the other day on one of the other Baanboard questions: You may get very good help from the help functions in Baan. Go to the menu: Integration with other modules and press help.
In here you have detailed info on the advised setup in debit and credit for the different combinations of transactions (transaction origin/financial transaction).
In my opinion, however, if this is your first attempt to do a finance integration, you will not survive. This is normally something that experienced consultants develop over days if not weeks, depending on the customer needs, and it must be combined with the standard reconciliation tools Baan offer. My advice would be to explore the help possibilities yourselves, and then get a minimum of qualified assistance.
