Well, sorry to say, but in Baan IVC MCR, this kind of errors are frecuent.
My suggest is to check on tfgld102 (transactions not finalized) an verify the number of decimal places that the amounts is stored.

I often had, document xxxxx is not balanced. 0.0 diference....
And when you check on tfgld106, you can see amounts with 3 or 4 decimal places. So, if this is the case, round it according to your definition of each currency.

I mean, if you have USD currency defined as 0.01, round all the UDS amounts to two decimal places.

Do it the same for all the currency you have.

Hope this helps.
