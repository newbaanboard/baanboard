I am afraid you have to correct the company number directly in the table. If you reverse it you will get another extra transaction reversing it so the problem will be double.
I think the financial company in the mentioned register is the logistic one (580). You have to change it to the financial one. Check the following tables:
tfgld410, tfgld415, tfgld416, tfgld417 and tfgld418.
