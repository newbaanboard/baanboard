Hi there,

What you can do is order in Purchase the Fixed Asset with a cost item. Put this item in an item group which is marked as being a "Costs to be specified" item group.
During the invoice matching in tfacp2541/tfacp2544 (match to order or match to receipt), the user can split up the costs through the specific command "Cost items" and then get to session tfacp1133s000. Here the user can put the costs amount on a ledger account, which is a Fixed Asset Investment account etc.
If the user approves the invoice, then the FAM module will be updated.

At least this is the procedure which can be followed to the best of my knowledge.
