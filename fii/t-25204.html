Std Cost of an Item: 100\-(Lot Item and valuation is lot pricing)

10 items received (Std. Cost: 100 and Lot price [PO price]: 110)

When the material received to the Stores:

Raw material Purchase Dr 1,110
To Accrued Purchase Liabilities 1,110

(Although there is a difference between Standard Cost and Lot price there will be no financial transaction for the variance at the time of receipt. The variance transaction will be generated only when this lot item is issued to production or sales (via the combination Production and Lot Result or Sales and Lot Result)
