Hi Orest26,

Please run this session as advised in error message-Print Unbalanced Ledger Accounts during Year end-tfgld6403m000 for currency UAH.

Preferably you can select-field value-'Check Finalized Transactions also'.

You have two report options viz . Compare History Tables tfgld201 & tfgld205 & second option-Compare Opening Balance Tables tfgld203 & tfgld206, advised to run both one by one.

The above reports will highlight the errornoues accounts which are not balanced for debit & credit.

Once you identify them, therafter you can run session Rebuild Opening Balance/History from Transactions-session tfgld3203m000 for the said ledger account.(Do follow precautions mentioned for this session) !

Hope the above works, if still not, kindly let me know.

All the best.:)
