We create a purchse order: Item A - 5 pcs * $1000

After delivery of the ordered quantities we pay the rest of the amount and the supplier prints and sends us the invoice related to the final payment.

Hi,

This can be done via CMG. If you are using the CMG module in Baan, then you can check in session Maintain Payment advice, there you have one option of Advance payments and when the receipts, matching and other procedures are done. Then after that you can link the two documents made.

for example
Advance payment Rs70
Receipt done Rs. 100
then after matching above two documents total outstanding would be 100-70=30
