As previously answered the integration transactions are posted in detail by cost component. This allows flexability in defining different debit and ledger accounts as well as transaction analysis.

To view your item's cost components you can open ticpr3501m000, find your item and last calc date and then under the specific menu open Item Standard Cost Prices ticpr3500m000 and you should see 5 components. Some maybe with 0.00 value. 0.00 amounts can be suppressed from posting in tcfin0110m000
