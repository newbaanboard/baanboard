Dear Dheeraj,

This is a general nature of Baan to withhold some entries, which may not have been posted correctly from distribution. As you have mentioned that Baan has corrected it when in finance you did matching. The problem is serious when all the entries from Distribution are not posted correctly to respective accounts. In some cases you may have to change tfacp200 for purchase invoices if entry at all is not coming in finance, you may be required to create records manually in distribution also.
