When you enter the session the system would have popped up a message to enter your expected closing balance " opening balance +- your transactions = closing balance" . This is a check, upon leaving the session, to confirm that all transactions were posted to the bank correctly. It is a bank reconciliation control amount.

You can go to "Special" and click on Maintain Bank Balance to input the correct closing balance.

Good Luck!

Did you compare the closing-balance you entered with the one on your window where you enter the transaction (cmg2100)?

The closing-balance is the total of the postings on the "Bankaccount" ledger-account for the specified currency. Did the currency change?

You may indeed try rebuilding the ledger-account; if the current balance in the system is not Ok, the warning is to be expected.

Maybe you encountered a bug in the unallocated receipts. Try deleting this entry and insert it again in a new batch...
