Hello,

there is no standard procedure for changing this which is the reason why I allways recommend to introduce 15 periodes before they start with LN. You will have to do the following:

1. Change the fiscal periods to 15 via ttaad4100 in tfgld000
2. Add the periods 14 + 15 to all fiscal years that are not yet finally closed.
3 Create Period status for the latter
4. Add the periods 14 + 15 to current fiscal year + status
5. Do that in your test environemnt first and perform Provisional Closure tfgld6201m000
