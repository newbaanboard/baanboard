Hi Rudolph
Please give me your email address. I have found the way to allocate cost. The key is the setting of your Chart Of Account. This is how the sistem works.

Main account cost where you use for transaction the set up is as follow, you set the Allocation type as primary. Then you make two additional COA, one as secondary credit and the other one as secondary debit. The secondary debit is the ledger account where the allocated amount. While the secondary credit is the ledger account for the total of all primary ledger accounts that you set to be allocated.

Those setting is important in order to be succed to run session "Import Actual Cost from GLD"

The Allocation Rules you can review the controlling document.
