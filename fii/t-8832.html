Can you not simply backdate the receipt to Jan 31, 2003? As long as the fiscal period that includes Jan 31 is still open, the receipt should post to finance as of Jan 31.

I dont think that you can enter a planned delivery date on the PO that is earlier than the PO entry date, but I believe that the receipt can still be backdated. You can test this fairly easily. Maybe someone whose focus is logistics can quickly affirm this or shoot it down.

From a finance perspective, I hate to suggest backdated logistic transactions, especially if they are dated into a prior period. Doing so really complicates reporting and account reconciliation. However, sometimes there are compelling business reasons There are several other threads that discuss the pitfalls of backdated receipts. You may want to search for these and review them.
