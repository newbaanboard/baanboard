Hi Neal

The problem relates to the fact that the actual Ledger Account Balance is probably made up of different currencies whilst the Opening Bank Balance on the Cash Transaction purely relates to the currency connected to that bank account.

If you print out the report Trial Balance by currency for the ledger account you should see the amounts in the different currencies and the balance being selected for the Cash Transaction.

Unfortunately there is not a solution to this.
