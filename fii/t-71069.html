Hi,

As per my understanding your Gross Profit value and formula is correct.
In you case Sales amount showing with negative amount (Standard Display Logic for Infor) for system it is a credit balance and COGS amount showing with positive amount for system it is a debit balance.
For Example:-
Scrap Sale = -1000 but for Finance it is a Credit balance of 1000.
COGS = 900 but for Finance it is a Debit balance of 900.
Gross Profit = Sale - COGS = Credit - Debit = 1000 - 900 = 100

I think this will help you to understand calculation under Financial Statement.
