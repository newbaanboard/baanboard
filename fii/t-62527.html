I would recommend that you do the rebuild by period as part of your month end close so when you get to year end this doesn't cause problems. Also a rebuild CAN change financial numbers since if the totals are not correct due to a system issue that happens and if you are publicly traded and have reported already these changes can be a big problem. You also will need the GLD open in the last period to do your autobalancing
