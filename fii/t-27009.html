Have you fully defined which staff (US or Mexican) will perform various parts of the AP process? Who is going to enter the invoices into Baan, match them, approve them, pay them through the system?


One possibilty, based on the information you provided is that you could set up a new Bank Relation and a new Payment Method specifically assigned for the Mexican invoices. That way you could have you US staff select invoices for payment by that Bank Relation/Payment Method combination (Mexican specific), review invoices for payment and then generate the checks using a check printer set up to print at the facility in Mexico.

Your Finance department should be able to provide answers to the above questions, that may help with a solution.
