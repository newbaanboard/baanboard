First of all, think how you want to integrate financially before you start poking in the tables.

It is possible to (technically) maintain the two financial companies, but link all integration transactions to the single financial company that should remain. After this is done, no more transactions should be posted in the old company.

The financial data should also be joined. Verify how tables are shared between the financial companies. Maybe you have the possibility to wipe the data together in one company.

Also here you may consider finalizing the remaining invoices in the old company before you close it down.

Before you delete the old financial company, consider the consequences for history data, archives etc.
