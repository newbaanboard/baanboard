There is no 'hard' link back from the Finalized Transaction record to the Purchase Order Line.

I would do this as a step by step search.

The session references below are based on BAAN IV c3. Yours may be different.

I will assume that you know the Document Type and Number, from your starting point of the finalized transactions (tfgld106)

So, starting with the session Maintain Purchase Invoice Details (tfacp2100m000), binocular to the document. That gives you the Purchase Order Number.
[I use the Maintain session to pull up the record, because the Display session does not have the Purchase Order Number].

Then, using the session Display Receipts [tdpur4531m000], binocular to the Purchase Order Number just found (with Position 0). From there scroll down to the position that has the part number you want. One of the forms will give you the price you are looking for.

And that is one way to get to the information.
