Hi, servicegirl,

For monetary items like AP in foreign currency, it has to be presented in financial statement at current rate, as per FAS and IAS. The way you do it in BaanERP is by writing off unrealized currency difference in AP. When you've done this, any transaction involving those foreign currency AP will use the new currency rate, based on the latest writing off of unrealized currency difference. In effect, this will cause any transaction involving those AP to be valued at current rate, if you update your currency rate monthly (coinciding with your monthly reporting period)
