Hi Sue -

Our company is multi-finance/multi-logistical in BaaN. We currently are on version IVc2, soon to upgrade to c4.

We create our financial statements (balance sheets, income statements, departmental operating expense statements, etc.) in BaaN, then export them to Excel to make them "pretty". We originally used a third-party software but ran into performance problems. We produce departmental, divisional and consolidated statements in BaaN.

Because we have all of our companies using BaaN, we are able to take advantage of the system's drill down capabilities - the amount of inquiry capability you would have would be determined by the level of detail that you bring in (import) from your other companies.
