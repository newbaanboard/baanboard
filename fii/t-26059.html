What does the "Reported Completed" (integration transaction) actually do? I completed the projet (in TP) and system generated integration transaction "Reported Completed". Why do I need it? The explenation in the help is too weak. :(
Eric12
13th January 2006, 13:21
The reported completed transactions are moving your work in process from the balance sheet to the profit and loss accounts.
This all happens when you close the tp-project, and it goes for both costs and revenue (which is called completed revenue)
