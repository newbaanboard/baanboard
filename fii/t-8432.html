MAUC is the Moving Average Unit Costs. It is an average cost price of the goods in inventory; calculated at the moment of receipt.

An example can clarify:
receive 10 pcs for 100 EUR each
MAUC = 10 * 100 / 10 = 100 EUR

receive 10 pcs for 200 EUR each
MAUC = (10 * 100 + 10 * 200 ) / 20 = 150

issue 5 pcs
MAUC remains 150 EUR

receive 5 pcs for 50 EUR
MAUC = (15 * 150 + 5 *50 ) / 20 = 115.25 EUR
