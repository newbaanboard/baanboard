tfgld3201m000 only agreegates balances for parent( higher sublevel) accounts. If u do not have any non-zero sublevel accounts (which is the case in many implementation) , it will not do anything for yoy and come out.

It is tfgld3203m000 which really builds from transactions and takes good time.
