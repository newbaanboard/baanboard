I think I may have mislead you a little when referring to columns.

There are actually 4 columns in the report:
Balance
Comment
Profit & Loss Accounts
Balance Accounts

each of these columns has a debit and credit side.

The comment column is empty of values (and even comments for that matter)

The value shown in balance, be it debit or credit, is repeated in the profit & Loss column and the Balance account column.

If this is an extended TB then it should actually be that the balance value is then repeated in either the profit & loss column or the Balance account column but not both.
