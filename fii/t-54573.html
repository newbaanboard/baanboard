I found the error too, it was a standard error.

We open an Incident, but until the solution comes I did a customization to solve the problem.

Just for register, here is a description of what was wrong:


On the calling of function tffam.dll0002.map.leac.dimx on the DLL tffam8505(line 4204) the fields of tffam100 used as arguments are with no values and them are not loaded anywhere.

The customization select this fields before the calling function.

Now everything works fine =)
