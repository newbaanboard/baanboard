Hi Steve,

The Provisional Opening Balance is created for the (single) "current" fiscal year. When progressing into the next fiscal year you may create an OB based on the previous fiscal year, that in Baan will be the "current year".

Baan only has one "current year" and that's the only year that can be used to calculate a prov OB from to be used in the "next year".

This OB is an temporary update of the tfgld20* tables and will be deleted when you eiter - create a new prov OB, or - create a final OB, or - when you recalculate the totals (tfgld3203 session). In the latter case you need to run the Create Prov OB session (gld6201) again.

In your situation I think you need to close the 2001 year first as Baan will see this as the "current year".
