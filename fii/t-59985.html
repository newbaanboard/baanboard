There is not a problem with Withholding tax/social contribution with regards to Advance Payments, for the simple reason that that functionality is not available for Advance Payments. Withholding tax/social contribution is only applicable for purchase invoices/credit notes.

In FP7+the functionality of Withholding tax on advance payments will become available.
