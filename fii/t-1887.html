1. You must create in this sesion tffas2130m000 the amount that you want to reduce.


2. Previously, you must define in tffas1110m000 the Db/Cr Ledger Account.


Profit and Loss Db
Amortization (Balance) Cr.


3. After this: You must execute tffas1201m000

4. The asset value in your Balance Sheet is reduced; because this functionality works like a adjustment.
