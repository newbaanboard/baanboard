I'm no finance expert, but it seems like no one is answering. You can create your own reversing journal voucher batch to set the accounts straight. Debit the amount to the 'bad' account, and then credit it to the correct account. I think this is standard procedure for correcting account balances after posting. These reversing entries because of error should be documented special in case of auditing later. Physically changing the original posting would be very difficult, the entries are in many tables, and I have not seen a supported/standard procedure for that (but again, I am no finance expert!).
