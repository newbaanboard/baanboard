I agree to the other writers that dimensions are the best to make the link. It is the easiest and most efficient way.

Besides, which version are you implementing Baan IVc4 or ERP?
In IVc4 a controlling package (German Localization) exists which uses also the dimensions. In ERP you have controlling functionalities integrated in the Finance Package, using also dimensions.

But anyway Abenett is absolutely right with the advice that you should get some professional help, at least for the finance part. Not only you won't know all functionalities but there are also parametres which are very difficult to change after implementation. You will be much more effective with a good finance consultant.
