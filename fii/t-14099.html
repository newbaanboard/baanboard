As you are already running MCR, to issue invoices in EUR you have the following implications:
1. The currency for sales order will be EUR
2. Check settings for generic format, (tools), as EUR uses , instead of the decimals
3. You will have to setup Currency EUR and maintain currency rates in Financial tables
4. There are also sales tax implications if you are selling to certain countries from within Europe, however, if sales are from MEX to Europe likely not applicable.
