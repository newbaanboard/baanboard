That's right. (Or you can get them into gld213 etc by a custom session for example)
When they are in tfgld213 etc you run tfgld2215m000 "Import Journal Voucher Batches by ID Number" to take them into tfgld100/101/102 as non-finalised batches.

I think you need to give them a transaction type with a fixed sequence (that is, tfgld011.iseq = yes).

I created a new transaction type to distinguish them and to achieve the fixed sequence.
