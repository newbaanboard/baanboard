That's standard functionality. And in my humble opionion one of the biggest design failures in Baan.

Baan considers PCS WIP as one big "cost container" that doesn't need to be splitted up in any way. As far as I know there is no way to overcome that problem.

The only thing you can do is to get the PCS WIP by item group from the logistic modules: It's basically the stock of the respective items valuated by their estimated or actual costs, depending on your parameter settings. However you will still have to add the WIP of the PCS production orders also.
