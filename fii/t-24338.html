With the GRNI tools you are able to explain and check the balance of your Goods received not yet Invoiced account. This account is populated as soon as you maintain a receipt and is contra-posted as soon as you approve the purchase invoice.

The accept functionality can be used as a kind of filter on your report tfacp1435m000. You have an option to only print the not accepted GRNI transactions.

Later on when you want to finally accept the GRNI transactions, all GRNI transactions for an order have to have the status accepted.

I hope this helps. :)
