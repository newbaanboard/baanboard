Production (SFC)

Revaluation - At the point of cost rollup

Sales
Revaluation - When you backdate a delivery

Hitesh Shah
6th August 2004, 12:23
There is 1 more revaluation. Purchase/revaluation .

Warehouse order revaluation is revalation of items in stock physically. All other revaluation are the result of backdated transaction entry after which there has been a cost change. All these (other than Warehouse order) are generated so that the overall revaluation effect remains in tact in all circumstances (even when u create a transaction before the date of cost price change).
