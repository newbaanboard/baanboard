I believe you are on ERP Ln 6.1.

You can creat separate category and link depreciation code having method "None".

In asset life you can enter maximum life for year is 200 and month 11.

Since depreciation method is None system will not calculate depreciation for Land

Please try and put result on Baanboard for information to others.

In case of Baan 4 maximum life can be entered 99 years and enter 0 for the method "Fixed Amount"

In Baan 4 maximum life can be entered 99 years and enter 0 for the method "Fixed Amount"

End date is getting calculated based on number of years of asset life we enter. it will be 99 years in case of land asset. End date is applicable for the depreciation method "No. of Years in Use". So in your case it is not relavent. In any case 99 years are too long.
