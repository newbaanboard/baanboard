You can write a simple sql query on the payment details table.

something like:

break on t$suno skip 2;
compute sum of t$amnt on t$suno;
spool 1099_detail2005.txt;
select t$suno,t$amnt,t$pdoc,t$cheq
from baan.ttfcmg110200
where t$crea >= to_Date('20050101','YYYYMMDD')
and t$crea <= to_date('20051231','YYYYMMDD')
and t$step between 6 and 7
order by t$suno;
spool off;
