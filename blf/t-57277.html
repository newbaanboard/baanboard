Hello, someone can help me with this novelty in Baan V

At some stage of planning, we have simulated orders:

1) With consecutive route operations, the next operation begins when the other ends. In this case the end date = date necessity.
2) With overlapping route operations, according to the amount of data transfer and production times, the last operation has the highest date. In this case the end date = date necessity.
3) Error: With overlapping route operations, according to the amount of data transfer and production times, the last operation hasn´t the highest date. In this case the end date is greater than the date necessity.
