No,

the FBU is on top of the German localisation.
so you have a vrc-structure like this :

B40 c4 Standard
B40Uc4 Patch Standard
B40X c4 cnt0 CNT
B40U c4 cnt0 Patch CNT
B40S c4 ger0 GER0 VRC
B40U c4 ger0 Patch VRC
B40S c4 fbu3 FBU-3 VRC
B40U c4 fbu3 Patch VRC
B40C c4 abcd Customer VRC

Solutions for FBU-3 comes as PMC-Solutions .
For Installation you had to create with PMC-Tools a new Update-VRC. The Base-VRC for this must bei named as B40S_c4_fbu3.
