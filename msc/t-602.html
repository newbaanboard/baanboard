Hi,

The Demand Forecasting in EP will look into the sales data that is available in the sales order and sales order line history (tables tdsls450 and tdsls451).
Data is populated in these tables after running session tdsls4223m000 (Process Delivered Sales Orders).

Hopefully this helps. If you have any questions/issues please let me know.

19th December 2001, 17:23
It is a stretch.

What you would do is basically make the history into a straight line forecast by advancing the year field and putting it into 830 EDI format. That would make last year's numbers look like next year's forecast.

It is not scientific in terms of smoothing and projections (unless you use some of those techniques to adjust the numbers before the go in).

However it would at least get the numbers into the system as a starting point.
