Hi again,
Can you go to the session Report Operation Completed {tisfc0102m000} and check while completing an operation if the field under the heading "Backflushing" [To Do] has the same quantity as what was finished(in your case Backflushing To Do = 110). If this field is zero at the time of completing the operation, then no backflushing happens even if the materials are to be backflushed. The subsequent delivery quantity in the session Enter Material Issue For Production Orders {tisfc0101m000} will not be issued even after the operations are completed. So check it out...

I wouldnt know why your subsequent delivery quantity is miscalculated when you reported 10 more than ordered quantity. But i hope you are aware that the subsequent delivery quantity will be calculated on what will be the final reported finished quantity. Therefore in your case the quantity will be calculated for 110 numbers instead of 100..
