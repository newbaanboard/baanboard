Is the sister plant in the same country, and does it share the same logistics company and enterprise unit? If so, it may be possible to just use the sister plant as a work centre in your normal routing. Since FP5 there have been many options for handling subcontract work and the handling of the materials used in it. If you have Infor Xtreme support cover, I suggest going to the website and downloading the FP5 User's Guide to Subcontracting, and then reviewing the options.
