Leslie,

Are you looking to undo the confirmation or prevent it from happening in the future?

You may have two different problems.

If you just want to unconfirm the MRP purchase and production orders, evertsen is correct.

If you want to stop this in the future, check with your administrator and limit the access to the sessions timrp1220m000 and timrp1230m000. These sessions allow for the confirmation of planned purchase and production orders.
