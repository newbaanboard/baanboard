This was one of my last fixes on B4_c MRP fixes... (just before I got kicked out......)

Solution 133089 is the one you'll probably need


MRP processes all items on a planninglevel. When a manufactured item is processed, the BOM is exploded and the components lowlevelcode is updated.

Here comes the technical part: MRP started with an 'as prepared set' on all planninglevels... Now the 'prepared set' is re-read for every planninglevel and thus including the 'updated items' from the previous level'....
