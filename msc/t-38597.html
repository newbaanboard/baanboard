Expanding the period is possible, it is just changing the start date of the next period in the period table but I don't think that is an ideal solution. It is possible to close a production order with a back date. But I am not sure about invoicing. Since Baan has been shut-down and no work is done in July as of now, why don't you reset the system date in Baan server to say30th June 6 pm and do only the pending works? But you must quickly complete everything before the time elapse. For example, the closing date cannot be before the completion date and so you can't reset again and again. You can select an appropriate date, may be 27 th so that you have enough time to complete. As a precaution you can keep the July periods closed.
