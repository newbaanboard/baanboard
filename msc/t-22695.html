If you can produce the error again by running the transfer session, then quickly take a look at the last few lines in log.oracle8 (assuming you are using oracle - it will be log.something according to your database) you will see which reference it is complaining about - these lines will have your loginID, so they will be quite obvious to spot. With that you should be able to create the necessary records and proceed onwards.
