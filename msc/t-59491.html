I tried transferring a range of Planned Purchase Orders (for the same Buy-from BP) to Purchase Orders in LN FP6 and they combined into one Purchase Order as long as the following fields on the Planned Order were the same:

Purchase Order Type
Buyer
Planner

If any of the fields were different, separate Purchase Orders were created.

Hope this helps.
