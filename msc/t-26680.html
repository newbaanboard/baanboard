24th April 2006, 19:33
If Baan finds a phantom item in the BOM of a manufactured item, it uses the BOM of that phantom item to create the Estimated Material list for the manufactured item. Because you have 3 different phantoms, you will get the BOM for all 3 phantoms in your Estimated Material list, independently from each other. Therefore, the common raw material is listed 3 times.

Hope this explains it.

-tom

The componets mentioned in BOM of a Manufactured item, can be a sub-assembly or ram material.

While calculating estimated material, the non-phantom componets and lower level products of phantom componets, are sorted and combined per operation,item,w/h,length,width. reason being the same item may apear when phantoms are used.

Keeping item same, if any of the parameter (i.e operation,w/h,length or width)changes, then an additional estimated material line will be created for the same item.
