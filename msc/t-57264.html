Hi Szuch,

1stly maintain a item code for scrap item. at the time of BOM maintain in the Main item, just insert a 2nd line for Scrap item code with -ve qty.

at the time of Production order -ve qty line will be automatically generated in Estimated lines, at the time of Production Order Releasing System automatically generated a extra Warehouse receipt Order. After ROC the Production order just Process the 2nd Warehouse receipt Order.
