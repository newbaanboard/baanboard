First maintain quality combination for your item with tick in qm only recommends.
create prd. order.
it will create inspection order(routing TI)
Process a lot of 100 pcs recommended quantity is 50 pcs ok & 50 pcs as rej
Complete & Process your ins. order dont close it.
Then go to report operation completed session during entering qty in completed field system will pop message quality mgt recommend 50 approved & 50 rejected.
now you can enter as per this result or overwrite whatever the result is.

without tick in qm only recommends
as per quality result report operation completed field will take result approved by quality mgt.
