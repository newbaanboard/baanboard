under project control there is a maintain activities and act relationships. float is defined as the number of days between activities. the activities can tie to items through BOMs and routings or through Module Planning.

Are you trying to schedule / simulate a project schedule without allowing the amount of float time defined by the network plan? Try reducing float time in the activity relationships to zero and rerun PRP.
