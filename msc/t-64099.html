Hi,

The Complete Production Order Cycle is as below :

1. Create Production Order or Transfer it from MPS/MRP
2. Release Production Order and Print Order Documents (Material List, Routings...)
3. Complete all operations for the Production Order. In this step , the system will ask you to backflush (materials & hours) automatically based on BOM or you can issue actual material & book actual hours manually.
4. Once all operations are completed, then you can post the inventory to your FG Warehouse.
5. Close the order.
