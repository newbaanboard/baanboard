I assume you have lot control turned on (otherwise you would not be able to track what is old or is not old). If you wanted to manually take them out of production you could transfer them to another warehouse (non-nettable) that is not available to MRP. I assume that is what you would be doing with overage inventory anyway, to avoid accidental usage.
