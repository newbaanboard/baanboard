You second scenario will never work, since you need to define way too many logical table links.
Your first scenario however should work. This is what multi site planning is all about. I cannot determine what you have done wrong in your set up, did you define the appropiate supplying rules?
basically you should have 2 logistic companies, in which the demand is created (drp or sales), and the third logistic company, which should take care of the production. The production planning therefore will only take place in this third logistic company, there will be no aggregated production planning from the other 2 companies.
I understand this will not help you sufficiently, ut in my opinion your first scenario will be supported 100% by Baan, if the setup is done correctly!
Good luck!
