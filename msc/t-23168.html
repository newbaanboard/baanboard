I noticed this problem with Baan 5.0c. I developed customization for automatic lot generation and I faced the backflushing problem.

I wasn't so sure what the problem was with my customization or Baan standard procedure. I tested manually Baan and saw the actual problem. If you put little amount (i.e. 0,0001) of finished good in "Report Orders Completed", backflushing of purchased material will make a mistake.

Only thing I could do is that I am going to do manual backflushing at the end of production order or I am going to exclude the posibility of doing backflushing at all.
