Your problem, I believe, is inside the MRP session, not capacity plans or Baan tables. In running MRP, the program builds a table of orders for each item in calculating demand. When you have too many orders this internal table overflows and you receive the error. I've run into this problem with extremely deep BOM's that create multiple orders for some common items (screws being a good example).

If the demand is real, then making the item a floor stock item will remove the problem, as you will not be generating orders for it.

Expect Baan to come back with documentation of the limit on the number of orders rather than a fix.
