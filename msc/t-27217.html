Very old question, but now I have just run into this trouble,
maybe answer helps others (working on old Baan IV):
Check "Min. No's of Days for Demand History" timps100.mddh at Plan Code.
It should be less than past period ("Ref Date" - "Plan Start Date").
Too big faulty value blocks generation of Demand Plan.
