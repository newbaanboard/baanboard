Dear lli-baan,

Even if BaanERP can support those characters, it's still a good practice to only include alphabets and numbers in item code to minimise confusion for end users. End users usually only remember the alphabets and numbers on top of their heads, and only use alphabets and numbers in communicating item codes with other users. Saying, "I need item ABC1234" is much easier than saying, "I need item ABC dash 123 dash 4", for example

Rgds,
Paul
