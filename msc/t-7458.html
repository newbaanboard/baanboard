We too are running BaanIVc4 NAS, and are in the process of implementing Purchasing Supply Chain (Sales Supply Chain launched 6 months ago). My own experience shows that you can't have a Delivery Schedule (862OUT) without having first set up the Purchase Schedule (830OUT), which itself must be linked to a supply chain contract. This also applies on the Sales side, no Shipping Schedule (862IN) allowed without first defining the Sales Schedule (830IN).

If you try to insert a delivery schedule it will error with message "Schedule not found" referring to the purchase schedule.
