Hi Angela,
First you would be getting the planned purchase orders as per your planning setting from the demand you have booked in the system (in terms of sales orders / quotations / extra demand / forecast, etc.). You can confirm and transfer them to make them purchase orders. Or you may create the POs either may be manually (as indicated by you in your post) for many items with fixed delivery period.
After that when you take a weekly planning run, if the demand is more BaaN V planning engine would give some exception messages, etc., which you may ignore.
For shortage quantity, BaaN V planning engine would generate additional planned purchase orders, which you can confirm and transfer to make the purchase orders.
This cycle can continue forever (except for the initial PO making).
