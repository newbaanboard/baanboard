System will generate only three orders.
1) From warehouse to shop floor warehouse.(Transfer)
2) From shop floor warehouse to work center.(Issue)
3) From workcenter to warehouse.(Receipt)

Now yuor question is, why system is not generating warehouse transfer order for W001 to W002

Let us consider
Parent Item is X, & child item is Y
Operations are 10 & 20

When you completing operation 10 what item your getting? Neither X nor Y, it is in between X & Y.

Because after 10 th operation is completed there is value addition on item Y therefor u can not say that item as Y & you can not say that item as X because 20 th operation u have not completed yet.

Warehouse order is generated only when there is some item.

But in above case there is no item is manufactured between op 10 & 20. therfore system is not generating any warehouse order.

I think this will resolve ur problem.
