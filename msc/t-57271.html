You can define modulesfor projects in the Project Control (PCS) module in BaanERP Manufacturing.

If you select the Fixed Plan Quantity check box in the Module Planning (tipcs4520m000) session, you can

box, BaanERP determines the required quantity based on the BOM.

BaanERP determines the date of the requirement on the basis of the network planning in the Project

If you plan a customized item with order-based planning , the plan quantity of the module is the basis for the
generation of planned orders.

If you want to generate orders on the basis of the module planning, the item must have an item master
plan. You must not use this master plan for master-based planning; the master plan is only used to
temporarily store the requirements.

If you transfer planned orders from Baan Enterprise Planning to the execution level , BaanERP
