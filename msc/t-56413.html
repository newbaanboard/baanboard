The Sheet metal when purchased was taken for Cenvat entry & Rg23A was updated. The sheet metal is now issued against Production order.
Cut pieces or rather scrap generated after operation on sheet metal, is the bi-product of the Production Order, and these can not be taken into RG23A again.
It does not happen in BaanIV & LN6.1
If the scrap is in good condition, then you can use it by issuing against another production order. And if you want to Sale it as scrap, then just thake RG1 manual entry & against Sales Order-Sales Invoice-Excise gatepass you sale it.
