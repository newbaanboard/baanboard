Hello,

You can also check the session, planned inventory transactions for getting pending purchase order details.

The session code is whinp1400m000. In the Order Origin field, you can specify Purchase Order in To and From field as given in the attachment.

If you look at the session help, it gives you useful information.

NOTE: One minor caveat of this session is, if you have pending purchase orders of cost items, it may not appear in the report.

Hope this helps.
