It is not possible to do so. Basically, you want the material issued as you need and not at the start date. One way is to create 4 operations and in BOM maintain the 4 line with each line linked to 4 operations. You have to make sure that you have defined routing correctly so that material appear as needed. I think this may give you what you are looking for.
