First of all, i want to share my view on EDM

i. Create all the item in E-item session.
ii. Create revision of all the e-items and in sequence the revision of child should be created first.
iii. Approve the revision of purchase items.
iv. Maintain the E-bom of mfg items and approve the revision of Mfg E-item.
v. Then follow the releasing & finalizing process.
