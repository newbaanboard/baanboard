It is very much possible to share Item General table -tcibd001
But it is mandatory to share following tables also
1) tcmcs023 -Item Groups
2) tcmcs006 -Unit Sets
3) tcmcs001- Units

subsequently if u have define Product type/ Product line or any other field than that table is also required to share.
