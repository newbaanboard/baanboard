I assume you have more than one supplier per item. The standard functionality of generating forecast is by item, so it will not generate the forecast. Question- How are you tracking inventory when you ship to your customer? I guess one way to generate the forecast is at item level and then when system generate the planned MPS order break it down by the supplier percentage. Also, I don't even recommend generating forecast at supplier level as that will be in accurate. The forecast tends to more in accurate as we go more fine. S my suggestion is generate the forecast at item level and then breakdown the planned orders by supplier percentage. We had used this in supplier scheduling where we gave one year forecast to our suppliers.
