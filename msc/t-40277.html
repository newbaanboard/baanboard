Anubhav,
I guess now you are referring to production orders now and probaly your questions stems from MPS. When a production order is created, at the same time, firm requirment are created for the components - like planned transaction. And based on whether one is backflshing or going for manual issuing the component requirement gets affected. So, in my opinion it is nothing to do with the parent scheduled receipt. All, it depeneds upon what is the open requirement. And as you know one can always manually go and zero out the component requirement, resulting in production variance. I feel, though your question is relevant, but component requirement and scheduled receipt are failry independent of each other.
