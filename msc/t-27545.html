IN LN MPS/MRP are being replaced by Master Planning and Order Planning. An item can be Master Planned (if you check Maintain Master Plan in Plan Items) as well as Order planned. Master Plan will result into Plans that can be converted into Planned orders while Order Planning will result into Planned Orders. Go thru the help of the sessions and you will follow the entire procedure.
