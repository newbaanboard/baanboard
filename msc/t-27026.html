Did you copy the new BOM over for the new revision? I can't vouch for LN6.1, but in IVc if you create a new revision, you have to copy both the new item and the new BOM (even if there are no actual changes to the BOM) for the revisions to show properly. Just copying/creating a relationship for the item isn't enough. The PBOM knows that there's a new revision but since it hasn't seen any detail on what the new revision is, it leaves the field blank.
