The reason for this message is that hours are booked for the production order. You can verify this is session "Hours Accounting by Production Order" (tihra1503m000).

If the hours are not yet processed, the can be deleted in session "Hours Accounting by Employee" (tihra1501m000).

(If you are on BaanIV the latter session is "Enter Hours Accounting" (tihra1101m000)).
