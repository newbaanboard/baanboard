What is the use of cost component in Baan system?
Paul P
25th August 2005, 06:54
It enables you to classify your production cost breakdown in very detailed manner. You need to ask your accounting consultant to ask the accounting manager at your client on how he/she would like the production cost to be broken down. From that information, you can structure the cost component in raw material/semi finished good/finished good as well as in work centre hourly cost and subcontracting cost to handle that need
