Bryan,

Just FYI:

In 5.0c and later, on production orders we have split the delivery date into:
- Requested Delivery Date, and
- (Re-)Planned Delivery Date

The first one remains unchanged.
I don't think it always matches with the Sales Order date. There may be other lead times in between. (Extra Lead Time and Safety Time, in Item Ordering Data, and Item Plan Data, all 5.0 c)
