30th December 2008, 11:59
For purchased items lot price works fine and enables users to get the online stock valuation at actual purchase cost (tdilc0401m000 ) (if u dont use surcharges ) and tally the same with postings in finance . U can track the variances at the time the purchase prices is varied or at the time it is consumed (as againtst FTP in which it is calculated at the time of purchase or at time purchase price is varied ) .

For manufactured items , I remember prduction receipt is calculated at standard cost and not actual cost . Please do good testing before u put it to use .

Lot result is nothing but variance between standard and actual cost . But it's calculated at the time of issue or purchase price change in tfacp1140s000 or tdpur4122m000 . So if u check the postings in finance report of tfltc0401m000 , the stock value will be actual purchase price .

I also recommend u do good testing o this .
