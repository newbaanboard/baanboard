When the WO(production order, also called work order) is in active status, you can't compare the Estimate and Actual hours. Because, at this moment, the Actual hours has not been reported, havn't been inputed for the WO.

The actual hours data has been key in in BP(people management) with (bptmm1111)session. or you can use the backflush function to report the hours automaticly. only after this action, the actual hours is available.
