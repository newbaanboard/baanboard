Hi,

The tick for Use Warehouse Order Price is defaulted to Yes/No based on various conditions which is based on the kind of Warehouse Order , Valuation method defined and a few other things.

Like for Purchase/Purchase Schedules its never used. For Warehouse to warehouse transfers, its not used in case of Shop floor warehouse for a floor stock item. For Project / Project manual orders its always used. Also, in case of Sales/Production, if the material is being returned or if its a WIP Transfer it will be used.

One thing what you can try is, you can use FTP valuation method for the Item-Warehouse. It will never be checked then. But again, keep in mind that it should not affect your on-going business logics.

Or the second way is to do a smaller customization to uncheck it before processing the warehouse order.
