Not sure if this is helpful. After one upgrade when we completed orders the utilization tables were not being updated. We got a fix, but I had to run tisfc1201m000(WARNING - it deletes all the records then rebuilds them). This was back when we were on 4c3. It was my understanding back then that when some operation was completed these records (tisfc011 table I remember) were deleted. This prevented them from appearing on the reports(Priority and Utilization). Sorry I do not remember more, but it has been at least 2 years since I looked at these tables. You might want to check tisfc011 after an operation is completed, see if it is gone from the table, and then see if it is on the report.

I did find one order on our system that had operation 10 completed. This operation was not in tisfc011 and did not come out on the utilization report. I think we are on SP16 or 17. We have SP19 to install in the next month or so.
