Hi,
As a last resort, you could try the following.
In the Maintain Item Data session for the item in question, go to Form 5 and then change the value of field [Order Qty Dep. Rout.] to "Yes". Next in the session Maintain Routing Codes by Item change the value in the field [Up to Order Quantity], for Routing Code 001, to 1 and for 002 keep it at 99999999...
