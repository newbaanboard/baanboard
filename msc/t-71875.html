I am not an Expert Manufacturing guy but here are some pre-requisites for closing PCS projects:

-All the purchase orders for PCS items (customized items) should have processed
-Production orders of PCS items should be closed
-All the sales order lines of PCS items should be processed
-All the sub-contracting PO's of the project should be fully processed
-There should not be any planned inventory transactions against project
