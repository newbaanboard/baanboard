Since you already ran out of sequence number for this project.. i guess what you could is to change the Project Type of the current project to "Subproject" and then create two additional projects, one(another subproject) to record general cost and the other(Main project) to act as the parent to these two subprojects(use session Maintain Project Structure:{tipcs2115m000})...

If you do go ahead with this solution, please dont forget to close all three projects(subprojects: first, main project: last) if you want the costs to hit your accounts...

There might be another option i.e to pass general cost entries directly in one of the finance module sessions.. but i am not sure which...
