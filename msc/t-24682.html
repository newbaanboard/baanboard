We did this once and it wasn't really that successfull due to the fact that in general the item table and some of its fields gets updated very frequently by various processes. ie. Stock levels tiitm001.stoc updated by inventory transactions.

This means that to find anything you have to sift through a lot of audit data and you also have to be careful about the amount of disk the audit is consuming.

Try it out but keep an eye on it.
