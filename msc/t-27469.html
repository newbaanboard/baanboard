Hi Sandy,

Estimated qty : The qty of material estimated to be required to produce the goods
To issue: The qty of material currently being requested by production department
To issue by warehouse: Qty of material already requested by production department but hasn't been delivered by warehousing department
Subsequent delivery: The remaining qty of material that might be requested later by production department, partially or in full

Paul

Hi Sandy,
If the Backflushing is set to 'Y', then the 'To Issue' gets grayed out and the Qty in that field moves to 'Subs Delv'. (This will happen even if you have partial issues from inventory (Actual Qty) or Partial Qty in 'To Issue by W/h'.
If it is not doing that, There might be a bug.
Which service pack are you on. I remember there was a related change in SP 13 and SP 18.
