Yes but this means a lot of work.

You can expend the domain, but you also have to enlarge all forms and reports.

There is a solution for german customers having some tools to check whether the forms and reports have enough space to enlarge the domain from e.g. 6 digits to 7.

You also have to check things like hard coded max numbers in forms like 999999 ... you have to change this manually ... better is to use the functionality max in accordance to domain lenght.

Please find further information here: http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=44151&highlight=enlarging
