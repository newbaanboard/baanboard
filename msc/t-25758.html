Hi, lordofe,

When determining main measurement unit of an item in BaanERP, somehow accounting got the upper hand. This is because in most cases, it all comes down to what measurement unit the accounting department would rather use to account for the material cost. If your accounting dept has chosen to value those boards in sq ft (which can be a little tricky during cycle counting, an activity which should be monitored closely by accounting), then unfortunately BaanERP will still make production material request in sq ft. However, you still can issue the material from storage in number of sheets, even though the request is in sq ft
