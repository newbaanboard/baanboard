HI,

You can't define several machines againest one and one operation..... What u can do is maintain more than operation say 10,20,30 with the same TASK & same W/C but with the different MACHINES.

Eg.

Operation10- TASK1,W/C1------MACH1
Operation20- TASK1,W/C1------MACH2
Operation30- TASK1,W/C1------MACH3


May be ths will help u in mapping ur requirement.
