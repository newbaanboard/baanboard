Dear Pegas,

Routing & BOM are set up to calculate the standard price. By standard price, it means that you agree to fix (standardise) the value of the product at that price, for certain period of time which is deemed appropriate by your accounting dept.

Now, during execution of production order, you may find that actual production cost will differ than the one implied by the standard price. However, since you agreed early on about the standard price, BaanERP will proceed and value the finished good still at that standard price. BaanERP will also automatically book the difference as variance.

That's how standard costing work
