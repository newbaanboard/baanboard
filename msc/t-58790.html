Hi,
One thing is not clear that why you want to generate planned order for A as you dont want to maintain inventory of A. This is not possible in system. System only create planned orders for inventory items. Hence there is two work around solutions for this scenario.
1. Make A phantom item. (Even there is option to maintain phantom item inventory.) Them when you will create production order, that time in estimated line you will find the child items of A. Planning engine also will generate planned order for child items only, not of A. While issuing you will only issue the child items.
2. Other view is make A kit item and maintain child items in kit component. Then you can find A in estimated line of production order. But planning engine will generate planned orders of child items, not A. A will not be a inventory item, while issuing system will consider child items for outbound.
