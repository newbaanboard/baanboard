Baan EP does have a finite capacity scheduling engine. It also considers constrainted materials. What it doesn't do that SCS Scheduler does is to optimize multiple constraints. Now, having said that, Baan EP can arrive at a better result my running multiple interations of the plan as well as building a constrainted plan on the basis of an existing Master Plan (as opposed to an infinite plan). Doing this will allow the planner to arrive at a more optimized solution.
