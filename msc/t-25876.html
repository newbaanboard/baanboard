The Time Horizon indicates the period in which Inventory Transactions must fall in order to be included in the MRP Run. The Time Horizon starts with Current Date.

The MRP Sales Forecast means additional Demand. If you have demand in December 2006, this will not show with Planned Inventory Movement as you will only go out 180 from current date in you MRP Run. If you want to see December 2006 you would have to change your Time Horizon.
