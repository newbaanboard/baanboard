Hi,
Backflushing is done "Only for the Items present in Estimated Materials". if you want to replace Item B with Item D for a Particular Item, You need to change the Estiamted Material of the Production order( Item B should be replaced with Item D).

After this if you backflush, Item D will be Backflushed.

But, there is no automatic Mechanism in Baan to check the Availabilty and replace with Alternative Items.
