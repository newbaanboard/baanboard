We don't do DC Shipments but we use Sales Schedules for everything we do, and table ssc003 has the same ssc003.dref field that ssc033 has. Many of our Non-edi customers give us multiple PO's and we lump them together into a sales schedule. We often put the Customers PO Number into the ssc003.dref field and have not had any problems doing this. I also use the ssc003.ican field for various customer related information without any problems.
