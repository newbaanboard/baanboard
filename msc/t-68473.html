When you convert 2 different units measuring 2 physical quantities such as ton (weight) and litre (volume), you can set up conversion factor at either item level / item group level. e. g.
- in case of paints of various colours which are grouped in one item group. Even though their colours different, their specific gravity would be same hence you can define the conversion factor at item group level
- in case of multiple different liquids such as petrol, diesel, edible refined oil, etc you can define the conversion factor for a single item
Depending upon your requirements, you can use the conversion factor. The session help in this case is quite useful for you
