I know two ways to update the estimated material of a planned production order in BaanIV. First if you change a field like ordered quantity, version, ... the system deletes estimated material and production plan and rebuilds is.
Second is a session, it might be running in job mode. I do not longer have access to a BaanIV menu, so I don't know the exact name in your system. In InforLN it called Reprocess Production Orders tisfc1203m000. It does the same: delete current estimated material and production plan and rebuild it from BOM and routing.

This must be a customization. Update of BOM doesn't change any order whether it be MRP or real production order . That is why we have net change MRP functionality to address such changes. Assuming you are changing a component in BOM, so in your cards it is going to change the production requirement for all the production order that system has?
