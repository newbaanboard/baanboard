We have a forecast for a Family of items of say 100 units/period. The Family item has a synthetic breakdown and is disaggregated into a number of top-level items for Order planning.

Sales orders are received that are not in the synthetic. So there is no forecast to be absorbed. So the customer will be in a position where they have a total requirement for the Family of perhaps 110 units – the original forecast plus orders for items that were not included. Thus capacity and materials planning will be over-planned to some degree.

Has anyone else come across this problem, and ideally have a solution/workaround?
