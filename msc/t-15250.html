Baan 4 has only 1 standard cost field per item so you cannot have an individual standard cost per finance company. I am also not aware of a way to share only certain fields of a table.

You could use Baan Exchange to update from 1 company's table to another. I think you can use "by audit" so that for example if you create a new item in company 1, it can be replicated to company 2. However, I have never used this.

When item master is shared , the cost price , inventory at the item level , average price , purchase price etc will all be common for all units . So u may either not use this functionality or may eliminate the effects of this by customization or may not share the table itself .

There is no facility to share fields of a table.
