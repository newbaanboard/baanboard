Hi,

What about the inventory of the estimated materials? Was that reduced?

Things to check are -

1. Check if Direct Process Warehouse Orders is checked or not.
2. Direct Initiate Inventory Issue is checked or not.

If these 2 are checked for the item, the estimated materials would have been issued automatically.

Also, I guess if you are using Automatic Backflushing Method, the issue of materials will be automatic. You can find the Parameter in SFC/Shop Floor Control Parameters. Whenever you complete the operations, materials will be issued.
