Hi,

I am a consultant in South Africa and have worked with Scheduler for a few years now, including some implementations. Firstly, unless you move to BaanIVc4, there is no standard integration available. There are several options for BaanIVc4. The latest integration offering uses OpenWorld and ITK (integration toolkit) which I am told is database independent. If you cannot move off BaanIVc2 you will have to pretty much write your own interface, as there has never been any standard integration for that version.
Sorry for the bad news, hope this helps.
For more details you should contact the guys at BaanSCS support in Canada.

Chris Holdsworth - South Africa
