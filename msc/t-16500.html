Baan does not allow the copying of non-physical items, and therefore won't allow creation of BOM copy data for such an item. If you need to maintain this set-up, you will have to recode your level 2 item as a physical item, do the copying, then change it back again. Tedious and open to human error.

Note also that a non-physical item won't copy over as part of the BOM. When you try to copy the BOM for "B", the copy method for "C" will come up as "No Copy Action" and you can't change it. We use this for hardware and other small expensed items. They exist on the EBOM for reference, but they don't show up in production, so we don't have to inventory them.
