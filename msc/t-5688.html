Just prior to entering the Test Data tcqms1115m000, you have to go into Maintain Standard Inspections tcqms1120m000, select the inspection you are performing and click on Special and then Maintain Inspection Orders tcqms1100s000. In this session, you need to change the quantity to your test sample size.

If you do this, your test sample size can be different than the receipt size.
