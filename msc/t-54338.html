Dear Nishant,


First maintain ur main item and BOM item.In warehouse procedure(Manufacturing Issue) Activity keep Inspection Applicable.Now Maintain ur Charasteristics,Aspects,Test all qm Master data.maintain Quality Id and Quality Id Easy Entry.Now In Quality Combination keep origin (Material BOM) and maintain the header item (Prod.Order Will raise on this item)and Sub Item (which is BOM Item).Now validate it.

Now raise production order and issue the material.Material will not be issued unless u complete warehouse inspection and system will not allow u to complete warehouse ispection and give u the massage " Order SFC Prodduction has to be inspected by Quality Management".

Now goto QMS " Standerd Inspection" Origine"Material BOM" U will find the inspection order .Now complete it and the material will be issued.

Hi everyone,

In standard Baan Ln 6.1 while doing Warehouse transfer(manual) there will be no Inspection order even if we maintain in Quality Combination(warehouse transfer).It is only for automatic transfer not manual.
For Material BOM it will generate inspection order while outbound of material from stores through Production orders.
For Routing(TI) it will generate inspection orders for inprocess inspection.
