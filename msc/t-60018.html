Hi,

You can maintain operation related overhead costs (hourly basis) at ticpr1150m000. Apart from there if you want to maintain some fixed surcharges against the item then you can maintain it at ticpr1110m000 session and you also need to check the 'Surcharges by Item'checkbox provided at Item-Costing session ticpr0107m000 under 'Options'heading.
