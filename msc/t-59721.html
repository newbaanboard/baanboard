Hi,

For issue goods -

Warehouse activities should not be automatic. During running the session 'whinh4525m100 (Picking List)' select your preferred location from 'From Location' field.


For storing goods after production -

Warehouse Activities should not be automatic as we need to choose specific location for storing good.
During performing Report operation complete, once the operation is completed, during saving the same one pop-up window appears as 'Automatically confirm receipt when activating warehouse order inbound line?' --> select 'No'.

Go to warehousing order--> warehouse receipts --> Receipt Lines --> go to 'Item,Location' tab --> there you can find a field mentioned 'To Location' available --> select your location and save it --> confirm the warehouse receipt --> process rest of the warehousing activities.


Hope it helps.
