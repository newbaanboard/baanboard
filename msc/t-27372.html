Hi, I recently helped with a 4b2 AGS0 to 4c4 AGS2 migration. To avoid losing the AGS tables, you have to install 4c4 ags2 then migrate. Are you planning to upgrade hardware? We acquired a new box which meant we could avoid retaining the old b2 objects and VRCs on the shiny new 4c4 box. Also it meant far less risk to the business of messing with the old live box until everything was tested properly, plus the fallback for cutover meant we could revert to the old system easily.
