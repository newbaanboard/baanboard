Hi,

You can copy that item to create another fresh item and change the inventory unit of the new item as per your requirement and for the old item whatever the inventory is available at warehouse, once that being consumed fully, block that old item for any transactions and keep using the new item therefore.
Changing of unit at production order is not possible as it picks only the unit being maintained at Item General.
