Hi,

a practicable workaraound to do purchase schedules is to maintain a purchase order with first order line with the total quantity. If your customer is giving you a delivery shedule you have to add new order lines for each delivery date and you have to reduce the first order line. So you have the whole overview of the total quantity all the time.
