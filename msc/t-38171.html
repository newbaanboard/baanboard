You raise a good point - the users should not be able to report production without the proper release of the corresponding materials from inventory.

I have seen the issue several times where production is reported and the user has elected to ignore the backflush procedure.

With a modification in place, you could reconcile the inventories before the POST production procedure, but my fear would be adhoc additions of inventory to the system leading to bigger issues when you do the EOM inventory reconciliation.
