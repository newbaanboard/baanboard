Answer to part of your question:-

The frozen period is calculated from the MRP run date (system date) of the particular item, not the start date of the MRP run.
The frozen period also includes this date as the first date frozen.

Do you set your frozen periods up in your SCH contracts using Calender or Working days? If it is Working days, are your weekends set as working days?
