Martin Jung
2nd October 2002, 12:56
The percentage is related to the safety stock of the item. It allows the system to 'work' with a certain percentage of the safety stock before generating a reschedule message. For example: the factor for reschedule-out messages is set to 30%, safety stock of an item is 100. Baan will accept an inventory on hand down to 70 before generating a reschedule-out message.
