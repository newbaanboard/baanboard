Hi Renee:
There are many advantages of ERPLN over BaaN IV in SFC module. Very difficult to specify all of them. However few of them are given below:

Item-Production Dashboard
Order Grouping and you can execute the business procedures by order groups
Apart from Backflush, now you have foreflush as well
You can view work-center queues and then based on them take corrective / preventive actions
Single piece production of unique configuration (Unit Effectivity)

There are many more, which could not be listed.
