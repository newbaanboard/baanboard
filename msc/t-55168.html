The number of days needed to setup Enterprise Planning varies with each situation (i.e. # of warehouses & facilities, complexity of your product, ...). Five days ... assuming that the estimate has been given with that in mind could be right.

I will say one thing and that is to be careful not to make it too complex. You should have an idea of what the results will be and what your staff will be able to handle. You do not want to disillusion everyone by implementing something that does not reflect reality or the physical capabilities of your company.

If you'd like further advise or independent help with it then let me know.

Note: The training time required would really depend on the number of dept/staff as there may be different needs for each area.
