Well, I am not sure about 4C4 (I guess its your version), but in LN, we have faced some situations where the ordered quantity was say 100 and user delivered 50 out of 100. What we follow is, we return the issued material, we reset the operations and complete them for 50 quantity, we change the ordered quantity to 50 and we close the order.

Now, in your case, you have complete quantity being rejected, then I would suggest, you can return all the issued material, reset all the operations, and you can wait for the next production order of that item, because it's not possible to change the item and neither its possible to cancel the order.
