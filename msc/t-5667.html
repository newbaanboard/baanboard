If you change the delivery date at the sales order line for a MRP item, you will get a rescheduling message that tells you to reschedule existing SFC-orders (or other kind of ordres). The change in planning of the order have to be done manually in standard Baan (it's possible to make customizations to do resceduling / replanning automatically).
