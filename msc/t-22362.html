Hi sathasivan

If the extra unit you completed has been posted to the inventory and not release the inbound. You can do this.
Go to report production order completed on field Quantity to deliver enter -1, leave the field completed order to "No", then click on the Rep Complete button, your unit will be there(if it hasn't be realeased the inbound). Just selected and delete it. Then you can go to the operations and change the quantity completed to 10 starting from the last operation to the first one.
