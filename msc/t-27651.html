You MPS horizon is based on the MPS parameter Time Fence multiplier (timps0100m000). The value of this field, multilied by the Time Fence per item gives your MPS horizon. In "normal" circumstances (depending on your business requirements) the horizon is about min. 1 year but prehaps you can lay a bit with this parameter to shorten the horizon and see what the performance results are.
