Once order status is completed, tisfc001, tisfc010 order status, ordered, delivered, backflush qty changes. In finance- Completion, surcharge and Production receipt commitments triggerd. For changing the status all these entries need to be reversed.

Access to Global completion sessions, in future may be restricted. If volume of orders is not high, treat the present completed orders as short closed and create fresh order for required quantities.
