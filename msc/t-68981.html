If the MRP stopped all of a sudden, it means some data has been changed.
Probable reasons could be:
> Booking of too big a sales order (by accident) or
> Modification of BOM data / routing data for some item/s or
> Safety stock / planning data of some item/s is changed or
> last is there could be server issues in terms of database / log files, etc.
You to check each reason and eliminate the probable causes. I know its a long drawn process, but since very little information is available.
