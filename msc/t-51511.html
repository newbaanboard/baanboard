Hi:
For unapproved orders, instead of Sales Orders, you can use Sales Quaotations as one of the work-arounds.
Second work-around is at the time of creating sales order, use a warehouse which is not considered in enterprise planning. Before approving the sales order, change the warehouse to your standard warehouse, which is considered in enterprise planning and then approve the sales order.
