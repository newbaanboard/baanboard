When using rework orders, the standard routing en BOM are deleted. The BOM is filled with the manufactured material to rework. So the productionorder material is also the BOM material. It's intention is to fill the BOM further with the rework needed materials as positive amount into the BOM en the materials demontage as a negative amount. After that prepare the routing for the rework order and print-relaese the order.

As Janneman said, the routing is deleted when you flag the order as "rework".
You'll have to go to Maintain Production Planning and insert the necessary routing steps (tasks) required to do the rework.
