Hi,
Re-Order Point is generally used in SIC planning. In LN when you run the SIC planning engine it checks for the demand, supply and Inventory during order horizon. i.e. demand - supply - Inventory. If the net result is below re-Order point then it generates a Purchase Advice/ Production advice depending on Item type.
