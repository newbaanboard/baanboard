I am confused. Are you trying to produce 2 components with a single production order or are you trying to return an item to inventory when you report a production completion?

I had a similar problem in a casting company. We needed to return the forms around the casting when we moved it to a particular operation. This would make them available for the next casting. The concept is similar to how people handle tool constraints in finite planning.
In our case, we listed the form on the bill of material as a positive number at operation 10 and a negative number at operation 100. This kept our costing even. When we reported operation 100 complete all parts were consumed except for the form. We manually entered a negative outbound and processed. It was clunky but operational.

Hope this helps.
