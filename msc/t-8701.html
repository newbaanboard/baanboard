Hi,

Standard cycle counting functionality inventorizes subassembly stock directly and not for the BOM items. However BOM item inventorization can be approached in the following ways.

1. Take the stock of subassembly and inventorize it. Then you can define the subassemblies as phantom items so that in production order it automatically explodes the bom of subassembly and consumes the BOM items.

2. The second option is to create a purchase order for subassembly for some notional supplier. You can receive the subassembly as purchase order receipts and then use the option "post to components" so that the PO receipts will be made for the BOM items and inventorizes it. In this method, purchase order is used as a proxy for cycle counting order.
