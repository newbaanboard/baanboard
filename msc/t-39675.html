Dear DK,
Why should you be bothered of the sequence of data entry by the production
guys ? The global planning runs take care of all the mismatches in data entry time, as long as your cutoffs are correct. Best solution is to centralise Data Entry work ( which I did ).

More Raw Material in the system means that backflusing is low meaning-> Incorrect Data Entry / incorrect BOM / incorrect routing etc. ( you have to find the exact reason ).
Another reason can be excess inventory transfers from Raw Materail Stores, but chances of this happening are slim ( there are stores guys who count the material before giving to Production guys).

Refer to the thread which has "Production Receipt" in it's name in this forum, which addresses the same problem.

If you put this kind of a restriction, you might have problems in getting your Finished product out of the door.
