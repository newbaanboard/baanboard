Hi Sandy,
A simple explanation on the difference between these two would be that...

MRP calculates materials based on certain calculated figures that we set in the Item master(safety stock, minimum order qty, order interval etc) together with requirements generated in various sessions like Sales Orders and Production orders. This is then used in a caclulation process called "Generate Planned MRP orders" where the system gives you what and how much of an item should be ordered.

Replenishment order on the other hand is a means to replenish the stock in different warehouses by transfering materials from one warehouse to another. This however is done manually as there is no calculation process involved here. A Replenishment order is made and then delivered from one warehouse and received to another warehouse.
