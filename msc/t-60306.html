Hi Quelle,

Selecting CHM (Change Management) means that you will be using an Engineering Change approval procedure for the Engineering Item.

The approval procedure is defined in the Object Data Management module under Change Management and consists of the following steps:

Requesting a Change
Registering the Change
Configuring the Change
Reviewing the Change
Accepting the Change
Implementing the Change
