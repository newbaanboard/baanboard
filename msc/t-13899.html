Dear Hendra,

You can not set slack time, only calculate it. If the production order of 1000 pcs is due in 2 weeks and to produce 1000 pcs you need 1 week, then the slack time is 1 week. The more slack time you have, the more you can afford not to start production right now. Slack time of 1 week, for example, means that you don't have to start production now, but the latest time you can delay it to is 1 week from now

Rgds,
