Hi,

Have you reported the quantities at order level as well? In that case,

Go to the report order completed session, and put negative quantity (-1) in the additional qty to deliver.
Now, as the 'On Last Operation' qty is greater than the 'Delivered' qty, you will find the option 'Decrease Qty Operations' enabled in the specific menu.
If you use the 'Decrease Qty operations' on the Report Orders Completed session, it will decrease the quantities on all operation lines also. No need to change it manually on all the operation lines. Also, the 'Backflush Done' is decreased automatically.

If its not the case, some snapshots can provide some hints.
