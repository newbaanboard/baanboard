In Baan Closing of production orders means, Closing of order for all practical purposes./ also Finanlcial closing . So to close the production order all the open inspection orders shoul dbe with the status "Processed" However you can have open (Free) inspection orders with quantity zero . These inspection orders will be delete automatically when compleitng the order itself.
