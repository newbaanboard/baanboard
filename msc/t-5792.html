Filp is correct. A "Phantom" is an item that appears in the product structure, but which is not seen as a separate manufactured item for production. The item will be included in the product structure, but will not be stocked in a warehouse. However, its components will be included as material requirements. HOWEVER, it is not necessary for a phantom to have components!!! You can define an item in the Item Master on form 5 as a phantom and it can simply be a component of the product you are building.

This allows you to have the item and its cost on the product's BOM, without carrying that item in inventory.
