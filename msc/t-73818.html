The production department, according to the 10.5 release notes, is part of improvements
to the Repetitive Manufacturing module.
It is a first step to enable the mapping of the commonly used hierarchy “site, building,
work center, machine”.
It groups work centers, work cells, and repair cells. You can maintain
production departments in session Production Departments (tirou2100m000).
More about the session can be found in
https://docs.infor.com/ln/10.5/en-us/lnolh/help/ti/rou/tirou2100m100.html?zoom_highlight=production%2Bdepartment.

However, Production Departments not only affect Repetitive Manufacturing.
They work the same way that Departments used to in previous versions, and
are mandatory for the use of work centers in Shop Floor Control.
The main difference with the way Departments used to work is that now you can
link multiple work centers to a department, as well as work cells and repair cells.
Production Departments work as a grouping of work centers that may be in the same physical location.
They define the default calendar for its work centers. The production department can
also have a different enterprise unit (therefore, a different financial company) than its work centers.

The use of the concept depends entirely on the layout of your factory. If the concept is not
needed, you can create just one production department to group all work centers.
