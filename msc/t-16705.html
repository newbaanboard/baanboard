You have not indicated baan version you are working with. My comments apply to IV4c4 -- probably same for V.

Baan is very flexible with uom's (inventory, sales and purchase) The help screens are very good.

In one of our operations we use sales quantity units and sales prices of pc (ea), kg, t, m. With conversion factors set up to handle the translation. We may price in one unit and sell in another. eg. for same item: 2,000 meter @ $2,000 tonne, 3 pc @ $8.00 kg, 200 meter @ $1,500 tonne

If ILC is being used, one point that may not be so readily evident is in order to automatically select for outbound the item may have to be "stored" in the unit that the sales quantity is expressed in. If sales unit sft then storage unit sft.

Hope this helps
