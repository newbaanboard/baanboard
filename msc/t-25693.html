CTP Fence: If it is to short you never get a ATP message cause after CTP Fence everything is possible (no check after that Fence)


For BCC Check following:
-Workcenter tirou501 check "critical in masterplanning" YES
-Plan item Check "capacity CTP" YES
-Ressources cprpc2101s000 set "CTP critical"
-run tirou2230m000,
-proof by cpdsp4420m0000
-proof by tirou2530m000
