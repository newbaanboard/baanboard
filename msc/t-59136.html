Suppose you have a lead time of 10 days and a safety time of 2 days. Then if you have a factor of 2, then the system will consider the demand from now till 24 days. If you add a constant of 3, then it will consider 27 days. The constant typically reflects the order processing time and the factor is a multiplication factor on the lead time + safety time. Hope this helps.
