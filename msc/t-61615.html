Hi Raman,

To generate the storage inspection you have to create a quality group (qmptc0129m000) and link the items with this group, then create the quality ID (qmptc0110m000). now create the quality combination of that quality group and quality ID. after this you have to go through the session
(qmptc2120m100) click on new button - specifiy the item - save - and then click new on the line of storage inspection orders and fill the required fields. in secound tab specify the warehouse and location of item and finally process your storage inspection order.
