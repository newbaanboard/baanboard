Dear Satha,

You can keep such complicated work centre assignment of operators in BaanERP for recording purpose only. BaanERP will not actually use such complicated scheme to determine production order resource assignment. BaanERP only needs to know when work centre or employee are available, but won't go so far as to check which operator are available at which work centre when
