Standard price of manufactured items will always follow those implied by their BOM and routing.
There's no weighted average price of manufactured items in BaanIV. In BaanERP (i.e. version 5.x), it's possible to specify some manufactured items to be valued using Moving Average Unit Cost (MAUC) method. When valued this way, MAUC of manufactured item will be weighted average of its standard production cost and purchase cost (if the item is procured both ways)
