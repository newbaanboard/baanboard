There is no way to skip the As-Built registration, because without that you would not be able to do serial tracking. That is the crux I think.

Wonder if it is possible to create a negative inventory for serialized items?
A situation could be when you sell a serial, while you have to report the SFC order yet. With serials not in inventory that might work...

The “Register Serial Issue During As Built” parameter is about tracking component serial numbers during issuing the materials (outbound) and not about the end item receipt.

We have built a customized solution for this type of problem.

1) Custom software to generate serial numbers and serialized labels.
2) Custom software through ddc to record serial number travelling down production line (using stationary bar-code scanners)
3) Custom RF Shipping system to record serial numbers as they are shipped, and link them to sales orders.
4) Modify packing slips \ invoice reports to print serial numbers on document.
5) Custom tables with serial number master, transaction logs, etc.
