Dear Mohamed_alsadr,

In process industries like that, indeed it's a common occurence to have co-products beside the main product. In manufacturing industries (as opposed to process industries), co-products are rare, except in cases like injection moulding etc. Hence, for process industries, Baan created a special Baan Process module, which is available in BaanIV, but unfortunately unavailable in BaanERP.
