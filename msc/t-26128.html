Hi bvrao54,
There may be a simple reason why the problem of expired lines dissapear in the report. What's the value you enter in the print session for the Check Date? This field refers to the expiry in the BOM. If you leave it blank all lines of the BOM should appear on the report regardless to the expiry date. If a certain date is specified, all lines (expiry date > check date) will be printed. This is standard functionality. This option is not available in the Maintain BOM or Display BOM session.

By the way we use the copy functionality as well rather than to create a new BOM manually. But as I already said, manual postprocessing will be reqiured anyway.
