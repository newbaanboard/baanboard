Hi Doug,

Very tricky requirement you are asking for. The shopfloor planner has accepted the order by releasing the order with his current BOM and Routing.
Now you want to change these automaticly, which can impact the shopfloor planning significant.
When I was a shopfloor planner, I would not allow automaticly.
Stil I can imaging your requirement, but I think the only right way is to release the order as late as possible, thus when engineering is ready. When customers require a change afterwards, the order can be changed manually. For new orders for the same item, they will pick-up the changed BOM and routing.
