Jeff Ball
7th November 2014, 17:12
OK, that makes sense. If the item is set up with master planning, then the safety stock would have shown on the item master plan the next time the scenario was initialised. It should then have shown on the order plan if the master planning is an input to order planning.

By unchecking the 'also generate item master plan' box, you have taken master planning out of the equation entirely, so order planning sees the safety stock unconditionally
