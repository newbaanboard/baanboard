You may have one of the following issues:
When you run MRP how do you answer the question on form 2 "Maximum Number of MRP Orders per Day". If you answer more that one you may get more orders.
In the Item master what do you have for the Order Method? If it is Fixed Order Quatity and your requirement is greater that the fixed order quantity for that item you may get nore ordres.
In the item master what do you have for Maximum Order Quantity? If the requirement is greater that the maximum order quantity you may get more orders.

Check these things out.
