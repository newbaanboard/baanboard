Priority doesnt matter...

The quanity percentage of business does though. BaaN wont let you have a number greater than 100% in that field... If 2 suppliers have 50 percent for a part number, that means MRP is going to split the requirments 50/50, especialy if the priority is the same.

50/50 is the only example where the numbers for the part can be the same, unless you have used GTM to change the values on the form. In which case, users will get an error each time they look up that record.
