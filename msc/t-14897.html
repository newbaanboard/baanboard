I have run into this before, and as far as I know this is a standard problem. I feel it's a bug, because it allows you to enter into hours accounting, but generates an error on processing. Not good.

In Baan V they just remove the functionality of reporting to operations via hours accounting.

Since I have written several HRA automated processes, what I have done in the past is delink the processes. During automated processing, I can send the hours to hours accounting and the quantities to sfc, separate transactions. Probably not much help for someone directly using the Hours Accounting entry system, sorry.
