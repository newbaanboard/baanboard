E-BOM :- engineering boms are used to create bom for a new item & this is under controlled revision released (under development) or an existing item having any engineering change.


P-BOM :- whereas production boms are used for existing items and those bom are approved by Engineering & Production Dept.


Genric BOM :- Generic or general Bill of Materials (BOM) structures make˝ it possible to handle product variants in a convenient way.
User specifications are given dynamically, as the generic BOM is˝ exploded. The system will then generate a conventional BOM for this product variant.
