The field opcp shows a drop down Yes/No option in tihra1101m000. The fact that a zoom option was attached on the Form was probably a development mistake/try that was never removed, you can't zoom out from an enumerated yes/no type field.

What it indicates is if you want to mark that order complete, along with any quantities reported. This will not directly hit Report Operations Complete, you first have to 'Process Hours Accounting', then it will update the operations with the quantities completed/rejected from Hours Accounting, and complete the operation if the flag is checked as yes. An error report should print if not.

Does that answer your question?

Hi Nikos,

Using hours accoounting to report operations completion is not a very good idea, specially because the process of those records don't produce the same results of using the process operation completions session.

Also, sometimes you will process Report order completion before procesing your hours accounting records, and certain validations will not happen like.

If you have a planned qty of 20 and operation number 30 reports 18 completed 2 rejected and completed operation. Then when you report production order completed it will validate that your reported quantity is less or equal to 18; however if your record is still waiting to be processed in hours accounting NO validation will occur.
