Hi Raviraj,

I also prepared a process chart of the processes in shop floor control with LN. I could write much about new functionalities in LN but it would take too much place. I just can tell you that I do not like it but I love LN (with some expections).

The main difference for shop floor control is that there is a new module for warehousing. All material is handled in this module regardless if the origin of the order is production, purchase, transfer etc. Thus there is no more negative receipt and no negative shipment/issue.

The steps for issues and receipts may confuse you but it is possible to define simple warehouse procedures in order to skip them. In addition it is possible to automate mandatory steps. Backflushing and direct issue to warehouse when you report orders completed is also still possible.
