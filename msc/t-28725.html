Hi,

Yes, tdinv0250m000 will do the trick. This session will rebuild both tipcs500 and tdinv150. No users should be performing update actions on these tables, basically meaning that no one should be working in the logistics area. But in order to perform a quick rebuild you can also choose to rebuild the planned transactions for just the one project via the project selection in the special-menu. I suppose that in that case you don't have to worry about users being logged in either when that project is not being actively used anyway.

Good luck,
