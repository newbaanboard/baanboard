Baan doesn't store by year. Every time a cost price is updated and it is different, it gets stored in ticpr300. The number of years system will store will be based on the cost price parameter- No. of years to store the history. If you update the cost price once a year, you can also adopt the practice of defining new cost price calculation code for every year.
