Hi,
You can change the status of the production order to "To be Complete" only when you post the quantity completely. In other words, you cannot post any FG once the status of the Production order is changed to "To be Complete" (In newer versions there is provision to re-set the status and report more FG). However you can create a receipt for the partial qty in the Warehousing Module. Once the total quantity is received in Warehouse, the production order status will get completed automatically.
