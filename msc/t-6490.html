Francesco

Estimated and actual costs co-exist in Baan.

You cannot calculate your actual costs until you finished your project.
After all, not until you know the exact amount of material and operational costs that were required are you able to determine your actual costs.

However, if you want to know how much a project will probably cost you before you start manufacturing, Baan can provide you with an estimated cost, based on BOM and routing.
