If you have an operation performed by the subcontractor for which the price is related to some characteristic of the item (such as weight, length, volume) then the subcontractor will quote you a price per kg, m, m3... This price goes into the subcontracting rate. In the routing of the item you then specify the multiplication factor to be applied to the rate for this specific item. BaaN will calculate the price as the rate * ratefactor.
