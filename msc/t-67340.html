I work in a company that has recently implemented Infor with Ming.le.

The page size in the "D" option (Display) to print reports is not configured properly on our system. It looks like the last few lines of the report are always printed on the next page and then a new page starts. Hence a 4 page report is doubled to an 8-page report.

Hi,

You can check 2 things:-

1. In session ttaad3500m000(Device Data) for device "D", check the page length. If it is set to something less, increase it.

2. For the report, go to ttadv3530m000(Reports). Double click on your report. Under specials tab, make the page length as -1. Compile the report.

Please check the results after doing this.
