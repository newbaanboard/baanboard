Hi

WE ARE ON BAAN IV C4

The following is the reply :

For Manufacturing 1000 kgs. Of item 108A1  your bom specifies the following requirements :

1) SA01012001 300 KGS.

2) SA01012002 300 KGS.

3) SA01011003 400 KGS.

NOW YOU HAVE REPORTED A TOTAL PRODUCTION IS 1000+25.6 (REJECTION)

I.E. TOTAL PRODUCTION OF 1025.6 KGS.

SO FOR PRODUCING 1025.6 KGS OF ITEM 108A1  YOU WOULD REQUIRE  1025.6/1000*400 = 410.24KGS

OF ITEM SA01011003 , LIKEWISE IT CAN BE CALCULATED FOR OTHER TWO BOM ITEMS.

NOW REGARDING THE QUESTION OF REJECTION QTY  25.6 KGS, IT MUST HAVE BEEN REPORTED BY YOU / YOUR COLLEAGUES, EITHER THROUGH SFC OR THROUGH QMS  IF IT IS IMPLEMENTED.

IT IS NOT CALCULATED BY THE SYSTEM AS I UNDERSTAND.

THANKS.
