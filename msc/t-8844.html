I looked at it for casual interest very long ago.

You can find excellant reference matter on the BAAN On-Line help:

Start up the menu browser
Sit on BAAN IV Manufacturing
Choose /Help/Contents and locate Product Configuraton
It may be necessary to click on <Relations> hyperlink and then locate what you need.

Unfortunately, you will have to work yourself on deciding about advantages/ disadvantages, but I found it to be fairly comprehensive, though advanced use requires some BAAN 3GL Programming knowledge.


Hope this helps.
