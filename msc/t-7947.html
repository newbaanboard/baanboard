I suspect it has something to do with Hard Allocation in Inventory Control. Hard allocation is when you allocate specific stock to a specific Customer. Soft allocation if when the order is generated but the specific stock has not been allocated to a specific customer but there is a demand created on the stock in general.

The same is done when you generate outbound data. (ILC switched on)

Hard allocation in INV Control is the same as Outbound data without ILC switched on.
