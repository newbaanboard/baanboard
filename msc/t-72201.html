In our case all of the items on the phantom should be issued to the operation on the phantom on the next higher. So if the phantom part had op 50 then all its components went to op 50. We have quite the same topic in our BOM structure. Since triton and today also in InforLN 10.x we are using a customization to handle that. Once the production order explodes the BOM, the original operation is written to a local table. When the system has finished that, a DAL method reads this table and changes the estimated material records. Works fine.
