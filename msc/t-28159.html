Hi Sarawut,

Have you checked your warehousing orders?
Allocated material means that warehousing orders have been created for them. If somehow it could not be cleared, then something must have gone wrong in that whs order, like not enough qty, the line got blocked or anything.

If u use lot selection in BOM, u might want to check the session "Material To Issue" and see which lot the material to be issued is referring to and then check the correesponding whs order and inventory.
If u have enough inv for the material in the specific lot, then u should be able to clear the allocated mat'l.
Usually those are the things that I looked for when facing this kind of issue.

If the consultant has tried those things and still facing problem, then it might be best to log a case to support and have them solve your problem.

Hi Sarawut,

I suppose u have figured out the problem now...
If u wish to modify inventory date without GTM, then the only way is to do forced cycle counting and make the current inventory zero. After that u insert the inventory again with the desired date.

Be careful with cycle count as it will make entries on the accounting side. Check with those ppl before doing CC..

Good luck..
