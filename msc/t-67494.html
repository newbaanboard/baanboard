While generating Planned Orders, BaaN considers, BOM, Routing, Calendar, Quantity on Hand, On Order Quantity and Safety Stock figures, earlier production orders getting executed in that work-center, etc.
So you have to check all these set up details for one test item and check all the above details to arrive at the desired data.
If you are facing some specific problem, then please mention those specific details.
