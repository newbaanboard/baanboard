Indeed you might consider setting safety time for relevant items.
For Purchase items MRP will use the buffer at the end of the throughput time, so it will generate orders x days before normal order date
For manufactured items, the end date of the advices will also be x days before normal del.date, so it is used as "slack" at the end of the order.
