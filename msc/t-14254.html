The setting of the Purchase Price Priority field in the session "Maintain Cost Price Calculation Codes" (ticpr1101m000) only determines what purchase price Baan (IV) uses to calculate cost price in Calculate Cost Prices (ticpr2210m000), it does not control when the latest and average purchase prices in the Item Master are updated.

The setting of "Integration with Baan Finance" in Maintain Financial Integration Parameters (tdpur6100m000) determines when the Average Purchase Price and Latest Purchase Price in the Item Master are updated.

If "Integration with Baan Finance" is Yes, the Average Purchase Price and Latest Purchase Price are updated on approval of the purchase invoice (in tfacp1140s000).

If "Integration with Baan Finance" is No, the Average Purchase Price and Latest Purchase Price are updated by Process Delivered Purchase Orders (tdpur4223m000).

Please see thread http://www.baanforums.com/baanboard/showthread.php?threadid=9932&highlight=latest+purchase+price or search the forums for Latest Purchase Price and Average Purchase Price for more discussions.

Scott
