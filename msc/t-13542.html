Hello,

To implement mfg module suggest you first study the whole enterprise mfg process, identify WIP, repair, rework loops, role of inventory, the need for work centres, and operations.

Reading material is ideally Baan's Foundation Logistics, and then followed by Enterprise Logistics. As you are running single finance, consider the need to control new parts generation, multiple warehouses, production centres.
