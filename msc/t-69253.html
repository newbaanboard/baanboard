In this scenario, the best option is to identify the items based on the rejects and copy the rework production order from earlier similar item. This would reduce your efforts in maintaining the customized estimated material for that production order (as this kind of item is already having a completed production order having valid estimated material).
You may make a list of such items and their corresponding production orders in MS Excel / similar software for easy copying.
