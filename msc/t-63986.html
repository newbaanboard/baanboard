Hi,

Initially the production rate was rounded off to Item inventory unit and maybe that is why decimal were not allowed.
Check with infor support, i think they have released a solution in this regard in late 2012.
Excerpts from KB 22902252
SITUATION DESCRIPTION:
The production rate for the routing operations was always rounded to the inventory unit of the item. This resulted that it was not possible to define a production rate of 0.5/h for an item with an inventory unit of pieces with a rounding of 1.0.

Resolution:
SOLUTION DESCRIPTION:
The production rate for the routing operations is now not rounded anymore to the inventory unit of the item.

Regards,
Abattoir
