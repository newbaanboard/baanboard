You are correct in discovering the rough material requirements do not consider the current inventory. The rough material requirements is created by multiplying the planning percentage time the unsatisfied demand in the MPS. It is used by MRP to determine additional material needs to satisfy critical components. The rough material requirements do not consider material levels until MRP is run. This is the way I have used it in a couple of locations.
