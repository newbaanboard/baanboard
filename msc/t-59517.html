Yes, you can perform subcontracting process, only purchase order will take place for the same here, not the sales order. How many operations are there that need to be performed for that manufacturing item? in case the first operation itself is subcontracting process then at the begining you need to send the material to the other company for subcontracting process.
Apart from subcontracting you can split the production order; the WIP product will be considered as the semifinished good which further need a warehouse transfer from current company warehouse to other company warehouse and followed by second production order will take place at other company. However subcontracting is better approach i guess.
