The only option I see is to set the inventory unit to the sales unit. In order to do this you must first clear the field Origin Standard Item on form one to blank. After this you can change the inventory unit. When doing this you may also need to change the purchase units or provide a conversion factor for these. Don't know if this will work for you but may be worth thinking about.
