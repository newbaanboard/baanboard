In baan 4 you have several texts that can be attached when releasing a production order. When we converted into baan way back when we pulled all of the text we needed into the routing text. Form 2 on print order documents tisfc0408m000 shows a lot of what can be printed. We even added an addition flag to print like engineering config notes. In our case we would have included anything needed in the routing text for the production order paper work to travel with the job as it moved thru the shop.
