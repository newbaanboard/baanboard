You need to implement unit effectivity to peg supply and demand orders. If it is make to order and only standard items are ordered then you can also implement PCS with standard to order functionality which is a concrete hard pegging technique.
Clever Implementation of unit effectivity can also solve major hassles of PCS, and it will give a soft pegging with a dedicated session to track your supply chain.
