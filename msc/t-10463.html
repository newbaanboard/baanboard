We are on Baan IVc4 and have a similar effect when using RPT-items. When reporting production schedules complete, the last production order is automatically replanned by the system. Only on fridays(!) the start remainder is planned to saturday instead of Monday.
We logged a case and got the information that this problem could not be solved without serious impact on the MRP-run. As sdvries already mentioned: it is a problem of time calculation especially at the end/beginning of days.
