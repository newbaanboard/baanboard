We have 2 manufactured items, say A and B. They have routes and BOMs. On the level let's say 3 in their BOMs we have item C (also manufactured).
We do MRP planning and create SFC-orders for all manufactured items. Now we want to track progress in items A and B (and their components).

Finishing SFC-order for item C we want to understand for which "main" item was it manufactured - for A or for B?

If you are running strict lot for lot, the completion dates and quantities on the production orders (tiscf001) for item C should match up with the required dates and quantities for item C in the estimated materials (ticst001) for the production orders for A and B.
