In second case (avpr calculation based on inventory), if your inventory is zero and you perform new purchase then avpr will equal to ltpr (because old avpr weighted with zero qty)

In Baan IV it's tdpur4100m000 (Purchase Order Parameters), on the "Form 3" tab.

In LN it's tdpur0100m400 (Purchase Order Parameters), on the "Parameters 2" tab.

Screen captures attached.

If you use the Purchases(Cum) setting, you are supposed to run session Equate Balance of Receipts with Inventory Level on a regular basis. That session is tdpur4225m000 in Baan IV and tdipu0201m000 in LN.
