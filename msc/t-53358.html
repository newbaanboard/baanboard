Hi John,
Consumption of items in Baan is based on its Inventory Unit, afaik. From your description i take it that your Inventory Unit, for the item in question, is "pcs".
Therefore inorder to store this item in "pcs" but consume in "kgs", you would have to turn on Location Control. With this, you get to choose your Storage Unit as "pcs" and your Inventory Unit as "kgs" (provided conversion factors are defined) while your purchase & sales unit could be "pcs". The downside of this process is that it kind of lengthens your data entry process and can get a bit frustrating to start with (atleast with the users who would have to tackle new windows opening here and there and couple of new sessions to get their job done).

If on the other hand you can live with the fact that the item is stored in "kgs" then all you need to do is to change its inventory unit to "kgs" and purchasing and sales unit to "pcs", without turning on Location Control.. You may have to create a new item code if you wish to do this as if there are transaction reported against an item, you cannot change its inventory unit..
