Hi,
We are using PCS project in ERP LN 6.1 which I feel may be similar to ERP 5.0C. In ERP LN 6.1 no of units (hours) and total amount for processed hours get registered in PCS financial transactions by project session tipcs3500m000. You can have data for reporting from the table related to such session. However please note that this table does not store operation rates but stores no of units and total amount from which one can have operation rate calculated for each transaction.
