In V there is an item type of KIT but it seems you are on IV.
In IV you can enter a manufactured item on the sales order and copy the components. You will then need to create a BOM for the KIT.

In Baan4 another option is to define the Kit item as a phantom with a BOM. You then enter the phantom on the sales order. When you generate the picklist or outbound, Baan will tell you to pick the components and extend the BOM quantity by the Kit sales order line quantity.
