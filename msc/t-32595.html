Can you tell me if you have reported excess qty completion in the prod order, if yes then you can issue additional material even in completed status of the order. I think this will take care of the final item cost in the finance module.
But changing the status of the order through GTM is not advisable.
Afters excess material issue then you can go for closing of the PO which will post the variaces entry in the finance module.

But if the reported qty completion is less than the ordered qty of the PO then create an additional production order and report qty completed in this new PO. You have to check if you have to issue additioanal material or simply completed the difference qty in this prod order without issuing the material in this order.
This will also take care of the final product cost.
Please do not use the GTM for this type of correction.
