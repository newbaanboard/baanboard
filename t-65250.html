At moment the sales order is approved the SyncSalesOrder BOD will be published. With a conversion in the document flow the BOD can be transformed into a LoadPurchaseOrder BOD. The receiving LN company will create a purchase order out of this. So have a look into this ION feature of conversion.

Regards, Adriaan
