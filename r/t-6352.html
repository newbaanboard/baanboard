Next information is available:

First of all, Baan offers migration solutions and not a migration product or tooling. The software part of our migration solution Baan IV to Baan 5.0c is free of charge for customers who pay their maintenance/innovation fee. The software part of our migration solution will never be distributed alone to
customer or partners, only the complete migration solution (software + services).

Means the Migration Competency Center (MCC) from Baan will be acting as a sub-contractor of local Baan parties to perform the data conversion process.

A complete migration project is split up into two different parts, first part (75% of a migration project) is business consultancy , functional and technical consulting including education, complete responsibility of local Baan party. The other part is data conversion (25% of a migration project) and can be done by Baan MCC.

You can read more in the attached document 'Powerful MCC Solutions' where you can check the Q&A (Questions and Answer) part.
