How many of this board users were attracted to BaaN from the first sight?
I think they must change their politics about information; and BaaN partners must not be afraid of that. How many companies were canceled the support contracts just because they have a couple of good programmers or admins? How many times a BaaN consultant doesn't want to talk "the same language" with an end user?
