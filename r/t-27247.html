Well, it is not exactly random. After they have 32 teams, they are devided into 8 levels of ranking. Ranking 1 is the strongest teams with the best record of the national team in recent world cups and other regional games. This group includes Brazil, Germany, Argentina for example.

So, in every group, we have a team from ranking 1, another from 2, 3 and 4. This is done using a random draw

This way Brazil does not meet with Germany in the first round, for example.
