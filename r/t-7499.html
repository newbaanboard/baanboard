Dear Michael,

Do you classify your suppliers and tracks purchase activities by geographical area? If not, then you can use the area code for your budget articles. Change all labels saying "Area" to "Budg" or something similar. Enter budget article in area code field every time you create PO. You can even record the purchase budget per budget article (area code) in PST module and have your purchase budget vs realisation report right out of Baan

Dear Michael,

There is a short desc of PST on the following thread

PST & archiving (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?s=&threadid=7124)

If your purchase budget article scheme is not too complicated, without too much hassle you can use the PST purchase budget recording & reporting feature. The purchase budget entered will be included in the so called dimension 3 in the short desc above. Hence you can make report detailing purchase budget vs realisation per budget article per year for the last 5 years, for example.

If you think PST is suitable for you and ready to embark on the journey of setting it up :) there are many people here in BaanBoard who can help you
