Try Crystal Reports from Business Objects. You can use it to report off of multiple data sources. They include connectivity to data sources like Oracle, MSSQL, Sybase, DB2, Informix, Access, Excel, and many others. They also have a Baan interface you can get too. We have had it for over six years and have done several multi-data source reports. We have several finance people currently creating and updating financial reports. Users such as Finance people can create and maintain these reports, but there is some training that you'll have to invest in for them and you may need to occasionally help them. However that would be the case for any tool.
