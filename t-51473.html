Company: Future Pipe Industries

Qualifications Required:

Minimum Experience:

- Ability to understand the business’ financial and logistical processes

- Ability to understand solutions, purpose of solutions and benefits to the Business.
- Ability to configure and setup and plan an implementations

Generic Skills:
- Well versed in office applications (MS office, report writing…)

The company has a strong base in the GCC with factories also located across the world in Egypt, Holland, Lebanon and the USA.

The Business Systems team is an internal team assigned to support the business and enhance the efficiency of the business processes and activities via the implementation of various business systems and reporting solutions, providing maintenance and support of those systems thereafter.
