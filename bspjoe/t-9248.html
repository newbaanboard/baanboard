STUTTGART (Reuters) - Hasso Plattner, the co-chief executive and dominant figure of Europe's largest software maker SAP AG, is to step aside to join the supervisory board from May 9, the company said on Thursday.

Plattner's counterpart, Henning Kagermann, will continue as sole CEO, ensuring continuity at SAP after the departure of the last of its original founding members.
