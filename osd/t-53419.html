In regards to the OS, any OS supported by BaanIV also runs Oracle11. It is mostly a question of:

- personal preference
- how deep your pockets are?
- what other OS are you using and have admin skills inhouse?
- what are your scalability requirements (ie. no of users)?
- what are your availibility requierments?

In the high end, you most lilely weant to opt for AIX from IBM or HP-Ux from HP. In the middle to low end you will look at Windows and Linux.

And like always avoid the flame wars on these topics. It's not worth it. If somebody recommends you a specific platform, then simply ask for a reference, which is a close as possible to your own environments and requirements.
