30th August 2007, 19:24
Sorry to take your hopes away, but life and backup is obviously a bit more difficult, than you approached it here.

So first of all, naturally all your errors are due to the fact, that you try to backup open files of running applications. But unfortunately the "Advanced open file" option is not your saviour, because it is just a trick of the backup SW to delay the backup of open files, until they are not open any more. This works fine under the assumptions of temporarily open files, but does not work at all for a running database.

If you are new to the whole backup story, then stick to KISS and try an offline backup, by shutting down Baan and the database below first.

Second of all, do not mix desaster recovery (OBDR) and normal restore/backup scenarios. The requirements are very different and so are the advised implementations.

I can only advise you to take the whole backup topic very serious and if requierd, get some professioanl advise on it.

Regards

Markus
