Hello,

the error about srdd_init6.1 has nothing to do with your licensing problem.
You should put your hostname "wixpro" in the license6.1 file and in /etc/hosts so that the hostname can be resolved at OS level ("ping wixpro" while logged on onto the server must give a reply).
