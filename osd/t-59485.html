The fastest way to get your database running may be to clone your existing database to the new one using Oracle RMAN with "duplicate database".
So you can save a lot of time running bdbpre/bdbpost and have all tables and the complete grant structure, passwords and so on with no risk of any error during export or import of data.
All that you need is a backup to tape or disk by RMAN. In common the duplicate database process may take 30% more time as the backup ( regarding to the harware ) plus the time to recover the needed archive redolog files.
