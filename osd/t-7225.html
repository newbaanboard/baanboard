There are no published documents on Baan ER Model. I guess that Baan Development will have a good bunch of them, but I don't believe they will share it. However, to build a model by yourself should not be a hard thing. But keep in mind that the Baan data model is not always purely relational. Partly field values indicate which (of multiple possible) relations actually is valid for a record.
