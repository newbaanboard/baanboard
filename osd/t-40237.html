Go to Application Development=>Reports=>Maintenance=>Maintain Reports {ttadv3130m000}. Search for the report you want to check out. If the vrc of your username is not the same as that of the reports then click on Copy button on the toolbar and make a copy of the report in your current vrc and save it. At the bottom of this session you would find Edit Scripts button, click on it and it will show you the script behind the report..

I hope this is what you were looking for...
