Hi there,

lets assume your PVRC is B40C4sam and you want to dump all the tables of package tf td and tc.
Then cd $BSE/dict/ddB40C4sam
for i in tf td tc
do
ls -l d$i[^\.]* | awk '{if (length($9)==9) print substr($9,2,8)}' >> filename
done

Now you can use bdbpre6.1 -Ifilename -pB40C4sam -Cxxx,
where xxx is the company number
