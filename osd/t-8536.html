Hi ,

We did the upgrade on lunch time , it is a very short work

I Did it not by myself but it is easy.
Here are the steps we did.

1-first make a level 0 archive.
2-stop baan and informix
3-make a copy of your informix folder.
4-locate in $INFORMIXDIR and mount cdrom (upgrade came with cd )
5-install the new informix (cpio -iBvdcum < /cdrom/IDS/IDS.*)
6-start informix and baan
7-make a level 0 archive
