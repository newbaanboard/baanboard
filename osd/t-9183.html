Dear Roland,

We have been randomly receiving this error 850 (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_errors_850_EABORTON) in our 5.0c system as well. It basically means that at that point in time, BaanERP can't perform the transaction. However, if you tried again right after that, it might just work. It was deemed harmless by Baan Support, although this error is indeed a bit annoying to us.
