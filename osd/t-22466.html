All these files need root ownerships -


$BSE/bin/badmin6.2
$BSE/bin/blogind6.2
$BSE/bin/lp6.2
$BSE/bin/pdaemon6.2
$BSE/etc/rc.start
$BSE/etc/rc.stop
$BSE/log/log.asm_srv
$BSE/log/log.blogind6.2
$BSE/log/log.pdaemon6.2

Quick Guide 1029 - Security Set-Up in Baan/UNIX
