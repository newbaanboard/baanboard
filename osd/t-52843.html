You'll need to check the release notes of the portingset on the Baan support site. Here you should be able to find out which portingset supports your version of the o.s. Be aware the various portingsets are related to the servicepacks of your Baan environment. Upgrading to a newer portingset can result in a mandatory upgrade of your Baan environment ( the need to install one or more servicepacks.).
