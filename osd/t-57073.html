First you will need to implement PAM on your UNIX system and possible upgrade your Porting Set to support Password Aging in UNIX.

Please review the following solution from the INFOR support site:

201830

This would be a good place to start and make sure all of the necessary solutions are installed.

It is also highly recommended to keep up to date on the Porting Sets as they have a lot of fixes in them for Password Aging.
