Not sure what the problem is, but its probably client related.

The 2 hour difference might be caused by a time-zone being off and not all zones switching to DST at the same time.

Remember, Baan only "thinks" in Baan-time. Any discrepancies in dates are therefore always caused by either user settings or the user's pc, or the server settings.

In 9 out of 10 cases the PC is simply located in Acapulco (thanks to wishful thinking of the OS installer).
