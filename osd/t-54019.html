Maybe it depends on the parameter of the rexec daemon.
When rexec runs without the parameter -c, only clients can connect to the Baan-system when they have an entry in the /etc/hosts.
Please check your entry in the inetd.conf file an start your rexec with the parameter -c.
I think this solve your problem.
