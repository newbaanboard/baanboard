Why don't you stick with HP and just get new servers from them? That will also give you the performance improvement, due to new servers, and your delta is much smaller (especially for peripheral subjects like printers, ...).

Also if you do not have high availibility requirements to justify a cluster, you should strongly consider a host mode setup, where DB and App run on the same server. This will speed up your jobs by around 30-50%. With the currently available HW from HP (and naturally also IBM), you can easily run 800 and more users on one server.
