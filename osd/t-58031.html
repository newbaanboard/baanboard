16th March 2010, 15:57
In general, the OS/database upgrade is a lot less intrusive to the business than a significant SP upgrade. Since the OS/DB is strictly on the technical side, it is usually transparent to the users; however, since an SP involves potential functional changes, it can change the way some users interact with the system. Also, as Hitesh mentions, your customizations need to be tested with a new SP in case any standard functionality that you have customized, or that your customization may interact with, may have changed.

That being said, SP 9 is pretty old, and if you have the resources, you may want to upgrade. There are several companies on this site that can help. If you would like to talk more about it feel free to PM me.
