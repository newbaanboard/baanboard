Hi Roy,

To my opinion it must be possible. However be aware of some facts:

- You might have to upgrade your portingset also, check Infor's support site
to find out which portingset is needed for your upgraded O.S.
- Upgrading the portingset may cause some solutions to install your Baan
environment also. This is normally mentioned in the release notes of the
portingset involved.
- First try this on a simular test-enviroment, you could mark this action as a
major change, therefore you need to be sure that you don't have to expect
showstoppers before you perform this change on your Baan production environment

Never gamble with your production machine!

Good luck

JP

Roy,

We had upgraded several test servers from Windows Server Standard edition to Enterprise Edition ( as required by MyDay), and we had not encoutered any problem.

The only things we can think of the problem is networking. If the system is heavily networked, and those information are stored in the Windows registry.Such registry information will not carried from Standard Edition to Enterprise Editition.
LN is just an application sitting on top of the OS layer. I t should be intereedred by change of the underneath OS.

I suggested that you do a complete backup of LN before you carry out the upgrade of OS.
