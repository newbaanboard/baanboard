was the original system 10g but compatible was set to 9.2? or was it a 9.2 system?

there are a few differences between optimizer behavior between 8->9->10.

So that compatible=9.2 might have been intentional. There is a solution 'Important 9i/10g parameters' on the support site, you might want to read that.

Other than that, did you generate statistics after your import? that could do it.

And Finally, raw devices are pretty 1999, on most modern file systems (ext3 or better) and disk systems (good caching, etc. )
you're not going to see a huge gain vs raw disks. or at best - it's usually not big enough to overcome the hassle of having to use raw disks.
