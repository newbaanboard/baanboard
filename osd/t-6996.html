Logon locally is normally enabled on Windows 2000 Servers.

But when a Windows 2000 Server becomes a DC (Domain Controller) an extra Policy is applied. Within this Policy normal users cannot logon locally anymore, so they cannot start a proces under with their user-account.

Take a look at Microsoft Knowledge Base Article - Q234237 (http://support.microsoft.com/default.aspx?scid=KB;EN-US;Q234237&) for futher info.
