Hey Dan,

try sourcing your .profile in the script (assuming ksh)

#!/bin/ksh
. /home/howells/.profile
/baan/bse/bin/qptool6.1 -c200 -f /home/howells/a.qp -o /home/howells/a.txt


also maybe try adding >/tmp/error.out 2>&1 to the end
