Hello BaaNovva,

Error ENOMEM 12 is a UNIX message and as you stated it means not enough memory.

I suggest you look at your OS limits. At the Unix prompt you could type
ulimit -a

which could output something like this:

time(seconds) unlimited
file(blocks) unlimited
data(kbytes) 1048576
stack(kbytes) 51200
memory(kbytes) unlimited
coredump(blocks) 4194303

Depending on the UNIX flavor, the ulimits file can be in different locations
AIX /etc/security/limits
Linux /etc/security/limits.conf

etc

I'd advice to talk to your Unix admin and set whatever settings in the ulimits he deems necessary to -1 (unlimited)
