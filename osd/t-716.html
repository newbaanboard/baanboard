One point is that your old information cannot be used anymore and you will have to re-license.
With a new server you will have a new NIC, thus invalidating any try to input the old license information.
In case of a disaster like that, Baan will provide you with new license keys in a couple of hours.

In case Baan refuses to start in demo license, you can always remove your current object information and that will defenetely give you enough time untill you re-license it (session ttiex3228m000, runnable from the command line).
