It is indeed a 'permissions' issue.

It is necessary to give full read-write permissions to the folder (for example) c:\Program Files\BAAN\tmp. We did this by assigning the group called "Authenticaled Users" to this folder, and giving that group full permissions. (For us, the BAAN client is 'pushed' out as part of the 'image' when a new computer is set up, so usually, such intervention is required only in rare cases.)

I am not sure that it will be necessary to change permissions on C:\Windows\System32 to be different from what the administrators decided it to be.
