Hello,

There are no majors differences between c2 and c4. You need to migrate the data (change VRC for company) and this reorganize your tables.

There are some new parameters in finance, common and transport.

In tools the biggest difference is the presence of PMC to install the fix pack.

If you have do customizations in Baan you need to redo it: I think that it is the most important job; you need to do it before migrating your data .

And as usual test and retest before migrating to c4.

Good luck:)
