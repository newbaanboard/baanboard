Hi there,

tiitm001 ist the items table, if you insert an item to be manufactured, the following tables will be involved:

tiitm100, Item/container
tdinv001, main stock
tiitm004, conversion factors
tibom010, bill of material
tirou101, production plan header
tirou102, production plan details
