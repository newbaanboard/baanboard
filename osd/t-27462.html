George,
thanks for the links, but we solved the problem yesterday: I didn't have an account to test the scrolling myself in the old farm, so I looked up a couple of users who did. It appeared that my testers could scroll in the old citrix farm, but users in my building couldn't. After some testing I discovered that the Citrix servers on the other location (same farm!) were installed with a newer version of baan, B40.c76(instead of c61). Copied the files over the baan files on a testserver in the new farm et voila: scrolling works!
