When using Oracle 8 or higher, Baan can handle more than 255 char in a column when not setting ora_column_format:7. When this has been set: don't change it back on the fly as probably your tables are created in Oracle 7 format.

Baan does not support ways to do insert in non Baan tables but this can be solved by views or triggers.
