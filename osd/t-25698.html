Yes the normal informix setup of the 9.x version takes care of your problem.
Because the upgrade from version 7.x to 9.x is not a straight forward thing I advise you to make a cold backup of the informix chunks.
If the migration fails you can just restore the chunks that were changed and try it again saving you a lot of time if the database is large.
Consider a big increase in the locks parameter.
