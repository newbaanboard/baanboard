We booked BAAN infor case and they provided the solution.

They sent us http://secure2.support.baan.com/ftpdownload/updates/port/8.4a.02/ file and downloaded the shdbapi.dll file and overwrite it in c:\Program Files\Business Objects\Integration Kit for Baan 11\Native\bin\SSA_BaanERP50c

Now new porting sets work fine with crystal.
