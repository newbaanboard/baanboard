Hello,
the tablespace definition are located in $BSE/lib/ora/ora_storage

e.g.

#Real companies
*:000,100,101,103,104,105,122,124,125,127,130,133,155,160,165,195:T:group:0214:5
:PCTFREE 5 TABLESPACE BAANDAT INITRANS 3
*:000,100,101,103,104,105,122,124,125,127,130,133,155,160,165,195:I:group:0214:5
:PCTFREE 5 TABLESPACE BAANIDX INITRANS 3

#Archive companies
*:300:T:group:0214:5:PCTFREE 5 TABLESPACE ARCHDAT INITRANS 3
*:300:I:group:0214:5:PCTFREE 5 TABLESPACE ARCHIDX INITRANS 3

#Test companies
*:*:T:group:0214:5:PCTFREE 5 TABLESPACE TESTDAT INITRANS 3
*:*:I:group:0214:5:PCTFREE 5 TABLESPACE TESTIDX INITRANS 3
