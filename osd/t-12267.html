According to the last Baan Sales Guy I spoke with , it looks like your choices on the HP platform are Informix and Oracle. I wasn't aware that Baan had a porting set for DB2 on HP, in fact I thought that it was only with the lastest version of DB2 (version 8?) that it was even supported on HP-UX.

You will probably be OK on Informix for the short term...the next 2-3 years, as I don't see it going anywhere in that time, and unless you are going to move off of IVc4 or need level II drivers. It might make more sense from a $$ standpoint to wait for Gemini and see what options are available for DB choices then...DB2 might be a more attractive/more stable option at that point.

Or perhaps that ever elusive Linux port will open even more options....but that's another post.
