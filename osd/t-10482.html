Hi,

You could try this;

:s/<WORD or LETTERS you want to change>/\U(for upper) or L(for lower)&/g(if you want it to be global)

ie change name to NAME

:s/name/\U&/g

and back

:s/NAME/\L&/g

Hope this helps
