If by "Baan tools" you mean the "Reorg Session" in Baan, then the results will be minimal. The reason is simply, that this is done table by table. So a fragmented database often stays fragmented.

If by "Baan tools" you mean "create sequential dump" or bdbpre/bdbpost, then whis will normally help defragmentation. But then you might as well use Informix Tools. The results will be the same or better and should be faster.

I have to admit, that I am talking a bit theoretically here, as I am more of a Oracle person. Maybe somebody else here oon the board can give you some practical experience,
