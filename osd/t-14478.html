Hi Peter,

the scape sequence are :
\E&l1S for Portrait
\E&l2S for Landscape

Yuo must insert the escape sequence in the "initpr" line.
For example :

initpr=\EE\E&l26a66F\E(12U\E(s10H\E&l5H\E(s0p10h0s0b4099T\E&k11.6H\E&l1S,
