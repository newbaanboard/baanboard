We used to be on NT4, SQL 6.5, 4c3MCR.
We found SQL 6.5 really unreliable and migrated to SQL7 around 6 months after going live in 1999. SQL 7 was much much better than SQL6.5 - I am surprised you are still using it!

We migrated to Win2000, SQL2000 & 4c4MCR just over a year ago. All are on improvement on what we had before but the biggest improvement was the move from 4c3MCR to 4c4MCR. It is so much more of a stable system and then the benefit of PMC on top.

If you can i would advise going the extra mile to 4c4MCR if you can.

We actually bought another server to do (and test) our upgrade. It was the same spec. as our live server but being 2+ years old was pretty cheap. We actually migrated on to it over a weekend.

Sorry, but I do not know all the tools steps involved in the migration but i think it was quite straight forward, just time consuming.
