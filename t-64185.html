From Standard Doc (Help topics):-

The work center in a sales order is automatically loaded from the ship-to business partner. If no warehouse is related to the ship-to business partner, the work center of the sales office is used instead.
You must previously define the default work center in the Departments (tcmcs0565m000) session.
