Nice problem:

Scenario: You start model BP-activities in EME; let´s try a simple process with only 1 activity in between: ttaad3500m000 (Device data). You save your process, but keep the EME open. Out of EME you launch the application ttaad3500m000 and it´s open normal, means not with the find-option.
Now you close the EME and open the EME again from that BP. If you now again launch that application out of EME, it open´s with find-option. But either that with closing/opening the EME this field was pre-set to "find record" and that it is not maintainable anymore is - from my perspectice - a bug. Maybe it´s a simple setting, but which ...
