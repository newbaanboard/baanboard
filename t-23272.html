You can find the subsession you've to call in table tfgld011, so you can do something like:

|select tfgld011.ses1:subsesion.x
|from tfgld011
|where tfgld011.-index1={:tfgld101.ttyp}
|selectdo
|endselect

Now you have the subsession in "session.x"
