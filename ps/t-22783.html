When we generate control data and then planned orders the first time system creates records in the tables tppss600 - Maitain Planned Material Transaction, tppss610 - Maintain Planned Purchase Orders and/or tppss615 - Maintain Planned Warehouse Orders. If we try to regenerate planned orders the system first looks in the table tppps600 and then in tppss610 anr/or tppss615 if there there are any records BaaN checks-up their status if they are with status Planned they will be deleted and will be recreated again.
If there are records in table tppss600 and the are not records in tppss610 and/or the system "suppose" that the records has been processed and transfered in package Distribution. In this case new records will not be created.
In my case there was no any records in tppss615.
