22nd April 2003, 12:48
Seems that one (or more) service order has a invalid entry in the field contract (tssma301.ccon). The contract number "107" does not exists in the contracts table tssma220.

Search for the corresponding orders with the invalid entry in the field contract and delete the field via GTM (ttaad4100).
