Hi,

When printing from tssma4105m000 you have to make sure the components are not only maintained in tssma1105m000 (Maintain Components) but also in tssma1106m000 (Maintain Component History).
You have to identify the status of the component in tssma1106m000 first.
This is required to get a print from tssma4105m000.

Please make sure your components meet this requirement.
