Please check the locations in your warehouses X1 and X2. You can do this in session whwmd3500m000 and then double click on the locations that apply here. This will bring you to session whwmd3100s000 and in the <Transactions> tab you can see whether these locations have been blocked or not.
The goods located in warehouse X1 may be in a blocked location.

If this is not it then check session whinh6110m000 ((Un)Block Warehouses)

Hi

I had a similiar problem .

The warehouse from where the material was to be issued ( say x1) was actually connected to a service car. ( Making it the warehouse in the car!!) => tsmdm1545m000

incidentally, I found it out because the issue button in actual material cost lines was enabled and I could issue the material if I would click on this button.

As Edwin said, a warehouse blocking can occur due to multiple reasons like cycle counting , location blocked, Lot expired and blocked.
