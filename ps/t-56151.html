In fact eService (24*7 web-based self service environment) was the first extension application for which an integration with ERP LN was released (on FP1).

The latest release of eService 2.4 - that is certified on FP5 and released in April this year - integrates on more levels. Not only can customers, dealers or internal heldesk employees register Calls (in tsclm) via the use of Service Requests in eService. They can also register equipment (warranty cards) on line - and create serialised items (in tscfg) associated with the appropriate Business Partner in ERP LN automatically.
