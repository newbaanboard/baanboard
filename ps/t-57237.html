In ERP LN FP5, there are three Sessions, which contain this Message:
1) tppdm6100s000 (Projects)
2) tppdm7840m000 (Copy Projects)
3) tpppc0100s000 (PPC Parameters)
There is always the actual period compared with the parameters in tpppc000.cptf and tpppc000.year.
"Financial periods must be equal: Fiscal period in financial company and PPC financial period in actual company."

I hope this helps...
