We benefit from TP budget and control processes therefore it is not easy to go to PCS.
Think of a project delivering an installation specified in a contract. During the project you inevitable will be in situations where you need to ship additional standard products from inventory to the customer. This could be additional business, warranty issues and various parts or kits for installation.

The sales order procedure is good because first of all you have the best external documents like invoices including deliveryadresses, multilanguage texts and packing slips. The replenishment order processes just bring the items to the TP project. Then you need to customize shipping documents.

Key issue ia that we want to consolidate revenue and cost in one TP project and have streamlined processes for shipping standard products.
