Hi Nuno,

The relationship between BAAN V Service and BAAN V Enterprise Planning is only applicable in case you would like to schedule the maintenance of your own machines as defined in the tirou module in BAAN V Manufacturing.

Since you have indicated you are not using this functionality you can use the Global SRP and Detail SRP functionality in BAAN V Service for replenishment purposes. These planning engines will create the corresponding planned orders in BAAN V Warehousing.
However, this all depends on your set up in Service and Warehousing.

Almost every BAAN customer using BAAN V Service is using these functionalities in BAAN V.
