Hi,

Surcharges will be posted from TP to TF if surcharges per costs are being in use. Surcharges will be posted TOGETHER with every transaction if linked by surcharge definition. I recommend to test it with hours registration.
(Define a surcharge, type labor, give it a % - Value, dont forget the status)

When you do the process transaction (tpppc4802m000), the surcharge record will be created and posted to Finance and to Monitoring.

If you have any question, feel free to ask.
