What is the status of the activity?

"Item is used in Work Order %1$s - Activity Line %2$d Material Resource Line."

This normally means that the MWO still owns the item. You can't do too much with the item until the activity is completed, even when the material resource is a 'to warehouse' with an actual quantity present. If the activity in the material resource is zero, the MWO has to be closed before using the item in other MWOs. Don't know of the change to serialized would work once the activity/MWO is complete/closed. This is based on fp7 and 10.2.x.
