If you look at the customized item in Maintain Customized Item Data you will see a field on form one at the bottom left side under the heading "Origin" called "Standard Item". Clear this field and the system will always plan Purchase Orders. The planning looks at this field and if it finds a standard item it will check for inventory of that item and plan a transfer. If it finds nothing it will plan a PO.
