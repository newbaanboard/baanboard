Hi Anushea,

When you mention "a machine in BAAN Service" do you mean 'Installation' (tssma1102m000) in this case?
And when you mention "cost and non-stock items" do you mean 'Installation Components' (tssma1104m000)?

If you do then standard BAAN IV Service does not allow items of type "Cost" or any other non-stock items to be linked to an Installation.
The 'Item Type' has to be either "Purchased" or "Manufactured".

Please let me know if this is what you were looking for. If not I would like you to be more detailed in the information you give us.
