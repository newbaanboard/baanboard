You can reuse project numbers and sales order numbers after archiving and deleting old sales orders and projects (and all related data, that references the projects).

Until recently it was not possible to delete the projects (archiving projects doesn't remove the project entry itself) but Baan has just released an additional session: "Delete Archived Projects" (tipcs2290m000) comes with Baan solution 161529.
