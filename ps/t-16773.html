Hi Nuno,

The functionality regarding the booking of costs in BAAN V Service has changed so many times with the different Service Packs.
It is not easy to say anymore why a certain procedure was intended to do this or that.
My suggestion is that you built a script such that whenever you want to register hours in tssoc you first create an estimate line of zero dollars and then execute the transaction you want to do.
In that case you will follow the business application logic and will be sure that with new functionality in the next SP you most likely will be safe.
If you try to work around the zero dollar estimate line you will run a big risk of bumping into future problems.
