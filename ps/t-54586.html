Hi cyprus,

You cannot generate a PO in Global SRP (tssoc2260m000) because of two messages coming up:
- for item KO 100 146 you have not yet run the cost price calculation (message: Valuation Price not found on 19-12-08)
- you are using a "blank" unit of meassure on the estimated material line that has not been converted to unit 'ea' (second message).

Correct these and things should work out assumed all other settings are correct.
