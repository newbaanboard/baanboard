Hi,

As far as I know the RMA and Depot Repair functionality is not the same for 5.2a and ERP LN 6.1.
These are two totally different packages and so the functionalities of RMA and Depot Repair cannot be compared.
Needless to say, ERP LN is much more complete and has robust RMA and Depot Repair functionality which 5.2a has not.
