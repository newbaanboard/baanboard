Table tssma301 has ddt1 (Orderdate) , dtm1 (Ordertime) and
ddt2 (servicedate) . dtm2 (Servicetime)
therefor you have to calculate the difference between date and time.
datediff can be calculated with (ddt2 - ddt1) in days
time is stored in seconds since midnight
calculate difference of dtm1 and dtm2 gives difference in seconds.
