26th February 2004, 14:05
Usually companies would like to generate a new Installation in Service based on the shipments of the products (when printing Packing Slips for example) and not at the moment of Sales Order Line entry.
This has to do with the fact that at the moment of shipment the serial number (usually the Lot ID) of the end item is available and at the moment of Sales Order Line entry this information is not yet know.

It will all depend on the business process that is preferred.

Regards,

Edwin
