Hi Karthik,

I was wondering why you are using Detail SRP to release service orders and service order activities.
Can you please explain?
I would also like to know if you are using the Planningboard in Service and/or if you are using the Service Scheduler.
As you may know you can also release service orders and service order activities via the <Released> button.

Based on my experience I know Detail SRP is a very difficult session to work with because it only works at the service center level and you should only be using this if your business is scheduled maintenance.
Most companies I work with are more in a "reactive" mode and do not really schedule their service orders. They wait until the customer calls or sends in the equipment for repair. In those situations it is best to just use the Planningboard and/or the <Released> button in service orders and service order activities after running Global SRP.
Only if you are really in the preventive maintenance business you should be using Detail SRP.

Please let me know and I will help you further.
