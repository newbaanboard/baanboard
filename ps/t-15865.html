Hi Riazsayeed,

Unfortunately this is standard functionality in BAAN IV Service.
You have two options basically:

- Create material lines after the service order has been executed so then you know exactly how much was spent on materials
- Follow the procedure as described by you and also make sure the deletion is done at the PO line level.

I would go for the second option eventhough it will mean more work in the system.
