Hi,

maybe its of interest to you! We have designed our Interface with a different approach! We build a Project Plan in Open Plan! With Activities, Relations, Resources etc.

Activities that need to go to TP will be marked in Open Plan. The OPP Interface moves just those Activities to TP, or updates the Activities that where moved to TP till then.
This Approach is strait forward. An Activity is created in OPP and moved with Standard Settings to TP. No set up in TP, only in OPP. In TP we just have the output of the scheduling work in OPP. Thats it!
