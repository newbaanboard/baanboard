Unlike BAAN V there is no direct relationship in BAAN IV between service orders and internal preventive maintenance on machines.
I have seen BAAN IV customers that manually create service orders for internal preventive maintenance to register their costs (labor, parts, etc.).
So far I have not seen anybody that tried to integrate BAAN IV Service with things like Resource/Constraint Planning in BAAN IV Manufacturing.

Planned maintenance in BAAN IV Service is only based on time (number of times per day/week/month) and not based on measurements/counter values and/or usage. These can be planned for in BAAN V Service however.

Of course, anything is possible via a customization but I expect that building this in BAAN IV will be a pretty big job. Especially if you would like to have maintenance schedules for your machines based on measurements/counter values and/or usage. If these schedules have to be based on time only then the effort will be a lot less.

Let me know if you would like to have more info to determine whether a customization is something you want to do.
