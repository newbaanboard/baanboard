I haven't used PCF in estimates but have used it on the budget. In the budget after the product variant is created you need to generate the product structure. Depending on weather the generic item is to order or anonymous it will either created a customised item or a standard item.

Once the standard or customised item is created, then a question will pop up to ask weather you want to cost the item. Only after this then the cost is updated in the budget.

There may be a similar function in estimates that you could explore.
