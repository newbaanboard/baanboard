These are not for Baan but some of you may have these products...



Informix 3.30 32 bit ODBC driver installed
Easter Egg: 1. In the W2K ODBC Driver Manager, click on the
System DSN tab.
2. Click Add.
3. Select Informix 3.30 ODBC Driver from the menu.
4. Click tab "About Informix ODBC".
5. Click any other tab.
6. Click tab "About Informix ODBC" again.

Instead of the hum-drum version stuff a picture
of the developers appears on the tab, with some
miniature developer pictures embedded on the
right margin.

Seagate Crystal Report 8
Easter Egg: 1. Select Help About Box
2. Double-Click on the Icon
