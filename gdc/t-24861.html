Well, let's think about that for a second. Moving from Baan 5c to ERP LN is a huge jump. It's not just a 1 weekend deal. You have to redefine your business processes, determine what will work and what won't for you. Look at new functionality, etc. Then you have to migrate any customizations, etc.
