I guess there are about 3000 customers in Europe that use BAAN IV, BAAN ERP and/or ERP LN.

Still the US has the largest number of customers in a single country with around 1200 customers.
Worldwide there are about 5500 customers and almost 80% is on BAAN IV.

I have no information about the number of users per country/continent.
