Actually, it isn't like the texts, for the following reasons:

Texts do not exist in all Baan records. ZetaNote allows you to link notes to almost any record.
The Baan text linked to the table may be used already. For example, the Purchase order header text is shown on the PO printout. ZetaNote is intended for personal notes. If you want to write some information for yourself regarding a specific PO, this is where you'll write it. Our customers use them for Items, Purchase orders, Suppliers, etc'.
Even when texts do exist, it is not easily visible. Not all baan sessions show the existence of texts. You have to look for them. ZetaNote will show you a popup when texts exist.

For more information, please go to http://www.zetanote.com and choose "Tutorials" from the menu.
