We are live with Baan 5.0c on a little subsidiarie and we will go live on our main company the 7 january.

We did begin the project with sp4, we are now between sp5 and sp 7 (we have not installed any sp from the beginning but we have instal thousands of solutions, and we know that we hav all solutions included in sp5).

We did open 250 cases on baan support, we have still have now 25-30 case still open, all other was solved by solutions. I did some project with baan 4 before and think that 5.0c is not as stable as 4c4. But it i heard from another cutomer that it is more stable than baan 5.0b.

We don't have very few technicalm bugs, one is link to the last porting set (bdbreconfig problem) and another is specify to AS/400 printing. We have some problems with developments sessions.

In finance and industrie we have near zero bug (normal it is very near baan 4, onky few finance integration bugs). We use also EDI (here we have few bugs), planning (no bugs), central invoicing (no bugs). Most of bugs comes fom order manegement, general data (especially item management) and warehousing.

For most of case support is very good. We have some old issues taht are not solved, most comes from bgl0 localization.
