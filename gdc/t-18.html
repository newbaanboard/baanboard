Baan would certainly be a lot brighter with the added touch of a game, but they wouldn't be the first company to do this. Now, Tic-Tac-Toe using 4GL sounds quite feasible, but does anyone remember or know about the Flight Simulator hidden in Excel 97? Thats right, a flight simulator! Well, ok, flight sim is a broad term and its more like an early version of Descent, but this was programmed over 5 years ago and was quite impressive at the time. You could fly around little blue and red mountains and eventually find the programmers' "Credits" for Excel scrolling away somewhere.

Before you ask, here's how to do it if you've still got Excel97.

* Load Excel 97
* Hit F5 ("Go To")
* Enter "x97:l97" to go to cell x97-l97 (thats a lower case L)
* Hit Tab
* Press Ctrl+Shift+ using the mouse click the Chart/Graph Wizard icon on the toolbar
* ... and there you go, use the cursor keys, spacebar,enter, to control ... :D
