The forum software is indeed the same: vbulletin (see http://www.vbulletin.com). For a full-fledged portal you will also require a portal front-end. Some popular open-source portal softwares are post-nuke (http://www.postnuke.com) and php-nuke (http://www.phpnuke.org). vBportal (http://www.phpportals.com) is a commercial portal that integrates seamlessly with vbulletin.
