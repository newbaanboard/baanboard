Hello,

I am not certain about Denmark but it might be similar to Germany where it was neccessary to have server located on site.

Solution was quite simple

First we bought cheap intel machine just for replication purposes in Germany.
Live enviroment was located in Denmark and our technician made automatic data transfer from operating enviroment to German "legal" enviroment. Basically ftp transfer of compressed data dump from one server to another during weekend. Question if you have enough time during weekend to make a dump.

I know this was sufficient for German authorities. Possibly you could do the same in the opposite direction.
