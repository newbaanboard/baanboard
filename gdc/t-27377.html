In the Enterprise Planning module in Baan there is rule based finite planning on planned orders. This is standard and basic

Planner in the add on for finite scheduling on a meduim to long term basis.

I don't know, if the Baan4-Planning against finite ressources is working.
You can find it in the menu under "Constraint Planning". In Baan5 and LN it works, but it is better to get some training by a consultant, who has implementad this package more than once! It is not easy to use...
Alternativly there is the SSA Scheduler, which is an Add-On from the SCS-Suite. That works fine.
Of course you can use external Add-Ons like GTT FAST/pro (www.gtt-online.de) or others.
