I believe there is no Single Document to explain the Differences between FP6 & FP10. You have to compile different documents to get what you need. Example: There will be one document which will explain you the differences between FP5 to FP7, and another document to explain the differences between FP7 to 10.2.1 (FP8+), one more document for FP8 to FP9 & then FP9 to 10.5.

This is how I have got to know the differences. There might be a better way, but I'm not sure.

You can find the documents on InforXtreme.com. Hope this information will help you.
