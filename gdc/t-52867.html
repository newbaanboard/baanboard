I can share a little bit of my experience. But I think it is difficult to formulate a step to step guidance on that.

1. Setup an intranet for sharing the knowledge. You don't need a specific KM software.
2. Try difficult kind of media, like short video, not limit to plain word documents
3. Each document is better short and specific, covering a piece of product knowhow or answers to common problems
4. Categorize, make it easy to find a certain topic
5. Encourage participation, make it a norm for colleagues who are about to leave, retire or being promoted to contribute the knowledge
6. Conduct short training sessions when necessary to reinforce knowledge to new comers.
7. Persistence
