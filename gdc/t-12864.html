2. Do not stand-up to offer your seat when someone comes in.

3. When walking with your wives and daugther, do not walk behind them.

4. Use your righthand when receiving or passing something.

5. Do not shake hands with woman.

7. Instead of saying "Thank you", say "May Allah reward you."
