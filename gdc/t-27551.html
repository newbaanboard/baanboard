Mass Production - is the production of goods in large quantities. It aims for low unit cost and high output.

Flow Production - involves continues movement of items through production.

Line Production - set of sequential operations to produce an end product (assembly line)

Batch Production - producing product in groups. Used primarily in industries like bakeries, paints, adhesives, pharmaceutical products, etc.
