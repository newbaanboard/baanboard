Hi,

I just returned from the BWU Fall Workshop in Charlotte, NC and one thing has become clear: SSA Global will not sunset the older BAAN versions.
That means as long as customers keep on paying the Maintenance Fees for the BAAN version they are using they will be supported until the end of times. This may mean that if a customer is more than 2 versions behind of the most current one the Maintenance Fee will be increased.
SSA Global will keep on developing new functionalities in older versions as long as the Support revenue is coming in.
They will never force a customer to go to a higher version as the "old" BAAN management wanted to do.

There was a special announcement that a b40_c4 web client will become available in Q1 2004 and this will be given to customers for free if they pay their Maintenance Fees.
More interesting deals for customers using older versions can be expected.

The general feeling I got from customers attending the show was that they have received many nice stories before but want to see some actions from SSA. They seem to be prepared to give SSA a new chance but remain sceptical. I would say "Actions speak louder than words".

Regards,

Edwin
