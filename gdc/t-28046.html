Here is an interesting link to OST's SSA BAAN Education Symposium:

As far as I know this is the first time an event like this is organized specifically for European SSA BAAN users.
On Monday October 16th there is an opening reception and this would be a great opportunity for European BaanBoard members to meet each other as well.
This would be the first time ever since we started BaanBoard in August 2001.
