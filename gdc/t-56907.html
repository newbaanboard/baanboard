Virtual Inforum 2009 - UK meeting 21 Oct 09

The UK Baan & LN Usergroup is arranging a meeting in Farnborough on Wednesday 21st October 2009.

We will be able to attend the virtual Inforum event, participate in the interactive sesssions in the morning and view the on-demand content in the afternoon. Between sessions there will be the opportunity to discuss what we've seen.

The get-together is free to UK usergroup members and lunch will be provided. There will be internet access available so you can bring a laptop and keep on top of your email.
