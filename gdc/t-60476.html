(1) Yes Baan can do EDI transactions, I just do not know anything specific on this. You can search this board for EDI and see what comes up. I know there are questions and answers around this.
(2) The type of order determines what steps or status it will go through. Are they fixed or customisable - well with source code anything is possible. Not sure that is the answer you want. I also know for something like purchase orders - you can setup the steps that order type has to go through. There is a flag on one of the purchase order tables that can be read.
(3) I won't say it is common, but EDI transactions are used. Most of what we do is transfer files between systems.
(4) Contact infor - sales rep or someone to get more documentation on EDI. There are probably infor partners that would help you get something like this up and running.

A lot of how this works will depend on the Baan version - I am sure it is different between the versions and possibly even the extensions.
