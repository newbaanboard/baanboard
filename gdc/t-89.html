For migration to Baan V, I agree that you have to take a serious look at your business requirements. There are major functional changes that need to be managed properly. Baan V does present many solutions to current Baan IV problems and create some problems out of your current Baan IV setup.

Although we still have to implement for a client, we have simulated conditions at sites that are currently running Baan IV and Triton. Our finding is that we are bound to do less customizations and consequently implement faster due to the new functionality. By the way, the sites we simulated are electronics on multi-site scenario.
