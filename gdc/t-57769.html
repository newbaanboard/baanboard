Infor has officially informed that ERP LN 6.1 FP6 is available for world wide users.

ERP LN 6.1 FP6 now delivers additional support for supply chain collaboration intensive businesses. The latest enhancements to Infor ERP LN include:

Improved support for business practices in packaging, shipping and transportation designed to result in more flexibility to help deal with last minute customer changes and shortages. It also improves handling of packaging, returnables and labeling.

Improved support for manufacturing operations with multi-level material shortage checks to proactively address shortages before releasing to production. Scrap reporting has been improved and the line assembly solution has been further enhanced with scheduling and sequencing capabilities.

Improved support running Infor ERP LN in a virtual environment combined with improved performance.

More intuitiveness of the user interface.

So Enjoy ERP LN 6.1 FP6 :)
