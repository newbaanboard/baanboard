On May 30th we will give a workshop in our office in the Netherlands on Sharepoint integration with SSA BAAN ERP.

The Sharepoint and Baan interface solutions are coming from Ometa and it's build on top of Windows SharePoint services (WSS), which is included in Windows 2003.

The unique additional webparts in this solution are:

-workflow build within SharePoint
-navigation, enhanced maintainable tree structure
-consolidation webpart, gives overview over multiple sites
-interface with SSA Baan V (Baan IV interface planned end 2006)

This SSA BAAN ERP interface goes via the business logic layer, so it will also work on a customized environment.

See for more details www.actnow.nl and www.ometa.be
