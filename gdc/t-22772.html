Baan IV webtop is available. you can do most functional tasks such as entering orders and printing. However, you cannot do many tools tasks such as developing reports and maintaining users.

Webtop has the ability to cache forms from your sessions so the presentation is fairly quick, it then relies on a bshell backend to handle the processing. So that isn't any different than your typical Baan session.
