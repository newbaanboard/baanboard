Hi Umesh,

There are two PDF documents, one a programmer's manual(No. U7168AUS) and the other is functions reference(U7167AUS). Try to get those two documents from baan support and read. After that, as far as I know, there is only one website where you can learn the Baan programming. That site is www.baanboard.com. You can search the tools development forum here for any of your doubts and if you can't find any answer, post a query and you will get a swift reply. (The functions reference is also available here at Baanboard.) Baan Tools is not Visual Basic. You can't google and get millions of answers.
