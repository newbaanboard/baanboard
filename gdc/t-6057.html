Here's a little entertaining bit of software and a means of team-building: Project Dolphin (http://project-dolphin.net/)
Project Dolphin is a project established for a small circle of contacts on the internet, in order to track how many keys were being typed by them.
Basically, you download a small agent to your computer that runs in the background and counts the number of keystrokes you make. It sends that number to their main server hourly / daily / when you want to, and your account is updated with the new keystrokes.

I have formed a Baanboard team, so users can sign up and join the team, and the accumulated keystrokes of all members is that team's score.

The team page:
http://project-dolphin.net/cgi-bin/browse.cgi?mode=team-look&step=look-this-team&TeamID=1293

To join the team, you first need to sign up for the program:
http://project-dolphin.net/sign_up.shtml

Once you have activated the account, download the Pulse program:
http://project-dolphin.net/pulse_download.shtml

And finally, go to your account:
http://project-dolphin.net/members.shtml

Click Join a Team, enter "Baanboard" in the Search String and press Name. On the next page click the team's name, and join it (no password is needed).

Let's see if we can get in the Top-10 Ranking!

A couple of days ago the owner of 'Project Dolphin' decided to dismantle the project, to shutdown the databases and to stop key counting. That's the reason why - if you did check - the numbers on our Project Dolphin stats page are not changing anymore.

Fortunately, Project Dolphin is and was very popular and is in the process of being taken over by another group of volunteers. This is now Project Orca (http://www.project-orca.net/index.php). Project Orca is still being worked on at the moment but you can already sign up and join a team. However, you will not be able to take forward your original Project Dolphin key count. So we all start from scratch again.

To enable key counting for Project Orca you must change the pulsing server in your Pulse client or you can download the update Pulse client program from the Project Orca home page.

There's no WebAPI yet so we are leaving the old Project Dolphin statistics on Baanboard. Once the WebAPI is ready, we'll switch to Project Orca.

However, feel free to sign up at Project Orca in the mean time and join the Baanboard team!
