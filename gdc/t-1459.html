Perhaps this may not be what you are looking for, but just for your information...

You can use Baan Openworld Broker to connect to messaging systems like IBM MQ Series or Microsoft Message Queue to pull and push XML documents from the queue.

I recently worked on a project where EDI files were used as source and converted into XML documents (output) with some data translation using Baan Openworld Broker.
