Hello,

First of all welcome on baanboard.

Via www.infor365.com you can ask for a account for https://infor.subscribenet.com on which you can find the software of your baan release. That software does contain a documentation cd. However your company need to have a valid support contract in order to get an account.

Second option is to get a training. Not sure what the possibilities are in your region.
Third option: get an experienced consultant for a couple of days to get you up and running. Probably this is the most efficient way to get the knowledge.
