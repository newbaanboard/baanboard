In a great marketing move by SSA Global Users, they are now offering a free pass to the SSAU Mosaic 07 exhibition hall, to be held in Anaheim, CA between June 24 and 27, 2007.

The invitation is really interesting for Baan customers in Southern California, and also in Northern California and Arizona. If you cannot come to the full event - but would like to hear more about Baan and ERP LN, you can come to the exhibit hall for free (Invitation is attached) and meet lots of companies that have a lot to offer.

If you are a Baan user and are interested to hear more about ERP LN, then you can meet people that will tell you stories about projects and how they can migrate you

If you are a Baan user, but are NOT interested to hear about ERP LN, then you can come and meet with lots of companies that offer very interesting solutions for getting more out of your Baan 4 and 5 system.

So, it is a really unique opportunity to meet with vendors, and also meet with users from around the USA and Canada. If you can come to the full event, then it is better, with tens of breakout sessions that meet the interest of all.

I look forward to meet some of the BaanBoard friends in the conference!
