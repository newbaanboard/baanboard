Boy, tough crowd. Sorry, I have not worked with the software that you mentioned either. But my company puts out a Baan Time and Attendance solution, and I have researched a few that are used within Baan. What I know:

High End:
SSA - Infinium: The high end HR Package that I thought SSA was pushing. I saw a demo on this, very impressive. Built on Cognos. This was a strong stand alone HR package that SSA bought. At the time the Baan integration was fuzzy, but that should be resolved by now or easily, as they own both products.

Mid Range:
Kaba Benzing: Another fairly high end package, though not as big as Infinium. They have been working tightly with Baan for a long time. This is probably the most popular system that I have run into.

Kronos: Also have a collection driven system that has been working with Baan for a long time.

Lower Range:
RMCis (My Company): Collection based system that integrates with Baan Hours Accounting (Baan IV and V) / Baan People (ERP Ln), and provides some additional HR related functionality to standard Baan. This requires an outside Payroll service to process checks, deductions and taxes. We provide the integration.

Connectware, TriNET, other collection partners, generally provide their own flavor of this Payroll Interface through Baan / Data Collection. Of course, ours is the strongest of this group (yes, that's just my opinion, I have to get that in there somewhere!).

The Mid Range packages are good out of the box solutions. The Lower Range solutions are generally less expensive, not as robust, but more customizable as the collection partners generally have good Baan expertise and are willing to provide custom solutions. Infinium would be the 'bomb' as far as the HR functionality goes, try a demo if that is the range you are interested in.
