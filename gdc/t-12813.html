As I understand it, and maybe somebody will correct it, the "roles" are as follows:

1. Newbie - up to the first 10 posts you remain in this role.
2. Junior Member - after 10 posts
3. Member - after 25 posts, or after a certain period of membership
4. Senior Member - after 100 posts
5. Guru - after 300 posts (I'm chasing this status :D)
6. Moderator - appointed by the board owners to people who help them moderate, i.e. check and scrutinize, the messages
