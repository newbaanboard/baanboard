Hi Keyur,

BaaN (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baan_ERP) is an ERP (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enterprise_resource_planning) system which integrates functional units of an organization into a single system.
Below are the few functional units...

1. Manufacturing
2. Logistics
3. Planning
4. Finance
5. Service
6. Project management
.
etc.

Any ERP system (like BaaN, SAP, Oracle Apps etc) comes with standard functionality but one can customize it according to their business need.
The hierarchy of BaaN ERP from older to newer version is as follows...

BaaN Triton
BaaN IV
BaaN ERP (Also Known as BaaN V)
ERP LN

BaaN is a pioneer in manufacturing thus mostly used in manufacturing industries & it's implementation cost is much more effective in comparison to other big ERPs.
BaaN is a product of Infor Global Solution (http://www.infor.com/solutions/erp/).
