Paying for customization may provide you with functionality you desire, but it will be static and expensive and with the lead time for the customization, your process may have already evolved. Since Quality and Business processes are continuously improving, going with an Off the Shelf solution that is being maintained and kept current in the long run will be a far batter way to go.
