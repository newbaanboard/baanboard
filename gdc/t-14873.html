I have not hooked up to them recently, but they used to use WebEx - www.webex.com. I know they have changed (the WebEx company is owned by Jan Baan), but the concept may remain the same. We use a similar service - WebDemo (see http://www.linktivity.com/home/index.html)

If they have the internet connection, these tools will allow for web meetings and desktop sharing. There may be a few Java Applets that get loaded when the meeting is initiated for the first time on that PC, but you do not have to do a hard install on the client PC. The hosting services cost $$.
