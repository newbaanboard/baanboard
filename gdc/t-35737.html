* Baan Constraint Planning

Baan Constraint Planning provides planning functionality that takes into account capacity and material constraints. "Finite" planning takes in to account the capacity of production in work centers. A planning method called "Infinite" planning takes in to account that production is unlimited.

Baan Constraint Planning provides Master Production Scheduling (MPS) with an integrated capability with finite and infinite planning methods. MPS plays a key role in the MRP II logistical planning and control of internal and external flows of goods in a manufacturing environment.. You can select different planning strategies for different parts of your production system with Constraint Planning.

Constraint Planning provides the following advantages

* Optimizes the constraint resources from costs and cash flow, customer service, and inventory

* Coordinates sales and production

* Coordinates the availability of material and capacity

* It becomes the "driver" of the flow of goods, or "input" to the MRP
