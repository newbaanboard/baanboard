Ways to measure a New ERP functionality

<span>Trend is changing so fast. Vendors are able to convince CIO’s to bring new Cloud ERP. Even it’s so much fancy to use words AI/ML/IOT etc. Often CFO/CIO/CTO’s must evaluate if a new ERP is needed. Reality of Cloud ERP or new ERP is with new functionality, enhancements, features which is good. But at large they merely cover deep & complex business needs. 1) Need of the hour is Digitization; but fact of the matter is that even applications are not providing solutions pertaining to SCM & other functionalities. 2) Cloud ERP cost more than traditional ERP. Increases your TCO by adding extra cost. 3) Down the line Customer have to replenish old IT Infrastructure at some point of time. Should they do right away or better to introspect by not compromising internal IT model. 4) Feasibility of deploying solutions may fascinate ROI.
