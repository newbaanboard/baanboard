Hi.

Your problem is printing barcode from a UNIX system, right?

Have a look at the directory $BSE/lib/barcode. There you should have a "driver" for your printer type and your barcode type, e.g.
$BSE/lib/barcode/hp/type01 which is a UNIX executable or a shell script.
