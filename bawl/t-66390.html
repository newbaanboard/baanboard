Logistics From a Finance Perspective

Came across this and thought it might be helpful to some Baan IV users out there. One of our Finance consultants put this list together when creating a class that we call "Logistics From A Finance Perspective". Basically, this gives you the transcactions in PCS, Cost Accounting, SFC, Hours Accounting, Purchasing, Sales, RPL and ILC that have implications in Finance, and the logistics tables that are involved.
