The predefined variable e is used for storing error codes when executing db-actions. In your case you get an error 100 during the PRP-run, meaning EDUPL or in normal words 'This indicates that a duplicate value exists'. Most probably a records is inserted with an already existing key-value.

This variable is used in the older db-calls and is comparable to db.error().
