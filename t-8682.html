Dear Burniou,

stpapi.find() mimics find action while stpapi.change.view() mimics new.group action. If find doesn't work in the session, and it doesn't work in quite a few session, then stpapi.find() won't work there. In such sessions, to be able to find record correctly, you may have to issue stpapi.change.view() if you're on form type 3 then do recurring stpapi.browse.set() until you get to the correct record

I only know one session where stpapi.change.view() doesn't "change the view" and that is in whinh3520m000 Receipts. In normal usage, when you do new.group in this session, BaanERP goes to a long routine to assign new receipt number, instead of blanking the view field for us to fill in. So, in normal operation, new.group doesn't "change the view". That's why when you issue stpapi.change.view() to whinh3520m000, you wouldn't "change the view" but rather assign new receipt number

Rgds,
Paul
