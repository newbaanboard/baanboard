Hi Chris

For one of our divisions we use Lot Control to record the serial number of every product produced and sold (make to order environment.)

When each item (spool of wire/rod each with slightly different weight) is produced a unique serial number is generated and a lot is created for this item/serial no.

When each item is sold specific lots are selected in the outbound procedure and shipped. This all works fine and gives us an impecable lot history.
