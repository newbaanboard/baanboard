Gaurav,

What exactly are you trying to report or analyze?

Inventory valuation report tdinv6230m000 will tell you what the system now thinks was on hand as of a specific date, based on the records in tdinv700.

If inventory transactions were backdated, however, the reported qty and valuation may not be what the system actually showed as on hand on that date.

Hi,
Your parameter "Log Physical Inventory Transactions by Location" would not be set to "Yes" in ILC parameters. IF you change that to "Yes", from now on your table 'tdilc301' would be populated on every inventory transaction.
To get the earlier data, maybe you have to dig out from Outbound and Inbound data tabl (tdilc401 & 402), inventory adjustments (tdilc102), inventory transfers (tdilc103) and maybe some more tables for details of history of location inventory transactions.
I think there should be some way of populating the tdilc301 table from rest of the tables, but I do not know.
