Yes , cp history table ticpr300.

even i had also done a small cust using the same logic long back.


select ticpr300.item ,ticpr300.amnt ,max(ticpr300.indt)
from ticpr300
where ticpr300.item =:tdinv700.item
and ticpr300.indt <=:tdinv700.trdt
group by ticpr300.item ,ticpr300.amnt
selectdo
i.val = ticpr300.amnt * iqty
selectempty
i.val=tiitm001.copr * iqty
endselect
