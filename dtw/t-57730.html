Normally in header it is defaulted from Sold - to- BP - Terms of delivery i.e. tccom110.cdec.

However you can overwrite the default value and select other terms of delivery from the list.

When you create a Sales Quotation line it is defaulted from header. Now if you don't have a practice of different terms of delivery against different line, they its better to consider the Header Terms of Delivery else you can take lines.
