A new set of EDI messages has to be developed in Baan to do the Warehouse EDI transactions. The number of EDI messages to be developed depends on the bussiness model. I had once developed a set (3new EDI messages 940,943 & 945) of ware house EDI messages. One of my cleint has the bussiness requirement sames as yours i.e. the PO's/SO's will be raised by my clients and the receipts/Deliveries are done by Warehouse personnel. EDI trasactions are made such that at any point of time the inventories at my client system has to match up with the invetories in public warehouse.
If you have questions on this, let me know. For developing the new EDI messages in Baan, the person should know the complete Baan EDI functionality apart from Baan Knowledge.
