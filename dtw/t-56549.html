You can try to do Partial Receipt. then you will see planned inventory transactions by item by next of sequence.

Example . Open Purchase Order , position 10 for 4000 qty.

You can see plan receipt for line 10 sequence 1. after you do confirmed / final receipt for line 10 is 1500 qty.

You will see plan receipt for line 10 sequence 2.
