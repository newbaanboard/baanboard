Listed below are the steps required to correct shipment line quantity after confirming the Picking List but before Confirming Shipment:
1. Go to Shipment that you want to correct

2. Open Shipment Line you want to correct

3. Change Quantity Shipped to correct quantity (Inventory remove from Quantity Shipped will remain in Stage Location)

4. Save Shipment Line

5. Freeze/Confirm Shipment

6.Create Transfer Order to move inventory from Stage Location back to Picking Location
