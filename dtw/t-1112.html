There actually is at least one situation in Baan IV where alternative items are taken into account. If you have
- a customized item 'X' without stock
- derived from a standard item 'Y' with order system FAS,

and that standard item has:
- no stock
- but an alternative item 'Z' with stock

then PRP wan't give you a production advice for your customized item X, but a PRP Warehouse order from that alternative
item to your customized item (Z -> X)
