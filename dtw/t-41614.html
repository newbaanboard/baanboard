I recollect the lot pricing work properly for purchased item when u want a lot price accurate in tdltc001 and tdltc004. With lot pricing , u can technically tally ur lot valuation report with ledger balance at actual purchase cost. Surcharges probably leads inconsistency if ur credit account for purchase receipt is ITBR / GRINYA / GIT .

In lot pricing lot result is calculated at the time of goods issue / price change for goods already issued out of lot . Revaluation of FTP price probably had some bug some years ago . There may be a fix probably in ur version.

When u change the lot price in table tdltc004 , do take in to account tdltc001 lot price and lot result . Further test price change (tdpur4122 / acp1140) and issue from all sessions (including warehouse / inventory adjustment ) . If u r through will ur changes in all situations , tally ur GRINYA, tally lot valuation to stock balance in finance , integration and finance does not have any discrepency , u can use ur changes to tdltc004 and tfgld410.
