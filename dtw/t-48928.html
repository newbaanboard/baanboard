I would delete the outbound advices tdilc4211m000 -> the reservation for the material will be removed. The situation should be corrected with this action.

If you can identify the "error" by the runnumber you will have no loss of correct outbounds, e.g. select on the mask runnumber from "user1" to "user1", order type "Production Order".

You could test it with one production order to see the result.
