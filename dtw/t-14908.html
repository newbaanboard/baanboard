1 purchase
2 sales
3 prod issue
4 prod receipt
5 stockcorrection
6 transfer
7 inventory

If you are running out of free numbers, customization would be the solution.

I echo the other replies. Since the key to the tables are actually warehouse order # and the date/time stamp we don't have any problems with accurate data, transactions and integrations. We probably now have better than 5,000 duplicate warehouse order numbers since we reset the first free number.

I do have one suggestion. Please consider archiving your inventory transactions when you reset. We did not do this for many months and it caused user confusion (looking at 2 warehouse orders with the same number). At least the first use of the number will be in your archive company and the nost recent use will be live.
