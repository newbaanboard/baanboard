Dear Sriram,

Please use tcmcs0139m000 and modify the details for Maintain Receipts here by putting the step number of Maintain Approvals in the Re-execute field. What this does is allow you to perform changes in Maintain Receipts before you finally approve the receipt using Maintain Approvals
