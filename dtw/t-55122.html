Hi,
If the storage and inventory units are different for that item then it could also be due to the shortage of the item in terms of its storage unit against its inventory unit. Sometimes the storage inventory reported can be less than what it should be (inventory unit x conversion). This is often the case when ILC Parameter setting "Convert Quantity from Inventory to Storage Units" & "Convert Quantity from Storage to Inventory Units" is set to [No]. With this set to No, whenever someone changes the storage unit value manually in the Maintain Outbound Data session, it doesn't automatically change the inventory unit value and vice versa...
