i have never worked on BaaN IV c2, but the setup in BaaN IVc4 is as follows:(same could be tried in yr system)

Default: Item Master "inspection" ='Yes'
Supplier Master Inspection field ='Yes'
Mandatory: PurcHase Order Inspection field ='Yes' (If above two settings are done, PO inspection automatically becomes 'Yes')

In order Type: Add Maintain Approval session.
Now, when the material is received thru "maintain Receipt" the next step will be "maintain Approval"
