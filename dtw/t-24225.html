Hi,

Best way is to cancel the back order Qty giving a reason as "Customer Cancellation".

Select the Sales Order Lines....Go to Deliveries and Select the Back Order Qty of 2000 Pieces and click Ctrl+F5, given Reason for Cancellation and click on Cancel.
