I would not import the costs from excel as there are several tables and item fields that get updated when calculating the cost. What you need to do is to correct the surcharges and then run a cost roll. The changes will be made and the inventory costs will be updated automatically. You will see an adjustment to inventory and an adjustment to what ever account you specified in the integration tables. There should be no need to adjust inventory in and out.
