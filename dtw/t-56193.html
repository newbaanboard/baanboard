I assume that the item is an Inventory item and you are trying to move it from one physical location to another. But the inventory still remains with the company.
In such a case you can use Replenishment orders and print a RPL packing slip.

The other alternative is to have a zero value sales order to a one time customer and follow the normal sales cycle in which you can print the packing slip. Even though you invoice it will be of zero value and have no impact on your AR.
