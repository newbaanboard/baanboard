This has more to do with the principal of lot control. If lot control is switched on it means that you are controlling your stock/lot and need accurate info about the lot. This includes where the lot is physically stored. Without this info your lot info will be incorrect wich asks the question why you are using lot control? This is the same reason why you don't backflush lot controlled items.
