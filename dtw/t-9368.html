Could it be related to your parameter setting for time phased inventory?

If your parameter for this is yes. Depending on the sales order required date, order lead time of the item, allocations, outstanding purchase orders and production orders within that lead time Baan may or may not signal an inventory shortage. Should the available inventory be positive during the lead time you will get no shortage message.
