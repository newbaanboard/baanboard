Since there are transactions recorded on the warehouse, you need to follow a different route to enable the Location control in Warehouse.

1. Define the locations for the warehouse in session "whwmd3500m000" for Receiving, Inspection, Staging and at least one bulk Location.
2. In session "whwmd2500m000 - Warehouses", select the warehouse and click on "Change location system" from Specific Menu.

There enter the details and follow the commands in the sessions.

I hope this functionality is available in FP7.
