Hi Simon,

on a short view I think the problem is that the field tdsls401.dltp "Delivery Type" is not a part of the standard EDI set-up and therefore not defined in the responsible library tdslsdll4280 "Receive sales orders from EDI". If the field is added to the conversion set-up it will simply ignored during the processing of the incoming EDI orders.
