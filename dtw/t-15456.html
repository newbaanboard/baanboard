Hi,

Information usually depends on the way data is used, mostly sales information is related to turnover, costprice and quantities by:
- year/period
- itemgroup
- stat group
- orderline status
- customer
- etc

Important fact is that it should be possible to make a effective selection on how much data will be on the report, standard Baan only has a few session to print information, they are only based on the indexes. So I made my own session which has a lot of selection fields, so user can easily determine which information to print; of course reports are customised, but that is normal.

Additionally Key users can have following further stats .

1. Quantity sold (sales unit wise).
2. Average sales price realization (over various dimensions)
3. Average margin per sales unit (over various dimensions)
4. Price realization per key bottleneck (e.g. expensive raw material , time required to manufacture the item etc)
