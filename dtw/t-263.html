12th October 2001, 09:55
A cost item is the way to go. Once you have specified a cost item in the purchase order lines, you have access to the fields on form 3 where you can specify the ledger account and dimensions to be debited with the value of the cost item. If you make the purchase order a 'collect order', the transactions will be posted to integration immediately on saving the line.
