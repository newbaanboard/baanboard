Hi Reeyah,

Statistics report can be printed to act on the below mentioned procedures.

(1) Maintain the Statistics Level with attributes
(2) Put the default level of Sales & Purchase in Statistics Parameter
(3) Update the Statistics with check box on Turnover ,with all options
(4) Put the statistics sort, with attributes and make it active
(5) Create the statistics layout with combination of Statistics Sort and permutation & Combination of selection of option.
(6) Print the Statistics
