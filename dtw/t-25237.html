When you have stock left and the option "Assign to Inventory" is selected in session "Inventory Variances" (whina1516m000), the inventory value is updated. In that case a Warehouse Valuation/Retroactive Value Adjustment is booked in tfgld4510m000.

When the goods are not in inventory anymore, the variance will be posted to finance with a Warehouse Valuation/Value Correction booking.
