If you press the letter-combination Shift+F1 you get a "Help-Questionmark" in any case, even if it is not exisiting in the BaanV Session.

This does not work on some Citrix installation. It anyway works in our BaanV environment.

An other way to access the help text is by opening the form editor, doubleclick on the field and then press the Text Button (Help information)
