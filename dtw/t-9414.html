Hi BJ you didn't mention if the outbound records had been released or not. In the case of a released outbound advice where no inventory remains ilc101 won't have a corresponding record, in the case of a non-released outbound advice ilc101 should have a corresponding record with either an expected or allocated quantity reflecting the quantity on the outbound.
