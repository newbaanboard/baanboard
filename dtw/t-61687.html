Hello,

you have three possibilities zo achieve item inspection at goods receipt.

1st
Supplier Level
All goods of the supplier are checked
2nd
Item Level
The item whatever which supplier is checked
3rd
PO position level
The item at PO position is checked

If a PO is done with 1 or 2, the checkbox should be enabled by default.
