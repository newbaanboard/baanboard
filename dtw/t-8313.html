Credit limit can be set against the customer in Maintain Customer tccom1101m000 form 5 in Baan in5. I hope that u have upgraded to Baan in5 recently.

Now in the Distribution Parameters go to session Maintain Sales Order Parameter (tdsls4100m000) form 2, select the option for "Block if Credit limit is exceeded" to "NO" and "Signal if Credit limit is exceeded" to "YES". Then select the option "YES" for "Block if Invoice is overdue".

Check this step and revert back. I think your problem will be solved.
