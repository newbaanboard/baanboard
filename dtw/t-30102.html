From 'Items - Warehousing' session another session can be started called 'Convert (to) Serialized Item' (whltc5201m000). This will allow to switch to non-serialized. So it is not like toggling a field. Changing the field will be the result of running the session.
I guess this on its own might not justify the transition to FP3 but maybe there is more you are looking for. ;)
