Hi! In answer to your question about the tdilc402 table and when it gets deleted, it is deleted when the session tdpur4223m000 is executed. Please note, this session will also delete data from the following tables:
tdilc111, tdpur040, tdpur041, tdpur042, tdpur045, tdpur046, tdpur47, tdpur048

Updates:
tdpur301, tdpur306 and could tdsls041, tdsls045

I hope this helps out.
