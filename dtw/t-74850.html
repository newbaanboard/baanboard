Here is the solution i found if it helps anyone.

1.Go to the Stockpoint details which you can find the option once you open the shipment line.
2.Open the Stockpoint details and zero the quantity to ship. This will update the line with the Not Shipped
3.Freeze the shipment and then confirm it. You might get a message about the packing slip being zero and
4.Now, return the Not Shipped Goods so they will be moved from the stag location to the location they cam
5.Make sure your warehouse transfer processed correctly. You will get a message with the transfer number

Go to the sales order and cancel the backorder line if desired.
