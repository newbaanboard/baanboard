Dear Teddy,

I think in location controlled warehouse, the inventory is stored in different locations but one can only use the inventory stored in "pick' ( or bulk) location for any fresh transaction (Ware house issues,inventory adjustment, cycle counting even).Rest all locations (inspection, rejections, Receipt) carry inventory of a transaction that has not completed the full cycle, hence the inventory lying at these locations will be posted to any of the pick or bulk once you complete the receipt cycle and thus will be available for any fresh transaction.
