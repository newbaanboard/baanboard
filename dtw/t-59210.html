Not an LN expert but ... as far as I remember the LN trainings there is another concept in LN:

Adjustment Orders

The Workflow:

(1) Reasons (tcmcs0105m000)
(2) Itemstoc per warehouse (whwmd2515m000)
(3) Adjustment order (whinh5120m100)
(4) Process adjustment order (whinh5221m000)

The authorization is done within the peoples module:

Employee ---is member of---> Team ---- has n ---> Roles (with authorizations)

Employee: bpmdm0101m000
Team: tcppl0140m000
Role:tcppl0160m000
