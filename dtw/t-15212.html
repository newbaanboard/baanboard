There is no commission calculated for orders with a total amount of zero. This leads to severe problems when reusing the order number: the status of the record in tdcms040 remains "Free". Due to this status it never gets deleted by session "Print and/or Delete Commissions/Rebates" (tdcms0251m000). The automatic link process fails (I would expect an error message like "Duplicate Value in tdcms040").

CMS Parameters

Search for Commission Based on Date: Invoice Date

I had similar problems with tdcms041.
There were thousands of records that would never be processed into commissions (because of master data set up errors), and they had a status of 'free' in the table.

I wrote a simple delete routine that cycled through tdcms041 and confirmed that no commission would ever be paid on those order lines, and then deleted the records from the table.

I would recommend that you do something similar.

Removing the non-commissionable entries from these tables can greatly decrease the processing time of 'calculate commissions'.
