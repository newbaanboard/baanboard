Do you mean an in-transit warehouse as was used in Baan IV? If so they are not present in LN. When the despatching warehouse completes the outbound process the quantity transferred comes out of inventory and increases the 'in-transit' quantity from the despatching warehouse. An identical quantity is 'on-order' in the receiving warehouse. A parameter determines which warehouse is liable for anything that goes missing in transit.
