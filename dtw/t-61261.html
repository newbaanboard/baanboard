The switch "Enter Specific order" is yes.

There is a subform behind the switch where you can enter up to 10 specific order numbers. Maybe it is empty or an old number is in the subform (User defaults?). In this case the selection order from/to on the main form is not evaluated (with option Enter specific order = yes).

Another Point the field "Item to": are there all ZZZZ (16 times) filled or is one missing in the end? The item in the order has 16 characters starting with an Z, therefore will not be in the selection.

Please try to tab all fields of the mask, maybe then it will print.
