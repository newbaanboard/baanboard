It is difficult to give an answer, because a lot of little things can cause this message.

1. I suppose you did fill in the ranges correctly in session tdpur1202m000.
2. The rfq line should be set to "Convert to Order" in session tdpur1805m000.
3. expiry date on the rfq line should not be filled or should be in the future.
