Just to let you know we manage to find a solution. we are using a multicompany environment and company 141 ws not used any more, so as SSA suggested, we deleted tables and logical as well.

We noticed from the SQL that tables tcmcs065 and tcmcs003 are used. By checking we realised that departments and warehouses of deleted company were assigned to that company(141) so we changed that using ttaad4100 to an existing company. I think in tdpur4500m000 the next documents to be displayed were those of company 141 and that concluded in the error.

Now it works fine. Hope it is fine as well.
