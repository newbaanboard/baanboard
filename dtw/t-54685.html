Hi,
I hope its the same like as in how you do it in Baan4. Below is the step for extending the description field...

1) Go to Maintain Domains
2) Copy the domain "tc dsca" to your own VRC package (if such a thing exists in your demo company).
3) Click on the copy command after selecting the domain "tc dsca" in Form1.
4) Once copied, in Form2 enter the desired length in field String Length
5) Click on Save Button and then Click on Special menu and select "Convert to Runtime Data Dictionary {ttadv5215s000}" (you can even run this separately ttadv5215m000)..
6) Select the package combination you are part of, as in Step 2
7) Enable the check boxes Domains, Tables, Reconfigure Tables & Print Errors.
8) Select packages <Blank> to zz
9) Select tables 000 to zzz999 and click on continue.
10 Re-start your Baan (may need to stop and restart Shared memory)..
