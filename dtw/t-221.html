this can occur during cycle counting if you do not freeze transactions.
1 When you genererate a cycle count baan stores the on hand value , lets say 10
2 some issues 8 pieces for whatever reason oh now = 2

3 your cycle count goes and counts 2 :- baan posts the delta 10-2=8 resulting in -6 even with neg inv not allowed.
solution is to block during cycle counting.
hope this helps

As well as problems encountered if the warehouses are not blocked during cycle counting I have also seen negative inventory being created by replenishment order transactions trhough transit warehouses. The stock is correct in both the source and destination warehouses but seems to "hang" in the transit warehouse also leaving negative transactions. However in both cases tdinv0252m000 will identify and be used to clear these negatives. I have not yet had to resort to GTM to clear these.
