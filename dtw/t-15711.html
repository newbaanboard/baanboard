there are 2 fields "Inspection" in the sessions "Mantain item data" (form 3) and Mantain Suppliers (form 4) to determine if the receptions of an item (or the receptions of a supplier) uses the inspection procedure. If those fields have value "yes" you've to execute "Mantain Approvals" (tdpur4121m000") to approve the receptions.
