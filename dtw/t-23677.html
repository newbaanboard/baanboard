Tauhaina,

Just went over my old Baan Support cases and discovered that the inconsistencies we were getting when running Inventory Valuations were caused by the post dating of Inventory Transactions. This itself was caused by a defect fixed by solution 135414.
Basically, when Initiating Inventory Issues for production orders, there is an "Issue Date". This was being used as the actual issue date in Inventory Transactions by Warehouse instead of the actual outbound release date. This then changed Inventory Valuations retrospectively.

Not sure which SP this solution arrived in but the problem was identified somewhere between SP 10 and 11.

Test this scenario out and see if it's causing your problems.

Martin.
