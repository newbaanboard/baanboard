Part of the shop order release procedure will shift allocations from a BOM warehouse to a warehouse linked to a workcenter (wip warehouse). When I said transfer allocations, I meant the allocations would go down in the BOM warehouse and up in the warehouse linked to the workcenter when the Shop Order is released. You don't have to set it up that way. You can just put the same warehouse in the BOM that you would link to the workcenter and when the shop order is created the allocations are created.
