I have had problems with the parent customer for statistics functionality as well.
My problems revolved around the sorting of customers on sales stats reports.

Lets say you have 3 parents with 3 children each.
Parent A0 has children A1, A2, A3
Parent B0 has children B1, B2, B3
Parent C0 has children C1, C2, C3

When sales stats reports are printed by parent customer \ children, the sorting is

Parent Customer = BLANK
A0
B0
C0

Parent Customer = A0
A1
A2
A3

Parent Customer = B0
B1
B2
B3

Parent Customer = C0
C1
C2
C3

It will not group the parent with the children, and give a total for the parent + children.

Your problem could also be that you need to select print = yes to both the 'Parent Customer' and 'Customer' when printing sales statistics.

TIP : When dealing with multiple currencies in sales statistics, be careful, Baan IV will not get the correct historical exchange rate, it will take the current exchange rate.
