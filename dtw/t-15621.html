In Baan IV there is no standard functionality to print the supplier text in the PO.

A work-a-round is to customize the report and take the supplier text in the report. You could can use the rules of text printing, e.g. filtering with the first sign at a row to print or not to print a text.
With the way, You define the sign e.g. "#" as the first sign in the supplier text, that should be printed on the PO print but not the other rows.
