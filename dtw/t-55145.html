To determine the requirement date:
The requirement date is the date on which the projected on hand drops below the reorder point.
To determine the actual requirement date, SSA ERP takes into account the actual calendar. If the requirement date is not available according to the actual calendar, SSA ERP looks backwards for the first available date/time before the requirement date. SSA ERP sets the first available date/time as the actual requirement date.
To determine the planned receipt date
SSA ERP determines the planned receipt date by planning forwards from the requirement date and taking into account the actual calendar.
SSA ERP determines the planned receipt date as follows:

planned receipt date = planned requirement date + outbound lead time + inbound lead time + item safety time + supplier safety time

SIC planning will not consider Delivery dates. SIC plans material as if it needs it today. Hence it takes today plus Total supply time.

For Delivery (Requirement) date based planning, make the item as planned and then use Enterprise planning..
