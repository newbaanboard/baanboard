Dear Friends,

we can revert back the warehouse activity till the generate picking list. Once the picking list is confirmed, It means the shipment number is generated,

Now ,
1)If only outbond is generated then you can delete the outbond line to revert the allocated inventory.
2) If outbound is confirmed and picking is generated then by removing of picking you can again revert your allocated inventory.

3) In case picking is confirmed and shipment is generated , In that case you can confirm the shipment with 0 quantity. System automatically revert your staged Inventory to bulk inventory.



Hope this information is useful.
