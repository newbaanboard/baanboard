Hi VIP,

Baan IV tables
tdpur040 = purchase order header
tdpur041 = purchase order line
tdpur045 = purchase order receipts
tdpur046 = purchase invoices

You can see that info also via GTM (ttaad4500). I saw that you refer to Baan V in another post. So I added the Baan V tables also.

Baan V tables
tdpur400 = purchase order header
tdpur401 = purchase order lines
tdpur401 = purchase order receipts
tdpur401 = match and approve info from ACP
More info about WH receipts can be found in whinh210 (Inbound Order Lines).
More info about the purchase invoices can be found in the ACP module:
- tfacp240 (Order Data for Approval)
- tfacp245 (Receipts by Packing Slip)
- tfacp250 (Invoices related to Purchase Order Lines)
- tfacp251 (Invoices related to Purchase Receipt Lines)
