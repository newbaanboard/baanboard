All I write is reffered to BaaN IV c4

You define Integratin Ledgers in tdinv8150m000.

Integration ledgers must have this requisites:

- Sublevel 0
- Type No Intercompany nor Text
- No fixed assets Integration
- Not Blocked
All this can be checked in tfgld0108m000 (Definition of that ledger)

Also this account must not be present in accounts defined for Financial Customer Group nor for Financial Suppkiers Group:
See tfacr0111s000 accesed from tfacr011m000 (For Customers) & tfacp0111s000 accesed from tfacp0110m000 (For Suppliers)

Does your account fit with these?
