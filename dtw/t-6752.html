Hi,

Have in mind that the average purchase price calculation may be in different moments, depending on the value of the "Integration with Baan Finance" parameter (in tdpur6100m000).

If the value of this parameter is yes (an therefor you have integration from purchase to finance), the update on the average price is done when the approval of the purchase invoice is done. If the value of this parameter is no, this update is made when you process the purchase orders (tdpur4223m000).
