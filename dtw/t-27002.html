Found out from the forum know that direct delivery means delivery goods directly from supplier to customer.
That's it! In other words: a purchase order will be generated (manually, interactively, automatically) and linked to the sales order. In theory and practice the goods will never touch your warehouse. For that reason the warehouse in the salesorderline is empty and you won't find an inventory transaction for that salesorderline (tdinv150).
The ordersteps Print Picking List.... (if applicable) and Maintain Deliveries will be skipped an replaced by "Awaiting Direct Delivery". As soon as the "phantom" reciept for the purchase orderline is done the sales order can be processed completely (Print Sales Invoice..).

Hope this helps.
