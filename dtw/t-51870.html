Hi Umesh Mittal,

Good Day!

1. We maintain Production Order for subcontracting item (tisfc0101m000)
2. Release Production Order (tisfc0204m000)
3. Generate Subcontracting Order (tisfc2250m000) - a Subcontrating Purchase Order will be automatically generated thru this session

Note:
Subcontracting rates were properly maintained for subcontracting items.
Subcontracting Items for Manufactured items were maintained in a customized sub-session in Maintain Item Data which is Maintain Item Master Extension
The price maintained in Maintain Subcontrating Rate session is the price of the Subcontracting Item of the Manufactured item.
e.g. Manufactured Item = BC-293255-R
Subcontracting Item = BC-293255-R-S (Item Master Extension)
