If i am understanding correctly , then your question is regarding the difference between normal & cost order type. I have noted following things in LN6.1 FP2,
1) There is no Cost type Order, but you start with Normal Purchase Order Type only.
Here you have to choose any item you have to order. You cam choose Cost Type of Items, or Service type of item or Purchased or Manufactured type of items.
2) The Normal Purchase Order has step Release To Warehousing.
3) If in this Purchase Order, you choose Cost Type or Service Type of Item, and Approve it, then the system eliminates the step Release To Warehousing. While processing this order the order is not released in the warehousing.
4) The Service or Cost type of Items are not inventorised, and hence there is no need of it treating in the Warehousing module.
5) The Receipt is maintained in the Purchase Receipt only. And transaction is completed.
