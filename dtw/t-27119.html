If I remember correctly (am not in the situation to check right now), changing the delivery date after having already printed the order prompts you to print the order again. This makes sense since the output of course will be different.

Therefore the letter 'c' is put in the procedure mask to indicate that you have to execute that step again. I suppose it refers to 'change'.
