Hi - not sure what all you are wanting from a HR package, but we have developed a Time and Attendance add on for Baan called RMCtime. This would be a 3rd party product.

Our system tracks vacation, personal time, allows for better entry and supervisor approvals. It has new reports for HR including:

Daily Attendance
Exceptions (for just about anything)
Vacation
Overtime

The system tracks labor and attendace time separately, leaving the labor in Baan, but the attendance records are handled differently within our system, allowing for a more 'HR' view of attendance.

It has configurable options for time entry, and links with our shop floor data collection solution (optional). It has configurable financial posting for indirect and absense time.

This also has an interface to payroll (ascii file export), creates a payroll batch that is maintainable. It does not replace the payroll packages, such as ADP, etc.

Let me know if you are interested in more information, or post up on what you are expecting for others to understand.
