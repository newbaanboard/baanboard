Good Morning!
I think I have the answer. In the session tdinv3201m000 Generate Planned INV Orders (this is the SIC planning engine) BaaN allows you to generate planned orders for items with an order method of Manual. Check the drop down in the field titled Order System and you will see the possibilities are SIC, MPS, MRP, FAS and MANUAL. I ran my item this morning and selected Manual and sure enough BaaN generated a planned purchase order. I actually think it's neat to have this flexibility. Currently on our Manual items we make out purchase reqs. I'll be telling our planning group about this today. It's going to save time in our ordering process.
Hope this is your answer,
