Hi amarpreet,

the order date in your PUR-order should match the range between effective- and expiry date of the contract. If not, no contract is linked to the orderline. Be careful when recalculating price and discounts for the PUR-order when the expiry date is reached. This may unlink the contract from the orderline.

As a kind of workaround (when the expiry date is reached), you can "backdate" the order date of the PUR-order(line) to access the expired contract. This is not very convinient to my opinion.

Once the contract is linked to an orderline, you can recieve items against the contract, even if it's expired.

Hope this helps.

Martin
