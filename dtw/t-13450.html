In ILC there is a posibility to maintain your stock in stock unit (=inventory unit). This is the unit used also in inv.

There are different fieldnames used for the stock unit / inventory unit. In tdilc101 these fields read stk? (for stock): stks (stock on hand) stkb (blocked stock) stka (allocated stock) and stko (on order). These fields correspond with the qstc field in tdilc402 (quantity stock unit).

Also you can maintain stock in storage unit. This is like you have 6 pieces in one box. The box is the storage unit, the pieces is the stock unit. Note: there may be boxes that are not completely filled! There is no fixed conversion rate between stock and storage unit. In the end it is pieces that count.

The storage unit are str? In tdilc101 and qstr in tdilc402 (quantity in storage unit).

Then there are two fields astc (already) allocated stock (stock unit, allocated for inbound movement) and a recommended quantity stock (recq).

Hope this helps you a bit.
