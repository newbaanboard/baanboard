Inv unit is the base unit around which all other units revolves. Once transacted this can't be changed however others can be.
You can book purchase orders in Purchase Unit say Box, while making receipts system will inventorise it in storage unit & in inventory unit. You will make payment to the supplier based on boxes recd, which will be automatically be convereted after you save a receipt (in ILC implemeneted).
While issuing inventory to shop floor or for any other purpose you will use storage unit & inv unit.
