Manish,

You may have already found out what your issue could be, but I thought I would post a reply just in case.

In Maintain Definitions and Conversion Setups for your EDI message, go to the .cdel field. You are defining on form one what position the code is in. However, I wonder how you filled out form 2. For the .cdel field enter and on form 2 for that line, enter the position where the code "92" is. Then enter "92" in the Qualifier 1 default field.

I'm not an expert with EDI, but I know we have had th issue before too. I just thought I'd offer up this information.

We are on Baan IVc4 NAS0 extersion, but I think your session looks the same as ours.
