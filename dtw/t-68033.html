Hi:
To deliver the sales order, you may carry out inventory adjustment (-ve inventory for the item present in the warehouse & +ve inventory for the item required for the sales order).
As per my understanding the effectivity unit is prospective and not retrospective. So henceforth all the items "manufactured" / "purchased" by you would be having interchangeable effectivity unit.
