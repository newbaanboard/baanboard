Hi Bhushan,

You can make a contract for this particular XX item for period 01.01.08 to 01.06.08 , Only you have to change price while preparing sales order from contract.(Any way you are going to put different price for different sale order for this item)
Also you have to set parameter for Action on exceeding maximum quantity as HOLD or INTERACTIVE, so that system will inform you that you have reached to max quantity.

Hope this will work for you.
