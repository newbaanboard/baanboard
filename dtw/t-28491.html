I found out how an external text editor could be used. So out goes the curiosity bit..

Apart from that i also found out that, when using an external text editor like notepad, Baan saves the text entered in same setup as the text group which in your case is 50 characters wide. However while entering the text, it doesnt restrict you to 50 characters. Once you save and open the text that you entered, you coud find out that the text appears as 50 characters wide.

If you need restrictions at the time of entering texts, why not use Baan Text Editor. To do this, just remove the Text Group entry from the session Maintain Text Groups with External Editor (tttxt1116m000).

It would be interesting to see if anyone knows how to restrict Notepad.
