The average price and last purchase price are updated after invoice approval but the std cost price is not changed until you calculate cost price and update std cost. (And then, only if your Cost Price Calculation Code uses Ap and Lp.

The std cost price won't get updated unless you re-calculate the std cost price. So if the variance is too much then it is always better to recalculate the std cost before the invoice matching.
