Hi noorulhaq,
Unfortunately the Transporttation package is sparsely used and documented. We had a hard time to find the proper configuration for our needs. But in the end it's worth the effort. Here is what our standard salesorder type looks like:

1) Print Acknowledegement
2) Copy Sales Oders to Transportation (trtoc2200m000)
3) Print Picking List
4) Maintain Deliveries
5) Print Packing Slip
6) Carry out Trips (trtop4290m000)
7) Print Sales Invoice
8) Process Delivered Sales Orders

Unlike your company, we turned on the TR parameters from the very beginning of our projekt. This has several advantages for the integrity of the data which are involved in both packages TR and TD.

Good luck!

Martin

15th November 2005, 17:43
Hi noorulhaq,
You can not carry out a trip with status 'Free'. A trip with this status is simply nothing but itself.
You have to have transport orders, generate stops for those transport orders and the assign these stops to a trip. This trip automatically gets status 'Planned' instead of 'Free' as sson as the first stop has been assigned. Once the planning is confirmed, you have to modify the status to 'Firm Planned'. Only firm planned trips can be carrried out.

Regards,

Martin
