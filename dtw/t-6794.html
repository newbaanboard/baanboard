If you have defined alternative items, then, at the point of order entry should you have a stock shortage of the item you require the user is prompted to take action by the system. At this point you can choose to look at the alternative item. Alternative items are not used in MRP ect but are rather a tool which can be used to satisfy a customer order where alternative stock is available. It is not true to say that Alternatives are only used by PCS. Although this may be the only planning engine which takes them into consideration.
