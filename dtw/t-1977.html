(In case you are on BaanIV) you should use in this situation the inter company sales and purchase orders \based on internal EDI\ and you will have to set the Intercompany Transactions and Intercompany Processing Data in Finance. These settings will enable the system to distinguish the intercompany relations from external sales and purchases made by the logistic companies.
