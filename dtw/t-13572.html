The Cost Item is due to the use of installments. Look at the Sales Order Parameters (tdsls4100m000) form 3 and you will see that there are Cost items defined for installments. The field help will explain why this is done.

You may want to talk to Baan Support about the fact that this is causing problems with the invoice totals.
