In IVc4, there are updates to these tables as soon as you save the RPL line. The script calls tcmcs.dll0100.process.stock.transactions() for both the destination and replenishment warehouses. This creates an allocated quantity with a planned requirement transaction for the replenishment warehouse and an "on order" quantity with a planned receipt transaction for the destination warehouse.

Is this what you wanted?
