Each bit represents a particular blocking reason. In terms of decimal values, here are the reasons. If more than one applies, add the values.


1 = Doubtful customer
2 = Credit limit exceeded
4 = Unpaid invoices
8 = Electronically received (EDI)
16 = Margin control
32 = Manually blocked
64 = Warehouse belongs to other financial company


I hope that helps.
