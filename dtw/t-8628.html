Robert,

Use session Maintain Organizations (tcedi0103m000) to maintain date format. On page2 you will find the field for date formatting. Please note that this date format applies for entire EDI module. Other fields I doubt it can be controlled in BaaN. It has to be done outside of BaaN, in transaltion software.
