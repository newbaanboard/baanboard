On the Shipment tab of the "Shipments" (whinh4630m000) session you see the load number. Via the Specific/Loads option, the "Loads" (whinh4140m000) session starts with the related load on top.

Open the load and when the "Pick-up Carrier/LSP" is filled, the "Carrier PRO Number" as given by the carrier can be entered.
