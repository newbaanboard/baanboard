The table tcibd100 is indeed obsolete - it was phased out as of LN 10.2.1 to reduce locking during inventory transactions.

Main table for the "Item Inventory" (whwmd4100m100) session is whwmd400 - as it contains all items for warehousing.

The inventory overview in whwmd4100m100 is a sum of the whwmd215 quantities. ("Warehouse - Item Inventory" (whwmd2515m000) session)
