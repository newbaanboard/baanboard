The current Planned delivery date may be the same as the Planned delivery date but only if the changed delivery date and/or the confirmed delivery date fields are not filled with a different value. In other words the planned delivery date is what was the original planned delivery date and the currrent planned delivery date is what the planned delivery date is thought to be at this time.

the Current Planned Delivery Date is the relevant date for the planned inventory transaction. It's updated according to the hierarchy Changed Delivery Date > Confirmed Delivery Date > Planned Delivery Date.
