Hi,

In B5.0c, it is possible to print "Cost Components", as there is separate flag in the session.

The amount is calculated based on the 'To-be Consumed Qty' present in whinr112 table, along with the amount present in whinr113 table.

Note: In B5.0b, both quantity and amount are present in whinr112 itself. Since whinr113 is not present, the amount is the cumulative amount and is not possible to find amount by cost components.
