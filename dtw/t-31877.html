I am using BaaN 5b/5c, but I guess this also goes for LN.

You could do this if you would use more than one warehouse. In "Item Data by Warehouse", you can setup different warehousing parameters per warehouse (safety stock, EOQ, ...). You could recalculate these parameters afterwards with "Global Update of Inventory Parameters".

No, that's not possible. You will need to review your safety stock on item level or recalculate it with "Global Update of Inventory Parameters", using the new parameters. There's no session or "button" that allows to update, based on MRP results.
