Preethi,
The replensihment order purpose is to transfer the inventory between warehouses. The use of transit warehouse is optional. And transit warehouse is used only for storing inventory in transit.

You can also use the tdilc session. Both will do teh transfer, but the process and purpose is different. The replenishment order is more like a order flow, where once an order is genrated it can be picked, packing slip can be printed and then invenotry can be received at other warehouse. While the tdilc session is to do q quick transfer, there is no documents and paper flow in this.
