In the selling company, I guess you have:

- In the "Maintain Relations" (tcedi0110m000) you have the buying company as a customer.
- In "Maintain relation data by Network" (tcedi0128m000) you have that relation established for the network you are using. Check that the value in the "Network identification" field is the same as the value in the field "Our Identification" in Maintain Networks (tcedi0120m000) in the originating company (this is a normal cause of this kind of errors)
- In maintain customers (tccom1101m000) the customer related to the originating company (and therefor to the relation before) is an "Affiliated company" (form 5) with the correct company number under it.
