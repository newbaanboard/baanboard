For Performa Invoices go to session Print Sales Invoice in Invoicing part of Sales Order Control and in this session, keep the option of Print final invoice as NO.

This would help you print a performa invoice and whenever you have to print the final invoice make this field as YES and aging print it would print a final invoice and the transactions would then be taken to finance

Hope this help you.
