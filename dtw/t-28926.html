hi luann,
Couple of ways to do this ranging from simple to not so simple. Looking at the simple way, i could think of two options. To have the production order number entered into the line item text of the purchase order (like what Darren suggested) or entering a combination of purchase order and line number on the production order text(e.g: 12345601).
The not so simple way is to customize the item(s) in question and so both the purchase order line item and production order item will share the same project. I stated this as not so simple just because it involves extra steps like creating project, customizing items, project closing etc. But rest assured, it works...
