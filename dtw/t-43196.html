With ref to your points...
1. when we make a production order, thier bom material must be show in the ticst0101m000 session field "issue", but, the material was show in the subsequent delivery field.
This happens if anyone updates the "Issue" field. Depending on the figure thats put in the "Issue" field, the "Subsequent Del" field gets calculated

2. Second, we manual insert the material in the field of "issue" and subsequent delivery field shows that time is zero, then we run the Issue inventory session (tisfc0207m000). But again subsequent delivery field shows same quantity.
Thats unusual and strange. Usually or ideally, when someone enters or changes the "Issue" field once a material is estimated, the "Subsequent Delivery" field gets calculated automatically. If i am right then the equation would be based on the following conditon
Issue < Estimated
Subsequent Delivery = Estimated - Issue
Issue > Estimated
Subsequent Delivery = 0

3. Now, if any production Deptt. user use session "Issue inventory" that material again issued. This scenerio is shows in random production order not in all.
As i said earlier it looks like a bug and its further proven by the version of SP you are on. SP12 is very old and even though i am no fan of SPs, you cant just ignore them..Furthermore you could find out whats the difference among the manufactured items that gives out this unusual behaviour when compared to the items that dont have this problem..
