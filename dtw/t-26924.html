Hello bbnkamba,
We evaluate our vendors' performance, but we do this through a Crystal Report.
The source is the one you mentioned (tdpur401) plus the item supplier info (tdipu010). From the latter we take the expected lead time for the item.

The evaluation focuses on 2 variables: quantity and time.

So, for all purchase lines within a period we calculate the deviation (i) between delivered and purchased quantities and (ii) between expected lead time and actual delivery time.

After setting the parameters (% of tolerances for qty. and time) the vendor rating can be easily done and clearly communicated to suppliers. We had good results so far with this method.
