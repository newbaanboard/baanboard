Dear tgeorgiou,

On hand: inventory level you actually own
On order: incoming stock that hasn't yet arrived
Allocated: outgoing stock that hasn't yet been shipped
Economic = on hand + on order - allocated

When generating or creating outbound advice, the most important fields to be checked are:
- The on-hand and allocated at the location level (whinr140 and whinr150 tables)
- Location allocated field at the item level

The system will look at the on-hand and location allocation, and does not care about (soft) allocation and on-order at the item and item/wrh level.
