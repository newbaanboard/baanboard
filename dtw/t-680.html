Since our last post in 2002, we went ahead and created our own UPS interface. We also expanded that to include FedEx as well. We have successfully implemented this with customers running on Baan IV, V and LN. It provides a one way or two way integration, and handles all of the following:

* Interface to Sales Orders by optionally inserting a freight line
• Updates for freight amount, freight type, quantity shipped, tracking ID, etc...
• Exception reporting to identify and correct potential issues immediately
• Extensive historical tracking
• Option to configure sales order(s) into shipping cartons (Compose and
Confirm Shipments) and Pallet Assignment
