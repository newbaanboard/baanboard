Hi Bogdan,

You can do this in LN FP3 as long as you have not added any Line Items to the Sales Order. In the Sales Order Lines (tdsls4100m900) session, select Sales Order Details (tdsls4100m000) from the toolbar and then you can change the Sales Order Date.

Some people call it a Future Order.
