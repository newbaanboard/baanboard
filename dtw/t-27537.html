however if you use lot control or not, the receipt date is present. You can verify your data with session whina1512m000 "Inventory Receipt Transactions" which refers to table whina112. For each entry, correction and inventory evaluation a new record is added to the table and for each outbound the quantity of whina112.qskt (to be consumed) is diminished according the settings in the warehousing data of the item (FIFO, LIFO).
