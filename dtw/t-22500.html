Change Prices and Discounts after Receipt

Hi Monica,

The only way to create an inventory variance with origin 'Receipt Price Change' is by running session "Change Prices/Discounts after Receipts" (tdpur4122m000).

Other options are not possible.

Financial Integrations

If a price changes is performed, an extra financial transaction (Purchase/Price Variance) is registered in tfgld4510m000.

Open this line and on the 'Posting' tab you find the userID that created the transaction.
