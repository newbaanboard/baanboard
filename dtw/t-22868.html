Judy Miller
17th January 2005, 21:41
Depending on how your purchase order parameters are set the price may have been updated automatically. For example if the price in the item master is 1.00 when the PO was entered then the PO will show 1.00 and item master price. Let's say that now the price in the item master is changed to 2.00. If you go into the PO line and make any change then the new 2.00 price will be set on the PO automatically if the parameter is set to automatic recalculation of prices and discounts. If it is set to interactive then you will get a question as to whether you want to update the price. If you turn this parameter to No then no recalculation will be performed. Hope this makes sense.
