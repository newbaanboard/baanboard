To Restrict number of Production Order in ERP, We use the functionality of Phantom. When you make a Production Order and it child item also have Manufacturing nature. then you have option to phantom that child (mfg) item.

all Raw material related to that phantom item to be consumed by its parent item directly and all operation (Routing Operation) also considered with the Main Item. So, Operation & Material cost calculated accurate calculated.
