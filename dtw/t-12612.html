Hi

Purchase Price Unit can not be changed in the purchase order. So even if you change the purchase unit in the PO the purchase price unit remains as what is defined in the item master. To change the purchase price unit in the item master is not desirable unless the change is permanent. This is because changing the price unit does not update the price lists whyou may have entered

Dear John,

What you were seeing is a pricing flexibility designed into Baan. It allows you, for example, to buy in tons but specify the price in terms of kg. Baan wouldn't automatically change the purchase price to be in terms of purchase unit.

In BaanIV, please check out the purchase price by item/supplier/price group sessions and see whether using those can help solve your problem
