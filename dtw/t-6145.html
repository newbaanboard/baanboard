Hi Nazeem,

as long as you don't want to pay commissions to BP whih are not on the lowest level just set up an agreement for the lowest level with a rate of 20%. In all other agreements the rate is 0%. You will get a warning during calculation that the commisson amount for BP XXXX is zero but the lowest level is calculated correctly anyway. We had a similar problem in the past and solved it like this.
Are you calculating the commission for each orderline or each order? How many orders/orderlines have to be calculated each period? In our case the parameter is set to 'Order', 5000 up to 8000 orders have to be calculated every month.
