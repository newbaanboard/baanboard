If you have a warehosue for each production line then you will need a process to transfer the inventory from main warehouse to production line warehouse. Alternatively, you can use WIP warehouses- one for each production line.When the production order is released, the requirement will be transferref from main to WIP.Replenishment orders can be generated to do the inventory transfer.
I assume the spare parts are also the components of some finished product. You can stock the spare parts in finished warehouse. The only drawback is that say if you are short of spare parts for production run, MRP will not generate the planned production or purchase order as it will see the spare parts stock. You may want to consider to build some kind of safety stock. You can also make spare parts MPS and have the demand be projected-so spare part demand will be indepedent demand and requirement fromproduction order would be dependent demand. But this will make your MPS run longer, but this would be the good way to plan for spare parts.
