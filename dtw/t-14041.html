Hi,

There is a way to do this if you have sufficient rights in Baan :

In Tools / Text Management / Text maintenance / Maintain texts (tttxt1100m000), Insert a new text then notes the number of the text which you created. Then by GTM (General Table Maintenance) in tdsls041 go to screen 5 of your order line record and in order line text area insert your text number.
