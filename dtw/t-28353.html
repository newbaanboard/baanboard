rkathait
10th October 2006, 21:13
A separate storage unit cannot be defined in LN. However some of the warehousing sessions (in cycle counting and inventory adjustments) do allow the user to update the storage unit 'on the fly' and then enter the qty in the storage unit. LN does not allow you to maintain a different storage unit as in baan4. You can explore the functionality of Handling units.
