Hi,
as far as I understand your question, To link a purchase order to sales order you have to, first generate purchase advice from sales control tdsls4240m000

if this purchase advice is confirmed and transfered to purchase order you can see the link in purchase order lines tdpur4501m000 and from specific "Link order- line data"...

You can see the link for individual lines not for the order because you might have transfered the purchase advice into existing purchase order.

Hope this helps

Note: I am using Baan 5.0c
