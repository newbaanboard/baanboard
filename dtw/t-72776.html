When creating a new Sales Order, after choosing a business partner and then press tab, the system will display a message with the following text "Enter Address?".

I would like to know what is causing the message to display?

Thank you.
vahdani
27th February 2020, 18:37
This is because your business partner/customer that you enter in the sales order is defined as a one-time business partner. In such cases the system generates a new empty address code and asks if you wish to enter the address details. There is apparantly no way to circumvent this by one time customers. :rolleyes:
