I know it's a pain not to be able to change the Sold-to BP on a Sales Quotation or Sales Order.

One workaround for this is to:

- Duplicate the original Sales Quotation
- Assign the correct Sold-to BP to the new Sales Quotation
- Delete or cancel the original Sales Quotation

This would work as long as your customer does not care about the different Quotation number.
