2nd biggest source of differences is postdated or misdated inventory transactions. Inventory Valuation report is derived from inventory history tranasactions (I believe primarily inv700).

Any postdated inventory transactions entered after a monthend will impact ledger and Inventory Valuation reports differently. Need to reconcile these differences through tfgld410 analysis.
