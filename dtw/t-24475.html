Martin,
You may have to generate that label yourself. It is not difficult to generate the label. All the data is in the system. Since, I don't know your process I cannot comment on it. At our place we assigned SSCC label for every carton that is shipped from our warehouse. We also capture the content of every carton. If your requirement is similar then, you must be aware Baan doesn't capture this kind of information in system. It will need to customize or you can intergrate with packing and shipping system. In our case SSCC label is generated out of packing system and then that information is later on uploaded in Baan for visiblity to customer service as well as our B2B site.
