Hi, i have got solution.
In session Item-Inventory Structure (whinr1550m000) we look for our item, then we click Specific -> Handling Units. In opened new window we change lines status from In Stock to Closed (Specific -> Close).

Then we can change item line status from Open to Shipped clicking icon Generate Advice in session Outbound Order Lines. Now it's all ok.
