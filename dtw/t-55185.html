Hello Gary,

in ERP LN you could use MLE (Multi Lanuage Extension) and then you can use Multi-Language Descriptions (not only for items) in the whole system. With MLE a "data language" will be available which is independent from the user language and the customer language.

I am not aware of any good solution for MLE in Baan IV.
