Dinesh
As you mentioned, the order was booked on 6th July and costing of that item was done on 12th July. So when you receive the material, it is trying to check costing / valuation of the item as on order date.
Now solution is,
1. Carry out back dated costing of that item (before 6th july) and receive the item or
2. Cancel that order. Carry out costing as on today and received the material after doing costing.

See which option suits you
