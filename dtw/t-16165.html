In Baan inventory valuation is done at the standard cost price maintained
in tiitm0101m000 . The standard cost price is calculated based on purchase
price priority in ticpr1101m000. In ur case it must be latest price for
standard cost price calculation code (maintained in ticpr0100m000). When
user approves invoice in finance (tfacp1140s000) or changes price discounts
(tdpur4122m000), the average price and latest purchase price in tiitm0101m000
gets updated. After this updation (based on ur latest price 6880) the
cost price recalculation (ticpr2210/2220m000) may have taken place which
generated the new cost price and revaluation (u saw in GL). U can print
standard cost prices in ticpr3401m000 to get the correct cost price for
the item.

If u wish the revaluation at average price u need to change the purchase
priority in ticpr1101m000. Also the parameter 'Method of calculating
average price' in tdpur4100m000 is relevant for this purpose.
