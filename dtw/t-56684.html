Hi JP,
Credit memo might be generated in below scenario under of commission functionality:
(1) If you have settle your coms parameters on qty basis, Qty should be negative
(2) If you have settle your coms parameters on amount basis, Amount should be negative.
In my opinion it is not viable to get credit memo on the base of ‘0’ quantity when your comms parameter is based ‘Qty’.
