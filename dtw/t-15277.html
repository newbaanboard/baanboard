What the Final Receipt does when marked it allows for the Received Quantity to be Invoiced and the Quantity not received should be placed on Back Order. The Back Order must be confirmed before you can receive the balance of the quantity ordered. Parameter can be set to automatically confirm back orders. Hope this helps.
