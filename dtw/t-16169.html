All the questions you raise are valid. The firm recommendation is to not change the inventory unit (tiitm001.cuni) because of the problems you have identified -- and numerous others.

Had the item not had any transactions or orders raised/posted then I think you could try changing the unit. But because you have transactions the job would be really big (if you wished to preserve the integrity of the system)

The cleanest way for you to accomodate the change in thinking relating to the inventory unit is to load a new item.
