Hi,

You cannot use a different unit in BOM than the inventory unit.
Maybe you could play with the BOM unit of the end item.

Example : for end item A I need 0.01562 grams of component B. If I would use a BOM unit of 1000 for end item A, I could enter 15.62 grams as net quantity for component B.
