Well, I got it now. Indeed there is no particular setting in Baan that will exactly meet your needs :(
There may be a kind of workaround by using a profit margin. A lower unit price reduces your profit and leads to a reduced commisson rate. Of course the profit is calculated according to the cost price of the item. Depending on your strategy in costprice calculation the basis of commission calcutation will change within a week, month or year. Is that applicable for your situation?
