Hi Flysurf...,
Lot Control & Serialization are two different concepts, A lot is designated to a grouped quantity of an item which is produced or purchased at a single instance(batch). And serilziation gives individual serial nos to each of the items produced or purchased.. i.e helps in identifying each & every unit of the item produced or purchased at a single instance(batch).
However if we need to designate lot nos. of an item "by unit", this acts the same as serial Nos.
