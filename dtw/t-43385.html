Hi,
when creating a warehouse you have to link it to an enterprise unit. This enterprise unit belongs to a financial company.
This message is given when the setup for this financial company and your current logistic company do differ.
Check the setup in session tcemm1570m000 for both the companies.
The currency system should be the same and at least the reference currency.
And maybe even more but that depends on the currency system.
