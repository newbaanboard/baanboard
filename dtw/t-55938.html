Hello,

Yes this can be done with BaaN IV BEMIS, without using an external EDI Converter.

1.) Create PO orders in company A transfer EDI message to company B.
2.) Create Sales Order from EDI in Company B
3.) Create Delivery Advise in company B -> EDI message
4.) Create Purchase Receipt in company A
5.) Create Sales Invoice in company B
6.) ........
