Please check whether you have all number groups and series are filled in the "Inventory Handling Parameters" (whinh0100m000) session.

If e.g. inbound inspection is applicable for this order, but in the Inspection Numbers box, the Number Group and Series for Inbound is empty, no inspection order can be created. As a consequence, the receipt cannot be putaway to an inspection location.
