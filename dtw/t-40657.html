What sort of help you need isnt clear but "Cost Price Structure Not Present for this date" error can be overcome by changing the purchase order date to match the items cost price date. Therefore it doesnt guarantee any control.
To put a check or prevent or uphold SOX control(no orders with null cost) you could put the restriction in Maintain Data Authorization or Maintain Field Data Authorization sessions under Authorization module.

Hope this is what you were looking for...?
