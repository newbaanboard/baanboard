The Allocated Inventory mean that you have either a Production Order or Sales Order that need the Inventory. There is no On Order Quantity so you still need a Purchase Order Or Production Order to get the Inventory. The First screen show you have no current inventory. If you look at Planned inventory Transactions for the Item you can see where the requirements are comming from.
