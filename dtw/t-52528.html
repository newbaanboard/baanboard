if invoicing method, "collect sales orders" already selected.

Please find more details about composing criteria to spit invoice

1)The composing criteria
The composing criteria define whether invoice lines can be grouped on an invoice. For example, invoice lines with different invoice-to addresses or different invoice currencies cannot be grouped on one invoice. In addition, to support dimension accounting, invoice lines that must be posted to different dimensions cannot be grouped on one invoice.
Two types of composing criteria exist:
· Fixed general criteria (Its fixed/Predefined Which cant be changed)
Predefined general composing criteria that always apply to all types of invoices.
Fixed general criteria
· Financial Company
· Dimensions
· Transaction Type
· Sold-to Business Partner
· Invoice-to Business Partner
· Invoicing Method
· Pay-by Business Partner
· Ship-to Business Partner
· Ship-to Address
· Invoice-to Address
· Pay-by Address
· Invoice Currency
· Terms of Payment
· Late Payment Surcharge
· Invoice Delivery Method
· Payment Method
· Tax Country
· Rate Determiner

There seems to be a bug, that two deliveries are not put on the same invoice, even if "compose orders" is selected, if they fall into different vat periods and the cisli parameter for determining the vat date (the one which determines the percentage, not the one which determines the vat period!) is set to "delivery date". Try to set it to "invoice date" - and if that fixes your problem, please file an incident with Infor, so they will finally fix it - I have reported this problem for three customers now and still no solution.
