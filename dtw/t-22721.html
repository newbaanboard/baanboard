Hi ale_baan,

You can block the Sales Orders through a parameter setting in the session"tdsls4100 (form-II) if the Credit Limit Exceeds. Here system simply blocks the Order when the Customer Credit limit exceeds.

But if you want to block the Sales Order due to your supplier delay or long lead time, you can block the Sales Order- Manually.

Pls note that in BaaN IV c4, there no functionality blocking based on Shipping Date.
