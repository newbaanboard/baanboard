Hi

Actual Log date, is the time when you input the data.
Transaction Date is the date you want the system to post the financial transaction.
If you dont do any antedated transaction, then the trx date and act log date will be the same.
in your example trx date is < act log date.
If the user process the transaction in the correct sequence, then there should be no negative stock in inventory after transaction.
