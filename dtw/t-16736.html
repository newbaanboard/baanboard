Hi,

Be aware of the fact that the series used for warehouse orders are hard coded and can't be changed. Series 6 is used for warehouse transfers. If you get to the end of the numbering you have to reset the first free number of series 6. This means that double order numbers can be created but that won't cause any problem since the first index of the table consists of more than just the order number, but it might cause some confusion. You could also opt for archiving the stock transactions first.

Hope this helps.

Frank
