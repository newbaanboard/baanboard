Yes, it is possible. In BaanIVc4, the X12 856 (Advance Shipment Notice) is the EDI document you would need.

The Baan IVb IVc EDI Users Guide (U7041C US) has the documentation regarding the 856. This document is available on the baan support website in the documentation section.
