Hi Ganesh,

unfortunately I'm not on BaanV, but on ERPLN (FP3) you can retrieve that date from the the ci module. Table cisli245 - "Sales and Warehouse Order Onvoice Lines". Fields apdt (Approval Date) or codt (Cutt Off Date) should be the dates when the entry was created in the central invoicing module after session tdsls4247 "Release to Invoicing" was executed.
But not sure if that's also available in BaanV
