Hello Ramakrish -

I have experienced problems and gotten the same error message if the item being sold does not have the commission group field populated in session tiitm0101m000 (Maintain Item Data).

Another cause for the error message for me has been if the item being sold is a customized item, and it does not have a standard item of origin attached (which also must contain a commission group).

This may or may not apply to you, depending on the criteria you have set in your commission module.
