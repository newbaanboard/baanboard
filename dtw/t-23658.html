On-order:
Indicating at point of SO entry, what are the total incoming order for the item; i.e from Purchase Order, transfer order, etc
On-hand:
Indicating at point of SO entry, the total on-hand of the item
Allocated:
Indicating at point of So entry, the total of allocated(soft allocation) order for the item; i.e Sales Order, production order, etc. This inclusive of qty you have just entered in the SO.
