Check the CPR parameter for the number of years to retain history and if needed increase it. Say you maintain history
for 2 years and there was no change in cost price in the past 2 years or you didn't calculate the cost price for the past 2 years for an item. Now when you calculate CPR and the calculated price is different from the previous one, system will update the cost price as well as delete the old cost price since it has crossed 2 years. What you are left with is only the latest
cost price.

You can also look at the old sales orders/purchase orders(tdsls041/tdpur041) to get the std. cost price at that time of purchase/sales for inserting them into ticpr300.

Regards,
