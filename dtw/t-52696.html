Hi Nilesh,
Surely you could write an AFS program to do this for you but before you venture into AFS, it would be usefull to check the exchange schemes. There are two tables involved whenever you push or update new prices/discounts. Below are the table pairings:

sls030 - Prices by Customer & Item
sls036 - Price Parameters by Customer per Item

sls031 - Discounts by Customer & Price Group
sls034 - Discount Parameters by Customer & Price Group

sls032 - Prices by Price List & Item
sls037 - Price Parameters by Price List by Item

sls033 - Discounts by Price List & Price Group
sls035 - Discount Parameters by Price List & Price Group
