Consignment is inventory is a different concept in Baan 5.
This implies to those inventory which belong to your customer or supplier and not to you.

These types of inventory is not accounted for in your inventory valuation.

This functionality i have seen working well with the contract manufacturing setup and might be used for sub contracting types of jobs.

If you look at the warehouse definition then you will find consignment warehouse can be defined for the purpose.
This feature was not there in baan 4 and is introduced in baan 5 only.
