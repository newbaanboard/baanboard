Hi,

Direct delivery is a functionality confined to Distribution and it doesn't instruct finance anything. I haven't seen LN but worked in Baan V and assume the same would be the case in LN too. As far as costing is concerned everything is tied to master data set-up. For your needs you have to go for actual costing. There are options like LIFO, FIFO, MAUC, Lot costing etc. Also, if you want you can have FIFO for costing purpose but at the same time issue the item as LIFO in warehouse, for example. If you want the exact cost has to be applied then you have to go for lot costing(I think. I haven't used this). May be, direct delivery + lot costing combination may fulfil your needs in a simple way but I am not sure.

The 'lot pricing' that is available in Baan IV is only for inventory valuation. Integration entries will always be at standard costs.
