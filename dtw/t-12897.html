Are you using ILC? If not, you can in effect undo the delivery by zeroing both delivered and back order quantities. Then, if you truly want to delete the delivery (tdsls045 record), you can. Be sure that that is what you really intend, however.

If you have ILC on, you have presumably released outbound. It is much more difficult to back out the delivery from there...:(
