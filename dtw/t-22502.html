One of the problems that triggered this discussion is that we do not want the (price of the) software to show on invoices, packing slips, etc.

Customers who notice that the only difference between variant 1 and variant 2 of device a is the software loaded onto it, may question the justification of the price difference.

So we need to make the difference between variant 1 and variant 2 untransparant to the outside world, but at the same time maintain internal tracability of the actual BOM and routing.
