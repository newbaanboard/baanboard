Hi,

Im not sure about your version (Baan IV) but in LN it is possible in the following way.

During the SO Line Entry, Inventory Shortage Handling Menu will gets populated if the inventory is insufficient in the warehouse mentioned in the SO Line (Lets say "FG"). You can select the option "Generate Transfer Order" and give the replenishing warehouse (Lets say "RM") & quantity. In this case, ERP LN will generate the Transfer Order automatically to replenish the goods from RM to FG.
