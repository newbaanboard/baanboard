The client don't use Baan finance, but have a customization that have to be modified (linking item groups against GL-accounts).
They don't use sales statistics!
The procedure:

1. Print all items where item group is going to be changed to Excel,
2. We made a rule to uppdate all items with new item group in the spreadsheet,
3. Maintain new Item groups in Baan, (manually)
4 Maintain Item Default Data, (manually)
5. Maintain Surcharges by Item Group, (manually)
6. Modify the customization that links item groups to GL-accounts,
7. Import new itemgroups from the spreadsheet to tiitm001.citg by using Baan Excange.

Thats it.:D
We used approx. 6 hours to do the job and changed item group for 5900 items.
