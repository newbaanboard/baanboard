HI,

BaaN allows you to change the Sales Unit at the time of maintaining Sales Order, but the Sales price unit cannot be changed. You have top manually convert the price unit and then maintain.

Now if you want that the Converted Sales Unit should be printed in the Invoice or on any other document, then it will need a small customisation.

One more thing you can try is that change the price unit at the time of maintaining Sales Order in Item Data first and then maintain the Sales Order. Once the Sales order had been maintained, you can switch back to your original price unit. This will show every thing perfect in Sales Order as required by you, but how much it will effect integration transaction, that you have to change.
