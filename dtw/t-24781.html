I wouldn't bother to try to understand this session. The report is nonsense. Inventory turns is a common measurement for how well inventory is managed. It is calculated by dividing the annual inventory usage by the AVERAGE on-hand inventory. That's a good indicator for how well inventory is managed.

This report in Baan calculates the turnover rate by dividing the annual usage by the CURRENT on-hand inventory. That doesn't make any sense.
