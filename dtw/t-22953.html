SIC is standing for "Statistical Inventory Control". It is an order method in which planned purchase/production orders are generated based (mainly) on the reorder point and the economic order quantity.

The reorder point is a calculated value which is calculated by the item as a whole not by warehouse.
SIC will generate an order when your economic stock at Item level (form 2 maintain Item data tiitm0101m000) falls below the reorder point.
Therefore I believe, it will not handle it by warehouse, however someone else could know how to set it up.
