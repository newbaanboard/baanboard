Hi,

Sales and purchase schedules are no longer available in BaaN V. There is no "supply chain" module like in BaaN IV to activate schedules.
As an alternative, we use purchase contracts. When we enter a purchase order, it is linked to a contract and the called quantity is updated. Whenever goods are received, the invoiced contract quantity is updated. So, we "call off" against the contract.
