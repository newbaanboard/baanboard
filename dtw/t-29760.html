As suggested by Derrick, ensure inbound activities are not automatic.

As soon as outbound is done of Transfer Order(manual) inventory will ship from Source Warehouse and would Appeare in Destination Warehouse as On Order and In process inventory (Check whinp tables).

On making receipt.. this will come as On hand Inventory in Destination W/H. A customized repor can be developed on this basis.
