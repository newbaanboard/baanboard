Dear Abuibra,

2. It is possible to change the sales price after goods has been shipped so long as there's no invoice generated yet for that shipment. However, once an invoice has been cut, its financial amount would've been passed to Finance. Hence you can't just change the invoice price. What you can do is cancel the old invoice with a sales return and make a new one (with the new price) by reissuing the goods. It's a bit tedious, but it's clean
