Hola Alex,

You can acheive this by simply posting a sales order to the destination warehouse / company, such that the cost of inventory is transferred by issue the sales order with 0 dollars. Thus, there is not any revenue impact at all.

Another combination could also to use the direct delivery feature and trigger a purchase order from the sales order, and achieve transfer of costs.
