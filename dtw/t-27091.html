Hi Mark,

I suppose you have the warehousing parameter "Force Cycle Count if Inventory Zero" (session whinh0100s000) selected. When your stock point becomes empty, a cycle count record will be logged in session "Cycle-Count Data" (whinh5540m000).

When creating a cycle count order, the current stock point data is printed and the cycle-count data.
