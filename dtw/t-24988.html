I had fun a couple of years ago getting this to work when the customer wanted just service parts priced in euro and everything else in GBP.

I found the currency defaults from the supplier details so you have to create a new supplier delivery address and set that to euro. you can get to the sub session from form1 or form2 of maintain customers under the special tab. when you create the sales contract you select the customer and the delivery address if this is the one with euro as currency then it will default to euro's. you also have the option to have a fixed or variable exchange rate.
