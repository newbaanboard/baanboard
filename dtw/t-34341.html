Since you have a nettable and a non-netaalbe warehouse, I would suggest to use DRP (distribution requirements planning). You could compare this with MRP, but instead of making planned production/purchase orders, DRP generates planned replenishment orders from the nettable to the non-nettable warehouse as soon as the inventory drops below a certain level.

Maybe you could ask your account manager or look at the Infor site for documentation about DRP.
