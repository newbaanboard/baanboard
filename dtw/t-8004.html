- Receive the items at 0 purchase price. This will change the ave purch price though.
- Delete the lines or Order and do an Inv Adjustment.

Hi

Deleting purchase order lines is an option but what this means is that
a. Your inventory status will not be correct and
b. Finance has manually approved the invoice. Presumably expensed the amount. So your P&L is also incorrectly stated to the extent you have items in stock.

A better way (as suggested by Flip) is to do a cycle count of these items and the credit of this should be taken to the expenses account which was affected when Finance did the vendor invoice approval
