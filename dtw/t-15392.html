Do you own the program scripts? If yes, then you could retrieve the required date in cisli2200m000 and fill the date field on the form with it. Depending on your need, you can choose to fill it fixed, and disable it, or to fill it default, still allowing the user to overwrite it.

If you do not have access to the script, you could still try to use the form field initial value and expression on cisli2200m000 for the date field(s) by filling it there with the shipment confirmation date. I have not tested the latter; this will only be possible if the shipment data is selected by the program already.


Good luck!
