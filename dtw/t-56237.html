try this.

1.Create Purchase order header in session tdpur4101m000 with currency USD in form 2 & save it, (lets say PO - B) no need to maintain lines.
2.Open the PO - (that needs to be copied, PO-A) which has EUR currency
3.Click on Copy button - enter the source PO as A , target as B .
4.click on continue.
