Don't know about any session to do this, but it can be done the quick and dirty way, by editing all records in tdsls040 thru tdsls046 for this order via ttaad4100. Change the currency, enter the correct price & amount (both "foreign" and "home"), fix the conversion factor and rates.

I agree with Robert.
One has to be so careful with ttaad4100. Especially when all the integration transactions with Finance are involved.
I would recommend to run your invoice in USD, do a credit invoice in USD against this sales order as if you are doing a return shipment and then process the sales order with the correct currency and invoice it.

Unfortunately this is the safest way to deal with BAAN's "over-the-fence" way of programming.
