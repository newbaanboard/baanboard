Well, I'm not familiar to approvals but we have a similar problem with wrong entered quantities:
once the order step "Maintain Receipts" is done and the orderline is processed (tdpur4223m000) the delivered quantity can not be modified any more.
We enter a negative orderline for the same PUR-order and process is normaly. The effect is: proper inventory adjustment and no problems when matching the invoice :cool:
Dont forget to ask you finance poeple if they or ok with this procedure ;)

Hope this helps,
