U can change lot number by using Production Issue/Production Receipt.

As in ur case:
"Lets say u have item A, Lot 123, Qty 100.

item A, Lot 123, Qty 70

Perform Production Issue of "item A, Lot 123, Qty 30" and again take the Prodution Receipt to "item A, Lot 456, Qty 30".

this will work fine in ur case.

Dear all,

With this kinds of error, nothing is truly easy. Any method you use, including production issue-receipt combo (either through Warehousing or Production module), will always result in imbalance in Finance module if your inventory valuation method for the goods in question isn't Fixed Transfer Price. But I guess the Finance guys have to live with that. All the company can do is minimise such errors in the future so that such imbalance will be immaterial in Finance
