If you want to create a seperate warehouse for your Physical Location then you have to Define a warehouse in the Session " Maintain Warehouses" or If you want to create a new Location with in the Existing Warehouse (assumed the your Company implemented the LOcation Control Module) then you simply define a new Location in the Session " Maintain Locations".
