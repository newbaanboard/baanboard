You will need to add table tiitm001 to be audited.
Then you can see who changed the default sales price and purchase prices for that item.
However, you may need to add tdsls032 tdsls030, tdpur030, tdpur032 if the prices are also held in Price Lists (although if they have done it properly thay will have expired the previous price so you can see when it was changed at least directly from the table values) .
