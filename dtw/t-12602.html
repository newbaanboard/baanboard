What are your financial requirements? Should these charges be visible anywhere (special ledger account)?
What you can possibly do is create a routine that can be called automatically in session tdsls4105s000 that blockes the entry of the selling price and instead computes the price less the charge based on the quantity returned, the item master selling price and your formula.
There are two flaws to this method - firstly the item may have been sold at a manually entered price originally in which case unless you know the sales order number you won't be able to determine the original selling price. The other flaw is that this 'charge' won't have it's own cost price component and will therefore integrate into finance together with the normal sales transactions rather than somewhere of your choice.
