Hi all,

In the contracts contained in PSC (Purchase supply chain module) you can establish different percentage (pe 60-40) for each supplier. This works fine (it doesn´t have to be the same percentage).

In the case this module is implemented the MRP does not create planned MRP purchase orders, but planned MRP purchase schedules. Each schedule is created with the quantity and the frequency, as well as the reception calendar established in the SCH contract.

The allocation is made just as if you were working with "normal" planned purchase orders, you can see it in either timrp010 or tdinv700, both tables are updated.

hope it helps...
