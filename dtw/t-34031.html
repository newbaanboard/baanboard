Dk,
Go to session Maintain Table Field Authorizations {ttaad4133m000} and then insert the username for whom you want to restrict the price field and choose the company number or all the companies depending on your setup. Next enter the field name "tdpur041.pric" in the space alloted for it and against it select authorization as "Read". Save the entry & click on Special menu and run "Convert To Runtime Data Dictionary" to commit the changes.

The user will not be able to change the price thereafter in session Maintain Purchase Order Lines {tdpur4102s000}..
