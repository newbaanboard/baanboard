Significance of Inventory Trx:
- The Sales Order / Purchase Order are posted to Finance with Manual Sales Order / Manual Purchase Order as TRX Type.
- These TRX's are considered only from inventory perspective and no impact on ACR / ACP / Sales and Purchase statistics.

Posting of these TRX's depends on what version u r using. In ERP it is while closing the TRX.
