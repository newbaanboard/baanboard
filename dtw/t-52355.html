Also, check the delivery date of sales order and also check if the item is also on cycle count. I saw that you maintain the boutbound and not generated it. If you are able to maintain the outbound then it cannot be customer blocking issue, or lot or location blocking issue. So it could be either of following and you have checked some of them
1. Order blocked at the release outbound step
2. Item on cycle count
3. Application lock
4. Delivery date on sales order not in the range that you may be giving in session
