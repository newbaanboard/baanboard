Probably the problem is that EP generates planned purchase orders. That is meant for actual purchase orders. When the planned purchase order is transfered to order management, purchase decides that is should be a purchase schedule. And then it will not work.

In the EP - purchase schedules integration, planned purchase orders are not used. EP has a direct integration with purchase schedules. During the planning process, the purchase schedule lines are removed, updated and inserted automatically.
