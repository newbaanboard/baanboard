Hi,

The warehouse already set with location control.

So you have to maintain inbound & release inbound before the item can be received into the bulk location, else it will stay at the receiving location.

The system will follow as below:

Maintain receive - goes to receiving locaiton
Maintain inbound (with inspection on) - goes to inspection location
Maintain inspection - goes into bulk location

Hope this will help.

Good luck
