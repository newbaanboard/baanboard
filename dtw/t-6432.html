When you meant negative inventory, I assume that the negative inventory is reflected uniformly in all the inventory tables, namely tiitm001 (item) or tdinv001 (item/wrh) or tdilc101 (item/wrh/loc).

In that case, using either the inventory adjustment session or the cycle counting order session to increase the inventory to zero, would be a good solution. This ensures that all the related tables are also updated properly.
