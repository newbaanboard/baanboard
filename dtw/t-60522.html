For Tabular Reports, you can use B2Win (http://www.nazdaq-it.com/Baan-Infor-ERP-LN-Software/B2Win/):
http://www.nazdaq-it.com/Baan-Infor-ERP-LN-Software/B2Win/

For creating new tabular reports, you can use Baan Query Tool (http://www.nazdaq-it.com/Baan-Infor-ERP-LN-Software/Baan-Query-Tool/):
http://www.nazdaq-it.com/Baan-Infor-ERP-LN-Software/Baan-Query-Tool/

For adding graphics to your report such as logo, Background/watermark you can look at the NAZDAQ solution (http://www.nazdaq-it.com/solutions/Convert-Reports-to-Office-Format/Convert-Reports-to-PDF-Format/):
http://www.nazdaq-it.com/solutions/Convert-Reports-to-Office-Format/Convert-Reports-to-PDF-Format/
