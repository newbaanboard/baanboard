Baan IV
You can set the number of discount levels to 3 (max.) in: Maintain Sales Order Parameters (tdsls4100m000) and in Maintain Purchase Order Parameters (tdpur4100m000).

Baan V, SSA ERP LN 6.1
In newer versions the session is: Pricing Parameters (tdpcg0100m000). Maximum = 5 discount levels.
