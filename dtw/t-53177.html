Another work-around would be to go to GTM and open table tcmcs003 (Warehouses) and then change the value in the field [Warehouse Type] from "Normal" to "Transit". There onwards whenever you use this warehouse, the system would block you from comitting the transaction with a message "Transit Warehouses Not Allowed".
