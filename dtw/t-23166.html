INBOUND / OUTBOUND LEAD TIMES are set in Item Data by Warehouse - whwmd2510m000.
So you would not see them in the Item Supplier Data nor the Item Purchase Data. For example, if the Inbound Lead time is set @ 2 days, the finish date of the planned purchase order is set 2 days ahead of required date.
