Hi,

please to a search for proforma in your Infor help.

Some remarks which I found in the help:

For sales invoicing procedure
In ERP LN 6.1, you can no longer print proforma invoices. This functionality is replaced by installments in Sales Control. For more information, refer to Installments.

For Cash Management module
Proforma invoices Changed in SSA ERP LN 6.1. The Proforma Invoices business object was removed from the Cash Management module.
In ERP LN 6.1, this functionality was replaced:
In Order Management, by installments of the type Advance. For more information, refer to Installments.
In Project, by advance payment requests. For more information, refer to To use advance payment requests.
Installments and advance payment requests are processed in Central Invoicing in the same way as other types of invoices. For more information, refer to Central Invoicing
