Hello,

we had a similar problem in one of our installations.
If your service pack level is lower than SP 8 please try
to change the parameter "Time-Phased Inventory Check"
in session tdsls4100m000 from yes to no or the other way
round and check if the problem is still there.

In our case this solved the problem.
