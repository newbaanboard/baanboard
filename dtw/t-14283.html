When you can run generate inbound is based on several factors:

1. Location control must be turned on

2. If item does not have to be inspected  then generate inbound can be run after Maintain Receipts (Item will be in receipt location)

3. If the item must be inspected  then generate inbound has to wait until Maintain Approvals has been completed (Item will be in inspection location)

If ILC is Implemented , then u have to define Receipt Location and Inspection Locations in Maintain warehouse ( tcmcs003)

In case Item has to be inspected , as soon as u receive material , it will move to INSPECTIOn location else It will be in RECEIPT LOCATION.

Now after approval ( if inspection = Yes) u have to generate Inbound data , and release it.Releasing Inbound transfer material from Inspection Location to Normal location

While if Inspection is NO , u can generate Inbound , after Receipt of material .
