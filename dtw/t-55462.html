How do you currently manage the warehouse orders based upon 1 department?
Department of the employee has no direct link to a warehouse.

Secondly an employee needs to be linked to 1 department only. The department of the user is mainly used for booking costs. For example, hours spent are posted against the department of the employee. So linking the employee to 2 departments does not make sense.
