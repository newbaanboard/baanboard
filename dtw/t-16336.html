smanohar
16th November 2004, 05:43
You cannot do it with the standard Baan functionality in B4.0c. Only in LN (and I think in B5.2a) also they introduced the possibility of entering quantity and the price during cycle counting / adjustment order. But, in your version (B4.0c4), there is no such valuation.

The system will always increase the inventory at the latest standard cost price.

PS: If you change your standard cost price before cycle counting, then the system will do revaluation for all the existing inventory, which you don't want.
