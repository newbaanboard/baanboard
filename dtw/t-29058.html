hey Dhnish,
Before you go ahead with Archiving, just consider installing a patch that increases the number of digits from 6 to 8(E.g: 100000 to 10000000). Its there for production order and i dont see why it wont be possible for sales order series as both the data types are the same. If you check some of the threads of November in Manufacturing, you could get information about this patch.
If going ahead with archiving and you have a reporting tool outside baan, you would have to make certain modifications in the report to include data from this archive company. As for Baan default reports, i am not sure whether there is a provision to include data from an archived company as i have not yet done archiving..

Hope this helps...
