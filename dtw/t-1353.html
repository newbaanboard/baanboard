There is a long list of checks done in function tcmcs0036 to determine this. Essentially, it looks at various combinations of country, supplier, item group, project, budget, item, and postal code in the tcmcs146 (Exceptions for Taxes - Purchase) table. If there are no hits there, then it looks in tcmcs139 (Exceptions by Tax Location), followed by tcmcs136 (Tax Codes by Postal Code).

I hope that helps.
