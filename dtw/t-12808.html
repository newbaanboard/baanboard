Martin Jung
3rd September 2003, 15:42
Well, I guess you finally want to get a proper invoice according to the actual deliveries..
Normally you are able to change the delivired quantity in Maintain Deliveries until the invoice is printed. So simply change the quantity and print the packing slip again (if you want). Setting the delivered quantity to Zero sets the orderline status back to Maintain Deliveries and you can start from the very beginning of the procedure, including printing a new packing slip.
There is no specific procedure to 'cancel' a packing slip. It's done like mentioned above.

Hope this helps.

Martin
