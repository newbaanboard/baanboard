In short:
Update - Actual Cust Data Yes/No
Yes = Sales order table used
No = Sales History table used
Full update - Yes/No
Yes = Stats deleted and regenerated
No = Stats added

Stats tables are updated after Sort codes are activated.

btromp
6th September 2004, 12:45
The sales statistics are updated based on the history tables tdsls050 and tdsls051. Depending on the status in these tables processed or not, the data of the history is transfered to some basetables in the sst tables. Orderintake is bases on sequensenumber 1 in the history tables (orderintake), turnover statistics is bases on sequensenumber 3 in the history (turnover, which is filled after processing the order).
For Baan5c, the statisticmodule is working perfectly.
