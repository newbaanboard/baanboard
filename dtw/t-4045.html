Hi Bryan,

there is a functionality in Baan IV that allows you to use a calendar for delivery dates in salesorders. You have to run Transportation in combination with Distribution. Then you are able to maintain a calendar for every standard route. These standard routes are linked via different settings in Transportation to the order procedure in SLS or PUR. When entering a delivery date that does not match a date in the calendar of the standard route a warning is diplayed.
Maybe a big hammer for a small problem ;) if you don't have any other benefits from using Transportation.
