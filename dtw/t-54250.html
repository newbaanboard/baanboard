To Issue by Warehouse Field:
Displays the total activated issued quantity.

Note
This field is modified by BaanERP Manufacturing and BaanERP Warehousing. When Manufacturing activates a warehouse order, the To Issue field is added to the To Issue by Warehousing field.

When BaanERP Warehousing issues a quantity, the quantity is subtracted from the To Issue by Warehousing field.

TO Issue Field:
The allocations Quantity in the warehouse.

Subsequent Delivery Field:
The quantity that must still be delivered as a back order. It has not yet been issued from inventory. This will only be done if the quantity (possibly with backflushing) has been entered in the To Issue field and the related warehouse order line is activated.

Note
If the operation is blocked for issue, this field is disabled.
