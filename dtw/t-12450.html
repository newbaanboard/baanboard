Hi,
Here is a business recommendation, you do not want to interfere with tables, as this includes both finance and logistics and (a lot of them). I know what has happened in your company, process control is lacking: Goods are tranfsered from one location to another using RPL, when the goods are received nobody controls the process by maintaining the receipt, adjuestments are made when cycle counts are done since on hand goods are higher. Hence you have the situation here. Right Ya!

I suggest:
1. Go ahead and do the RPL receipt
2. This will clean out the in transit situation and goods in transit acount.
3. Agree with finance that you are going to then to an adjustment for these receipts with a reason code. The reason code will help keep a record for audit purpose.
4. Ensure process control is in place. Kick But!

Hope this helps.
