You can have Departments attached to BP Role that is linked to a BP.

You can specify the business partner's financial data per department, for
example, if a logistic company has sites (enterprise units) in different
countries, that do business with the same business partner. In the
different countries you can then use a different default currency and
different invoicing methods for the business partner.

You can define financial business partner data per department for the
following business partner roles:
- Invoice-to
- Pay-by
- Invoice-from
- Pay-to

It gets interesting as soon as some of your suppliers are also your customers (and vice versa). In Baan IV a lot of programming effort had to be done to consolidate open entry list for those customers/suppliers. Also, most of the functionality you need for customers you need also for suppliers, but there were different sessions, tables etc. I think this is one of the major reasons to merge it in Baan V.
