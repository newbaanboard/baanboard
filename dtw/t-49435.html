Hi,

The data you changed won't be visible in statistics unless you run a full update in tdsst0201m000. Be aware of the fact that this could take a full day, depending on the data volume. By the way, if your objective was only to have the new region reflected in the statistics, the history update would not have been necessary. You could have also run the full statistics update with the the box 'actual customer data' marked since in that case the customer master data will not be taken from the history but from the customer file.

Hope this helps.

Frank
