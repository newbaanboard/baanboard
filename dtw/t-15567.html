BaanERP provides handling of subcontracting through its Manufacturing module. If you're in a trading company, making use of this feature may not be that straight forward as you have to learn the BOM, routing, cost price calculation, and shop floor control concepts

In Distribution modules alone, Baan doesn't provide much automation for subcontracting, as mentioned by Martin above
