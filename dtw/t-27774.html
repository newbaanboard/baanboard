A possible solution may be to utilize the concept of Clusters in Enterprise Planning. (There are also Clusters in Service in LN.) Cluster functionality is available in Baan V and SSA ERP LN.

Have you explored this option? It might be overkill, and there might be a simplier way, but it would automatically create Warehouse Orders to fulfill the WIP Raw Materials demand from the supplying warehouses as a result of the demand created from the production order.

Also, in SSA ERP LN, Assembly Line Planning and Control functionality exists. This functionality addresses this issue as well but in a leaner way.
