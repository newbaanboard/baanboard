How the standard Baan IV software works
When you have a single financial company setup, the financial company is defaulted automatically. But in case you have multiple financial companies, no financial company can be defaulted and the session falls back to the current company.

Conclusion
Probably you work with multiple financial companies.
In that situation the current company is defaulted.
There is no way to change the defaulting mechanism, unless you customize the session.
