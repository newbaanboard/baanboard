If u r talking about the customer account statement in finance, u can
eliminate the invoice in finance by deleting the invoice in
tfgld1101m000 -->tfacr1110s000 . In order that u can modify the batch
in finance tfgld1101m000, u need to change the flag 'batch modifiable'
to yes in tfgld100 for the relevant batch.

If the sales invoice document is deleted,it will not appear in customer
statement account.
