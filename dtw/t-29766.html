Normally tdilc101 is cleared when none of the given qty's <> 0 (allocated, stock, on order, etc), so most probably a bit fell in the wrong basket due to software errors (don't know on which SP your system is, but it's wellknown that in the past ILC was not as stable as should be).

When it's just a single record which is corrupt, I should clear it out via GTM, otherwise use one of the ILC correction sessions.
