In Baan V, DRP has been combined with Enterprise Planning and there you can generate 'Planned Distribution orders' along with planned production and purchase orders. You have to define a cluster, cluster item etc. and the concept is new and different from Baan IV. Warehouse transfer orders have replaced Replenishment orders. I think if you search the board there will be a number of threads with details.
