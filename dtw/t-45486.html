Dear Natasha,

only purchase or manufacturing items can be in inventory thus only for these items inventory adjustments can be performed.

A generic item is not a real item. It replaces a product variant which still needs to be configured. The result of the configuration leeds to product variant. The product variant can be a manufacturing or purchase item.

Similary there is no inventory adjustment possible for cost items, subcontracting items, or service items. It is possible to maintain receipts for cost items or subcontracting items but after that they are not given to inventory. The other way round it is possible to maintain deliveries for cost items without taking anything from inventory.
