Location Control is set on a company level.
This does not mean that you are forced to create locations in the warehouses. You can have warehouses without any locations if location control is switched on.
In a warehouse that does not have locations in this setup you will have to deliver via outbound data but don't need to inbound data becaues you do not have locations.
Warehouses with locations you will have to deliver via outbound data and store via inbound data.

Flip is Right.

Within Location control you have 4 different situations in the scripts:

1) Warehouses With Locations
2) Warehouses Without Locations

and

3) Items that need zooming to ILC (date / lot / storage unit / loca filled)
4) Items that do not need extra information registered (no lot item, no storage date, storage unit equals stock unit, warehouse without location).

When you use items that are cat 4 and warehouses that are cat 2 then you can mainly use the inv sessions to do your thing.

(From the functional point of view, you can make a matrix: 1 and 2 on top, 3 and 4 on the left. In the matrix the result of al sessions used must be comparable.)
