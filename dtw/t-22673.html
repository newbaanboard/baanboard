If you have a transfer order for instance from 'RECEIPT' to 00001, yet the order has not yet been fully processed -> outbound/inbound, then the allocated stock in the outbound location will be filled, and th expected stock in the inbound warehouse too. When then the order is processed fully, the stocks will be cleared from expected/allocated, and assigned from/to the warehouses.

Dear Bartth,

The correct procedure would be
Receive the goods into RECEIPT through whichever order it was coming from
Create a warehousing order of type manual transfer to move the goods from RECEIPT to 00001
Send the goods out of RECEIPT (outbound procedure)
Receive the goods in 00001 (inbound procedure)
Did the worker follow this procedure?
