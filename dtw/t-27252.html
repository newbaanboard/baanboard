I am only responding to the first question - to be able to see inveentory at piece part / sell in 3PK or 6PK.
To do this you leave the inventory unit on the base item at EA (or whatever you are using) and create two new units of measure (Maybe 3PK and 6PK). You put whichever of these is required as the Sales Unit of Measure (in the Sales sub-entity - General Item Data. Set up the conversion factor (tcibd0103m000) as required to show that for the desired parts that a 3PK = 3 - EA or that a 6PK = 6 -EA.
