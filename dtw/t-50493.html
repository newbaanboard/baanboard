Hi,

The order type steps are defined the session - tcmcs0142m000 (assuming you are using baan IV C4).

Just following the steps defined in it for the order type until PDPO.

Eg: In our Scenario we have defined the following steps for Order type.

1.Print PO
2.maintain Receipts
8.Print Purchase invoice
9.PDPO

We just do it upto step 8 as said above.

and then make reverse by making PRN.
