Well,
on the financial integration side of things you can actually cause integrations at the item level. You can try it out in a dev. system to see what you get.
Using session tdsls6102m000 Maintain Invoice Details Accounts. Leave the first fields for Customer group, warehouse and item group blank, and enter the specific item you want to set up financial integrations for. (There are a number of different combinations for the 'header' field combinations but, this is the most straight forward for the information you have listed. )
Then, you can break down the invoice details into material costs, operations costs, and turnover. each break down can have different ledger accounts specified.
Hope it helps,
