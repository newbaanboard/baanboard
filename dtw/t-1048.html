Hello,

Some months ago we also had a problem with cost items. We found out that Baan has changed the functionality without documenting this (also internally it is not documented).

Please check session tdsls4128m000 'Maintain Items by Cost Set'.
If the Units for prices and Limits are the same dry to change one of them. If they are the same no cost item will be added to the sales order.
