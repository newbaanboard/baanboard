In the Supply Chain world, Purchase Schedules are for forecast and Shipping schedules are for shipments. The best way of converting purchase to sales schedules is to use Supply chain module in Baan. You can use EDI to load the schedules not touching the data or you can create Sales schedules manually. Before creating Sales schedules, the sales Contract has to be maintained for that customer, Part number and Ship To Code.

Based on the Baan IVc2 sch0 version
There are 2 ways of loading the DELFOR and DELJIT messages.
1.Loading Forecast/shipping quantites as sent by the customer in the schedules.
2.Calculating the Forecast/shipping quantities based on the cumulative quantities sent by the customer.

In the second case, you have set the parameters in the tdssc013 like use cumulatives to 'Yes' and then the corresponding reconcilliation and netting methods.
