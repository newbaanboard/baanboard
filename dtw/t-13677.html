Hello Donnie,

You can use these fields if the item your purchase has a quantity unit with dimensions. For example, your item has as unit m2 and this unit is defined of type "surface" (see unit definition). When you have done this and you enter a purchase order, a window will popup that enables you to enter the length, widt etc.. of the considered item.
