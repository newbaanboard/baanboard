Hi Joy,
the price calculation in Baan follows a certain hierarchy. Baan steps from the top level to the lowest. As soon as a valid price condition is found it is taken to the salesorderline. The levels are:

1. Contract
2. Customer/Item
3. Pricelist/Item
4. Price by Item
5. Item
6. Not defined

If your salesorderline shows "Item" it means that there is no valid price condition on the first 4 levels. Session tdsls0510m000 is a good tool to detect the errors in price calculation.

Hope this helps.

Martin
