A problem when posting a response close to midnight is that too often the key elements become clear just after I go to bed and try to fall asleep.

I agree with Baanana that the problem is probably not be related to phantoms per se.

In the thread I mentioned previously, Sturla pointed out that it is possible to link items in the BOM to specific operations. Logically, that is the way to force an operation to relate to a qty other than the main item/order qty. And it should apply to all items, including phantoms. But I suspect that unless you issue the phantom instead of its components, the subcontract operation may still be planned for the SFC main item qty.
