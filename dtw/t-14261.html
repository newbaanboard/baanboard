The fovn field is for a Tax ID, normally for federal or state / provincial numbers, for sales, or purchase related reporting. The field is only a text field, so it will not affect any sessions.

For delivery addresses, telephone numbers, you could also use fields such as the Accounting reference, or Sales Reference, You oculd also use text fields for additional info.
