BEMIS stands for: Baan Electronic Message Interchange System.

SSA defined an internal standard which is used for SSA's EDI Business Documents (Inhouse Standard). The EDI Translator Software converts SSAs internal standard to an external standard like UN/EDIFACT or ODETTE.
