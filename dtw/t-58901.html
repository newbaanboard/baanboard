Hi Stevenlam,
Stock Point Inventory (whinr1540m000)displayes only the data of available inventory, and the allocation field gives the details of Demand for that inventory item only. Where as Warehouse - Item Inventory (whwmd2515m000) gives the aggregation of all Inventory for the item and the total Demand for the item. From your screen shot it is understood that the allocation of 775 qty is not made against this Inventory Qty of 38. You can check for which location this allocation is made for from the session Planned Inventory Transaction (whinp1500m000)and it can clearly show that these demands are not made against the availble inventory Qty 38.
