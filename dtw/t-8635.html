If you set the warehouse as non-nettable, then MRP won't see it and will plan accordingly. As long as no items in the item master have that warehouse as their default, then the sales orders will not have that warehouse.

The only thing you would have to do is train you people not to look at just the total inventory level on the item master but instead the inventory by warehouse.
