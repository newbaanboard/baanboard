There is no possibility to switch off ILC again one it has been implemented. The only way to do this is by writing scripts which will fill fthe INV module tables from the ILC tables. An analysis on your specific sitiation and your way of using ILC has to be done beforehand. This is a larger project in any way.

Why do you want to switch it off again? Perhaps you can just switch off using ILC for the warehouses you are using? Then there will be no inbound procedure at least. (You still will have to do the outbounding, however - but with using jobs you can turn down the manual effort here too.)
