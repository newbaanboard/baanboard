Dear Jay,

Say for example Brickwork is task - T101
and material required in Block 4",Block 5" , Cement and Sand River. Assume any qty.
In typical construction enviornment, all material is required Task wise like Excavation, Plinth Beam , Slab , Brick work, Wood work etc.

So if u use the BOM by operation,

Then Operation-10 --- Task T101 ( Brick work) ----- Material 1 Block 4" ( Operation 10)
2 Bleck 5" ( ---"---)
3 Cement (---"---)
4 Sand River (---"---)
This all material will get issues either by backflush or manually depend upon setting.
So in this case one can issue the material part by part till the full operation gets completed.
We have successfully implemented this BOM by operation technoque in our construction division last year and working fine.

I am sure it will help you.
