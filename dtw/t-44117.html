Strictly from an APICS perspective, I don't like the way that you are managing packaging. Specifically, if I understand your post correctly, you are linking a packaging item to another item, by means of an informational field.

You really need to undertsand how best to plan for the packaing item and then make a decision as to whether you control it with a BOM concept or not.

I would not want to be a planner responsible for packaging inventory if I had no visability to demand on the system. Visual systems are fine and definately reduce order cost but they are prone to human error.
