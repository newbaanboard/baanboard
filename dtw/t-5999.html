You are not allowed to change the delivery country of an order after you entered positions. The reason is that Baan stores the delivery country of the order also in the positions. You cannot see that data field in "Maintain order positions", but it's there. That field has to match with the order country all the time. You are not allowed to have a single order to be delivered into two different countries, because of the way Baan handles the VAT during invoicing.

The only way of changing the country (either by delivery adress or by country field) is by deleting the positions, changing the country in the order and re-entering the positions. However, this is only possible as long as there is no delivery made for the order.
