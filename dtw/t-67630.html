This session is not for reversing a confirmed Shipment...
I tried on system to reverse a Shipment as my User had entered a wrong quantity and wanted to change that.
Session "Remove Confirmed Shipments/Loads (whinh4250m000) " only deletes the Shipment Data and doesn't show any changes in Logistics/ finance.

To confirm this I logged a incident on Infor Support. See the reply that I got from inforextreme.com---
The removal of shipments is just a process prior to archiving to reduce the number of records in the tables "Shipments" (whinh430) and "Shipment Lines" (whinh431) without any logistic or financial transaction behind.
