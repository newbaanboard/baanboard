We have developped our own customised report for this purpose. In the item master our outbound priority is by location and not fifo. Due to this in the ilc tables, the inventory date is not stored.

So the report has two logics : for lot controlled items, we pick up the age based on the lot dates and for others based on the last receipt date and going backward until we reach the on hand quantity.

This works fairly well and is ok with the logistics people also.
