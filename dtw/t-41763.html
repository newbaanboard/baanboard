in Item-warehousing data for each item, try the switch "serials in inventory" to "no". --> study this functionality.

advantage: you will be able to select the serial number which you want to outbound. does not follow FIFO.
disadvantage: you will not be able to view, which serials are available in the inventory.. in that case, you will base on actual serials during outbound.
