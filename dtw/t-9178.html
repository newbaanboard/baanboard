Dear Jon,

What you'd need to configure is your invoicing method. You can find this under BAAN Common Data\Tables\Financial tables. You'd need to make one invoicing method with Collect Shipments=Yes and Collect Ship To Address=Yes, and then later connect this invoicing method you just made with this customer that is giving you the headache :)

Essentially by doing so, you enable your company to bill goods sent to this customer by multiple shipments and multiple addresses using just one invoice. Voila!
