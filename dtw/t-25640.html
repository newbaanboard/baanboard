Please read the below details for your solution:

Purchase Retrun Order Made for Item X with -1.00 qty from warehouse WRH which has location REC (receipt location) and INSP (inspection location)

so to have the purchase order fully processed you need to have the inventory in receipt location i.e. here REC

In warehouse it may be showing that the inventory is 100 but if the inventory in location REC is 0.00 then no receipts would be possible for Purchase Return.

Firstly transfer the inventory to REC and then start processing.

Hope this resolves your problem.

In a location controlled warehouse it is necessary that for purchase return inventory should be available at the receipt location. If you are getting the message the inventory is not vaialble even at the time of inventory transfer it means that you have already consumed the inventory of that lot. In this case the only solution is to adjust the inventory from an existing lot and transferring it to the lot which you wish to return
