This is because you have maintained extra outbound of the given line(this is not released)

You have maintained one qty in the sales order line so order quantity is 1
You have released 1 qty from outbound so delivered is 1
Then you must have maintained outbound of 1 qty but not released
So Recomended qty is 1+1= 2

Delete that outbound & it should work
