Hi,

with SP 16 and higher in Baan IVc4 the delete option in sessions tdsls/pur4223m000 is removed and new sessions tdsls/pur4227m000 are available for deleting OR archiving Sales / purchase orders.

Before deleting the orders, be sure that it is allowed to delete.
In Germany it is not allowed to delete an order until 10 years old (because of "GDPdU").
Therefor SSA has created the new sessions.
The "old" session have become new options!
For the same reason the sessions tdsls/pur5201m000 are change, too.
For deleting or archiving sales/purchase order history new sessions are availble: tdsls/pur5202m000.
