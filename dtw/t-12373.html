Dear achavezf,

When PO is manually created, at first BaanERP will assume that the PO will use the currency defined as business partner's usual currency. When we deal with business partners which do transactions in several different currencies, I've seen 2 approaches:
Create separate business partner ID for each currency the business partner uses. This is not recommended as it'd be a bit harder to consolidate transactions per business partner
Use Purchase Order Input Control and, through it, only give access to absolutely necessary fields including currency. When Purchase Order Input Control is on, the clerk have to go through and check every single of those important fields before he/she can even enter a PO line (i.e. you can't save the PO and enter PO lines unless the clerk has "Tab" through every single important fields defined in PO Input Control). Using this has minimised PO input error in one of our clients

Rgds,
Paul
