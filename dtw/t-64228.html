Non Nettable Warehouse : are ignored by MPS/MRP algorithm and the resupply of such warehouse is done by DRP.
Nettable Warehouse: are ignored by DRP algorithm and the replensihment of inventory in these warehouses is done by MPS/MRP/SIC.
