Hi,

The allocation at the item and item/wrh level are soft allocation. It is created when you create demand for the item using sales order, RPL order, etc.

You can the tdcor0250m000 for the item and see whether there is any change in the allocated qty. If the session doesn't correct the allocated qty, then it means the mentioned allocation is correct.

You can check the planned inventory transaction for the item, where you can see where the demand comes from.

Having a negative Economic qty just indicates that there is demand which is more than on-hand and it is not a negative inventory.
