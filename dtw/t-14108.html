Hi Martin,
We do direct delivery sales orders and we are able to modify the PO price line. The only difference between our set up and what you have posted is that we do not associate a price list with the vendor (field is blank). We can then modify the PO price on the line (you will get a warning that Sales Lines are Present, just click OK). We have our system set this way because we don't use vendor price lists. Therefore I'm not sure how this set up would affect your ability to actually select a price list in the PO header (or even if you wish to).
