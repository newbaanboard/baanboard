Dear all,

It's OK, we found out what we want. We needed to know how B5c determines what can still be received and what aren't. It turns out that B5c will allow receipt if it could find WO inbound line with sequence number multiple of 100000 (0, 100000, 200000, and so on) which is not blocked and not marked as final receipt. Just in case anyone would find this info useful.

We don't know about the rest of WO inbound line sequence number assignment, but we're happy with this for the moment
