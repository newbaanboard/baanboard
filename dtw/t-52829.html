Hi Riaz,

You can change this from backend itself. For this change...
(1) go to the table tdpur406 (Form- 2) and change the status of final receipt and confirmed
(2) go to tdpur401 and change the status of final receipt (in Form-12) , status code & procedure mask (in Form-13).
(3) After that go to tdpur4106m000 - correct receipt and change the quantity.
(4) After doing all changes, Update the status of all table like tdpur401 & tdpur406
