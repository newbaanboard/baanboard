Although Baan does do a good job with pick list definitions, procedures for inventory movements and shipment/load consolidations, it doesn't address some of the 'glamour' functionality typically found in a good WMS such as the concept of interleaving.

Warehouse design recommendations, based on physical and demand product characteristics, is one of the next big things in WMS.

I looked into Baan/WMS combinations several years ago and was surprised to find that the choices were limited if not non-existent.
