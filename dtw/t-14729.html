I've meanwhile found the cause of this issue:

In Baan 5.0 the calculation of delivery date based on supplier-item info is apparently very primitive. In our calendar the days are defined as working days from 08:00 to 16:30, i.e. 8.5 hours per day. When setting a delivery date of 10 days, Baan will calculate 10 * 24 hours, with a base of 8.5 per day, thus resulting in 28.235 (working-) days!!

I find this very incorrect, because it is not relevant for me to know how long the working day at my company is when calculating the delivery date of my supplier...!! I logged a case at Baan Support, and the response was just that this is the functionality at Baan5.0, and that in Baan 5.2 (reger) it has been improved...

I was waiting for a suggestion from Support to upgrade to 5.2 but lucky enough that didn't come this time.... :rolleyes:
