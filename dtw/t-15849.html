We have a product called RMClabelmaker that exists completely within Baan. It allows you to create and print labels entirely within Baan without needing external applications like Loftware or Bartender. To print labels, you only need to make a library call within the appropriate session. Some of our clients have used this for:

Tagging subassemblies and finished goods, packing and labeling boxes of finished goods, and composing and labeling skids of boxes in production
Tracking serial numbers, creating shipping labels, and automatically "delivering" goods in sales
Creating receipt and cross-docking labels in receiving
Tracking cycle counting locations and items w/ labels


In addition to just printing the labels, we have -- as you probably guessed -- created or modified functionality for these customers to help Baan fit their business needs.
