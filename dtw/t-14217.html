Baan doesnt archive sales orders only the sales order history. tdsls4223m000 (Processed Delivered Sales Orders) is used to delete the sales order (tdsls040,041,045) records and just keep the Sales Order History.

You will need to create your own session if you want to archive the actual sales orders.
