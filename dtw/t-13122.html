Hi Tigroo,

If you really want to transfer the Item from LOC1 to LOC_EXIT, you can transfer the material from one location to another location through RPL Order but provided that there is a Transit warehouse, In your case, you have to receive the material through Maintain RPL Receipts session,then you can track the material in LOC_EXIT.

Pls check the Order Status in the system & complete all the steps.

Hope this will solve your problem.
