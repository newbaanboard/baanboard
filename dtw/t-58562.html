I would not propose to perform GTM on the final receipt flag. Once a transfer order is received final (for a partial quantity), a shipping variance is booked in the financial integrations.

Opening the transfer for new receipts will result in an imbalance on the interim transit account.

This is the reason why receipt correction is disabled for a transfer.
