Dear Sudipta,

if you modify your conversionfactor in Session tiitm0120m000 this changes will not updated in the tdsls301 table for the sales contract, i. e. your sales order are created with a possible wrong conversion factor.

Please check the following fields and update them with the value of tiitm004:

(1) tdsls301.cvps
(2) tdsls301.cvqs
