These two sessions (tdsls1103s000 and tdsls1104s000) where
called from the program script (with the zoom.to(...) function).
So they are not present in the menu and therefore you can`t change this entries.

If you want to disable one (or both) sessions you must modify the special options in Maintain Forms for Form tdsls1101m0001.
