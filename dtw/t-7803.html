Check the NT application eventlog it will give you more details on the error. This is the same type of problem I had when setting up archiving. for example your error could be that someone used GTM to remove a part from the item master table and when the transaction records for the parts are being moved the session is checking that the part exists in the item master but there could be many other reasons.
You will have to correct each error as you find it and you can expect to have lots of them.
