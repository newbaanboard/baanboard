Because lot control at the item level is by lot (as suggested by ramkrish),
the system will always go to the subsession. If it had been not
applicable , outbound priority were by location (as in ur case) and
inventory/purchase/storage unit were same , Baan allows to eliminate the
ilc receipt session depending on a parameter in ilc .

I understand ur requirement and it appears logical because lot selection is
specific. I think u may refer the same to SSA support .
