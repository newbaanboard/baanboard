Is there a way in ERP LN 6.1 to keep an accurate shipping schedule (ie delivery date is the day we are actually shipping out the door) and maintain static "promise" and "request" dates?

Our old ERP had three date fields, "request", "promise", and "required", where the required date is the day the product needs to ship in order to meet the promise/request date, or the revised day the order is scheduled to ship (if late).

Hello,

you have to add an additional field to the sales order and sales order line and define

planned delivery date = required
planned receipt date = promised
new field = requested

"required" should be calculated automatically upon promised. If freight management is not used, it could make sense to add an additional field "transport time in working days" to the ship-to role of the business partner and to calculate it.

For reporting you have to compare the following values
if promised <= requested --> available
if deliverd <= required --> reliable

For reliability it makes sense not to differ only between in-time and too late or just to calculate the avarage of too-late days but to create classes

broken promise days = delivered - required

BP <= 0 --> Class 0 (reliable)
BP = 1, 2 --> Class 1
BP = 3-5 --> Class 2
BP = 6-9 --> Class 3
.... or however
