Hi,

There are below mentioned activity which you have to perform for auto display.
(1) Maintain Manufacturer data in the session "tcmcs0160m000"
(2) Put Manufacturer in tcibd0101s000 which will be printed in PO defaulted (Item General Data/Grouping Data/Manufacturer)
(3) For other flexibility with Manufacturer, you can also maintain data in session " tdipu0130m000". In this session Manufacturer-Item can be maintained which print on PO.
(4) In session"tdipu0135m000" multiple business partner relation with Multiple Manufacturers can be maintained.
