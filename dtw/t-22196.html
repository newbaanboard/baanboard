Hi,

Are you sure you mean whinp1500m000? There's no 'on hold'-field in that session. If there is then the session has been customised and you have to check where that quantity is read from.

If you mean 'on hand' inventory, then you have corrupted inventory. The on hand in whinp1500m000 is read from tcibd100 and the on hand in whwmd2515m100 from whwmd215. These two tables should coincide. You could try to run whcor0200m000 in test mode in order to find out whether you do have corrupted inventory and base your actions on the result.

Good luck,
