Hello,
Within Maintain Order Steps (tcmcs0139m000),
You can set 'Re-execute' steps.
By including the remaining steps in your order process in the re-execute fields, you specify which steps this session can be re-executed after.

So on our packing slip & BOL we specified Print Sales Invoice & Process Delivered sales orders as the re-execute steps.

This allows you to reprint the documents after print sales invoice & process delivered sales order

This should solve your problem.
