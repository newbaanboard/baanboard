Hello,

If you have no stock on hand, then i suggest you can change from Purchase to Cost, or vice versa but only via GTM. If you do make a change you want to also revalue the item via cost rollup. You have choices for cost calculation either simulated purchase price, or purchase price based valuation.

If you want to have these BOMs into you could also use an item coded as a Phantom, which makes it like a Cost item even though it is a purchased item
