In Baan IVC4 the calculation is as follows:
If there is a full 12 month history will take total for annual. If not will take first month of issues and all succeeding months. Will then divide by the number of months of history available and multiply by 12 or 13 depending on the parameter setting. If there is less then 3 months usage and the Expected Annual Usage field in the item master is > 0 than it takes this figure as the annual usage.

I assume it tis the same in LN.
