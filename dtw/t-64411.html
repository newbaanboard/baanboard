Structure of EDI messages in Baan IV and ERP LN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Attachments (0) | Linked KBs

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Description:
The structure of the EDI files is different between BaanIV and ERP LN:
In Baan IV the files looks like this:
Header
Line 1
Details for line 1
Line 2
Details for line 2
.
.
And in ERPLN:
Header
Line 1
Line 2
Detail for line 1
Detail for line 2
.


Steps To Reproduce:

The data structure is indeed different between BaanIV and ERP LN.
The BEMIS definition of the EDI messages in ERP LN has been designed in such a way that the structure is :
SA1
SA2
SA3
SA3
SA5
SA5
SA6
SA6

etcetera.
This is by design.
It would require a redefinition of the EDI conversion setups to change this.
The best way forward is to modify the external EDI software package to read the file according to the new structure.
