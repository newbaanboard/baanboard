I think I figured out. Just to confirm with you guys.

1. Confirm backorder, set confirmed field to "Yes"
2. Go to Maintain Replenishment Order Deliveries
2.1 Select the delivery record for the order.
2.2 go to "Form 2"
2.3 delete the record. Backorder gone!
