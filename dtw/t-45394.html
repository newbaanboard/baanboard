Hi,
You have this information in:
-Warehouse -item inventory(current view)-whwmd2515m000 by selecting a line and double click you start Warehouse-Item Inventory-whwmd2115s000 and on Dates/Prices tab you'll find MAUC (by warehouse) at this time
-Inventory receipt transactions -whina1512m000
-MAUC by item and warehouse valuation group - whina1534m000 MAUC by warehouse val.group at this moment.
-MAUC transactions by item and warehouse valuation group - whina1536m000
If you choose for an item, MAUC and Valuation by warehouse valuation group as inventory valuation, the system will calculate MAUC for group of warehouses and also, MAUC for every warehouse in that group.
Hope it'll be useful.

In BAAN IV c3 (A&D) there is a session called "Display Accountability Transactions (Part Cost History) [ticpr9551m00b]" that shows the MAUC after each transaction. The information is stored in a table called ticpr951.

Hopefully, you can locate a similar session/table in your version of BAAN.
