Unfortunately, I don't think SIC will work with seasonality. Re-order point logic, regardless of Baan, assumes a flat, predictable demand pattern.

Work around...

Try defining order quantifiers per warehouse for a certain period of demand and order parameters for the company for another period of demand. Generate recommendations based on the appropriate order quantifiers...warehouse or company. This wouldn't give you true seasonality based on a gradual, sloping curve but would give you a stepped curve to plan against.
