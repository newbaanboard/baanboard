U may consider following options.

1. U may sell an item as service or cost item for which no physical stock transactions are kept.

2. U may enter the sales invoice in finance itself. U may enter 'Sales order' type warehouse order in tdinv1101m000 / tdilc1120m000 if u wish physical stock to reduce in warehouse / location . And financial transactions will be generated in integration 'Sales (INV) / Issue' .

I think option1 is what u r looking for.
