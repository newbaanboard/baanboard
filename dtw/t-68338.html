Do you mean to say, you want to put a particular inventory stock-point from Stock (Bulk Location) to Quarantine Location during Purchase/Production Process?

Inventory in a quarantine Location only comes through result of Inbound/Outbound Inspection Rejection (for both Purchase and Production) You can not directly transfer inventory to a quarantine location without going through inspection, although there workarounds.

For production you have option in session Report operation Complete session 'Move to quarantine' after you specify rejection quantity and quarantine functionality is enabled.

For Purchase, Inventory gets moved to quarantine location after you reject inventory during inspection and quarantine functionality is enabled

You can find help on detail setup process in LN Documentation No U9876AUS
