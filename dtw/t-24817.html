Supplier = Supplier of Bags
Sub-contractor = Filler of Bags

1 - Buy the bags from your supplier.
2 - Sell them to the sub-contractor [at 0?] (***OR on Sales Consign. Replenish in order to monitor stock levels of empty bags at their site).
3 - (*** Process Sales Consign. Invoice of consumed bags at sub-contractor)
4 - Ensure that "Direct Delivery From" item parameter is set to 1 for the filled bag item
5 - Ensure the sub-contractor is the default supplier for the filled bag item, in item master and item supplier data
6 - (I assume you will keep no inventory on hand) Now sell your filled bag item to your end customer. Because of the direct delivery setting, Baan will auto generate a purchase order to your sub-contractor, instructing him to directly deliver the filled bags to you customer.
