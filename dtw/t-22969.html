When you process a cycle count you will get a GL entry for any difference from the count and what Baan thinks you have. This entry will adjust inventory and an adjustment account. The integrations for this are set up in Maintain Inventory and WIP Transaction Accounts as Warehouse/Warehouse Adjustment Transactions. Warehouse adjustment orders will be generated with 7 as the first free number. I am attaching a planning document.
