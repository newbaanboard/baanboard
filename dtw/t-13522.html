Hi,

As I understand you correctly you are using the Consignment functionality (customer owned warehouse) in b50_c Warehousing as part of your RMA process.
As far as I know purchasing goods for a consignment warehouse (not owned) will have no impact in your inventory valuation.
Are you using MAUC as your Inventory Valuation Method? I am not familiar with weighted average inventory valuation

I know that in b50_b there were major bugs with MAUC (Moving Average Unit Costs) but they have been resolved in b50_c.
I also know one b50_c customer in Europe that uses MAUC and they have no problems at all with their inventory valuation and cost prices. They have been "Live" with b50_c for more than one year now.

Hope this helps for you.

Regards,

Edwin
