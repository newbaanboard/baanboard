Hi,

Bit late maybe but it might be useful in the end.

The minimum order quantity will not be taken into account when running PRP. This is quite logical as well since the purpose of PRP is to plan customised items based on true demand, not on order parameters. That's why those order parameters (order method, minimum order quantity, etc.) are not present in the customised item data either.
