Are you first making sure that you have no Inventory on hand and no demand on open orders? All of that must be removed prior to converting - then adjust the quantities back in and redo any orders. Actually if you have an open demand on an order, just delete the demand for that part then add it back in rather than dealing with the whole order. Hope this helps - Donna
