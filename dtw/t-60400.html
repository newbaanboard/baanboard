One way to handle this would be to use Maintenance Sales Orders (MSO) in the Service module.

An MSO Line of type 'Part Loan' could be entered, issued from the warehouse and delivered to the customer. When the customer returns the part, it is received back into the warehouse and the MSO is closed.

If the customer does not return the part, after a period of time he can be billed using the Maintenance Sales Order.

Another way to do it might be to use Consignment Sales Orders.
