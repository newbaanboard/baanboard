Hi,
It looks like you are setting up a new company.

It checks for the warehouse currency in the following way.

In tcmcs0503m00(warehouse), there is a enterprise unit field.
Go to enterprise units(tgbrg0530m000), there is a company attached to it. Check for the currency fields in this compay in tcemm1570m000(Companies). You might not have filled the currencies Properly in the Company Data.
