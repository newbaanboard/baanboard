The driver you are referring to was released in 1999 as a beta release from Seagate. They have since then released several supported production versions. The Seagate Info software (Crystal Reports and Crystal Enterprise) are now owned by Business Objects (www.businessobjects.com), and the driver is now called Crystal Enterprise Solution Kit for Baan v10. It is licensed with the Crystal Enterprise system and can only be purchased through Baan or Business Objects. If you are able to find the beta driver, it is not supported with current releases of Baan IV or V.
