Hello Stachia,

you already know this, but since it might interest others as well:

The basic archiving concept is the same in Baan 5 as in Baan 4. Baan offers several sessions that have to run and will move data to an archive company. Add on sessions are usually required for archiving purchase data, sales data and service data.

A list of sessions that might be needed for Baan 5 can be found on www.maxsc.com as well as other information about archiving. I have started a lot of cases with Baan support for archiving in Baan 5 and have received a lot of solutions recently. The main bugs have been solved although a few problems still remain.

I use an archive planning tool, which allows me to do a much more thorough data assessment than manually possible and automatically generates an archive plan and other important information. I've used that for Baan 5 and Baan 4 archive projects (exampes on www.maxsc.com).

Anyone interested, please contact me at karin@espelage.com .
