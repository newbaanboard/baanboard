BaanIV Process module is could be used, but more suitable would be the Baan Dimensions vertical for the paper industry.

BaanIV Process is more suitable for the chemical or food industrie.

BaanIV Process works with the Constraint Planning module instead of the MPS module. This makes it possible to plan against finite capacity.

You can use formula's instead of the standard BOM's with coproducts and byproducts,etc.
