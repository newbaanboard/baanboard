Most archiving sessions contain a possibility to archive your master data. However this means the master data is copied to your archive company because it is needed there otherwise you get reference errors. The master data is not removed from your operational company in that case.

When you archive all your data, sometime it should be possible to remove certain master data from your operational company, however it is not so easy to remove an item for example.

Deleting master data should be done using the normal Baan sessions for maintaining this data.
