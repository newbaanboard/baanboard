Hi Jericho,

your Portingset should be 6.1c.06.X when you use SP9.

Some Sessions will have problems when you use an older PS.
When you just install SP9, but not SP9-Tools-Patch the risk of having problems is not very high (tried that once), but if you install SP9-Tools, , too, you will have trouble e.g. in compiling reports etc.

If possible, i would update SP, SP-Tools and PS...
