Hi

You must have different enterprise units that point to the two finance companies and each department belongs to one enterprise unit. Therefore, you must have different department codes in each of the two enterprise units which in turn point to the two finance companies.

Alternatively, you can have multiple enterprise units in one logistics company that point to multiple finance companies.
