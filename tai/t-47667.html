Dinesh,
You can keep the files that you downloaded in any folder in any drive of your choice. I am not sure about the structure of the files that you downloaded , as to whether they are all in one single folder or if they have subfolders amongst them. But ideally all the files that needs to be scanned should be inside one single folder.

As far as your second question is concerned: In the column named "Solution File" in Scan Solution File {ttpmcs2200m000} session, enter the path where all the files are located.
For instance, if all the files are located in "Temp" folder on your C drive then you give "C:\Temp\". Once you click on continue, Baan would ask if you want to scan the whole folder. Click on "Yes"..
