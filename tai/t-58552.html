Finally, i resolved my problems : i had two problems :

1) in tcemm1170m000 : compagny must be created but without inserting exchange rate type. After that system allows to enter "exchange rate type"(tcmcs0140m000), and "currency rate"(tcmcs0108m000). 2) french language pack causes problems. i switched to english language and i ran tcmcs0295m000(initialisation for parameters) without problems.
