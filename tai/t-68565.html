Usually in such cases, When you enter the Process Solutions Session, the system asks "Installation is in progress,Do you want to continue?(Y/N)" and in such cases if you say yes the process is restarted from where it has been left of.Do You face such question?

When i faced the similar Issue, and again after continuing the process of Installation the system hangs, i deleted the Solution in Process Solution from GTM. In this case you might need to check what dependencies the Solution entails and uninstall every Solution dependent on this and delete from GTM.

What system is it Test or Live? Would suggest to go ahead with Solution deletion in case of Test System.
