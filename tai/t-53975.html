Hi,
The session ttaad2131m000 and ttaad2132m000 are one and the same, except for the way information is displayed.

The logic is simple, Session ttaad2130m000 lets you define what modules a user is allowed to start while sessions ttaad2131m000 and/or ttaad2132m000 gives you the option to disallow users from starting sessions of modules that you have allowed in ttaad2130m000.
