Watch out for the Oracle 9i installation. IT has just been supported and has not had large scale performance testing done yet in customer environments with Baan. Our preliminary information shows a significant performance hit with 9i and we are researching ways to resolve these issues today. Depending on your timeline, I would recommend 8.1.7.2 (or best version for your platform) unless you have 2-6 months for a test environment and full scale benchmark to solve the inevitable issues.
