You should make sure that you enter from company 100 to company 100 and not from company 000 to 100 to avoid the company 000 errors.

No definition in definition file for sopen(F_BRDD:dtfgld465, dtfgld465)

The error message for company 100 is due to an unknown domain, run a conversion to runtime of domains (check the mentioned domain) before running the reconfig of tables.
