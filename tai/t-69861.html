Error 514 is a generic one, irrespective of DB & OS.
It is more on the permission level on the DB and not the OS. (514 is a DB error)
As per the help file, below.
514 ESECURITY - No permission
Description:
This is a security error. It probably indicates you do not have the correct user or group permission.
Solution:
Get the correct permission.
