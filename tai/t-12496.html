Just a notice regarding the ufiles: other super users can also create users, so to block all users except bsp, you must not only do a chmod 600 on the ufiles but also make sure that they are all owned by bsp.

For bsp only, the preferable way is to do a chmod 700 on the directory $BSE/lib/user and not the ufiles (to re-enable, then do a chmod 770 or 750).
