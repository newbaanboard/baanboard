You could update your baaniv environment so that you can use slm as license mechanism. With slm it's pretty easy to install a license cluster. However without a high availability option you will fall back in 3? days to half of the licenses.

On the other hand: if disaster happens you could also use the patch objects after system crash option to get your system up. After that you have a couple of days to get a new license.
