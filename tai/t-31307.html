18th February 2007, 15:50
You need to define a WINDOWS type printing device. If the name on the device matches that of a windows printer defined in your workstation printouts will go to that one. Otherwise it will use the default.

Once in your report session fill up the fields as necessary until you reach field "choice".
Type Y in the choice field.
Select your WINDOWS device.
Type Y at Select Device choice field.

On any form typing O in field choice will give you the options you have for that session.
