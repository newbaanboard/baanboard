Hi,

As Vamsi said, check in the preview what Paper Type are you selecting and also the Margins. Make sure user has not stored any user defaults for any other report in ttstpsplopen.

Also, check what are the fonts defined against the selected paper type and the size of the fonts in Fonts by Paper Type session.

Printing in PDF and printing directly to the printer are 2 different things and needs to be handled likewise. PDF device converts the baan report into PDF and then while printing you can do your own print setups and get proper printouts.
