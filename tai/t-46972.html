When you connect via ba6.1 you're not using blogin, so the logfiles and errors you're seeing are unrelated.

My guess is that your dd files still have CRs in them (windows is CRLF, unix is LF) which is causing baan problems. or else possibly you didn't update your FD file to move away from windows paths to unix style paths.
