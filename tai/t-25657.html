You should be able to get that info from the SSA support guys...

Improvements in order management are:
New: Customer Approval concept in Sales Orders
New: Multi Main Table for purchase orders
Improved: multiple purchase receipts (also called: administrative receipts)
Improved: Header- Line synchronization in Purchase Orders
Improved: Header- Line synchronization in Sales Orders
New: Print Prices via Price Matrix
New: Print Discounts via Discount Matrix
New: Global update of Prices in Item Purchase Data
New: Global update of Prices in Item Sales Data
New: Equate Balance of Receipts with Inventory Level
A lot of other (smaller) improvements are made in order management.

May be it is good to consider upgrading to SP2 directly, as it will become available in the coming weeks.

SP2 features (for order management):
New: module sales and purchase statistics
New: delivery scheme for sales contracts
Improved: tax handling
Improved:receipt corrections (also called administrative receipts)
New: Print Potential Back Orders (purchase)

And of course: a lot of other (smaller) improvements are made in order management.
