Hi Frank

There is always a risk in these kind of situations and they maybe hidden to some users out there, as the main problems could appear in solutions linked to your DEM or EDI!

The tools solutions may change a few things which could alter the way that SP 9 solutions are used. Baan say that you can install tools before doing the applications but as with most things, this is not the best way. A total upgrade should be done really and not be in a state of limbo between SP9 tools, SP 8 solutions or the other way round.

In other words, do the full upgrade on your test server then the live server in full before trying to do anything halfway.

Having recently done this procedure, I know how problematic a half installed system can be. If you want to talk it through with me, you know how to get me.
