If the thread the NPRao refers you to does not have what you need, please post your further questions.

If you are running Baan IV or 5.0a/5.0b (<7.1b tools) you can keep all of the audit services on the application servers.

Boeing runs with remote audit servers as the client access to the app servers is somewhat arbitrary (and they have >140 app servers) and they want to minimize the number of servers they have to look at for audit.

The downside of that is having to config Baan Net and remote users for the combined audit. You have to decide if the value of consolidated audit is worth the additional administrative hassle.
