In session "Maintain Domains" (ttadv4100m000) the following domains must have the same alignment, numbers of chars, etc:

1. cpcom.item and tcitem
2. cpcom.cwoc and tccwoc
3. cpcom.plnc and timps.plnc
4. cpcom.cuni and tccuni
5. cpcom.cont and tccuni
