Hi Sumesh, there is such a session: Delete Unchanged Components of Package VRC (ttadv6260m000). You need to specify the B40Vc4Ns VRC and it will delete all components that are equal to the one in the B40Tc4Ns VRC. But keep in mind that it does not have a notion of 'newer'. It has a notion of 'different'. So components that are different are kept in B40Vc4Ns.
