Hi,

With new FP9 Tools, a new session has been introduced (ttadv4105m100) to change the length of the fields.

Check KB# 1526253. Not sure if that will help.

And as Vamsi said, you can use CDF for storing additional data into the tables. Changing the field length in standard tables requires deep analysis including taking care of various reports, forms, scripts (where the length has been hard-coded) etc which is quite time consuming and may lead to problems.
