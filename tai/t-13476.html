Connecting through the internet is extremely difficult if you want to use the normal Baan Windows client. Making the initial connection is not too hard (depending on your firewall) but the next problem is that time-outs occur, and the connection is lost. That's one of the reasons for the BaanIV Web client (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?s=&threadid=10116). Also, have a look here (http://www.beest-boer.net/Delphi).
