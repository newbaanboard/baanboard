I don't agreed with reboots, and never for Baan on UNIX!
Check for any locks on the table before working on it, do not reconfig/drop tables when people are accessing the company of that table.

If no locks exists and nobody is working on that table, for bisam: a restart of Baan is enough (and check for bshell related processes!); if on Oracle, check for locks within Oracle. And before anything, check your Baan logs for other possibilities.
