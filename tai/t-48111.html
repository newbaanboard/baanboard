You will need to still be within the 15 days, and move your date forward past the date / time you originally pushed it back. Then restart the license server. Also everytime you push the key back onto the license server, there is a revision number that gets incremented, so the key only works once. Once you change any information, the revision get incremented and no prior key will work. You will need to requrest another key.
