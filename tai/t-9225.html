So..you know which week? Use week.to.num function.

long week.to.num(long weekno, long.yearno, long week_dayno)

The long it returns is a Baan Date number. For your example:



returned.date = week.to.num(2, 2003, 2)



** assumes Sunday as your first day of the week.
