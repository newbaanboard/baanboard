Got it from the other board.

*************************************************
From the command prompt of your client (msdos)

1. Set the BSE env. variable.
2. %BSE%\bin\bw ottiexpatch

it will say the demo license expired, but the option dialog box in the bottom bar will stay up for a few minutes. When it is done your 30 day license will be active. You have 30 days to license your software.

*****************************************
