There are 2 ways.

1. Create 4 crontab entries to suit 4 month types (viz. 29 days for month 2, 30 days for month 4 ,6,9,11 and finally 31 days for month 1,3,5,7,8,10,12 ). Schedule always for Friday for last 7 days in each combination. For Feb use last 8 days . It will schedule only the right day (except where in a leap year where 29th feb is a friday) .

2. Schedule the job for each friday and in script determnie if it's the last Friday of the month .
