Hi Mick,

Have you changed the sytem date or did you validate Baan? With the validation you must always leave the startdate unchanged. Don't use a startdate in the future. Baan does not check this date if you want to validate. If you change it your validation key won't work.

Is there any time synchronization with other servers?

The last thing you can do is export the table ttiex300 in company 000 and check the date:

bdbpre6.1 -Nttiex300 -t'&' -o. -C000
