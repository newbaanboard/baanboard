Have you tried going to SSA Manager under the SSA ERP folder in your Start>Programs folder. In there you would find the name of your environment. Right click on it and go to properties. In the environment box you would find BSE_TMP. Click on it and type in a new value(new drive\folder) and click on set button. I guess you should do this for another entry which comes under your environment name called SSA ERP. Do the same there as well..(Right Click> Properties > BSE_TMP etc)
