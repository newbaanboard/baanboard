Just been to the Baan UK Spring Conference. I thought you may like to be aware that in the future (FP4 & Tools 8.4) Infor will be integrating the open source Eclipse BIRT (http://www.eclipse.org/birt/phoenix/) (Business Intelligence and Reporting Tools) into ERP LN.

Initially the focus is on pull reporting, i.e. running predefined queries to extract and report/graph data from ERP LN data, then later (FP5ish) the focus will be on formatting the output of push reports, e.g. purchase orders, sales invoices, customer reminders, remittance advices, etc.

I don't believe that BIRT is positioned by Infor (or Eclipse) as a splice and dice tools, ETL tools or OLAP tools. I wonder if it will only work in Webtop since BIRT is java based.

I've not seen it demoed and it is still in development, but it is nice to see that Infor is finally doing something about improving its reporting capabilities. :)
