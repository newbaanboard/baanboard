Please Check the Logs of the Error Message.

I once had the same Error. Commit Transaction Failed in the Audit Server.

And when i checked the Logs it was saying Maximum Audit Records Reached per table. Finally it was due to the Lack of Upgrade of Porting sets. When i upgraded my Porting Set the Value was gone.

For example, 32767 is the Value of AUDIT_MAX. After upgrading the Value increases.

Try Upgrading your Porting Set.
