509 are tricky to determine, check if you have other errors issued just before these appear, they are usually the casue of the 509 afterwards.

Otherwise, these are several times caused by a big mismatch of BW, portingset and standard objects versions. Get these updated according to, more or less, same date, check with Baan to get the versions synchronized.
