Ian,

You can your 7.6_a3 with the latest tools solutions for that version. That should be no problem.
However you can also upgrade to e.g. 7.6_a9 (10.3) if you upgrade your portingset to 8.9.a first.

That works too but you will notice some minor changes. Did this lately for one customer as first step to upgrade the whole application to 10.3.

BTW: ES is the name for both tools and portingset.
