You need to specify the table in ttaad4111m000 (Tables byDatabase). In ERP you have to turn the option audit on to on, convert to runtime. After the convert your users have to logon again.

In Baan4 it's slightly different, you have different db definitions and for the table for which you want to turn audit on you have to specify it with the db definition which has the audit option.
Of course you have to convert to runtime in Baan4 as well.
