Hi dk,
The other day i came across a thread on Baanboard which talks about automatically making the job session go back into waiting mode, for the next day's execution, once a job has been terminated successfully. In that case there wont be any message but Baan would get the session to be in waiting mode for the next job execution interval. For this purpose one of the checkboxes(dont recall which now) has to be checked in the Maintain Jobs session. You might have by-mistake enabled this checkbox. From your description of the problem this seems to be the likely problem. As for the errors, can you tell me what time the error was logged? Was it when you had to close the session yourself or after the job was executed?
In your attachement the time shown on the error log is 15:02. Its unusual for a job to be run at 3pm which is why my suspicion whether the error was generated when you closed the window abnormally or after the job executed...

sk
