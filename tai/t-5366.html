The tool, which exists for the migration from Baan IVc4 to ERP 5.0c can be adopted to the IVc3 datadictionary. This means, you do not have to switch to IVc4 before. But on the other hand, you have to pay an additional fee for the customized migration tool. As far as I know, this is 10.000,- US$.
