Hello simplerosy,

with the newer BW (tested with 8.4.3.3) you have to modify BW.exe in the directory:

c:\program files\baan\baan windows\bin\

You can use the following nice tool:
http://www.wilsonc.demon.co.uk/d10resourceeditor.htm

Load BW.exe and choose Dialog, 145. Click on a button and click to the "styles" tab. Set "Disabled" to "True" Voila !

Feel free to change nearly everything for your needs (you can even add your own logo).
