The biggest change I can think of in tools is PMC. This is the new way of patching that actually has dependencies between solutions and does checking of those dependencies, can download automatically, and let you know about customizations that use the objects in the solutions being installed. However, you may not be using that much because I don't believe there will be solutions for the IVc3 application side.

Beyond that, just test as you would for any other upgrade. Make sure people can do their daily/weekly/monthly jobs. Not necessarily a full functional test but enough to make sure that all tools interaction is working correctly.

For the level II upgrade, you just want to make sure that the tables have real indexes and no more hash columns after the upgrade. In theory, you can save 40% +/- of disk space because of the loss of the hash columns. If the indexes are there and the hash columns are gone, and everything is functioning correctly, you should be good.

Hope this helps.

Vince
