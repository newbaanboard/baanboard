Hi,
Since you havent mentioned what you have done so far to include this paper type in Baan Device Data Section, here are the steps that you would need to do..

1) Maintain Paper Types {ttaad3110m000}
2) Maintain Fonts by Paper Type {ttaad3111m000}
3) Maintain Device Data {ttaad3100m000} (Change the Paper type defined for your device (EPSON LQ 680) in this session.)
4) Maintain Report Defaults {ttaad3105m000} (Optional)

Hth..
