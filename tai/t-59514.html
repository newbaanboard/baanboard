Hello Frank,

I wanted to post a workaround for this particular issue. Apparently, there are two workarounds available:

1) Users should have write-access to the <JRE>/lib/ext-directory on the webui server or
2) The administrator should copy the jdic-files [c:\program files\java\jre6\lib\ext\*.*] from the server to the client JRE (same directory).
