We have some printing-issues with LN, too. Infor has introduced a new parameter to scale the output onto the selected page-format, when printing with bwprint.exe:

-set WINFONTCORR=1

Using this setting the output will fit on the selected pageformat. (It will even scale a landmark output to fit on a normal page.)

I havn't check wether this works on BaanIV but Infor KB 699923 lists it for BannIVc and BaanIVb, too.
