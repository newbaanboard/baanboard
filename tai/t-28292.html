jp.aalders
3rd October 2006, 12:33
Type licmon6.1 -h for all available options

Note: Sometimes users are still present in the licence daemon despite of the fact that they are currently not working in Baan. Often this is caused by not closing Baan normally ( e.g. kill the process with the task manager, turn of the client etc. ). Look at te date and time to currently logged in users. To get rid of licenses in use caused by not closing Baan in an official manner you can restart the licence daemon periodically. This will set the licence count to zero again
