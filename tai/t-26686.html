I believe that there were serious bugs in IDS 9.3 so SSA never
certified it. Their porting sets go from IDS9.2 to IDS10

How do you find out for sure? Go to solution 15219
Look for your porting set, read the Technical Release Notes that are
assigned to each porting set.

This contains info on DB version that is certified as well as OS level, etc.
Much better than in the old days.

Keep reading the technical notes until you find your database version or run out of porting sets =) then you'll have the answer for sure.
