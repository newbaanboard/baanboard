Markus Schmitz

As dave said you will need to check your include6.1 directory, but also you will need to get to a pretty recent tools patch level. Even though the portingset version and the tools version are not directly related, there are some dependencies. Also you should be aware, that the combination of old tools version and new portingset version was never tested by Baan.

Also check things like printing barcodes, as this was changed inbetween.

Enjoy

markus
