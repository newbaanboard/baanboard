When the application dictionary is wrong / corrupt , the export also may dump incomplete or wrong information because application dictonary is based on 000 company data only.

According to me, Backup of Baan home (BSE) and 000 company sequential dump is ok when there is no suspicion as data corruption in company 000.

So the question is there should be no corruption in 000 company. By corruption I mean wrong information also (like somebody meddling with GTM in 000 company tables without changing the dictionary) .Further with oracle 9i possibility of corruption in 000 company with right procedures is virtually none.

Further it's not possible to import / restore single component / program / table from the export dump. It's possible with sequential table backup & BSE fiel system backup.
