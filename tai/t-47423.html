If Printer 1 is not configured on PC2 then you could do with the default printer setup. That is, you have a dummy printer assigned to the user and when he/she prints on PC1 where Printer 1 is default, the print out goes to the default printer (as it fails to find the printer assigned to the user). The same happens on PC2...

This ofcourse works when printer 1 & printer 2 are not installed on PC2 and PC1 respectively..
