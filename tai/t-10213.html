Changing the BSE to another disk under windows would be a bit too risky for me. I would try to free some disk space by moving subdirectories to another disk, e.g.

- the audit directory
- the pmc directory
- the BSE_TMP,BSE_SORT directory
- the help directory
- if that is not enough: the biggest subdirectories under $BSE/application (via Maintain Direcories of SW Components).
