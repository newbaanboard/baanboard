As soon as notepad is terminated, this is notified by the BW to the bshell, and therefore subsequent editing in wordpad is discarded. Search the web for replacing of notepad. On W2K and higher this might pose a slight problem, but it can be done. Changing it in Baan isn't a good option. Maybe something for the wishlist, allowing one to specify the text editor?
