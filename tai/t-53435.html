Tools sessions change between triton and baanV.
For tracing sessions, you can use this sentence:

-- -dbgfile –logfile [file.log] –keeplog [session]

Or this other, which gives you more detail:

-- -set PROFILE_ALL=1 -set PROF_DIR=<dir> -set ORAPROF=0.1 -set EXPLAIN=ON <session>

Transaction audit is done by sessions ttaad4461m000 or ttaad4463m000.
