Do you mean you copied the standard table to your production level VRC, and added a field to it. That is the common scenario.

When you do the service pack upgrade, it will upgrade the base table, adding a field. However, your VRC search path will not see their new version of the table, it will see your old version of the table in your production VRC.

You have to upgrade your table in your VRC by hand.
