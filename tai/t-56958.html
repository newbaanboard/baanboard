Another product is Infor Decisions. It has more capabilities than MyDay. MyDay is for free and will e.g. show you the number of current open Sales Orders. It's meant as a start screen for ERP LN users. With Infor Decisions you will be able to see how many open SO you had the last month and before. For Decisions you have to pay. Decisions is more positioned for key users.
