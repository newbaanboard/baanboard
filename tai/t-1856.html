NPROa,

I am not expert enough on PMC to answer your specific questions. Though PMC can carry all types of objects across to other systems, I see the dependancy chain and pre-requisites as a major problem, especially because PMC does not know the concept of 'superseding obejcts'. However the most recent PMC improvement seems to be addressing that problem somewhat and also answers the question when and where a CRDD is necessary. The CRDD in itself is still not automatic as far as I know.
