Hi Ganesh,

If you need the entire baan application then it is better to store the $BSE except for the temp folder on the DAT monthly.

In case you do a sequential dump of 000 company you amy not get all the objects (compiled versions) of the components. So you can store the entire $BSE for full backup. And if you need backup of customised sessions you can backup all the developing sessions and its respective components through the session ttiex1280m000
