The best would be going thru the Baan Support website and check the available Quick-Guides thru the link they provide in the Knewlege Base, but if you want them faster, thru anonymous ftp, go to ftp.support.baan.com and get the files (.exe mostly, autoextractable word documents) from /documents/tools
