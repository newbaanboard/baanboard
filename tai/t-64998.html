Hi,

What kind of Authorization do you use? DEM or Role Auth?

Also, if you are looking forward to take table dumps, you will need a lot of tables which depends on the number of tables you have used i.e. Table Auth by Table, Session auth by session, table data auth.. etc. So, that can be a tedious task which can also lead to inconsistencies.

You will need to study all the tables before taking dumps.

There is a similar KB # 1177956 for an enhancement, but I guess it has not been implemented yer. The KB will give you list of role data tables. But again, it can vary on requirements. So, take care of that. In case if there is any standard way, I would like to know it as well. AFAIK, you will need to do a customization.

And, in the KB 1004158, they have mentioned, ttcon61import/export is not available in LN. Also, they have given the list of tables to import. I hope that serves your task.
