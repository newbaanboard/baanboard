The supported OS is defined by the supported portingset (the runtime environment of baan or LN). Supported platforms are always listed in the portingset covering-letters of Infor.

You need to go to www.inforxtreme.com and look at KB 22923520 (search for the number).

There you'll find links to the various unified portingsets. Follow the Link of the portingset you want to install. There you can download the technical notes pdf. The pdf gives detailed information on the supported platforms.
