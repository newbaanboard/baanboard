catwalk
12th January 2005, 07:25
After validation Baan patches the tools objects, based on the validation and security key.
It is necessary to patch the tools objects with the information of license key which is used by the license daemon. Licensing information, validation and security keys are stores in ttiex300 and ttiex301 tables. Hence when you have two systems sharing the license damon it is necessary to have this information synchronized.
In your case you must have copied the "000" company to other environment.

Please try installing Baan from scratch on other system and point to the system running license damon by modifying the licence6.1/2 file.
