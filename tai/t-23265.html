If you can do business in the US, then you can do business in Canada. Here we have a federal Goods & Services Tax (GST) of 7% and each provice has a provincial sales tax (PST) (here in Ontario it is 8%). Some of the provinces aligned their provincial tax with the rules of the GST and it is called the Harmonized Sales Tax (HST). Just like in any jurisdiction, different items are taxed differently and the only issue here is that you may have to set up multiple tax levels.

Have I ever done it... no. That is for the accountants.
