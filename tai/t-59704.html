Hello lebowski

I have found the solution for it!
I just installed the worktop 2.5.8 on an windows 7 prof 64bit. After the installation I had the same problem. As the language is determind from the regional settings of the windows installation, i checke it. It was in my case "Deutsch(Schweiz)". I switched the settings to "Deutsch(Deutschland)" and started the worktop again -> know the Language was correct. I changed now the setting back to "Deutsch(Schweiz)" and the Language remaind German! Also after a reboot of the System.


I hope this helps you.
