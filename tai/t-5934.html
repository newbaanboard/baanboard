In such situations, I think you have do use a litte redesign. remoge the report from the session, do the formatting and output in your session script e.g. like:

string buf(1024)
long fd,i

fd = seq.open("file.txt", "w")
buf = ""
for i=1 to 500
if i > 1 then
buf = buf & "|"
endif
buf = buf & sprintf$("%d",i)
endfor
seq.puts(buf, fd)
seq.close(fd)
