JamesV
10th October 2002, 23:29
You never need to stop Baan for an online backup of the database. However, keep in mind that an online backup of the database done during the night may catch a batch job in the middle of a major update and when you restore to the point in time of the backup, you may have to perform some cleanup of the data.

The ArcServe2000 agent does perform an online copy but may sure you are at Win2000 SP2 otherwise, the backup may not be restorable due to nasty interaction between early versions of ArcServe and Win2000.
