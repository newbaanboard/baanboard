Youp,

What you can do, is the following:


Start session ttiex1284m000 in Baan.
Insert the patch-location and run this session with "Extract only" --> "Yes".
Leave the session and go to your shell.


This way, the patch will be extracted without being imported. On OS you can now check the components the patch contains.
I don't know a way to extract the patchdumps on Unix, actually.

This is the way I sometimes "fool" PMC. But beware: since Baan launched PMC, most patches have a security against extraction by ttiex1284m000, because Baan wants you to use PMC instead... (Nothing to worry about, just an error-message.)

Hope it works!
