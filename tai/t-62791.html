Hello, Camilla.
Talking about minimal permission, required to start Infor LN you can find them in "DEFAULT" role of your Infor installation.
I used to give Delete/insert/modify/read remission for while company in Table authorization by company session (ttams3144m000) for all users. This authorization is given because it is hard to consider all relating tables.
To limit access for known tables, field and data ranges you can enter appropriate data in Table authorization by table (ttams3142m000), Table data authorization (ttams3145m000) and Table Field Authorization sessions (all of this session are accessible through Specific menu in Role data session (ttams2100m000). All setting in mentioned before sessions has higher priority over Table authorization by Company.
To understand what exactly wrong with your role settings I need to see the entire list of setting for this role. You can print them and put into your next post.
