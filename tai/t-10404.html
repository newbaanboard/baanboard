Hi,

you should do the following:

1. Start bwprint.exe on the client
2. go to Help/Fonttest
3. Change the font by doubleClick on the right line

the new one is written to the registry of the client.
You can provide this change to all other Users by exporting and installing the tree HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Baan\BwPrint\Fonts

good luck and
