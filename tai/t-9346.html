Dear amolrp,

I hope you are aware that Tools authorisation and DEM authorisation are completely independent in most versions of Baan. Hence, if you used DEM, in most versions it will only consider DEM authorisation and completely ignore your Tools authorisation. Please read Ivo's explanation on this in the user authorisation (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?s=&threadid=7257) thread
