Hi Dmanick,
I know this is possible in Unix systems. You create an OS user and a BaaN user. When you configure the connection in the BaaN client, there is an optional area called "command." In that space we give the following command.
-- -set USER=baanuser
This works on BaaN 5 under Unix. I think it works under the BaaN 4 client also, however I'm not sure if the syntax is different for MSWindows.
