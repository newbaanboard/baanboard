24th March 2005, 19:04
session_timeout, ds_timeout_detect and licmon6.1 -c are how you deal
with this problem. They are all porting set related and have nothing to do with the Baan version. You just have to be on a recent porting set version.

session_timeout will cleanly kill idle bshells (i.e., people not doing anything in Baan at that time)

ds_timeout_detect will kill orphaned bshell's (i.e., user logged out but bshell remains)

licmon6.1 -c removes "hung" licenses (i.e., bshell has terminated and not updated the license count)
