The message is not clear, search for more clues in the event viewer, if your OS got corrupt it may be that the corruption damaged the Windows services or Baan binaries/libraries.
If the Baan directories were not affected then it seems a registry or services problem. Maybe re-installing the portingset can help or have it checked with Baan or Microsoft.

NT is not supported anymore, why not take the opportunity get a new machine with a more recent and supported OS?
