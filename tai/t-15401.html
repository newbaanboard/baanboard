The errors you see have nothing to do with the demo period. In the demo period Baan is fully functional.

You have to set up your user as a developer (look in Application Development for this) and you have to specify your current VRC in "Maintain Current VRC of user".

You still might not be able to see any sources, because most likely you do not have any!!!
