Saravanan,

I think there is some misunderstanding.
There are two sessions involved.

a)First you have to define the "read/write" authorisation for field tdpur041.pric in Session ttaad4133m000 (Maintain Table Field Authorizations).

b) In the second step you have to define the "read" authorisation for field tdpur041.pric for orders not between 160000 and 169999 in Session ttaad4135m000 (Maintain Table Field Data Authorizations).

This works in our IVc4 environment.
