Hi there,

I guess the reason, why you did not get many answers is, because the question is too broad.

First of all most toolies doe not know much abozut the actual application and therefore also do not know which "cleanup job" would make sense in ti/td/...

Second, what is needed will depend on the modules you use, the localizations you use, the parameter settings etc.

In Tools alone you do not need much. Mainly a job in Baan, which will delete old entries from the printer queue and another job in your OS to delete old file in the tmp directory.

Other jobs would also be possible, like deleting unsused texts, repairing reference counters, building statistics in your database. But you would need to look into these a bit more carefull.
