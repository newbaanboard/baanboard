In "Start Program" you can execute a menu typing "ttdskmbrowser -mmxxxxx -d", where "xxxxx" it is the menu name. e.g.: to excute the menu "tccom0001m0001" you need to type "ttdskmbrowser -mmtccom0001m0001 -d", but this action it will open a BW menu
