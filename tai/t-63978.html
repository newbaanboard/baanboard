There are some possibilities to make your life easier: you could install the product 'Services for Unix', (http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=2391)
MS delivers it to make conversion from unix to windows more easier.

Another tool you can use is cygwin (www.cygwin.org). That contains even more unix commands.

However in my experience also these tools are slower than native commands.
