I would try to set the parameters to make the modules independents. And to test sales, you could create a purchase item (with no integration in finance), entering stock by purchases and by a stock correction.
Also you could make warehousing transactions without other modules.
Testing production and finances separately also is easy.
But the majority of the problems that you could find out in a new version can be related with integration processes.
