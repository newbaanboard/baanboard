1. The first part is easy to take the list of the Super Users- ttaad200 - User Data.

2. this is the hard part, you need to verify each and every role/sub-role authorization template and check the settings for authorization at the tt-package, tt-aad-module and session-ttaad4100 and then verify which use has this role attached in their user data. (or) verify each normal user's role data, this seems more performance intensive.

Related tables to query-

ttams330 - Maintain session authorizations by Package.
ttams331 - Maintain session authorizations by Module.
ttams332 - Maintain session authorizations by Session.
