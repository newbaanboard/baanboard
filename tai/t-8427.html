I think your main worry besides capacity - which is not really an issue I assume since it's for evaluation purposes - is the licensing problem. However since BaanIV and BaanV use different brand files, you are safe there. Just make sure you are installing into a separate database instance and that you have your application-database connectivity configured correctly (e.g. Oracle SQL*net)
