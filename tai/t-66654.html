Hi,

tfgld102 does not store the text's. The text is stored in other table.

For example,

If you are booking a Sales Invoice, the text will be stored in tfacr200 with the field tfacr200.text

If you are booking a Purchase Invoice, the text will be stored in tfacp200.

Please check and let me know if it helps.
