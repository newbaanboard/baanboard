Hello,

In ERPLN or Baan ERP you must create a file in the $BSE/lib/user library.
the filename must kxxxx where xxxx is your login

format of the file is
fn:process(sessioncode)
where fn = f1-f12 (normal key) or F1-F12 (shift +key)


your request:

f1:process(ttaad9598m000)
f2:process(ttaad5500m000)


this works under unix never tested on windows, but I think that it must also works
