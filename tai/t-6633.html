Hello,

I suppose that you are working with Unix.
The printer cartridge was a optional device to be installed on old hp LASERJET printer (laserjet III and IV).
AFAIK hP doesn't more produce this part. It exist some other vendors of compatible cartridges.

If you need specific barcode you should need to update the file type01 ($BSE/lib/barcode/hp). Therefor you need the technical manual delivered with the cartridge

You can also use other printers, like the Kyocera 1750 printers, that support barcoding without cartridge

OCR-B is a retail barcode HR font, not likely to be any use. You are likely to be looking to use code39 or code128. If you persevere with the HP website, you will eventually be directed to a 3rd party partner who specialises in barcode fonts for HP laserjet printers either available as DIMMS or soft fonts (dependant on printer model). More info at...
http://www.hp.com/sbso/product/printing/laserjetfonts/barcode.html

However, I would strongly advise you to look into using BWprint for your barcode printing - it is MUCH easier!
