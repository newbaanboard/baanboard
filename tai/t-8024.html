Hello NR,
It took us, to migrate from b2 to c4, 20 days!! There was around 45 GB of data. Of course there were customizations, which took another 10 days, but nothing standard was changed.
Also, there are two methods of migration -
1. Upgrading b2 to c4 on the same machine.
2. Installing c4 on a new machine, and then importing the customizations of b2.

Option 2 is a safer one.

Good luck.
