Hi Mohamed_alsadr,
the p* files are the sourcecode files of the Baan application. They are only applicable if you have bought them.
In PMC patches and solutions the sources are included but they are just installed if a previous version of the source is installed.
So the error message only states that pmc could not export the old source!
You can ignore the messages. No problem!
