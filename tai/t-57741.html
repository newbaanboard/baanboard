I got it fixed.

Two problems existed. I didn't think this would have caused a problem but we had also moved the client software to new computers and the %BSE% Path was not defined. This fixed printing to word and excel.

The other problem with the Outlook part was that for some reason the "/a" switch was not present in the printer config. This fixed the Outlook problems.
