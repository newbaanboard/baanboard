It depends on what you are creating/converting runtime (RTDD) for.
Since application data dictionary (APDD) vs RTDD is one of the most important aspects of Baan it will not be easy to find a document that explain all.

In resume, most of it happens at OS filesystem level; certain tables will be, however, updated with information regarding the creation/conversion.

Some tables also store RTDD information (like user authorizations) so, here, most happens at database level (storage of information).
