ecarceller
6th March 2007, 18:31
Yes it will change those numbers. tfgld9004 does not know whether the tables are shared or physical because that distinction is done at a much lower level.
This should not affect other companies even if they are sharing tables because the affected records will be those referring to transactions in the other company and not their own.

However if what you are doing is creating a test environment try if possible to copy the whole group of companies. Otherwise you are taking the risk of creating reference problems, inconsistencies and orphan records.
