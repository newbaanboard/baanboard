Hi,

Our w2k is on SP2.
We do not use baan job daemon on Windows platform due to it's unstability even when we're on NT (if you're on UNIX it is ok).
I would suggest you not to use the baan job daemon. Alternatively you can call your baan jobs from batch(.bat) files and use the w2k scheduler to schedule the jobs.

Hope this help.
