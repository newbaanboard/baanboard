I was able to do an de-install via the control panel/Add Remove programs.

However for one of my three environments I am not able to do it. (Still waiting for a solution of SSA support)

In the de-install you can specify that you only want to de-install the logic so the application itself will not be removed.

After the new installation I had to:
remove BSE_REM in the registry
remove rbaan in ${BSE}/lib/user
copy ubaan- to ubaan in ${BSE}/lib/user
restore my tabledef6.2

Rgrds,
Han
