When you click in the desktop on Application ==> Menu browser,
then look for the session in the Menu browser and one click on the session,
then click in the menu of the Menu browser on Options,
then click on Item to Desktop.
If you do the above: You first have to select a Group on your Desktop into which the session has to be placed. Or you have to create a new Group on your Desktop.
