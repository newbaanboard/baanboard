First of all, are you running any of the heavy modules (PCF, Big projects, etc)?

How many batch or CPU intensive jobs do you have running concurrently? Win2000 does not handle this well and you need one additional processor for every 1-2 batch jobs. I generally do not like to have less than four procs in an app server.

The database server can probably be a pair of processors. Each server can most likely have 2-3 GB of RAM. But this is a rough estimate depending on your answers to the above questions.
