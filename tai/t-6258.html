What bandwith is required depends on what each user will do in Baan. iread somewhere in a sizing guide I (BaanIV, BOP=Project Estimate Module):

- Minimum bandwith for a typical BW user: 8kbps
- Minimum bandwith for a BOP calculations BW user: 32kbps

A maximum latencey of 200ms (roundtrip) was considered.

For 30 users, with 1 Mb/s thru TCP/IP, each user has available an average of 33+Kbps, this should be more than enough.
