This problem has to do with the paper size, or better the number of characters that can be printed on one line.

Check how many characters your report wants to print on with line, check the number of characters for A4 with different font and correct accordingly.

Normally it should be 80 for 10CPI, 94 for 12CPI and 132 for the small size in A4.
In landscape it's more, 132, 162 and 194.

However if you use windows printers then its slightly different and I know that there is a solution for the reports which tries to print up to 215 characters but I have to look that up.
