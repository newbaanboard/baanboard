You can only uninstall PMC based solutions/patches/service packs and not the export/import dumps.

The session ttpmc2101m000 - Process Solutions, ttpmc2102m000 - Process Patches has the uninstall options in the Specific Menu.

After you have uninstalled tables/domain/sessions/forms/menus, you have to execute runtimes, ttadv5210m000 and ttadv5214m000 and no users should be present in the system.

If you're using ttiex1284 you cannot uninstall the solution via PMC as
ttiex is outside of PMC.

Since you cannot run ttpmcXXXX you must not be on Baan 4c4.

To uninstall an old style solution you need to (prior to installing the solution)
make a backup of the existing objects. First you would find out what components were changed in the solution (from www.support.baan.com) or ttiex1284 i believe has a report. Back them up at the OS level and then, to "uninstall" you could copy them back.
