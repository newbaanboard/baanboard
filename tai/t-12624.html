The BW client is more or less in sync. with the portingset and with the standard objects.

It's usually not a problem to use a more recent version with an older portingset, but sometimes weirdo behaviours show up.
If you don't have problems with your existing version and you will not change portingset for the upgrade, then keep the existing one.

As usual, test the newer version on one client PC before updating the remaining ones.
