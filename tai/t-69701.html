to your question "How could I Know the version of library used in a session?":
On my UNIX machine, I would use for example

$ explode6.1 otisfcdll0224
-> /baan/bse/application/tiB40U_c4_stnd/otisfc/osfcdll0224

So if you have source code, you can copy that source to your VRC and compile/debug it as well.
