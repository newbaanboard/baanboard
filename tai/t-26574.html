In the colors section:

#*********************************************************
# Colors
#*********************************************************
p_black=\E*v0S,
p_red=\E*v1S,
p_green=\E*v2S,
p_yellow=\E*v3S,
p_blue=\E*v4S,
p_magenta=\E*v5S,
p_cyan=\E*v6S,
p_white=\E*v0S,


The color White is set to black back.

In the setup section append to the initpr (before the comma) \E*r3U

It works with PCL printers.
