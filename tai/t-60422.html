The in Condition works like an or and should be defined on one line of the Filter.

So, if you want to display only Business Partners SUPA00029, SUPA0003 and SUPV00006, your Value field should read ...

SUPA00029,SUPA00030,SUPV00006

Make sure there are no spaces between the Business Partner numbers.

IF you are looking for just a range of Business Partners, use the equals or after and equals or before Conditions.
