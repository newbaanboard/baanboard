Have to agree with Pat there, this has been a common question on the board before and it is viewed that many problems can occur with rushing SP updates. They should never be rushed or even short-cutted in anyway as problem solving then becomes a nightmare to sort out.

Also check out the details of ALL patches to find out what might be needed, you will be surprised how many times you might need to restart Baan, Dump Data Definitions and many other things that are not listed in the manuals or documentations with the SP's.
