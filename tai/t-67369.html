Hi,

ttstperrlog involves multiple log files. Each of them signifies something. They act as a guide to track errors and trouble shoot.

e.g

ttaad4100.sox gives you information about the GTM changes.
log.tlpublishbod gives you troubleshooting data for BOD publishing.
log.ttaad4225 gives you troubleshooting data for reorganize process.

etc.

To understand action="50" or action="21" etc, you can go to GTM , make some change like add record, copy record, modify record etc and check in the log what action is specified.

The purpose behind the usage of these log files are two way-

1. Troubleshooting Errors
2. Technical Auditing.

You need to study each of them individually to understand what they specify. Or you can open an Incident with Infor to understand them.

Also, search on Board for the errors people face during reconfiguration and how they use log files to track them-

Here are a few of them -

Thread 1 (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=6793)
Thread 2 (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=41450)
Thread 3 (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=38370)
Thread (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=57187)

Log files might change depending on the databases used as well and varies according to the different error logged.
