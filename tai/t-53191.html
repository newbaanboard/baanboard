Hi,

Follow the following steps:
1) Got to Maintain Menu session (ttadv3160m000)
2) Copy the menu to your package VRC
3) Click on the edit button and edit it as you wish
4) Save the menu
5) Compile the menu
6) Re-login to baan and you should see the changes.

I hope it helps,

- George
