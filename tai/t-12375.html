Go into PMC:

Tools/Software Installation/PMC/Process Solutions

and look at your update VRC. It will list all the service packs installed. They should have the "Installed" Status.

That is session ttpmc2101m000 in BaanIV.

You can also look in Version Scan Tool, session ttaad0500m000 and on form 2 it shows what service pack you are on.

May be all kind of... most probably administration problem.
Try to run with:
-- -keeplog -logfile tellmewhy -dbgfdev ttaad0500m000

You can also install the latest version from a solution. And run ttaad5213m000 to convert session to runtime.
