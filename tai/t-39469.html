Sp3 to Sp18 is a big leap and you could expect a lot of changes, some new tables, changes in domain and structure of forms and sessions and most importantly the script behind them.
Customized VRC is a good approach as it gives you room for testing your customized objects in the new SP before applying it on the standard VRC objects. Dont forget to go through the SP documentation as it details out what changes are brought forward with each release.

To move tables, the best way(according to me) is the sequential dump. Take a dump of the table and restore it.

Good Luck on the migration..you need it!!
