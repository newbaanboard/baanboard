A package combination is a collection of Packages at a VRC level. Companies are attached to Package VRC's, and cannot be attached to more than 1.

You cannot have one company look at 2 different Package Combinations, so one company cannot look at 2 different versions of a given component.

Ok I got your point now, your right we dont have differences :)

To make things clear for our understanding -

Companies are linked to Package Combinations and not VRCs.

Package combinations can have same/different VRCs.

A company can be linked to only one package combination at an instant but a package combination can be linked to many companies.

One can switch between companies linked to different package combinations as long as the Domain/Table-DD are matching else the tools will give an error message.
