You need to add
and tcibd001.item = whwmd215.item
to your where clause.

select
tcibd001.item, | Item
whwmd215.cwar, | Warehouse
whwmd215.qhnd, | Inventory on Hand
tcibd001.dsca, | Description
from
tcibd001, | Items - General
whwmd215 | Item Inventory by Warehouse
where
whwmd215.cwar >= whwmd215.cwar.f and
whwmd215.cwar <= whwmd215.cwar.t and
tcibd001.item >= tcibd001.item.f and
tcibd001.item <= tcibd001.item.t and
tcibd001.item = whwmd215.item
order by
tcibd001.item
