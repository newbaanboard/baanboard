Hello learner,

the space consumed on OS level is the size of the SP-files (spb40c4_11stnd, stb40c4_11stnd etc.). This files are split into the single solutions and shifted in the directory you specify in the PMC "Maintain Parameters" session.
Then there is the Uninstall information (also specified in "Maintain Parameters". Add roughly half of it´s actual size.
The space consumed on DB level I don´t know, I can only say that it is not relevant.
