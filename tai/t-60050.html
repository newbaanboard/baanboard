If you had another server that IS running licd6.1 and is running a valid license and shares the same information in ttiex300 and ttiex301 you could simply point $BSE/lib/licence6.1 to the server that is licensed temporarily until you get a key for it. Once you get a key for it do NOT forget to change the licence6.1 back to itself so you can complete the revalidation process. Otherwise, you have to keep monitoring your jobs or put them on hold and have someone manually run them.
