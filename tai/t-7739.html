Just some quick answers :

1. Yes. For Oracle 8.1 support you need at least 6.1c.05 but even that one is old.
Also your current AIX version does not support Oracle 8.1.7 (4.3.2 is minimum)

2. Do not think so.

3. Yes, setup a distributed environment with the demo company in another database.

4. If your box is big enough and is 64-bit, you might consider 9i, but it is heavier than 8i, and a LOT heavier than bisam

5. no, unless a porting set upgrade requires it

6. Just do not specify ORA_LEVEL1. Leverl 2 is the default, and with the right tuning it performs as good (or bad) as level 1 for BaanIVc4
