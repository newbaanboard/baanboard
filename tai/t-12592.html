I believe you meant "failed to create next extent".

perform a dbavail to see you devices. That will tell you which device is giving you the problem. Also Informix online.log should give you more information as to which chunk is filled up and when the next extent is being created there is no more space left on device. There are two solutions resize the tables on that device. That tends to free up some space or add more disk to that chunk.
