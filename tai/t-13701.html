As it seems you need to change your bw-print font settings

You have to start bwprint (select a report in "preview" mode), afterwards you do a font test, right click on the font that you want to change and compress it or make it smaller.

Note that these setting are client dependent and are stored in the PC-registry. Hence, you need to do this setting again on all Windows-XP clients or import the corresponding registry setting.
