On our portrait printers we use paper type LETTER, on our landscape printers we use LANDSC (see this in ttaad3100m000 Maintain Device Data).

These two paper types are defined in ttad3110m000 Maintain Paper types and have an appropriate Orientation.

Finally, in ttaad3111m000 Maintain Fonts by Paper Type, there are the following entries

a) LETTER
Large (10 cpi) - 80 char wide, 62 lines high
Small (17.1 cpi) - 132 char wide, 62 lines high
Middle (12 cpi) - 96 char wide, 62 lines high

b) LANDSC
Large (10 cpi) - 132 char wide, 66 lines high
Small (17.1 cpi) - 200 char wide, 66 lines high
Middle (12 cpi) - 132 char wide, 66 lines high
