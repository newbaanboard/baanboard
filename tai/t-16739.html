Me generally do these following after creating new finance companywe are Single logistics and multi finance setup
1) Make sure I logically shared the few master data tables with Logistic Company.
2) Add company number into tgbrg0100s000
3) Add company data into companies tcemm1570m000
4) Add record into new companies tcemm1570m000
5) Add new EU units into tgbrg0530m000
6) Initialize parameters via tcmcs0295m000

All the steps depends on what data you brought into new finance company
