No, there is no way to automate that. Of course, you can do a search-and-replace in your favourite editor, but since it's not only the table names that change but also table semantic that changes (you just have a single item master table, for example, where your Baan IV code is likely to look either in tiitm001 or tipcs021. For another example, tdsls401 is *not the same* than tdsls041, in 401 there are additional total lines if a position gets partially delivered), you will have to go through your code and migrate it manually to ERP LN.
