Bring Baan to "single user mode" or "restricted users mode":
Rename $BSE/lib/user to $BSE/lib/user.backup
Create a new $BSE/lib/user (attention to permissions)
Copy the user ufiles (baan or bsp, for example) that will be allowed to login from $BSE/lib/user.backup to $BSE/lib/user (attention to permissions)
Restart Baan and onlyh users will ufiles will be allowed to login.

When you want to bring the system back to "multiple user mode",
Delete $BSE/lib/user
Rename $BSE/lib/user.backup to $BSE/lib/user
(And no need to restart Baan)

This has been discussed a long time ago in the baanboard, search the Tools section for restrict users or something in that context.
