The ORA-01450 error is related to your db_block_size and the maximum key length for your database is approximately 40% of the database block size minus some overhead. However, you have a bigger problem, getting the CBO to use an index with a large key value! Indexes with long index keys are rarely used!
Refer to -
http://www.dba-oracle.com/t_ora_01450_maximum_key_length_exceeded.htm
