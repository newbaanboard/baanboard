I use exp/imp (or if you have it expdp/impdp)

They have a 'consistant' option which means that you don't have to bring the database down
to do the export.

Export your production company, excluding tools, and load it into your test database.
just truncate or drop the tables in the test company first.

this is a very easy process if you are able to have production Company # == Test Company #.

If not then there are still options.
