Murphy I believe is correct. This session runs out of company 000. In the ttaad4226m000 add your parameters and then click tools and make a job. Enter a new job name. You will see that it creates the job in company 000 since that is where the session runs from. Then you can log into company 000 and activate the job. Worked for me.
