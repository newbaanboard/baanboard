Pegas1,
The field Device Queue in session Maintain Device Data {ttaad3100m000} is specifically meant for assigning a Device to a particular printer setup on a PC.
For E.g:
Suppose you have two printers connected to your PC
"Printer1" - default
"Printer 2" - Networked
Where "Printer1" and "Printer2" are the names of your printer found in the Printers & Faxes section of your computer.

In Baan you create two Device Data
"Device1" - In Device Queue field you put "Printer1"
"Device2" - In Device Queue field you put "Printer2"

Whenever you print using device "Device1" it would print on Printer1 by default and when you print using "Device2" it would print on Printer2 by default..

Check it out!!

Note: Without changing the Device in session Select Device (whenever you try to print), i dont think you would be able to select different printers on your PC by default (except if you do what joy had suggested). Other ways include assigning a device to a report or to a user but from your post, thats not what you are looking for..
