First analyse the problem:

Problem could be large tables like mentioned before. Are all finance sessions slow or just a specific one. In case of f.i. customised reports wrong indexes could be used. This may result in search actions where each record is searched sequential in an related table causing major performance problems.
Other causes could be slow disks, almost full disks, bad tuning of the database or processor capacity of the server.

Good luck!
