For BAAN IV, look here:

BAAN IV Tools
--> Database Management
--> Maintenance
--> Change Password for General Table Maintenance [ttadv0144m000]

The password will apply to both Maintain and Display session - that is, you cannot have a different password for each session. Also, it is possible that you may have to request the BAAN Administrator to do this for you, depending on your organization.

Hope this helps.
