In $BSE/lib/users, you can change the uxxx file (xxx = user).
But you have to do it again in cas of convert.

Example:

session_timeout:120
ds_timeout_detect:30
ds_max_wait:60
exec_perms: 100
unixuser:mrl
usertype:N
...

The 3 first lines can be add with a batch or on hand.
