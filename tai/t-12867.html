6th September 2003, 10:46
After the command bdbpre6.1, strip the backslash with the following commands:

mv t<tablename>.S t<tablename>.$$
sed "s/.#/#/g" t<tablename>.$$ > t<tablename>.S
rm t<tablename>.$$Then you can send the file to your site database.
