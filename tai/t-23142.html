Baan wouldn't store the path to your datafiles because Baan doesn't need that info - (i.e., you cannot maintain them from Baan). Instead that info is
stored within SQLServer where it can be used by the database.

All Baan cares about is what Database am I connecting to, and it can do
that by the DB name and serverhost...

hope that helps!
