I guess it is happens when you activate the job instead of putting in queue.
I suggest you do the following:
Make sure the job is periodical (The periodical check box is checked).
Cancel the job if it is waiting or running.
In fact cancel all jobs at a suitable time.
Restart your job daemon.

Rechedule all your jobs.

Should do the trick.
