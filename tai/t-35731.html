Hi Sachin,

The difference is, like the titles state:
- development menu is for devlopment, which includes customisations AND new development. This is only operative for customers who have a development license. In this menu the sub-menu's Program Script/Libraries, Functions, Compact Development menu (and PMC) are available.
- Customizations menu is for the technical Baan administrators who do not develop and/or who do not have a development license... Therefor the abovementioned sub-menu's are not available here...
