I think I may have solved my own issue. Instead of trying to think VRC's ( which the more I thought about it, the less convinced I was taht it was gonna work ) I stumbled across another thread based on Report Groups and this code:

Hi,

in "Maintain Sessions" put yur reports in different groups and the use the predefined variable "reportgrp" in your session script as follows to switch to the correct report group based on your condition:

Code:
choice.print.data:
before.choice:
if (some.condition) then
reportgrp = 1
else
reportgrp = 2
endif__________________

I would just set my condition to = the new COmpany number and that should do it :) ....
