Namaste,

on the installation medium where you can find the vault server there is also a subdirectory with a sequential dump of dm-tables. With this dump you populate the dm-tables. You import the dump with bdbpost (""Create table from sequential Dump").
The setup dumps can also be found on the Infor support homepage, check solution 149002.
