BOBBY,

If you are using Cycle Count Orders to conduct your inventory and are worried about users updating the inventory during the Cycle Count, you can block inventory transactions by checking the Block During Warehouse Cycle Counting check box in the Inventory Handling Parameters session - whinh0100m000.
