Yes, the log is useful despite of the fact that is doesn't show changes. It indicates who, when and what table was modified. Normally the ttaad4100 session is not a session all users have access to. Normally only a few people should have access to the ttaad4100 session because the session should be used very carefully. You may supose that people that use the session know what they are doing.

For monitoring the modifications on tables made by your used you could use the audit functionally provided by tools.
