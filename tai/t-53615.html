Hi there,

1) the table will have field(s) containing the company number 303, this will cause errors when these contents remain unchainged.
2) I'd bet that there are references to other finance tables, in this case the corresponding tables should be exported and imported too.
IIRC there is a session designed for this purpose (moving finance tables from one company to another, in detail chainging all fields containing the company number), but I suspect that the purpose of this session is NOT to merge two companies (tfgld9004m000).
Especially the design of key1 of tfgld018 calls for duplicate entries, so you have to do some research before you can merge :-)
bdbpre and bdbpost alone will not help.
