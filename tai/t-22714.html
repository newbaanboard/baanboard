Hi Learner,

I think the problem you have, is that you must migrate the two item masters to one table before you share it. eg.: you migrate item master from co.200 to co.100 now you have a table in co.100 with all records and you could share this table with co.200. But if you only share the table without migrating you get the problem that you use in your logical co.200 the item master of co.100 and the physically item master of co.200 is only not in use. The table still exist but without a link to a physically or logical company.
