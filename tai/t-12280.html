Dear Suhas,

You can print barcode from Baan easily on HP Laserjet because it is handled by BWPRINT using Windows printing mechanism. However, I don't think you can control the Intermec barcode printer from Windows for printing sake, i.e. I don't think you can print a MS Word document to the Intermec printer. The Intermec printer requires its own instruction on how to print barcode and most likely it doesn't listen to Windows instruction on printing much. You'd have to read the barcode printer manual to find out how to issue the correct instruction to the printer. Many times this means sending certain character sequence right to the port to which the printer is attached.

Rgds,
Paul

The Intermec Printers must have forms defined in their own printer language and either saved in the printers memory, or uploaded to the printer as part of a print job. You then send data fields that fill in the blanks on the form. The forms defined fonts, barcode type and size, lines, positions, variable fields and data types, etc.

I have developed my own label application that use a network of Intermed Barcode Printers at all of our six plants to print inventory,Work in Process and Shipping Labels based on the AIAG & ISO requirements for Part Identification. My Application has 13 forms saved on the printer for use in my application.

The Printers use a Generic/Text Only Driver in Windows.

Roy
