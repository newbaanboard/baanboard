First of all, I would go to ttaad1107m000 Maintain Formats by Generic Unit. Format Codes 003 (Prices), 004 (Cost Prices), 005 (Amounts) and maybe some others for your Currency Code should be checked. Normally (? at least on our system ...) there are 14 "positions" (digits, decimal sign, thousand sign) which should be enough anyway.

After that, have a look at your report and forms that show overflows.
