I am not an expert in DEMs, but will try -

1. tgbrg4100m000. Select version and click Employees.
2. Select employee (arvind in your case), and click Roles by Employees.
3. Identify the role under which you want to place the session/activity.
4. tgbrg8140s000. Click Roles.
5. Click Where Used.
6. For the identified Role, Check only Project Model & Business Processe. Take the printout.
7. Identify the Process which needs to be modified, and use session tgbrg5100m000. Select the identified process, click editor.
8. Modify the DEM. Save and Exit.

Hope this helps.
