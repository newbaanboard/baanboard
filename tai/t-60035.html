If your schema definition for the indexes does NOT allow duplicates and you have duplicates in your data then you will have issues whether you load all rows then create indexes or load a row update indexes repeat until done. Most ERP indexes are unique and index1 always, but others (index2,3...) uniqueness or allow duplicates depends on needs. You may need to find duplicate keys and cleanup data.
