No, it is not possible. For changing of any Tools components, you need to unlock this, with a hidden session in Baan, and a password that is only known to the development team in Baan. Even I don't have it! :(

What you can do, is build your own Tools menu (in, for example, tc), including all the sub-menu's of the regular Tools menu. And then, in this same manner, create the sub-menu where the validation stuff is, and just exclude the sessions you want to hide. Since the user is new to Baan anyway, he will not know the difference.
