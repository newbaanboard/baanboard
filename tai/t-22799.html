It is not necessarily a bad thing to export your texts as plain text (as opposed to reference numbers). For one thing, it saves you from losing sleep over matching texts and reference numbers back up in your new database.

To read the texts back in the text manager, set the field type in tuxch0103m000 to alphanumeric, and set the field text number in tuxch0122m000 to "no".
