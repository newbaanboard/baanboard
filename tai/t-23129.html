It depends on the BaaN Version you are on.

In BaaN-4 series, the labels are store in ttadv140 (Labels) and ttadv130 (Language Dependent Descriptions).

In BaaN-5, 6 series, the labels are store in ttadv140 and also there is a session to compile labels (ttadv1243m000) and the compiled labels are located in $BSE/lib/labels. You have to compile lables when you have other languages than English due to the LTS feature.
