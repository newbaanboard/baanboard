Check out this thread (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?s=&threadid=6649&highlight=fax).

I think that any fax software out there can work with Baan. All e-faxes that I know either operate as a printer device, or will send anything that lands in a specified directory.
Both scenarios are easy to accomodate from Baan.

Emailing can be done directly from your Baan server, providing you are running mail services of course.
