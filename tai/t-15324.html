Developers are typically authorized to change to Package VRCs in ttadv0141m000. I do not think you can authorize anyone to change the Baan standard VRC. So changing to the standard VRC gets the error you mentioned - You have no authorization to change the standard. At our company we are only authorized for company created packages/VRC combinations. Not sure why you would want to change standard. I recommend copying to a development VRC and then attempting changes. Just my thought.

14th May 2004, 09:39
Yes, Mark is right; noone is supposed to be authorized to change the standard. You need to create a new vrc, derived from the standard, which will be your development vrc. For any change you want to make on the standard, you first copy the standard component to your package vrc, and then apply your modifications in your own development layer.
