In my experience, it is always a good idea to have a separate VRC structure/package combination for production and whatever else you have (call it TEST, DEVELOPMENT, whatever). This way, you can test not only changes you make to forms, reports, scripts, etc., but you can also test service packs, patches, etc. without worrying about your production environment crashing.

As far as a DEMO environment, I guess that would depend on your intended use. Some people use it for training. In that case, you could just make a separate demo company that utilizes the production package combination.

I hope that helped some. I'm sure others will weigh in on this, too.
