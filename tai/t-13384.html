Usually you want your status to be set to 'free'.

rc.startjobdm will start your job daemon. You're probably better off starting your jobs out of cron (or other schedulers), using the rc.startjob command.

First test the job execution by manually running rc.startjob JOBNAME.

For questions on how to set up jobs in Baan, refer to document AG1001 - Job Management, available from the Baan support site.
