Darren gave the only correct answer, because it varies between Baan versions and it depends on your settings.

Alternatively, you can simply look in your first free numbers table and see what types you have set up.

Typically it will be anything that requires a unique sequential number, like:

Sales Orders
Purchase Orders
Production Orders
Cycle Count Orders
Invoices
Business Partners
Quotations
Sales Orders
etc.
