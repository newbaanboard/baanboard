How complete is complete?

Here is a high level procedure from the top of my head

0. DB needs to have the target space (dbspace, table spce, or whatever it is called) created and accessible from Baan.
1. Add the new company's lines to the <DB>_storage file. Don't forget defaults for the company.
2. Add company in Maintain Companies (Convert RTDD)
3. Create the "Databases" inside Baan for audit and for non-audit.
4. Assign tables to DB without forgetting to setup mandatory audited tables to the audit DB (Convert to RTDD)
5. Specify shared tables if aplicable (Convert to RTDD)
6. Create tables.
7. Export DEM from source company
8. Import DEM into the target company.
9. Initialize parameters.
10. Complete company's parametrization (out of my area of expertise)
