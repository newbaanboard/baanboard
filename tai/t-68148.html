Without a development license you cannot create or modify scripts/libraries, tables and domains. For example reports you can create and modify without development license, even with a report script. You can also create and modify sessions and forms, the restriction is that the session should not have a script, or uses an already existing script; so, duplicating a session and modifying the form is possible.
