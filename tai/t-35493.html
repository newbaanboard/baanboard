Jordi, if you use Baan's multiline editor, you have all the Copy/Paste functions available...

Just in case you don't know how/what/where...:
In Textgorups (session tttxt1110m000) link 'Multi-Line editor' as 'external editor' to a textgroup, and link this text group to your user. Convert changes to RDD, close your Bshell, and start again. Then create a new text in any session, you should get Baan's MultiLine editor..
