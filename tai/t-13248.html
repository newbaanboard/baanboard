I have 2 solutions for you, but both tricky - and maybe not so goods.....

FIRST ONE (maybe the most complicated and with a lot of other problems):
Copy all the sw components to a language you don't use, let's say "3". Pay attention to the tt components, you have also to copy the RTDD of the language 2 on the language 3.
Configure in this langage the other date format you want to use.
Connect the users of this company to this language.

SECOND ONE (the most simple but I'm not sure...):
create a second copy of $BSE/lib/datecurr (in this place are stored the data related to the date format); Call this datecurr2 and the original datecurr1.
Create 2 differents unix scripts, let's say bshell1 and bshell2: both must have:
rm $BSE/lib/datecurr

mv $BSE/lib/datecurr1 $BSE/lib/datecurr #for bshell1
# or
mv $BSE/lib/datecurr2 $BSE/lib/datecurr #for bshell2
bshell

I'm not sure, but I think any instance of bshell loads datecurr ...

My personal advice - don't mess with it without do many tests....
