For errors > 1000, substract 1000 and you get the database error; here, Baan error 2555 translates into Oracle error 1555: "snapshot too old", a typical Oracle error when your rollback segments are too small or the session causing it is not doing enough commits.

I would check the session first and it too old get it updated (Baan increased the commits to avoid this), otherwise increase your rollback segment size (if using automatic undo, increase your undo_retention size), have it checked with your DBA.
