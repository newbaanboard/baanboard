2 options -

1. convert utc to date/time format and substract 7 hours from that date/time, and convert back to utc using -

utc.to.date() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_date_time_zones_utc_to_date) and date.to.utc() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_date_time_zones_date_to_utc)

2. 7 hours = 7 x 60 x 60 = 25200 seconds. Substract 25200 from the utc date/time you have.
