to give u a hint, there is a job deamon service which runs on Server like your Licence Service. You need to start that service.

in case ur on Unix, then look for rc.startjob i.e. located in ${bse}/etc

in windows its pretty more simple , look out for BaaN Service Manager on Win Server or look out for Service in Control Panel.

Once a service is started u can use baan session to maintain jobs and specify time intervals at which should automatically execute the job
