I have been involved in many BaanIV to ERPLN migrations with with different scenarios. A "full" migration is definitely one of the options to migrate almost all data(there are exceptions), including transactions and history data. In december last year we migrated Ferrari from their baanIVc2 environment to ERPLN FP7 with open production/sales/purchase orders.

Please note that when you want to change your setup/way of working in ERPLN, it is oftern advised to do a masterdata migration.
