Sales invoice is printing last line of invoice but this is happening randomly on production server for direct processing order. I did some reseacrh on this below is finding

1. All invoice line have different report process id.

2. Status of all invoice line (excluding last line) in printer queue (ttaad320) is premature and requested date is one day less than print date.

3. Last invoice line have status removed in printer queue and requested date is equal to print date.

This problem is not consistent.
