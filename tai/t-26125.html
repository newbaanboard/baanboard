The conflict does not have to be in the program script linked to the session you are performing. If let's say the problem occurs in tdsls4101s000 - sales order lines, it could be the conflict is in tdsls4101, or in the DAL tdsls401, or in dll tdslsdll1234 or tdslsdll4101 etc etc; could be in minimum 30 different scripts.

So if you give a bit more details about your problem, your environment and your customizations, and the errors given, and at what moment they are given, then I am sure we could help you further...
