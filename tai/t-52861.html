If you're using rexec to connect you most likely have to disable reverse DNS lookup. (option B being what JP said about putting your IP in the /etc/hosts but that will get cumbersome with too many users or DHCP)

http://docs.hp.com/en/B3921-90010/rexecd.1M.html

Would look something like (in your /etc/services)

exec stream tcp nowait root /usr/lbin/rexecd rexecd -s
