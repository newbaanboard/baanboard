I Understood you are putting everything (Baan, DB and Log) into the same physical discs (RAID5).

The general recomendation for these kind of systems (not only Baan), is to put the at least the redo logs into a separate physical space. Of course, some DB's are more sensitive to this than others.
My experience is that for Oracle this is essencial. For MSSQL you can also notice a performance improvement.
