Check your parameters in the distribution parameters. You can define i.e. A = 12 weeks / three times a year, B = 26 weeks / twice a year and C = 51 weeks / once a year. When new items comes in stores, they have no cycle count date, so, it will show up next time you generate a cycle counting order for the warehouse / location where it is stored. Then after x weeks will come up again, depending your parameters(A,B&C).
