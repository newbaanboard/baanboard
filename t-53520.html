If there are quality issues with the raw material then the same should get rejected while receiving (assuming they are purchased items). However if at a later stage the raw material is found defective then there is no way to track the same. The only option is to transfer the defective items to a separate warehouse (titled as Rejected Warehouse). But the same will not come in supplier vendor rating.
