Our product, Logistics Management Solution (LMS) is the result of our partnership with Baan to co-develop enhancements and new functionality necessary to provide supply chain management features to deliver an industry leading transportation system. Additionally, we are active in procurement, manufacturing and light assembly using the baseline BaanERP system. This project requires dedicated, technical, adaptable individuals to assist with the client implementations, conversions and system improvements. You will be helping to lead a core team of similar minded developers responsible for the design, development and implementation of LMS/Baan projects for new and/or existing clients.

This is for a Sr. System Engineer position working in the LMS IT team in the Portland, OR office. A Sr. Systems Engineer reports to a Development Manager and is responsible for understanding business requirements as well as developing and implementing solutions that fit nicely into the overall offering and meet customer expectations. The primary functions of this job include participating in interpreting business needs into technical requirements, collaborating with other functional experts to design solutions, resolve business critical issues, and providing extensive support to our end customers. Additionally this position requires the ability to multi-task and to complete projects under tight timelines. Strong organizational skills are a must. The ideal candidate will have strong knowledge of business processes in the area of Warehousing, Freight Management, Order Management, Finance or Warehousing or a strong transportation background
