These Automotive Functionality features have been included in the initial product release of ERP LN.
- Purchase Schedules
- Sales Schedules (Not supplier release management functionality  to be developed)
- Sales Contracts (Raw and Fab authorizations)
- Self Billing
- Lean Manufacturing (Production and Suppliers)
- Line Assembly Control
- in Warehouse management - Packaging Support for Containers/Pallets, Dynamic Cross Docking, Serialized items, Effectively Units, Line Supply (TPOP / Kanban)
- Lean Dispatching
- General EDI/ASN support
- Vendor Managed Inventory
- Freight Management

Some features was add in FP1 & FP2. See Release Notes.

SSA currently reviewing the functionality we need in ERP LN to meet the requirements of all our automotive customers  OEMs, Suppliers and Aftermarket.
