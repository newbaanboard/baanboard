Hi,
I have to say that i am no Finance user and the reply was the result of a passing interest. What i could tell you is that this is somewhat the simplest way of taking care of your problem. Your customer account will be hit properly with the amount you invoice and there is no need for any outbounding of inventory and no involvement of any distribution session. Once you conduct the tests you will find out for yourself what i stated above...
Steps are
=> Maintain Sales Invoice Header {tfacr4100m000}
=> Maintain Sales Invoice Lines {tfacr4101s000}
=> Print Sales Invoices {tfacr4410m000}
=> Post Sales Invoices {tfacr4220m000}

Hope this is handy..
