In Baan 4 localisations are available for almost any European Country taking the legal situations into account.

But only B40c4 is Euro comliant. Anyway if you want to implement new software you would certainly not buy B40c4.

And this takes us to ERP. In ERP all localisations are included.
But still there are a few things unsolved e. g. Grinya.

The FAM module is much better than the FAS in Baan 4 but there is still room for improvement.

If you get in tuch with the Sales staff remember that they will tell you anything to get your signature. (promise functionalities)

This does not mean that Baan is a bad software. If you want to implement it you must know what you want. Does the Baan Software offer the functionalities that your company needs?

You need a good consultant that helps you to analyze your needs. With a propper planning you won't get in trouble.

And don't believe that the implementation can be done "in no time". There are some simulation phases necessary!!!
