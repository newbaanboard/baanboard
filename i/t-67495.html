ERP LN Finance Support Analyst


The candidate should have experience in all ERPLN Finance Modules (General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivables, Cash Management, Taxation and Fixed Assets) along with good accounting knowledge.
Minimum 5 years of working experience in ERPLN Finance Application Support and/or Implementation is required.
Should have good coordination and communication skills to interact with members in IT and Business teams spread across the globe.
Experience in identifying bugs, designing a new custom solution, testing, documentation and training is required.
Decent functional knowledge in ERPLN Logistics and Manufacturing modules is required.
Some technical knowledge of ERPLN and/or Baan Tools will be a plus.
This position requires strong analytical and problem solving skills at times need to work on multiple support issues concurrently.
The position is based in India and supporting a client in USA as per US EST (Eastern time zone).
