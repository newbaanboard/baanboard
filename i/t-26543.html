Hi Sriyanthaw,

sorry for the late reply but we have just gone live and had a busy week.

Regarding your question, you don't have to attach a supplier to a warehouse. We use a normal warehouse and call it after the subcontractor. Every subcontractor got his own warehouse. Then we use replenishment orders and print a delivery note with it. Goods being shipped to our supplier independently from our call-offs (because he has to keep the stocks on our behalf). The production / subcontracting order informs the supplier about our demand and the material is backflushed from the suppliers' warehouse.
We automated the steps with the replenishment order using barcode scanners so that we don't need to key in replenishment orders, deliver them and print them. Instead a delivery note is being printed after we scanned all items.
