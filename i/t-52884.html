Dear Kapil,
This is with reference to ERP LN 6.1 FP2 with India Localisation, with issue in SubContracting.
Please note,
# Sending "one form/ itemcode" ( XXX ) of material to SubContractor for working, where form changes, and then recieving back "in DIFFERENT FORM/ different itemcode" ( YYY ) of material back, is the process involved in the case.
# This type of transaction involves Inventory transactions in Warehousing. This is supported with the help of BOM & Production Order, where the itemcode & Inventory changes when you recieve the material in different form back.
# Hence your present requirement is not getting support from the ERP system.
# This type of transaction out of Production Order route is possible only if the form & Itemcode is not changed during the process, and the same material is again recieved back. Just like in case of Heat Treatment.
# In that case the 57F4 Challans are prepared without reference of Production Order. where you maintain the Outgoing & Incoming material same & after transaction completion, the 57F4 is closed.
Hope you understand the case.
