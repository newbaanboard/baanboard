Ok, in ERP LN 6.1 FP2,
Use tcibd9138m00l to note down the ASV values which will be eventually used in Orders.
Here, the assessable value ( ASV ) is defined for an item and a business partner. The value is specific to "Duty/ Tax Type : sales tax, service tax, customs and excise duty" and is defined for orders originating from "sales, purchase, transfer (manual) and production". The effective & Expiry date can be set here as well.
The remaining things are explained by Krunal & Vinod.
