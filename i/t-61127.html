Self billing is typically used if you have:
▪ A contract with the supplier for the price of the goods you are buying.
▪ An agreement with the supplier that you will pay for the goods without receiving an invoice for
them.
The supplier does not need to send an invoice. Instead, you use the self-billing procedure to automatically
generate invoices for these goods.
