Hi,

BAAN People (or the intended HR solution) will not be available before Gemini.
So, at least not before September 2003.

We have tested the HR solution from DST and noticed that there were many issues with the English translations and that the product is not yet ready for the US market from a legal perspective.
It may be okay for the European market.

In any case: when you build an integration with a 3rd part app make sure you will meet all local legal requirements. These will be very different per country.

Regards,
