Embed ur code in web pages that access BOI's that reside in baan server. BOIs can help u retrieve, update or insert records, in baan table through the application layer of baan.

BCK(Baan Connect Kit) based BOI is free of cost from Baan. I think Baan, India still gives them to customers. I do not know abt SriLanka.

Another mature product from Baan stable is OpenWorld. Though OpenWorld use BOI, the architecture enables better reliability than BCK based BOI.
