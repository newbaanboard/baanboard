But lets see in ERP LN 6.1 FP2, it says that,
1) If you define a purchase office as the enterprise unit's default purchase office in the Departments (tcemm1124m000) session, SSA ERP uses the enterprise unit's default purchase office for automatically generated purchase orders.
2) The next option for the system is,
The purchase Office maintained in the Buy From Business Partner ( Supplier ), that SSA ERP uses by default for purchases from the buy-from business partner.
Hence your findings seems to be correct.
