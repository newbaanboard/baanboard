Hello Indian,

once you uncheck the SFC parameter you mention the receipt posting will be done based on FTP value. The reason why this should be your choice is because cost can occur at at later stage (e.g. Subcontracting Invoices) thus the assumption is that FTP costs are closer to reality then MAUC at the time of receipt. MAUC will be adjusted accordingly as long as the item is still on stock.
