Hi crmadmin,

I have see a short presentation at Informum Germany about Epiphany.
All what we can see was only PowerPoint, none system itself :-(

Epiphany itself looks like now as sales plus (with funnel, opportunities and so on). But - a lot of restrictions: you can install it with or without Integration to ERP LN; but this decision you have to do first if you install. You can't reverse this decision. If you work with integration, than all offers entered in Epiphany but saved at ERP LN-tables (offer-tables) direct.
Other point is - Epiphany have a MS Exchange-Integration but not for mails, or - not for mails from individual inboxes, only for inboxes like "info" or "exposition" (means collection).

You can choose between two Epiphany suites: Enterprise and Business suite.
But - in both cases you install the Enterprise suite. Business suite is allways a limitation with the licencekey. That meanse you can use it until 100 users and you havn't some functions, that's all. If you need more users or this functions than you pay more money and get a full licence key, none installations are required.
That makes a lot of additional expenditure during implementation phase. You can't say - we implement the small Epiphany solution. It's nothing other than the Enterprise Edition.

I think, at the moment Epiphany is a big building site, a prototyp. No company with B2B-connection ca realy work with this system, not today and note realy at the future. To mutch limitations at this system.


We work further more with Sales Plus 4.3. In Germany we have from a consultant company a new integration to ERP LN, and this works very good.
A lot of customization are done, at the IT-Department we have employees with VisualBasic experience and we maintain the system and develop further with new functions. We don't use local database because we wouldn't have sync-problems. All sales-people can login remote.
Our consultant have test it- SalesPlus can also work with Vista. Office 2007 is for our company out of scope next 2-3 years. We hope, that we can work with this system next years and that we than look which problems we have, what functions we need and if we should change to other system.
