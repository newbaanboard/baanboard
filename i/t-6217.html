I learned quite a bit about BOIs, BCBE, BCKit, etc while still at Baan. No fair huh!!! We were involved in writing an interface between Baan IV and BaanSCS Scheduler & Baan IV and BaanSCS Planner. Both Scheduler and Planner are part of Baan's Advance Planning and Scheduling (APS) suite of products.

The BCBE (Baan Connect Basic Edition) and BCKit are the predecessors to Open World. We later moved to Open World (that we know now) as we developed interfaces for those APS applications and BaanERP.

I left Baan two years ago and have been working with various versions of Open World ever since. Unfortunately, there's no experience like hands-on experience. Give e-Emphasis a try. They came to our organization a while back and trained a handful of people. Though I was not in the training, I didn't hear any complaints :)
