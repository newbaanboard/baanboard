Dear jmathew

During the migration process there is a mapping between customer/supplieres and the new business partners.

Old Customers: 100, 101, 102, ...
Old Supplieres: 100, 101, 102, ...

In the mapping definition you could define how be a new businesspartner:
cust --> add a "C" before the old number
supp --> add a "S" before the old number

After migration:
Business partners: C100, C101, C102, S100, S101, S102, ...

I think you can also do some calculation during the mapping, like "Business partners for supp: add 100 to the old one, business partners for the cust add 300 the old one", etc.
