Hi,

Let say your fiscal period is from Jan - Dec. End date must be Dec.
Your reporting period 1 can be from Jan - Mar, period 2 from Apr - Jun, etc.

But if you are referring to reporting period end in Nov, while the fiscal period end in Dec then this is not possible.
