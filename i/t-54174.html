Hi Umesh,

Good question, Actually In MSO - Maintainance Sales Order, you have part receipt, part maintenance and part delivery functionality. Once you create MSO with Part receipt line, it will creat a inbound line for receipt from customer or subcontracter. At this time the status will be inprogress. So If the customer sends u some material and u need to do some work on that you can take receipt through MSO - part receipt. Once you receive the material you can complete the part receipt line, at the same time sys will update the invoicing information with cost.

Once you receive the material you can create the MSO with part maint line. Which inturn create work order, through which u can book your resources. Once you complete the work order and sends the item back to warehouse, sys will update the MSO line with the cost occured in WO. Once you complete the MSO, it will update the invoice information as well.

Now u can create the line to deliver the serviced item to the customer through MSO - part delivery, it will create an outbound line and also update the invoice information.

MSO is attached with Coverage line and through Coverage line you can see MSO invoice line information and update it as well. Once you confirm the amount you can make MSO as costed. Now you can create an invoice through central invoicing. In Invoice you will have three lines for receiving, maintenance and delivery with the cost occured in MSO.

I dont know much about indian localization as I dont use it I am in sydney, you can do one cycle in demo and let me know if you face any problem.

Hope this will help you...
