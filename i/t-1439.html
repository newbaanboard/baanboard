Darren,

Your request about BCK keeps popping up. The reason that we are not shipping
BCK is because we are phasing it out. Official announcements are in
preparation. The main reason for phasing out the product is that it serves a
purpose that is no longer valid in the way BCK is dealing with it.

This means we are only going to allow the use of BCK if the dependency is
known or if there is a custom situation that cannot be dealt with another
product from the iBaan suite.

BCK has no official successor. What we find is that in most cases the iBaan
OpenWorld Suite will be able to cope with it.

Please let me know if you would like to know more about this or OpenWorld.
