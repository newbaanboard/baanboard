Baan ERP is a very minor player in the market now, not only in China, but also in the worldwide market. ERP Ln only get about 100 new customers in the whole world after it launched in 2004.

Most Baan implementations in China are actually rollout of the system from foreign companies. After the leaving of Acer and HTC, there is almost no big Chinese manufacturing companies using Baan. The local sales force is very weak and the sales guys like to put their attention on the install base to cross-sell other applications.
