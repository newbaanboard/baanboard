Dear All,

Please note that the consumables are to be consumed in Production, and hence those should be Production Issued to the respective WorkCentres.

By using the 'Warehouse Order' with Origin as 'SFC Production - Manual' & 'Transaction Type-ISSUE', the material is transfered from 'Warehouse' to 'WorkCentre'.

By this procedure, the purpose is solved. Regards.
