Not to be a fusspot, but if anybody needs questionnaires designed for a small fee to keep food on my table while I wait for a miracle or another job...

Questionnaires are actually a lot more difficult to design than people think, for example, this one had overlapping and non-exhaustive categories. They also were sensitive to currency and location.
If you wanted to include Indian respondants and UK, you need to know if you want absolute currency or buying power scales.

For example, if you wanted to know if people were getting "good salaries" then you need an independant conversion factor, like the price of a Big Mac as a basis of comparison, or you need to factor cost of living numbers.

I know the poll was more for fun than serious, but many serious decisions are taken on the basis of invalid techniques.
