Dear Nenene,

The concept of Service Customer Owned Warehouse comes only for the "Maintenence Sales Orders" session of service package.

When you get the already sold item from the customer into your premises for any repair, then that stock is kept in the above said warehouse type. The reason being, inventory valuation session exludes all the stock present in this type of warehouse.

So once you perform all the operation to repair, you will send back from this warehouse to teh customer.
This is the functionality of Service Customer Owned Warehouse.
