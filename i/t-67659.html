Hi,

Unfortunately, Way2SMS doesn't support all the service providers in India also, it doesn't support new mobile number series. So, its better to buy SMS from a vendor.

Business SMS (http://www.businesssms.co.in/) provides you cheap bulk SMS's with their own webservice which you can invoke through LN usine soap.* functions.

This thread (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=64167) shows a way to use Soap Based webservice.
