I have not looked at infor cloud, but a few years ago I got to go to OAUG and every seminar and topic touched on oracle cloud. At that time you would still need some system admin expertise - if nothing else for user type issues and some setups they still need to do. Yeah - you could outsource everything to them, but I do not see how it would save us any money. I would assume infor cloud is like that, but again never really looked into it. The thing that stands out most in my mind is how the user community reacted at the conference - it seems like most of them really liked the idea. And there were some really good questions around security for company sensitive data. I know a couple of session I would not have wanted to be the Oracle rep answering the questions. I am not sure we will ever move anything to the cloud - but who knows I could be wrong. My 2 cents worth of opinions for the day.
