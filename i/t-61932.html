In addition to your first question: you should also set up reconciliation elements for Inventory 1 in tcfin0120m000 for a proper comparison of reconciliation data with your inventory data.
Possible elements could be Warehouse (001500000000) and/or Item (001400000000) and/or Item Valuation Group (001421000000).
