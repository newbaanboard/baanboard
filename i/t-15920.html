The cess 2% (Education cess) in the latest budget is applicable to all the taxes of Govt Of India. In order to accomodate this in your Excise Invoices, you have to correct the Tarrif rate from 16% to 16.32% and show the 2% amount in a separe column. I hope this will solve the problem.
