Hi friends,

First decide whether you need Functional or Technical Training.

For Functional you need some real time experience with an organisation with/without ERP.
You can get some functional documents for learning flow.
You may get functional training from any institute, but real time experience is a must for it.

For Technical training, if you are not working, then you may not require Baan technical expertise.
If you are working, you should have experience in SQL Scripting.
You can learn by on-job experience or from your seniors.

If you feel to have a standard Baan Technical training. You can get the training from Baan Support i.e. Infor Global Solutions.

You may also refer below link for other details:
http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=57376&highlight=Baan+Training

regards,
