Getting P&L through FST is definitely possible . In fact , you can prepare any kind of report in FST to get ledger and dimension balances the way u wish.

FST layouts allows you to write ldeger/dimension codes ,descriptions and columns where figures calculated from ledger/dimension balances can come in the format specified by u.Generating FST layout a report is created in Baan reports.

U can not get horizontal P&L reporting format. All u can get is vertical format.

Technically you don't need to change chart of accounts for that matter. However if there is not good discipline in coding (wrt sorting / grouping) chart of accounts then creating / maintaing FST statement may take more time. Because u need to link ledger accounts manually. In such a case it's better that chart of accounts coding is better recoded (wrt sorting/grouping ) keeping in view future possible changes.
