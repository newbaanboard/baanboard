Baan, an enterprise application provider for industrial enterprises, has been listed in the 'Visionary' quadrant for organization in the March 2003 Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) Magic Quadrant study prepared by Gartner, a leading technology research and advisory firm. According to Gartner, visionaries are vendors that understand where the market is going or have a vision of changing the market rules, but can still improve in terms of optimizing service delivery (as defined in Defining the PLM Magic Quadrant by Criteria and Use).
