I think you may need to ask Infor to create a process extension for printing remittance letters (I don't remember one existing).
We're migration to LN 10.7 and I have already asked Infor to create process extension for printing purchase orders and purchase invoices (well, technically our Infor Partner did). It took some weeks, but they delivered a solution containing those extensions.

It may be worth checking Infor KB 2003722 "Infor LN - Public Interfaces and Process Extensions". There you'll find documentation on process extensions and Information on how to request new ones.
