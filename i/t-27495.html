You should not only think about migration tools but take also another approach into account.

LN offers offers many new features. If you want to use them you also have to set up your data in another way. I you just ask SSA to start their migration tool much functionality will get lost.

First you should have a look on LN and then you can decide by your own which approach will be the best and cheapest for your company.

Regarding the migration tool of SSA I can say thant in Germany there is also a consulting company which has developed their own migration tool. They also say that the SSA tool is not so good and their tool is much better. My opinion about that subject is that other companies than SSA would not develop a new tool if the tool of SSA was good. I still hope that I am not right or that SSA improved their tool in the last time.
