9th October 2008, 10:37
Thanks for all your suggestions, I found the solutions so sharing it with all of ya, it's because of a parameter settings, i.e in SFC parameter- costing tab , if the receipt for actual cost in the finance is checked, then it will not post the inventory immediately after completion of last operation, and no inbound line is created in the warehousing.

I hope the above solution is helpful, and I suggest others to check with the altering the parameter and check the simulation.
