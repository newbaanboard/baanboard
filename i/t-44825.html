Hi,
Please note Baan calculates & posts depreciation for each fiscal period.

In the first period when the asset is capitalised, the depreciation calculation is determined based on the field "Start of Depreciation" in session "Maintain Depreciation Master Data" or "Maintain Group Dereciation Data" or "Maintain Fixed Asset Depreciation Data".

Hope this resolves your query.
