Dear Kapil,
Please note that,
1) Suppose Item M ( Qty 1 ) is made up of Item X ( Qty 2 ) + Item Y ( Qty 4 ) + Item Z ( Qty 1 ). That is the BOM you have.
2) You are issuing Item X, Y & Z to your SubContractor and then geting back Item M from him as finished processed Item.
Hope this is what you are doing. Right ?
3) Here please note that you have a BOM for above & then to manufacture it you must place a Production Order, wherein Item M is manufactured from these Three Different items.
4) Hence you will be running that Production Order, and then issuing those 3 above items against that Production Order & SubContracting 57F4 to that SubContractor.
5) That 57F4 will be having all those 3 Items on it as outgoing items from you as issued to that subcontractor. And then that 57F4 will be having Item M as the Incoming item from Subcontractor to you.
So there will not be any problem of handling those items & closing of that 57F4 Copy.
The real problem is,
How come 3 different items ( X, Y & Z ) are issued by you to a single Subcontractor on Three different 57F4 challans, if those are issued against a single Production order, which will be used to upload or recieve the final Item M.
Please note that in Subcontracting 57F4, whatever you are sending out, there should be an item to be recieved back. Hence Production Order supports very well to this arrangement.
You check up what is the Production Order SFC set up used by the Production People to carry out this transaction & then you will get the real route cause, for asking this set up.
OK.
