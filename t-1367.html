If you need to transfer goods from one company to another there are a few ways to do it. If you transfer goods, and you do not want to use purchase-sale, it is mandatory in my opinion, that those items have same standard price in both companies' item master data. And also there is another question to study, financial integration and, stock's financial values of this goods.

I mean, if item A in company 100 has price x
item A in company 120 has price y

When you move 1 Unit of A from 100 to 120, what are you going to do with x-y difference?

Also, there is another question: This transfer is a frequent movement or you want to move all stock from one company to the other?

If x=y use Inventory adjustments. If x<>y, I think you must use purchase-sale, but I need more details about your data.

Hope this helps.
