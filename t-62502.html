Well if you are lucky a simple stpapi.handle.subproc will work. So you would put all the fields just like a normal insert, but add a stpapi.handle.subproc right before the insert. Hopefully this would give you control of the subsession that pops up. Then you could complete it and end the subsession normally. Just keep in mind this does not always work this way - sometimes the subsessions do not start or they start and you can not control them. I have had to work around these issues and the solution can hard to find at times. Since we do not use these session I do not know a work around.
