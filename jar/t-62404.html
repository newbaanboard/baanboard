shanghai finance consultant

Senior consultant
Job responsibility:
* Responsible of financial analysis based on good understanding of customer's requirements
* Definition of Financial and Management accounting model suitable for both external and internal financial reporting and analysis
* Responsible to analyze day to day accounting, month-end closure, ensuring the accuracy of the monthly financial statements, related journal entries, accruals, cash reconciliation
* Support users on the preparation of financial statements and periodic foreign exchange declaration
* Responsible to analyze and support on periodic income tax, business tax and IIT tax declaration
* Responsible to analyze government statistical data declaration, salary payroll and personal payment and expense report management
* Support users on the cooperation with tax bureau and auditors
* Responsible to set up and the implementation of Infor ERP LN Financial modules
* Preparation of User manual based on project implementation and customer financial procedure
* Cooperation with Logistics/Manufacturing implementation team to integrate logistics data into the financial scheme
Requirements:
* Degree in Accounting or related and 3+ years of financial working experience related to financial and accounting management
* Strong know how of standard business practices in the areas of accounting and finance
* Deep knowledge of information systems (Infor ERP Ln) and finance environment, Chinese GAAP
