Opportunity for a Global Business Solutions Analyst to join this successful organisation.

You will be responsible for assisting with the continued development of the organisations global ERP implementation to meet global business requirements. Responsibilities will include gap analysis, requirements analysis, global impact analysis, and project management of various projects as required.

The successful applicant will have a solid background within ERP and specifically with Manufacturing, Supply Chain and Distribution modules. Experience of Baan V ERP software would be ideal, however, experience with other ERP software solutions would also be relevant. The organisation have a global Baan solution so you will need the ability to adapt knowledge to a new environment. A degree educated background would be ideal.

The role will be working closing with colleagues across the globe, liaising with business and technical managers, influencing colleagues and managing implementation projects. So the role will require an individual with first class communication skills and an ability to liaise effectively with and take a lead role with colleagues across the globe.

The role will be based out of the Hampshire offices but due to the nature of the role will require global travel to support regional and global activities.

The role presents an interesting and challenging opportunity within a global organisation.
