BaaN IV Project Manager
 As the Project Manager you must be familiar with the functional areas: Distribution, Logistics, Manufacturing,Technical.
Responsibilities include:
Responsibility for high-visibility Baan IV project for re-testing and redeployment
Accountable for timely project completion, optimum resource utilization and achievement of client expectations.
Coordinates System Integrators during the project implementation ad post go–live support phases.
Supports team in order to get the best strategy for maximum results.
The ideal candidate will have:
PMI Certification
Ability to multi-task and manage issues to resolution with little guidance.
The qualified candidate will have:
Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field
Minimum 5 years’ experience in BaaN IV Project Management, 10 years overall with BaaN IV and Baan V included.
Minimum 3 years’ experience in coverage complex and global projects throughout all phases of the project life
Strong interpersonal and communication skills
Demonstrated leadership skills
No H1B Sponsorshipor VISA sponsorship available.
