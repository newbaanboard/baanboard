Sr. ERP Analyst

Job Description
e-Emphasys Technologies consulting practice has an immediate requirement for seasoned ERP techno-functional consultant.
The role requires daily client interactions, liaising with support/development teams, technical support and maintenance of corporate wide ERP systems for e-Emphasys Customers.  The responsibilities include design, development, customization, technical implementation and maintenance of Baan programs; setting-up, running and monitoring Baan jobs; and developing, supporting interfaces between Baan and other external applications. We are looking for a candidate with broad based experience in ERP and other back office transaction systems.
 
The role requires candidate to be US citizen. The position is based at Philadelphia with no travel.
