A Toledo, Ohio based company is seeking a Senior Application Developer. Baan IV experience is a must. Salary $60K-$68K depending on experience. Relocation fees available. Must have green card or U.S. citizenship. No sponsorship is available.

Summary of Position
This position is responsible for assisting the Application Development Manager in planning, organizing, directing, and reviewing the ongoing maintenance and support of all corporate and shared applications.

Nature and Scope
This position reports to the Application Development Manager.
The incumbent is responsible for assisting the Application Development Manager in the ongoing maintenance and support of corporate systems including general ledger, AP/AR, payroll/HRIS, and Fixed Asset, and shared applications including BaaN ERP, as well as data warehousing, Notes application development and Web application development. This responsibility includes assisting in setting priorities and resource allocation for changes to these applications and for the ongoing identification of requirements for modifying and enhancing these applications over time.
Primary internal contacts of the incumbent include functional managers and staff of areas that are primary users of corporate systems, such as general ledger, and shared applications. Primary external contacts include current and prospective IT vendors and service providers.
