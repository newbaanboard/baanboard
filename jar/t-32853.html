This position provides senior level, expert business consultative services as part of the OneNeck customer team supporting Baan IVc4 finance users for a manufacturing company. This position will be initially located in Kansas City, MO and may require some travel.

OneNeck provides a comprehensive flexible suite of ERP hosting solutions designed specifically to help mid-market companies improve ERP system performance. We help our customers leverage their enterprise applications to deliver measurable business benefits. OneNeck enables customers to focus on strategic IT initiatives resulting in a greater return on their IT investment.

SKILLS NECESSARY:
• Demonstrates a working knowledge, skill, ability and aptitude for any Tier 1 ERP Finance Application Support.
• Demonstrates a working knowledge of Generally Accepted Accounting Principles.
• Design and document solutions for moderate to complex business requirements in accordance with established standards and methodologies.
• Use analytical skills to quickly identify resolutions to incidents and assist in overall problem resolution.
• Maintains professional demeanor and communications with expert customer service and support.
• Provides excellent verbal and written communications with our customers, management, and peers.
• Adds value and contributes to customer/project initiatives by understanding and anticipating business requirements of the customer.
• Serves as a knowledge resource for other consultants in their area of expertise.
• Responsible for customers' satisfaction with his/her deliverables.
• Develops a thorough understanding of and adheres to OneNeck delivery processes and the processes of the Customer(s) he/she works on.
• Leads technical and business related teams on project work under the direction of the Manager.

EDUCATION:
• Four-year degree or equivalent in a related discipline (e.g., information technology, computer science, business, Finance).

YEARS OF RELATED EXPERIENCE:
• Minimum 3-5 years Financial Industry experience in Application Support.
• Minimum 3-5 years experience with Finance in an ERP implementation and/or support role
