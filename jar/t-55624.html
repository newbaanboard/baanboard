Baan Tools Developer/Analyst

An international software developer, specializing in data collection for manufacturers, seeks a Baan Tools Developer/Analyst to join a growing team.   The candidate must have a minimum of 3 years experience and poses functional knowledge of Baan IV, Baan ERP or ERPLN applications, tools and systems design.  In addition, the understanding of the manufacturing environment including distribution and warehousing is required.  As the candidate will be working in a team setting and communicating regularly with customers, strong interpersonal, planning, organization, and analytical skills are also required.
Preferred experience:
•    Open systems/platforms: Java, Unix/Linux.
•    Database: Oracle, SQL.
•    Automated Data Collection:  function servers, development with BOI library objects, Open World, and developing business objects.
•    Must be a US citizen, have a work permit or H1B.  Must be authorized to work in the US.
