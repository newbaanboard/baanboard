Senior LN Expert in Logistics

Cargotec, the leading global supplier of container & load handling equipment and services, has an immediate opening for a Senior LN Expert in Logistics.  Position can be based in Ottawa, KS or home office environment. Cargotec’s brands, Hiab, Kalmar, Bromma and MacGREGOR, are international market leaders in their fields and their solutions are used on land and at sea – wherever cargo is on the move. Cargotec’s Information Management (IM) department is looking for a Senior LN Expert in Logistics to join their team. The role of this specific position is to manage the Multi Assembly Unit Global MAU business systems with focus on ERP LN and Logistics.  Your main responsibilities are proposing, implementing and supporting multi-site business process enhancements and managing projects related to the Logistics area with a good understanding of Finance integrations.  Tasks also include planning, maintaining and enhancing the usage of the ERP system across all functional areas, from Logistics to Finance, in order to meet the business requirements.  The Senior LN Expert will also respond and manage escalated help desk issues.
