SR. / BUSINESS ANALYSTS AND FUNCTIONAL EXPERTS IN THE AREAS OF BAAN FINANCE, MANUFACTURING, DISTRIBUTION AND SERVICE

WE CURRENTLY HAVE EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES FOR SR. / BUSINESS ANALYSTS AND FUNCTIONAL EXPERTS IN THE AREAS OF BAAN FINANCE, MANUFACTURING, DISTRIBUTION AND SERVICE TO WORK ON PROJECTS IN SEVERAL AREAS AROUND THE USA. The Consultant will work as key ERP team members in configuring business solutions, test business models, analyze & resolve issues, and successfully implement ERP Ln Finance, Manufacturing and Distribution modules. Candidates having exposure to dealership business environment will gain added advantage. Technical experts applying for these positions will work in the areas of design, development, testing and QA in ERP Ln software. Candidates with experience in ERP Ln / Baan 5.0c application are preferred.   THOSE WILLING TO WORK ON US ASSIGNMENTS SHOULD HAVE US CITIZENSHIP, GREEN CARD OR A VALID H1B VISA. 
 The candidates must possess good planning & organizational skills and excellent communication skills. They must be able to demonstrate ability to work as part of a team, however need to work independently with good leadership abilities.
