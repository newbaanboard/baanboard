Position: Programmer Analyst
Location: Fergus, Ontario Canada
Duration: 6 month contract

GSW Water Heating is searching for a Programmer Analyst to join our IT team, on a 6-month contract.

Candidates should have a Degree or Diploma in Management Information Systems or Computer Programming (or practical equivalent experience); 4 years experience supporting ERP systems; and a minimum 2 years experience developing and maintaining BaaN IV applications. Solid knowledge of BaaN IV Manufacturing & Distribution is a prerequisite. Previous experience working with Oracle databases or with web applications (ASP) would be considered a significant asset.

The position will require day-to-day interaction with our sales, operations, and planning staff. As such, you must have effective interpersonal communication skills along with the ability to present ideas in user-friendly language. Keen attention to detail, along with strong analytical and problem solving skills are also required.

If you are looking for a challenging IT opportunity in a fast paced manufacturing environment please submit your resume to: recruitment (at) gsw-wh.com no later than 30-November-2007.

Candidates MUST be legally able to work in the province of Ontario. Sponsorship requests will NOT be considered.
