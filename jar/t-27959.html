These applications involve design issues well beyond those normally addressed by generic catalog products. In recent years, we have increased our experience in the following areas: control of antenna RCS, antennas for the detection of LRCS targets, and high-resolution antennas. Please visit our website for more information: www.L-3com.com/randtron

Participates in the analysis, design, evaluation, modification, testing and implementation of the Baan ERP System (Baan 4c4/AND1) across functional areas. Develops test plans and protocols for evaluation of system performance. Requires a college degree and/or a minimum of five to seven years of related Baan ERP experience. Demonstrated analytic, written, and oral communications skills required.
