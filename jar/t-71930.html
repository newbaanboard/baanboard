Opening for Infor ERP LN Tools/Application Developer - USA/Canada


Do you have what it takes to be a successful Infor ERP LN Tools/Application Developer?
Do you have functional experience to take on requirements and design solutions with minimal guidance?
Do you strive to create quality solutions that will work with little maintenance afterwards?
Are you keeping an eye on the bigger picture, so that the impact of your program logic on other areas is handled?
Do you pay strong attention to detail? If your answers are yes, we need you in our team!

The RPM Performance Coatings Group of companies combines five of the world’s leading corrosion protection companies: Stonhard, Fibergrate Composite Structures, Carboline, Flowcrete and Universal Coatings.  You will be responsible for requirements analysis, development, and support of our Infor ERP LN environments.  Our ideal candidates will have a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or a related field with at least 5 years experience of Infor ERP LN development.

Our development team is primarily located in the offices in Maple Shade, NJ, USA, & Whitby/Toronto, ON, Canada.  Working remotely is a possibility, as long as it is in the same time zone to support full interaction with the team.
