BaanIV / Oracle consultant needed for Belgium

Belgium based company ‘Software Techniek’ is hiring a BaanIV/Oracle consultant to assist and support Belgium based Baan/Oracle customers. The ideal candidate must have
- a good functional and technical knowledge of BaanIV distribution and manufacturing.
- good knowledge of Baan programming (Baan.4GL).
 - knowledge of Oracle SQL and Oracle database administration.
- A strong interest in Oracle development tools (APEX).
You need to be a Belgian resident to apply for this job.
You need to speak fluently French, English and Dutch.
This is a permanent or freelance position, no contracting from outside Belgium.
