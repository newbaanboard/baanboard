Business Information Systems Manager

Reporting to the Director, Information Technologies, the Business Information Systems Manager provides the leadership and direction for implementing business information systems, related architectural projects and enhancements, while working through the prioritization process with the appropriate business groups. 
He/she is responsible for management and support of information/data systems including Baan ERP, Human Resources, Quality TIP/QA, Navcomm Great Plains and various complementary systems. She/he is tasked with being the primary focal point within the enterprise for the design, development and implementation of business systems.  Projects may include standardizing multi-site Baan system; investigating the utility of newly released functionality from vendors; and helping to determine business systems strategy.
