Immediate requirement for Functional Team Leads with Baan V implementation experience.

Responsibilities include:

- Functional/Team lead for Baan implementation project
- Defining business processes and mapping to Baan software
- Gap Analysis and recommendations for business process
improvements
- Provide training to core team members on Core Baan
functionality
- Assist in procedure development and business process testing
- Working with key departmental users in resolving process

members
