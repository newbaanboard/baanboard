Solution Architects

Looking for experienced ERPLN specialist to work as solution architects. There are openings in the area of logistics and finance.
Major job responsibilities are as below:

Work as part of the Global team to understand requirements, propose solutions, develop design, and write product specifications.
Co-ordinate design, development and deployment of solutions across different environments and business units.
Work with the implementation team to understand line of business specific requirements and suggest solutions.
Co-ordinate with Global data and process standards team.
Evaluate and make presentations for selection of bolt on products.
Work on interface / integration with non ERPLN solutions. Following are the skill sets for potential candidate:

Have domain experience preferably in manufacturing industry.
Implementation, Support, Customization, Migration experience.
Fair amount of technical knowledge to work productively with technical team.
Experience in integration with peripheral applications.
Experience of working in midsize firms that span multiple lines of business and geographies.  Job requires approximately 25% travel within and outside the US.
 Applicants should be US Citizens, Permanent Residents, H1-B visa holders.
