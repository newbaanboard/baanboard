Company Profile:
Bio-Rad Laboratories, Inc. ranks among the top ten clinical diagnostics companies worldwide, manufacturing and supporting an array of products used for medical screening and diagnosis. The group specializes in quality control systems, blood virus testing, toxicology, in vitro and genetic disorders testing, specialty chemistry, molecular pathology, and internet-based software. Location(s): Hercules, CA

Career Field: Information Systems

Education: BA/BS/MBA/CPA/CA with strong finance background

BA/BS/MBA/CPA or equivalent, eight or more years supporting manufacturing business systems with a focus on finance and accounting areas and applications. Four plus years of implementation and support of BaaN Finance and Accounting preferably in an environment with multiple manufacturing entities, Four plus years in analyzing and developing business processes. Develop, Implement, manage and support Finance and Accounting modules of BaaN. Provide functional and technical expertise by working closely with our internal customers (Business users) to extend, enhance and customize BaaN application and to improve and automate business processes. Key player in the implementation of next generation ERP system, likely to be SSA ERP LN
