Distribution Consultant for Infor

Details of the Vacancies:
We are looking for BE/Btech with 3-4 Yrs of domain experience, who would be interested in making a career as an INFOR ERP Consultant.
Job Description:
The incumbent is expected to provide consultancy to GIL customers in their respective functional areas. They will help the Customer to map the process of the Organisation in Infor ERP after understanding their existing process by taking care of the required improvement. The incumbent will also have to provide user training and work simultaneously with our customers in achieving mutual business goals. He will work under senior ERP Consultants & report to the Project Manager/Coordinator as a Team Member and help us in realizing our Consultancy Objectives.
“Interacting with Customers/Prospects (Pre-Sales), Understanding their requirements, Implementation of ERP LN/Add-on products to map the requirements, Training of Key users, Documentation, Completion of projects/assignment within stipulated budgeted costs/efforts”.
Candidates Profile:
Candidates should be Engineering Graduates with minimum 4 to 5 years of experience in functional areas of Materials, Stores, Sales, Supply chain management and Trade & Logistics.
He/she should possess excellent communication, presentation, people skills along with high degree of maturity.
Knowledge of ERP would be an added advantage.
Location : Mumbai/Delhi
