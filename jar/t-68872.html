Infor LN Logistic and Implementation Manager

The Infor LN Logistic and Implementation Manager will develop, coordinate and execute project plans for ERP global LN implementations, integrations and customizations. The Project Manager will be responsible for all aspects of successful delivery of LN projects including gathering requirements, mapping business processes, establishing responsibilities and timelines, coordinating internal and third-party teams, implementing modifications, and monitoring and communicating status to all stakeholders.
This role will also provide user and system support for LN Logistics packages including Sales, Distribution, Manufacturing, Procurement, Planning and Warehousing.
This is a project management not supervisory position. 
Location Ft Lauderdale, Florida USA
