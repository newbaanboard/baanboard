Business Analyst/Report Writer

Job Purpose



 


Position specializes on ERP and surrounding core applications ranging from ERP report writing, Microsoft Office applications, problem identification and resolution, and documentation. Highly knowledgeable on applications across all functional disciplines in a manufacturing environment. (MRP, Quality, Finance, HR, Purchasing, Engineering etc).  Focus on ERP application integration, report writing, problem resolution, and application planning and development.  Ability to accomplish projects on time and within budget.  Strong communication skills and ability to interface with all levels of users in the organization.  Research solutions, provide guidance and training as required.  A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected.
