It is the Receipt DATE from tdpur045 (for purchase item) or the production date for manu items.

This date ist used for FIFO / LIFO Logic, i. e. only if the item (tiitm001 / tipcs021) has FIFO or LIFO controll the date is inside the tdilc101.
