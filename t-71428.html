The invalid reference means that the underlying order/invoice has a customer code but the master data is not known at the BI side. (re)Publishing the missing one or just all customers should resolve this issue.
The reference missing means that the underlying order/invoice does not have a customer or item code attached to it. This is not wrong it is just a fact. Sometimes this is just how LN works in certain cases. Sometimes you could resolve this in LN by maintaining more data, like give each sales order a sales representative.
