The product works as advertised. Like any product that accesses tables in Baan/LN, the author has to know about the table structure and relationships to reduce full table reads. The language and views will provide necessary information to the users. We have somewhat technical folks and end-users creating and maintaining reports.

There is an Excel plug in that most of the end-users use. This lets you create the queries and views directly from Excel with output going straight to Excel. One of the stongest parts of the product.
