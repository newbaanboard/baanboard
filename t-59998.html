This is the solution to my problem. I need to use stpapi.enum.answer first.

stpapi.enum.answer("whinh3520m000","whinh3009",tcyesno.yes)
stpapi.form.command("whinh3520m000", 5, "option.confirm.receipt.li",error.msg)
