I don't whether it'd usefult to u or not. Baan table ttadv330 to 337 contain all the information necessary about a report. Especially ttadv335 may be useful for understanding the report layouts. Baan's report generator uses the information in ttadv tables to generate a script which effectively prints the report in the format decided.

Due to different programming styles , layout conditions and use /non-use of labels etc , understanding the Baan reporting is a quite daunting task.
