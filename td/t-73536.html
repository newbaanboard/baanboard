I would like to download data for quantity receipt from different company for intercompany transfer. I design the query as below and table whinh210 is supposed for 601. The query is designed at company 600.
your advice is really much appreciated.



select
tcitr300.ffcm, | From Financial Company
tcitr300.fenc, | From Entity Code
tcitr300.oboi, | Originating Business Object ID
tcitr300.pono, | Order Line
tcitr300.sitm, | From Item
tcitr300.quan, | From Ordered Quantity
tcitr300.tfcm, | To Financial Company
tcitr300.tenc, | To Entity Code
tcitr300.rboi, | Related Business Object ID
whinh210.qput | Put Away Quantity
from
tcitr300, | Intercompany Trade Orders
whinh210 | Inbound Order Lines
where
tcitr300.odat >= tcitr300.odat.f and
tcitr300.odat <= tcitr300.odat.t and
tcitr300.oboi >= tcitr300.oboi.f and
tcitr300.oboi <= tcitr300.oboi.t and
tcitr300.pono = whinh210.pono and
tcitr300.rboi = whinh210.orno
order by
tcitr300.pono,
tcitr300.oboi
