Usually I just add a button to the form for them to click after they have selected the records

choice.user.2:
on.choice:
if(not marked) then
message("No records selected.")
choice.again()
else
process_selected_items()
endif

Then in the process_selected_items I do this:

for i = 1 to filled.occ
if(mark.table(i)) then
hold.occu = hold.occu + 1
do.occ(i,store_item)
endif
endfor

The store_item routine will process occurance i individually. So in your case you could locate the record and perform some type of update.
