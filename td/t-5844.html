I found answer myself;
problem was a too short Menu name...

(solution No. 522 in Baan knowledge base):
SOLUTION DESCRIPTION:
When copying/customizing a menu, ensure that the menu name contains eight characters (i.e. in the menu 'tfgld0000m000', 'tf' is the package, 'gld' is the module, and '0000m000' is the menu name). The GUI expects menu names to be eight characters in length and it will not display menus with seven characters or less in the name. For example, the GUI will NOT display the menu 'tfgldfinance' but it WILL display 'tfgldfinance1'.
