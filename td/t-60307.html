Hi chris_kz,

you have to set the report settings in register "Specials" as following.

Top Margin 0
Bottom Margin 0
Left Margin 0
Foot Margin -1
Nr. of Multi Col. 2
Page Length <length of your page>
Page Width <width of your page>

Best regards
michael
