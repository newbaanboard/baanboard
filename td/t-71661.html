24th January 2019, 13:43
Check in your activity properties (right-click the activity, choose properties and then LN Studio Activity Information) the "Development License" property. If not checked, click Refresh. If still not checked, activate the JCA logging (Window>Preferences>Infor LN JCA Connectivity>Log Configuration) and repeat the Refresh. Check the JCA log file for messages. Probably you missed something during setup of Base VRC and Application.
