Dear Pradeep Joshi,

klick the "Start Shell" Button in the option dialog.

Then enter ps and check for processes. (e.g. a print on the display or a tfgld ... Session).

To end the hanging process enter e.g. kill -9 PID.

You can exit this shell with the "exit" command.

Be aware kill -9 terminates the hanging process without waiting for refering processes e.g. on the database.
