Hi,

If the delivery date and confirm Date both are in utc Please try the following

ret1 = utc.to.local(deliverydate,deldays,deltime)
ret2 = utc.to.local(confirmdate,confdays,conftime)
result = deldays - confdays

Hope this helps you.
