Hi

if the tigrt027.atab is your index then
in the called session use:

before.program:
query.rxtension="tigrt027._index1={:tigrt027.atab}"

if it is not an index then try to use a variable :

field.mgrade:
before.zoom:
zoom.atab=" GRADE"
export("zoom.atab",zoom.atab)

and in the called session:

before.program:
import("zoom.atab",zoom.atab)
query.rxtension="tigrt027.atab=:zoom.atab"
