Hi,

Run a simple script in your server.

printf "Enter mesg :\n"
mes=`line`
for i in `licmon6.1 -w |awk '{print(substr($3,index($3,".")+1))}'`
do
bshcmd6.1 -M "$mes" -u 1 -w 1 ${i}
licmon6.1 -w | grep ${i} |awk '{print "Message sent to "$3}'
done

It will prompt you a mesg to be displayed to all connected baan users
