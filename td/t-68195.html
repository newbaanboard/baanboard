Personalize form in Mingle/WebUI/Workspace for a user that doesn't have any current personalizations for the form. Go to the fields that you don't want and either hide them or change them to read-only. Go into the personalizations session (ttaad9100m000), find personalizations by user and session, mark (select) all records in the view for the user/session, use specific/"copy personalizations to other level" to copy the selected lines to a DEM role or company.

Yes, you can do that. Not sure about DFE, but you can do that using Session Personalization's(ttadv9100m000).

e.g. If I have to disable a form field from tcmcs0123m000 session, I can simply create a new record in the above mentioned session with the field I want to disable and uncheck the "Visible" field for that record.

In this case, you need to do that at company level though.
