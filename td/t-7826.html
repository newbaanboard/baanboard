Dear spartacus,

Such thing is a very complicated process involving several packages, ie WH, TD, even TF, etc. The scripts involved are spread not only in DAL of related tables used in the process, but also in several DLLs like whintdlltdpur and tdintdllwhinh. I think it's better if you make an API session on the receipt. That way, all the complication will be handled by the remotely controlled whinh3520m000 & friends :) . There are tips on how to make such session in the AFS receipt in BaanERP (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?s=&threadid=7101) thread
