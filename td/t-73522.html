I have finished designing automatic PO matching in LN. Earlier, I did the same customization in Baan IV. There are two routes- using AFS and other is using tfacpdll0060 which takes XML input. and both of them have their own intricacies. I designed it using third route of knowing what tables and GL accounts get hit and how to look for them. The PO matching is a simple process as it needs registering of invoice and then matching it to receipt. Once I match the invoice, standard Baan session of Approving takes care of it. In Baan IV I had designed the approving part too.

This customization can take a PO invoices through a file and match all of them in same batch.
My experience in Baan IV and ERP ln, is that LN is little easier to program as DAL takes care of some part of business logic.
I thought of sharing this.
