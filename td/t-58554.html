Check if your date formats are converted to runtime. The date formats are in session ttadv4580m000. They should be present in $BSE/lib/datecurr (lines starting with "D") for all languages defined in the system (ttaad1510m000). If not all format/language combinations are present, just convert to runtime.
Check also if the language which is used to print the report is present in ttaad1510m000. If not, add it and convert to runtime.
Note that some reports are printed in the language of the business partner (for example an invoice). So, those languages have to be present in ttaad1510m000. In session tcmcs0146m000 you have the link between the business partner language and the system language.
