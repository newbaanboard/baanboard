Alternatively, you can use the Window Title in $BSE/lib/bse_vars

BAAN_WIN_TITLE=[Production]-[%S]-[%c]-[%C]-[%h]-[%f]

Use search on forum with keyword "BAAN_WIN_TITLE" to look different options for it.
