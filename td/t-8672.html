The size of the paper depends on the paper type you did define with your printer device.

Your report should have a page length of -1, than it uses the paper size.

To make things transparant, make the layouts as wide as the no.of.char which you can define in the report header.

Using landscape with bwprint? You should be able to print about 44 lines.
