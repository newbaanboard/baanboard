Despite there is enum for the form in Forms-Session: ttadv3100s000, I am not so sure that this is an good idea!!!!
If you would have multi occ and single occ forms the layout of the form has to fit with the type. And the layout is not a thing, one can just switch. For that you have to work with the form editor.
There maybe further things, in your GUI-Script, which doesn't fit with an other type of layout.

So I think that is not to say in general how to change a form. May it's a good idea to create an other session with an other layout, maybe there is an other, shorter way.
