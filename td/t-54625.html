Step 1: go to the report you want to modify.
Step 2: select the report and click special, then click on layouts. The layout form will open.
Step 3: Insert the new layout of your choice.
Step 4: Select the new layout and click Special. This will get you to the report editor.
Step 5: Insert the field/lables of your choice there.
