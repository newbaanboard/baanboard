You will have to use something like the following:


double tothrs
long i

tothrs = 0.0

select tphrs100.nhrs
where tphrs100._index1={...}
selectdo
for i = 1 to 7
tothrs = tothrs + tphrs100.nhrs[i]
endfor
endselect
