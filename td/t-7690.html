The find dialog is a special type of field: the initial value is related to the field.<findfield>: sections in the form section of the script (including fields.all section).
nicklewe
19th November 2002, 09:26
Thanks for that..

works fine..:


choice.def.find:
before.choice:
tempvar=1

after.choice:
tempvar=0

field.all:
before.field:

if tempvar=1 then
mytable.field1=""
mytable.field2=""
endif
