If u want to show as a button in right side of the Field, then chose In Form Command Window Command Type as "Field" and give Field Number , But in this case it will not show as a Button in Tool Bar or not a new Item in Specific.

If u want to show it as a Button in Tool Bar or a new Item in Specific,then
u chose Command Type "Form" and click checkbox show as a Button in Dialog and click checkbox Show on menu Bar of detail Window.
