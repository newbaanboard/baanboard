The way MS-Word and Excel typically work is to convert the report to a text first and then downloads it to the client. This will strip out any of the fonts you setup for printers (or word). The only way I can think of to handle this is to use something like the NASDAQ product, or maybe launch a word macro to format the report the way you want it to look.
