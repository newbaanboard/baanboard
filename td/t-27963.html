To print logos in BAAn Reports follow the steps :

1) Download font creator very handy tool , n in any of its character block paste your logo and save it with .ttf extension . for eg. u pasted it in say letter B and then put the font file in c:/windows/font folder

2) Now in ur Report Editor , put a formfield and in print expression put "B" and print length as 1

3) compile ur report

4) And when u print ur report in winp device if u c "B" , then right click and change the font type 2 yourfont.ttf
