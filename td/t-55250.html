wiggum
10th August 2009, 12:13
It's like the VRC definitions. Usually you have your customer VRC based on an update VRC and the standard VRC.

Opening webhelp LN looks for a (customized) help in your "customer VRC" (a directory named like your VRC e.g. "B61Cacust" for the VRC "B61C a cust"). If there isn't a help file webhelp has to know where to look for the standard help files. This is defined by the fallback scenarios.
