I was curious so I tried out the following in EASY SQL and it works (in BAAN IVc3 at least):


select
tibom010.exdt, | Expiry Date
tibom010.mitm, | Manufactured Item
tibom010.sitm | Item
from
tibom010 | Production BOMs
where
tibom010.exdt > date()
