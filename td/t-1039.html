Hi Youp,
You could either send an email or page (as in pager/beeper).

There are a lot of posts on this forum on how to send emails
thru Baan. mailx is a very easy way to achieve it. Dont bother
buying an external product just for email.

I had done a similar thing at my previous client.
They wanted the "GUYS" to be paged thru Baan.

In our case it was simple.
The pagers were capable of receiving email "alerts".
For. e.g. If the pager # was 123456, then all I had to do was
send an email to 123456@nextel.com

Check with the "paging" service provider if their pagers can be
beeped via email. In most cases it is possible.

So  now if you can send an email thru baan u could send a
page .

Try the "page" option. It makes the client hazar excited :)

Hope this helps.
