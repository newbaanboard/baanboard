Are you using Backup Exec or Net Backup?

You can only restore to the individual table space or individual filegroup. You cannot restore an individual table. This is probably the most common question asked about backups. Since backups are writing at a block level not a logical row/table level you need to interpose another tool between the backup utility and the database.

I'm sorry I do not have better news. You will have to restore to a second instance and then export/import the individual table.
