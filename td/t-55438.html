Hi,

I have done the same at my End , but it is not printer specific on Windows envionment.

Steps todo:

in Baan
1.define the barcode in reports as required of your barcode size.
2.goto form2 of "maintain reports" ->"Nr. of multi col" as 2

In Windows Client (where the Printer is installed)
1.Define the label size as
width (including 2 barcodes) and height as one barcode.

eg: if your label size is 1.5(w) / 1(h) - then make define in windows driver printer setting as 3(w)/1(h).
