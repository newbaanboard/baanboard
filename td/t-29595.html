Normally a copy is made from tdpur094.pmsk (order type table) to tdpur401.pmsk. However, sometimes not all activities can be executed. For example the step 'Release to Warehousing' is not needed for cost items. When the purchase order line is created for such a cost item, the "y" that represents the release to warehousing step is removed automatically.
