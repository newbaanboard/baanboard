We have done something similar in other areas. For instance at the point that Print Sales Invoices is run, we print order acknowledgements, bills of lading, custom bills of material. The invoice is printed to hardcopy and a pdf for input into our document management system. The other documents are created as pdfs for inport inot our DMS as well. This way we get a complete snapshot of all the documents associated with a particular order or project at the time the invoice is printed.

We embed a dll in the report script. The dll is an AFS call to all of the appropriate print sessions. This way we can feed the field data to the appropriate sessions and let Baan handle the back end.
