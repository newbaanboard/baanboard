Not sure if you need a development license for this or not - there are other posts on the board about that. Search the board for that.

First copy the form to you development VRC, then modify the form. Just double click on the choice field. If it runs a menu then you can go add your session to the menu - and delete the form in your VRC.

If the choice field already runs a session then go create a new menu, add your session and the other session to the menu. Then modify the form to run this menu.

If the choice field is empty then just add your session into it.

Good Luck!
