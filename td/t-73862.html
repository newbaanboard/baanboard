JaapJD
1st June 2021, 17:30
A new session is also seen as an extension. In cloud-ready extensions you cannot call the standard DLL functions, only Public Interfaces. Quote from the guide: Functions in LN Application DLLs, even if declared as extern, are untrusted by default. A new specific trusted layer is available with functions that can be used by extensions. The trusted LN Application functions are called “Public Interfaces”. See also KB 2003722 in the Infor Support Portal.
