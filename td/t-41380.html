I think this will work - in the after.report layout put your text field on the layout printing sometext(i) (in this case the domain will be tctxtn). Sometext(i) will be your text array and no.of.texts is how may different text number you will be printing.

before.program:
i = 1

after.report.30:
after.layout:
if i<no.of.texts then
i = i + 1
layout.again()
endif

You can search on layout.again for more information. Of course I am assuming layout.again works in LN like 4c4.
