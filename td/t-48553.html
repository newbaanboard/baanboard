Hi Uli,

when you have a Windows-System you have to set the PATH variable.

"c:\program files\java\jre<javaversion>\bin" and "c:\Program-Files\java\jre<javaversion>\bin\client"

On a Linux-System its better you set the LD_LIBRARY_PATH variable in $BSE/lib/bse_vars file. Look the example!

LD_LIBRARY_PATH=/usr/java/jre<javaversion>/lib/i386:/usr/java/jre<javaversion>/lib/i386/client:/erpln61/bse/shlib:/usr/local/baan/shared/shlib
