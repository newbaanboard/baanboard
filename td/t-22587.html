Hi Andreas,

thanks for your help. Your post was very usefull. I made an entry on second form "Kommunikation" of session "tccom4135s000". I used the last free individual field, left field description empty and set line (to 6), sequence (to 1), priority (to 1) and space (to 1). The last field (space) is very important. If you leave it zero it doesn't work. But if you put at least 1 into this field, one empty line will be printed.
