kbartelds
7th April 2005, 14:37
It's your system, so no one can tell you not to do this. But it isn't a smart thing to do, because if you get an update on this component, your changes will be lost.

As said, first you have to undo the changes that you have made to the report. After that, change your package vrc to your "own" vrc, something like tdB40O_c4_in5p (at least with an O in it). Use Special, Change current package vrc.

After that, copy the report to your "own" vrc (use CTRL X) and make the changes and compile the report. Doing the copy to the own vrc will take care that this component is directly visible in the live vrc, which will be derived from the update vrc (tdB40U_c4_in5p).

Regards,
Klaas
