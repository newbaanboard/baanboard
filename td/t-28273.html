visible and invisible are supported in Baan V and higher. The best you can use in Baan IV is 'attr.input = false', to stop them from entering in the field.


field.myfield:
before.input:
if <some condition> then
attr.input = false
endif


Then it will skip that field as they tab around, but it will still be visible.


Hmm. EDITED: I see they have backported something, these functions now compile and do make the field invisible in Baan IV now. You need to mess with the labels as varaibles too then...not as clean as it is in Dynamic Forms, but it seems to work.


field.my.item:
after.input:
if <some condition> then
inputfield.visible("my.routing field")
else
inputfield.invisible("my.routing field")
endif
