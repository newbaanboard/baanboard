I'll speak from experience. I would use a sequence number within the date/time just to be sure. Baan stores the time as a number of seconds since midnight (Baan IV, that you are on, right?). A little different in Baan V & LN, but still only stores to the second. This means that if your users HAPPEN to enter something and it hits at the same second, you will have a duplicate error. While it may not seem likely, we have found that it surely is possible (but then, we run a heavily used Data collection system..).
