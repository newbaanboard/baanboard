When you hit the Continue button it starts a SMI session. In this session extra attributes of the customer can be registered. The possible list of attributes is user defined; like: "Does the customer play golf yes/no", "did visit Inforum before",...
Within SMI these extra attributes can be used to base campaigns on, like sending a letter to all golf playing customers.

If you do not use SMI then it also makes no sense to register extra attributes and therefor the message when you hit the continue button.
