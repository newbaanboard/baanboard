select
whwmd300.cwar, | Warehouse

whwmd301.incv, | Expected Increase in Volume

from
whwmd300, | Locations
whwmd301 | Location Capacity
where
whwmd300.cwar >= whwmd300.cwar.f and
whwmd300.cwar <= whwmd300.cwar.t and
whwmd300.loca >= whwmd300.loca.f and
whwmd300.loca <= whwmd300.loca.t and
whwmd300._index1 refers to whwmd301
