Hello to All, the reason why the menu was not shown is still unclear. But here is a workaround which the Infor-Support showed me:

I've not to open the Ming.le-GUI. Instead I've to open the WebUI. In the WebUI there is the program tree in the left upper section. In this tree I've to navigate to the last upper menu (where the new menu is included). Then I've to collapse the menu list with the "-"Button. The button changed to "+". After collapsing the list I've to right-click on the "+" Symbol. There is the Context-Menu with the "Refresh" option. After refreshing the menu list, the new menu was displayed also in the Ming.le GUI after restart.
