You could use ttaad4134m000 Table data authorisations. Say the customer that is allowed is 999999.

Insert a record for this user, all companies (or one company depending on your logistical setup), table = tdsls040, data authorization = read.

Then insert lines

Cond 1, sern 1, field = cuno, between 000000 999998 or
Cond 1, sern 2, field = cuno, between A00000 ZZZZZZ end of expression

Special convert to runtime

Then relogin to Baan.


Hope this helps

Ed
