Do you have inforxtrem-support-access? If you don't have access, go to www.inforxtreme.com and create a new account (this may take some time, till Infor activates your account.)

With an active account you can access the documents and solutions provided by Infor.
In Solution 22924522 you can find offline versions of the developer's guide to Infor ERP LN 6.1 ("progguide"). There are differences to Baan 4 but you can use the basics and the function references.

There's also an document giving examples how to program certain thinks. It's called "Design Principles". It's IMHO an "How-To"-collection, with lots of code-examples.

In inforxtreme you can also access documentation of various products. There's an "Infor ERP Enterprise Development Tools (LN) - Developer's Guide" (U8883J US) available. Again it's LN-based but LN is based on Baan4, so it may help, too.
