I recommend training or at least get development documentation from infor. LN might be similar to 4c4 but sure there are differences. So I basically do what Bernd recommends.

In 4c4 if I was going to change a report I would log into a development company. I would go to maintain sessions. I would then use special/Change Current package VRC - I would set it to what I needed for our DEV VRC I would then go to from 2 and hit reports. I would find the report I want to change and then just hit the copy. It would default it the report into our DEV vrc. I could then modify it and test it with the session. Once approved by the customer we would move the report to the production VRC.
