I would check out the Archiving Forum (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=32). But we have been on Baan since 99 and have not archived any data. We do not have any performance problems. And yes sometimes the programs use incorrect indexes or searches. You would not notice this with small tables and may only showup after the tables grow. You can use logging to find some of the problems and maybe even compiling in profile mode(that is if you have source).
