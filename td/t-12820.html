And the possibility to change the text on the buttons on the fly, depending on some field value?

Muheeb, to have more than 10 choices, you can do the folllowing: Create a second form, with maybe a slightly different layout, and change the options on that form. In the code, in the choice.user sections, then use:

choice.user.0:
on.choice:
on case form.curr
case 1: <specific function of form 1>
break
case 2: <specific function of form 2>
break
endcase
