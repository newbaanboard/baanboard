I am not sure about citrix - we never tried our scan guns with citrix. But I do know it will work. What we did was sent requirements to suppliers saying - when you ship us parts include these fields with barcodes. I know it was more formal than that, but that is what I remember. Anyway the part comes it we can item, quantity they enter a location - we then do the receipt, maintain approvals, and inbound for the customer.

I know we have a client on citrix, but I do not think any of our shop personnel use it. The process works, but I can't answer about citrix.

You may also want to play with your scanner - it might read and work with other barcode types also. Just gives you more options for your suppliers. Plus you might want to give your baan version - others might have better solutions.
