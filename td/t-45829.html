Hi Skumar,

i don't know which kind of query you mean, so i'll try to explain both (Baan IV c4):

1) Session-Query

This kind of query is mostly at display-session enabled, which shows datalists. Over "Extras" (Baan-GUI) choice "Query". After that you can enter an operator (i.e.: equal, between, etc.) and the parameters, which you want and start the query.

2) Easy-SQL-Query

Insert a new record at "ttadv3180m000" with a unique query-name (i.e.: test1), owner, a description, login, date, output-medium i.e.: "Report list", the report should be created by the system, print-options i.e.: A4-MIDDLE.

After that choice the button "Easy SQL". At "ttadv3181m000" insert your table(s) and mark them an click at "Tablefields". At "ttadv3582s000" mark the intresting tablefields and push "OK". Back at "ttadv3181m000" press "Query-Fields" and insert at "ttadv3182m000" the filters you like for the actuel marked query-field. Save and close the session.

Return to "ttadv3180m000" and choice "Create Query-Report" at the options. After that you can start the query with "Run Query".

Good luck! ;)

Hope this helps.
