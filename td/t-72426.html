JaapJD
21st November 2019, 17:02
This is an invalid mix between 'classic customizations' and extensibility. Your change of the tf-session in a standard (or update VRC) does not see the tx-session. So, two options:
1) Copy the tf-session to 105O_0_live and specify the zoom to the tx-session in the form definition
2) Create a session extension for tfacp2600m000 to include the CDF on the form and specify the zoom to tx in the Get Zoom Session hook.
