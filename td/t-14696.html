Sorry I'm not very available today, just saw.

I think maybe something else...our tdsls4102s000 has refresh available. We have several versions of Baan IV, I checked to be sure, and I don't see why the refresh would be disabled, that would be pretty unusual.

At one point, you said 'how do we get the refresh active again'....was it active at one point?

I am assuming since you have played with the options that you have a custom form. You can try a couple things.

1. Make a visible change to your custom form. Dump the form. Do you see your change? This will tell if you are converting / seeing the changes you make. If not, the issue is there, not in your change. Check the VRC and shared memory possibilities.

2. If you do see your change, but still not the refresh, try removing that form (copy to a different name), exit Baan and return. You should be seeing the standard form, yes? No longer your changes? Is the refresh active? That will tell us if it is active in your base version.
