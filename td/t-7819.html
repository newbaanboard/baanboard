If they are the external functions in the DAL, those are called 'Business Methods'. They are functions that are related to the table. Many of the buttons on the main table sessions are calling Business Methods in the DAL. Also, the DAL calls the business methods as regular functions, as does the UI Program, and yes, you can call them too.


dal.start.business.method("ttxxx999", "business.method.name", arg1, arg2...)


where ttxxx999 is the table / dal name.
