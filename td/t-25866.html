You can change | to ||. Click on the Layouts button in Maintain Reports then select & edit the wanted layout. In the editor, go to the "Mode" menu and click on Toggle Graphics.
Now every time you click on a pipe you can have a vertical line (longer than the original pipe, which is used in the reports)
