28th November 2002, 18:34
The "end" of the story was: artjuh was this one customer of mine and Baan is not willing to change things.

And we had to be more precise in the problem definition: the question pops up whenever you enter a subsession or perfrom an action that can also be seen as an edit action. Like open a field dialog to view it's contents (where you could also have changed it) and move a field (even if it is only too small distance to have actually moved it, but you was ++ at each side) etc.

Currently the check is: whenever you were in edit mode, the editor assumes you could have made changes that were to be confirmed.

The fact that it worked without questions in the past was an error: it was also not saved when you did actually make changes. In that case this is better than not saving any changes at all...
