george7a
7th April 2009, 15:43
"del" alone is not a command that will work from "start -> run". You will need to put "cmd /c" before it. Example:

run.prog("cmd.exe", "cmd /c del e:\erplnfp3\bin\prova.txt", RP_WAIT)

I hope it helps,

- George
