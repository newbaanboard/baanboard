It is absolutely possible to accommodate 30 fields on the list session. In fact we have tried with 50+ fields with out any problem. Though I think there should be some limit for the size which I am not aware of.

Though I would suggest not to accommodate so many fields in one tab, as it is always better to split the fields in multiple tabs, accommodating in the same form may cause performance issues and will have a long scroll.
