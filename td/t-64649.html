Hi,

Are these field in the same table? If yes then its easy.

field.field1:

when.field.changes:

field3 = field1 & "." & field2

field.field2:

when.field.changes:

field3 = field1 & "." & field2

You can also do it in on.input section of field1 & field2.

You can also use before.display section of field3.
