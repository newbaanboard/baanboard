I'm currently working with Baan Support regarding the problem. They believe that based on previous cases, this error can result from more or less one of three things:

1) The standard program (ottstpstandard referred to in the error message) is out of date with respect to some other component of Baan.
2) There is a problem with a European language set up that uses multibyte text
3) There is something wrong with a domain being used by the tables involved.


I'll keep this post updated with our results. Mark - Thanks for all of your suggestions and your time involved.
