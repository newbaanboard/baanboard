Hi Hiba_t,

1. Open the dynamic form editor.
2. Goto Insert menu on the toolbar, select insert for "Tablefield"(field from the main table) or "field"(any other table field or variable) depending on your requirement.
3. Right click the inserted field and select the properties.
4. Goto the third tab "Position" and tick the check box for View Field.
5. make sure your session is marked as overview session.

Let me know if you need any further assistance.

Cya.
