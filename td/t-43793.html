Hi,

in my opinion there is no technical reason not to index tdpur045.invn.

You only should always add tdpur045.orno, pono and serb also to the index (or with other words the whole primary key so you can create the index with duplicate = NO. Duplicate values can slow down you dramatically especially with ttaad4100/ttaad4500).

On the other hand, tdpur045 is not a historical table and you or some other will delete the old records if it becomes to large. I think thats why they used an additonal table for a history of tdpur045.invn.
