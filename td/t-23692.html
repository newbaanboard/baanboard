Hi Vishal,

Declare a form field of length necessary to make the label.

do the following:
extern domain tcmcs.strx (x is length you need, search in display domains)

field.<your form field>:
before.display:
if flag = true then
str.label = "First Kind"
else
str.label = " Second Kind"
endif
