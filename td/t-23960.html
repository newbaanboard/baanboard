We have seen this happen when you copy companies and skip the tg step or don't do it right. Did you follow the quick guide Copying Companies in Baan ERP? See solution #3934. When you dump from prod to test, you need to either remove the tg tables before import to test, or replace the company number in them, there's a couple of tables that hold the prod company number. These tg tables don't get corrected in the tfgld9000 series for Correct Company number. Maybe they have fixed that by now.

Also, I seem to remember something about the tcemm relationship that may not be set up 100% right, may also cause this error.
