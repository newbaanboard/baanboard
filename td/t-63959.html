Hi,

Yes offcourse its possible. What you need to do is:-

Copy the menu tccom00000000 in your current customized VRC and then add the menu fields to it.

That is the reason why you are not able to view the menu's.

I guess you know how to copy the menu in your current vrc. If not:-

Select the standard menu i.e. tccom00000000 and from specific do a copy to current vrc (check you are in your customized VRC i.e. cust).

Then add the menu fields to this menu. And then compile the menu and do a refresh.

This will solve your problem.
