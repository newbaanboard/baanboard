Why don't you use group by command in query.extension
group by shiftdate,shift , sono ,pono ,coil .

and add variable to program script for part, pono, weight.
Calculate these varilables in
main.table.io:
after.read:
select FASHION.part:part, FASION.pono:pono, FASION.weight:weight
from FASHION
where FASION.shiftdate=:FASION.shiftdate and
FASION.shift = :FASION.shift and
FASION.sono = :FASION.sono and
FASION.pono = :FASION.pono and
FASION.coil = :FASION.coil
selectdo
endselect
