If the values CH1 , CH2 etc are pre-defined and it's number is known at design time and limited say around 15 , then u can do so . U need to use aggregate function adn expression for each data value with same report field.

If the column headings are not pre-defined and it's number is not known at design time , u can still do so but with spool functions and variables which is very cumbersome and time consuming (not an easy task)

In any case the number of characters on a line of the report will be limited to 260 characters.
