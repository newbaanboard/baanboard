Declare fields to hold the count and successful.

long count
long successful

Put the formulas where appropriate (i.e. where the records are being read/processed).


select tiitm001.item
from tiitm001
selectdo
count = count + 1
display("count")
refresh()

if <condition> then
db.update(tiitm001, db.retry)
successful = successful + 1
display ("successful")
refresh()
endif
endselect
