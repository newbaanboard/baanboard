Yes that works great...

spool.report="rtTheReportName"
spool.device="W"
spool.preview=true
reportnr = brp.open(spool.report, spool.device, 0)

if reportnr>0 then
|...and now collect the data and stuff

brp.ready(reportnr)
brp.close(reportnr)
endif
