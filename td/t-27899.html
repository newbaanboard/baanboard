Yes, but not every time at any place :D . Like nikos1 wrote, there are internal functions. During executing this, you see all code, but you cannot set a breakpoint in your code... . The easiest way to figure out where you actual can set a breakpoint is try and error. If I remember correctly, you can set breakpoints in your code if you are inside a r.s. Function (r.s.detail.1: and so on). Then you can set a breakpoint at every of your own functions. That means, you have to wait and go step by step until you are the first time in your reportscript.
