I agree, not very different. Another minor difference, when you are upgrading it's kind of a pain, the messages that have % substitutions now make you say what order you are giving the substitutions in. For example, what used to be the baan IV / V message:

The Employee %s expires on %d

Now needs to be changed to

The Employee %$1s expires on %$2d


This I imagine is because different languages not only convert the message to a new language, but sometimes the order of the substitutions must change too.
