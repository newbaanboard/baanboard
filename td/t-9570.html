Where are they cutting and pasting from? We have had specific instances where users cut and paste from Word - and then have problems printing the text. Sometimes it does not print and sometimes it does print, but with a lot of garbage. What we found out was that when cutting and pasting between word and notepad you could not have any special formating in the text you were cutting from. You really need to make sure that the text you are "cutting" is plain old text. When we run into these problems we have to cut the text from Baan, put into into wordpad, save as plain text, re-open the file, cut it from wordpad, and then paste it back into baan. Our users have become pretty good at copying/pasting text, so we do not run into it much. Not sure if this could be the problem, but thought I would mention it. We also had some initial problems with Office XP - in word we turned off some formating characters. I will try to find that.

Good Luck!

Mark
