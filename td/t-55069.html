The recommended integration method for Baan products, and ERP LN in particular, is OpenWorld. Infor is moving towards an SOA architecture with InforSOA which will be rolled out over the next year +. That will eventually be the method of integration.

There are other methods such as application function servers (AFS) or direct database manipulation via something like ODBC.

If you search for AFS or Openworld in the forums you should come across multiple posts about both.
