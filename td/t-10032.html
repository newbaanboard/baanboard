You can use the functions - import() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_variables_interprocess_transfer_import) or import.4gl.var() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_variables_interprocess_transfer_import_4gl_var) in your session as long as the variables you are importing are defined as extern in the other script or if they are table fields.

You can also use get.var() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_variables_interprocess_transfer_get_var), e.g. something like get.var(parent, "cprj.f", cprj) to import the external var cprj.f from the standard session to cprj of your own session.

Btw. this version works even for reports scripts or zoom sessions. All you have to do is to determine the correct pid - e.g. via get.var(parent, "parent", pid1), and then get.var(pid1, "parent", pid2). You get the idea.
