If you want to execute something when you save (but not exit), you will need something else. choice.end.program is a save and exit. You could use choice.update.db, the program section after.update.db.commit, or even before/after.(re)write depending on when you want your action to occur. However, none of these distinguishes your "save" button press from a save happening as a result of other events.

The right approach depends on what exactly you are trying to do.:D
