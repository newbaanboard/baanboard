If you start up the BAAN Menu Browser, then click and sit on BAAN IV Tools, then from Help in the menubar select "Contents" it will open up a list of all BAAN Tools related 'books'. From there, you can go where you please. For your specific question, click on "Technical ..." and then click on "Manuals" - that will give the list of alphabets from which you can choose all the commands of BAAN Programming Language, except BAAN SQL - for that your need to search elsewhere in the list of 'books'.

Hope this helps.
