You can not have the check boxes as a multi-occurance field. Just looking at your script you use check(actual.occ) - and more than likely the only field on the form is check(1). I think you will have to put the check boxes on the form as single occurance and use the element field. So if you form as 10 occurances for the form then put 10 check boxes on the form with elements 1 to 10. Of course this will not allow you to track across screens. Are you trying to track across screens? Even baan does not do that, except with select range.

My suggestion was different . Creating an array of such a big size looks totally unwarranted . If u restructure ur requirement and program u can easily work with an array of max total occurence numbers in the form e.g. 20 .

Further the variable to be put on the form (ie i.check) should not be an array.

You can definitely let users use the check boxes on the form with 2 enum selection (not the order status ).

Ideally when u wish users to do multiple selections across forms either there should be a field in the table or selected secords be inserted /deleted in a separate table which keeps track of array values in after.update.db.commit section .

Nevertheless if ur solution is really working for u, u need not work in this direction .
