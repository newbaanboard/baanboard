Hello Bogdan,

Just wanted to add few things to what Mr. Nprao had mentioned.

1. Yes, you can use Infor ION for Document Approvals
2. I believe from LN 10.4.1 version onwards, Infor have given the standard feature (within LN) to enable/disable the Document Authorizations. Using this option, you can integrate your LN with ION for Purchase Order Approval

Prior to LN 10.4.1 version, only purchase requisition approval is supported by standard, and we have to customize the Order Approvals.

Hope this information helps you. If you need more details about the Approval process in ION, stay in touch. I'll be happy to help you!
