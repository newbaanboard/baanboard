Hi Mahmood,

I guess that you already used ttaad1110m000 Maintain Languages to assign 6 to the new language. As far as I remember, the next step is to use ttaad4180m000 Maintain Date Formats / Option "Convert to Runtime" to have $BSE/lib/datecurr newly created. An entry in that file could look like "D|001<yourl language>|MDY|%02m<date separator>%02d<date separator>%02y|". Don't forget to restart you bshell.

Günther
