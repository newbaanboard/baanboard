The empty spaces are not due to your report layouts, it is calculated during runtime based on the margins and the page length in your device data.

You may need to make use of report functions skip(n), skip.to(n), need(n) in report script to control the empty spaces between the detail and the after field layouts.

The P.O., invoices etc. always end up with a totalization of the order amount, invoice amount etc. In case you have just 1 detail the next report layout to be printer is the after.orco that contains these totals. As the totals need to be printed at the bottom of the page in the report script the function skip.to(spool.page.length - [X]) is used. This means that this sections starts at the point of the page length minus [X] lines. In case your page length is 66 and the [X] is 10 your layout will start at line 57. The lines between the detail and the after.field section are blank and you won't find them in the report layout as a result of the skip.to function.
