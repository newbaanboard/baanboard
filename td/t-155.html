Hi,

Here is a serious reply to your question :-)

Decision Manager is Baan's Business Intelligence solution (BIS).
It has 2 parts - Infrastructure (ETL tools for datawarehouse) and Business Content (pre-defined templates). The beauty of DM templates is such that it can be deployed very easily for any BaanIVc or BaanERP 5.0c system. The implementation time is very short (vanilla basis). DM tools can read data from any ODBS complient database and content from Legacy or Web can be also merged into the datawarehouse. Applications such as Crystal Reports (fixed format reports) and Crystal Analysis Professional (Analytic reports on OLAP) read and present the data for analytics.

Content is available for domains such as Sales, Purchase, Inventory,Finance,Projects, Mfg. etc. This solution helps users to get answers such as : What is the trend in sales, which customers have large open amounts, which product is slow-moving and so on. It also has KPI's that indicte efficiencies of various business processes.

Quite a serious and lengthy answer ? :-) Hope it got you some insight into it !
