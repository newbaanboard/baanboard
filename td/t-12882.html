If you are printing the logo in the footer, just check that there is enough room at the bottom of the page... What could be happening, is that the footer has a need of 1 line, but the logo (even though the field on the footer is only taking one line), may require multiple lines...

Either check the 'Need' column of the Footer layout in 'Maintain Report Layouts' or check the report script to see if there is a 'need' or 'skip.to' function. If not, try setting the 'need' column of your layout to a few extra lines...

Bhaskar,

Ruskin has the solutionl. If you were to open the the temporary file in the ${BSE_TMP} folder on your local client using a text editor instead of BWPrint, you will see that the footer line is still present. The logo must be taking up more than one line, and Baan continues to count it as one.

Adjust the number of lines for the footer to be a greater value than you currently have.
