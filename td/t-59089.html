27th August 2010, 09:58
That simply means that the business partner (Relation) send with EDI message (in field tcedi702.reno) does not exits in table tcedi010 "Relations". Check with session tcedi0110m000 "Maintain Relations".
Check also if your conversion set-up for incoming Orders and the received EDI file does match together.

Regards,
Juergen
