I just discovered something and thought of sharing with you.
The default mode of operation of GTM(ttaad4100) is "Dal Not Active" i.e. DAL restrictions are not functional. One can change it to "DAL Active" by pressing the * key before selecting the table which makes the DAL active for that table.

I dont know how many of us will like this *DAL restrictions* in GTM :eek: , but there exists a way to make any data changes within framework of DAL ;)
