Hi,

you must qualify the image files when the icons are in another module with then module name (here "tdgen:"):


form.1:
init.form:
create.extra.toolbar(
"line.status","tdgen:approve.gif"
,"line.status1","tdgen:approve.gif"
,"line.status1","tdgen:approve.gif"
)
