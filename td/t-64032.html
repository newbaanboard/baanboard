In field section of enum field write this:-

fieldname = empty

e.g. I have an enum field on my form say. whbed124.gen

I will write:-

field.whbed124.gen:

before.display:
whbed124.gen = empty


Also, you can use it in your maintain session like this:-

before.new.object:
whbed124.gen = empty

This will blank your enum field when New is clicked.
