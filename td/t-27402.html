Hi Spartacus,

Im not sure whether i got your requirement right but from what i understand, i propose following solution,

select tcibd001.citg
from tcibd001
where (:select.a = tcyesno.yes and tcibd001.citg = "a") or
(:select.b = tcyesno.yes and tcibd001.citg = "b") or
(:select.c = tcyesno.yes and tcibd001.citg = "c") or
(:select.d = tcyesno.yes and tcibd001.citg = "d")
selectdo
endselect
