Patricio,

Remove the statement - #include "ittadv0001"

And use - switch.to.company() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_company_operations_switch_to_company) or compnr.check() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_company_operations_compnr_check) your script will be compileable.

As I have mentioned the eMessage connector works if you have the include bic_cmf, ttcmf* and ttdllcmf* objects.
