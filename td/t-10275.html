lbencic
6th June 2003, 17:27
These things are all controlled by the domain. The only way to get to that information from a form level is to put a program field on the form and leave the domain blank. You will then be taken to that last form to fill in the domain attributes.

If it is a table field you are putting on the form, you cannot change or remove the domain, so you cannot get to those fields on the last form at all.
