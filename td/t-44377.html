Here is how I would approach the problem. First you state the detail record value is not correct. So lets get the detail records correct first. So on your report print the detail records - you can always turn off the layout by just changing the print expression to 0(or something false). Next put the session in debug mode step through and make sure your queries are correct. I can not help here, because we do not use any tables like this. From looking at your queries I assume that tffst300 has ONLY ONE value per tffst120.fstm and tffst120.accn. If this is not the case then you need to either sum on tffst300 or change where your rprt_send is located(move inside select on tffst300). So get the details correct and this may fix you after report field.
