Moved the thread to correct forum - Tools Development

To add new lines, Open report editor and go to your layout where you want to add new lines.

1. Select "Resize window" under "Form" option in menu.
2. Use your arrow keys to increase or decrease the layout size.
3. Press "space_bar" once you are done with the resizing and you are done.
