BaanInOhio
5th April 2014, 22:32
You cannot add CDFs to a form. They are added by the system and are not available in menu browser/worktop. By default, the CDFs are placed in a separate tab called "configurable fields" in alphabetical order by field name. If in 10.2.0+, you can use the personalize feature of WebUI to move the fields from the configurable fields tab to any other tab in your form. The configurable fields tab will go away once all fields are moved out. Once you are happy with the locations, you can copy your personalizations to the company or DEM role level so all who are in the company or role have these new form locations as a default.
