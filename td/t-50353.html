Hi,

Go to Maintain sessions and open the session you want, then go to the second form and click on scripts. In Maintain Program scripts, click on Compile + debug. That will be all ;).

When you want to disable the debugger you will have to do the same steps and at the end click on compile.

Of course this will work only if you have the script for the session.

I hope this helps,

- George
