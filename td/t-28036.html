Hi,

You will have to prepare 3 gif files named end1.gif, end2.gif & end3.gif. Then save them under $BSE/gif/gfd0002

end1.gif is the regular icon.
end2.gif the "clicked" icon (when you click on the save and exit)
end3.gif is when this button is disabled.

Don't forgt to backup the original gif files.

You can see the new icon when you re-login to Baan and not in the same run.

I hope it helps,

You have to take a solid picture of an icon and put it on top of the button gif. If you just repaint the image in Paint, it does not take the settings because of transparency settings on the gif. You can re-paint and remove the transparency settings with a graphics editor but even if you fill in the entire gif with the new color, only the drawing on the button will be recolored. You must make a solid representation of what you want to see and then put that on top of the button. Only then does it work.
