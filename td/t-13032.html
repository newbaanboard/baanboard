Example-

choice.cont.process:
on.choice:
if spool.open(report$,"EXCEL", 0) then
start.process()
spool.close()
else
choice.again()
endif

Also refer to - spool.line() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_spooling_spool_line),
