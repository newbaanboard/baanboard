Hi,

You can choose a specific location for your button from the form editor.

Open the Form you want in Maintain Forms (in the right VRC), Click on "Edit Form", add a special field in the location that you want, set the type as button and finally set the command (or option) number next to it.

Regarding the button size issue I am not familiar with a native Baan 4GL way to do this. However, a third party tool might do it. Also check this link:
http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=27291

I hope it helps,

- George
