Hi NotPro,

start BwPrint.exe from your Windows system. Click on menu Help->Font Test, you can see all font settings. Now you have to click with the right mouse button exactly onto the font which do you want to change. The font dialog appears. After saving your changes BwPrint updates the registry with new settings. Now you have to choose the font in your field settings of the report layout.

When all other clients of your company needs this font settings you have to export explicit this one registry entry and install it onto the client pc's.

My attached screenshot shows you on Font 24 my own font which I use it as logo on our reports. How you can do this, see the document.
