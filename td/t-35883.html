Hi,

This is what I tried and worked for me:

A) I went to maintain forms, clicked on the "maintain forms fields", went to form 5 & entered an already used help text code.
B) Dump the form
C) Create runtime using ttadv5230m000 for forms & fields.

I worked without logging out & in.

Make sure that:

1) Your text code is working correctly. Maybe try to put (replace) it on another session that already has a Baan help
2) The text is filled correctly in the maintain form field (form 5), as I did in (A).

I hope it helps,
