Removing scripts/reports from ttadv tables doesn't solve your problem (and it's potentially dangerous :) ).

The objects of the reports and scripts are always present (and still used by bshell), you have to remove them from $BSE/application directories.

In particular:
$BSE/application/PPB61U-a-lvd/oMMM (directories of the scripts object)and
$BSE/application/PPB61U-a-lvd/oMMML (directories of the report object

where PP is the package
MMM is the module
L is the language
