Hi,

You can use the pos function (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_string_operations_pos_rpos).

For example:

lond d.pos
d.pos = pos("abcd","d")
| d.pos will have the position of the letter "d"
I hope it helps,

- George
