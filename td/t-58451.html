What session? Is it a custom session? Some sessions in Baan will not allow you delete the records until you have cleared all the child or reference records first. And some session will not let you delete something that had any work done on it. Of course the messages are usually a little more clear, but I know there are a couple cases where it comes back with Update cancelled.
