I will assume that you just want to DISPLAY the City and Country on the FORM.

You will have to define another field with enough space and put that on the form instead of the two separate fields. Then in the before.display, assign the values:

strip$(city) & " " & strip$(country)

On the other hand, if the fields have to be entered into, then you have to position the country below the city in order to make it less awkward. It is not possible to control horizontal positioning much.
