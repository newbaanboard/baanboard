Hi,
you may want to take a look at Infor's U7850B US "Writers Guide and Reference for Infor XML 6.4 Help Customization". You can download the PDF via Infor365.com DocLink.

I've just recently "discovered" this guide myself. ;)

Basic help-customization (changing existing help-texts) in LN can be done by enabling help-customization in the users webtop-profile. This will add a "customize" button in the webhelp of the customized session.

Andreas
