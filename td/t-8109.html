Hi,
We did try printing of Invoices through crystal. But I wouldn't suggest this route. Crystal is a reporting tool and a document management system. Your requirement may be just printing of invoices, but in future you may want to fax, email invoices and that can be on demand or automatic. Also, if you are using tax, then it will require some logic in the report. We implemented invoices with Baan generating a Flat file. This is then picked up by Document Management system- e.g. Stream serve or Formscape. The invoices then can be printed in mail format, or faxed automatically or emailed.
To answer your question, yes it is possible to have formatted Invoice from crytal, but that will not be practical.
