Simple answer: not possible.
Forms ordering is always based on indexes. Indexes are alway seen in ascending order in the forms.
To reach your goal you need an extra index that is ordered (in ascending order) as you need.
By example, assuming you have an index1 made by field1 and field2 (and field 2 is numeric):

normally, you have this order
field1 field2
1 1
1 20
1 21
1 22
2 5
2 7
.... ....

You have to add, by example, a field3 that is filled in this way:
field3 = 1000 - field2 (or something like that)
so you can have:

field1 field2 field3
1 1 999
1 20 980
1 21 979
1 22 978
2 5 995
2 7 993
..... ..... ......

Adding an index2 composed by field1 + field3 you can have the result you want. (sounds little tricky, don't you?...:D )
