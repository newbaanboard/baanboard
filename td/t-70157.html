Hi Gianni,

You're correct in your statement that, the conditional formatting can't work on Multiple Tables.

Because the On-Hand field is a sum of all the warehouses, we can't put a conditional formatting on that.

I can quickly give you an idea / possible process that can be followed for this specific example.

1. Highlight the On-Hand Quantity in the Inventory Dashboard Session (standard conditional formatting feature)
2. Users must always go to the Warehouse-Item inventory Session to check the stock in their specific warehouses
3. In the Warehouse-Item Inventory Session, we can restrict the users to see the inventory in the warehouses for which he/she is authorized for.

I'm using ERP LN 10.5 version to map this and it worked for me.
