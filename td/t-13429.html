En -
Sometime when you add a form, the form before it has to be updated to allow you to switch to the new one.

In Maintain Forms, for Form 1 (the original), under the 'Specific' Pull down, go to the 'Standard Commands' option. Check and make sure the 'Next Form' / 'Last Form' options are checked (last tab).
