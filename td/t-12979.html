Hitesh Shah
15th September 2003, 16:09
Instead of writing

WHERE tcibd001.item LIKE ".*:char.item.*"

following code will help

char.item = ".*" & strip$(shiftl$(char.item)) & ".*"
....
WHERE tcibd001.item LIKE :char.item
