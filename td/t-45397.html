Hi,

After you have edited, saved and compiled your report:

1) Go to maintain sessions
2) Find the session you want to link the report
3) Make sure the session is in the current VRC if not Copy it to the current VRC.
4) Click on the reports button in form 1 (not form 2)
5) Add the report there.
6) Save.

I hope it helps,

- George
