Hi,

You can use uuid.format$() to convert the compact representation into standard representation which is 36 in length.

string temp.uuid(22)
string final.uuid(36)

temp.uuid = uuid.generate$()
final.uuid = uuid.format$(temp.uuid)

Or just generate 2 uuid's and concat them, or append the timestamp(dte$()) to the generated uuid.
