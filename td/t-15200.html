4th May 2004, 03:29
Create/Modify - $BSE/lib/bse_vars file
Add the entry with whatever parameters you like-
BAAN_WIN_TITLE='LMSV-[%s-%S][%c-%C]'
You can then see the Title on top of every BaaN session
