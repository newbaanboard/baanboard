Hello,

if you don't have any EDI (ElectronicDataInterchange) knowledge, i think it is to difficult to explain how it works.

We use this internal EDI between our sites with BaaN IV and LN for the process you've described.

Company A purchases goods from Company B
B delivers the goods to A
A receives goods from B
B invoices the goods to A

All this is done with EDI Messages.

In BaaN IV you'll find it under Distribution -- Electronic Data Interchange.

I only have a inhouse documentation in german. This doc can't be shared.
