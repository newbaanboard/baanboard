You cannot use a function call directly in embedded SQL. You need to bind it:

!Select tipcf400.amnt
!from tipcf400
!where tipcf400._index2 = {"S00000005",2," CA"} and
!tipcf400.cnsc = " PV1" and
!tipcf400.exdt < :1
!wherebind (1, utc.num())
!Selectdo
!myPriceV = tipcf400.amnt
!endselect
