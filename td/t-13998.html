We have specific devices with larger pages sizes for excel. We also use the print expression to turn off the header after the first time in prints. On some specific reports that we developed we set the pages size to 1, so we get the header once and then all the lines - nothing blank. Basically what I am saying is if you do not want to customize the report - then customize a device to eliminate some of the blank lines. The larger the page size the fewer the blank lines.
