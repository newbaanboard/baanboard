Hi,
Double click the field in the report editor. Session "Report Fields" (ttadv3534s000) opens. Go in the third tab "Conditions", there we have the field called as "Print Condition". The condition mentioned in it can be used to controls the display of that particular field in reports.
For getting the page number you can use predefined variable "lattr.pageno".

I hope it helps you to solve your problem.
