Hi,

in Baan IV you can use the text.to.buf() (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_text_fields_text_to_buf) function:

#include <bic_text>
string buffer(80, 10)
long lines
...
lines = text.to.buf("tdpur040.txta", language$, 10, buffer)
|buffer is now filled with the text
...


In LN you can also use TEXT_CONTENT (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=58301)
