ssamuel
4th December 2006, 03:57
The current package VRC is a development parameter. When you create a user, you indicate the startup company to work in. Similarly, as a developer, you must also indicate the package VRC you are working (customizing) in. This helps prevent copying, modifying or deleting standard objects.
Just as you have production, test and development companies, the package VRCs keep standard, production and deveopment objects separate.
