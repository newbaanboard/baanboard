You cannot set conditions for labels. To achieve what you want, you just declare a variable in report script or program script, something like this:

(extern if program script) domain tcmcs.str20

if <conditions> then
label1 = "ABCDEF"
else
label1 = ""
endif

and on your report you just put label1 as a form field instead of the label...


Good luck!
