This steps must be done by your Baan Administrator.
1.- Put a support order to SSA describing your problem.
2.- In the most of the cases, they will send you a object upgrade. This object have the name otfgld4201 o maybe the can send you a solution in a compressed form or in the format of PCM dependig if you have installed that tool.
3.- Depending on the format of the solution your Baan Administrator will install following the indications from SSA global.
