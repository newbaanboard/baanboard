We have had the same problem occur and Baan support had us modify a couple of db_resource settings to:

LOCK_RETRY = "10*100,10*500,10*1000"
ORA_TIMEOUT = {0,60,60,60,0} (We use Oracle for our DB)

This corrected the problem. Have you searched the Baan on-line knowledge base? It records a lot of previous solutions too.
