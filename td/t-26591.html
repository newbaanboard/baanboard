Huh, just found the thread in the manufacturing section about this problem.
So after reading that one it's more clear.

You can't use a generic item in production order because you can't produce a generic item. You first have to configure a variant of the generic item which creates a new 'real' item which is derived from the generic one. You do that by means of the Product Configurator Session (choose options of features).
After that you have to use the the 'Generate Product Variant Structure for Project' Session tipcs2220m000 (from which you can launch Product Configurator) which creates a new item, a Project(optional, depends on your pcf parameter), a real bom and a routing.
This newly created item can be produced in a production order.

Hope this makes it more clear,
