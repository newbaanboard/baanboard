Hello,

for barcode in PCL you have to extend the charactersets with BARDIMMS or
ELP (enhanced laser printing) Software.
(See www.stethos.com)

2D-Barcode DataMatrix ECC200 is typeset 24820T, PDF417 is typeset 24850T
(PCL font escape sequence)

Hello Richard,
if you have the BarDimm Module installed, create a Barcodescript in $BSE/lib/barcode/hp for Datamatrix.

I've done it, "type12" is my Datamatrix ECC200 barcodescript.

Barcode DataMatrix ECC200 is 24820T

In reportfield assign the barcodetype to the field.

That's all.
