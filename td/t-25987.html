We were getting the below message, when trying to click on a Form Command (eg: Process) in a session:

Illegal value for size argument(0) for sym
Cannot continue in <session> in DLL: ottstp_stddll (display.enumerate) [alloc.mem]

Reason:
There was a "question" eg: "tfcust0001" defined in the Form Command, but that question did not existed physically in LN.

Solution:
Created a question as defined in Form Command.
Ran the session again, it ran successfully without any errors.
