I know this is an old post, but I had the same problem of the select device session popping up before hitting continue. My co-worker found the problem and it is rather simple and will make you feel rather stupid. On form 1 of Maintain Sessions, change the start option. It is most likely on 23 - print.data. I changed mine to 7 - refresh and done deal it works fine. Just thought I would share this.
