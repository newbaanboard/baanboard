Look to see if you have the script and object present for tccomdll0025. Depending on service pack, you may have a mismatch in scripts/dlls used in the archiving process. (pre-Infor)/Baan changed the scripts involved with archiving between sp 13 and sp 15 and added/changed a few DLLs to make the process a bit more flexible - like not having to archive general data every time.

Before starting archiving, I would go onto the Infor support site to investigate/load archiving-related solutions if possible.
