10.5 is tricky. You can store the company logo with the company. If you use the SSRS or Infor Reporting connector, the logo will come out on those reports. 10.5 native reports won't support the logo unless you have some solutions installed and are running on 10.7 tools (I believe). You have to have access to the report personalization modeler.
