If form.f and form.t are your form fields , and you are using the standard print command in your session, then you can link it as follows



choice.print.data:
on.choice:
if rprt_open() then
print.table.data()
rprt_close()
endiff

.
.
.

function print.table.data()
{

select tdsls850.*
from tdsls850
where tdsls850.odat BETWEEN :form.f
and :form.t
selectdo
rprt_send()
endselect

}
