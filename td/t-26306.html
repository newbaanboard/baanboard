Seems to me I have done this a couple of ways, but the one I remember the most is:

fp=seq.open(filename,"r")
reof = seq.gets(buffer,1,fp)
while reof=0
count = count + 1
reof = seq.gets(buffer,1,fp)
endwhile
rc = seq.close(fp)
