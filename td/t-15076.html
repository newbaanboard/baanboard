I have learned that you can add custom fields in the applicable table(s) and they will go out with no problems. However, for incoming information, Baan EDI has most of the applicable table's fields defined in their DLL, but unless you have Baan source code you can not modify the DLL to pull in your new fields.

Please note I'm speaking from the Baan IVc4 version not Baan5.

Hope this helps.
