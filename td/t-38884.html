We print to a Intermec printer (similar to a Zebra) directly from BaaN for about 8 years already, also directly from BaaN, using a UNIX queue, printer is connected directly to the network, printer commands are written ('printed') in the BaaN report. Occasionally some commands are skipped, e.g. it would happen that in a serie of 10 labels suddenly on the 5th label a text field is missing, or on 8th label a barcode is printed 4 times as large as it should be.
Perhaps the network protocol of the printer is not doing a good job in error correction.
