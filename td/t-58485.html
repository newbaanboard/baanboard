startpos = pos(somestring,":")
if startpos = 0 then
startpos =1
endif
endpos = pos(somestring,"/")
x = val$(somestring(startpos; endpos-startpos))

Something like the above should work - assuming the strings are always formated the same. This will not work in just anycase.
