Here is broad explanation of broad purchase order cycle (quotation/contracts excluded).

1. PO entry in tdpur4101m000(tdpur040,041)
2. Maintain receipts/approval in tdur4120/21m000 (pur045,ilc401,inv700,ilc301,ltc102,tfgld41* all transaction table and history tables like tiitm001,tdilc101, tdinv001,tdltc001 for balances).
3. Optional tdpur122m000 (same as receipt)
4. Enter invoice manually in finance tfacp1110s000 (tfacp200,tfgld's)
5. Match invoice tfac1130/31s0000 (tdpur046,tfacp200)
6. Approve invoice in tfacp1140s0000( tdpur045,046,tfacp200,tfgld's)
7. process delivered in tdpur4223m000(tdpur045).
