Hi,

The flow of the standard program is
Main.section:
Subsection:

For the form, form.1: is the main section, but then you have to program your code in a subsection, for a form that would be
init.form: (first time form is read) or
before.form: (every time form is read) or
after.form:

For more info see 4GL Form sections (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_4gl_features_4gl_form_sections)

Good luck!!
