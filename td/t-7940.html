couple of ways to do it.
1. have your batch file launch word with a macro that will print the rtf document.
2. there are a lot of freeware programs to convert rtf to other formats so convert it in the batch file to ps and then send it to a postscript printer. I assume they want rtf as the format is nicer than the ascii output so could just use a postcript device in baan and then send that ouput to a postscript printer. If you do not have postscript printer then use a program called redmon that will redirect it to a non-postscript printer.
