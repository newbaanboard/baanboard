I believe that is a generic domain / formatting error.

Can you say - what is the exact calculated value with decimals that you calculate and return? Your format of ZZ9VD99 requires 2 places after the decimal, so if you return 12.0 or anything with just 1 decimal, there may be a formatting error upon display.. ? This would explain why it is solved if they add a 0 on the end. Also, whatever the user types is automatically converted to the display format, so no formatting errors will occur then, that may not be true of your calculated value. Also, you mention the error number - do you get a text for the error and what is the syntax of that..?
