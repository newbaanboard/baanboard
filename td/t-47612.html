If you want to arrive at work day you roughly divide by 7 and multiply by 5.So say if the difference is 21 then work days would be 15.If you get some number not divisble by 7 for e.g.23 then find the lowest multiple of 7 and multipy by 5/7 and add the remainder,so in this way 23 calendardays would result in 17 working days. Ofcourse, you can build holidays etc in this calculation.
