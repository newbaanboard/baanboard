Hi Pjohns,

why so complicated, if the sollution is trivial and at hand?

You are running HP-Ux11, with it comes a product called CIFS, which is Samba compiled with an HP logo. :-)

It is supported by HP, so just call them up and ask them to help you configure it.

It solves your issue, without installing anything new on the server or customizing anything in Baan.

Samba will allow all your windows user to map his or her home directory on their windows PC as a drive. The user copies his file to this drive with the explorer and just impports the file. Because this is the home directory and the Baan bshell is also running in there, they do not even need to specify the absolute path.

Trivial,

Markus
