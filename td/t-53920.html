Hi.

I'm on Baan IVc4, but I guess that the barcode principle hasn't changed since then ... And I'm on Unix. (We recently had problems with barcode, but that came from a change in porting set 6.1c.07.15).

So have a look at the files $BSE/lib/barcode/*/type*. These are shell scripts!

You may also have a look at the printer drivers in $BSE/lib/printinf/*/*; you should see an entry like "barcodedir=hp," there.
