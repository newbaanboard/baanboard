My first suggeston is to not use the mouse. Many new to Baan end up in situations that they can not get out of.

The control sequences is what you need. Many of them need to be done more than once. When editing your header, use the command to resize the window. This will give you 4 corners of your header. Us the arrow keys on your keyboard to go down 1 line and use the control keys again to leave resize the window. Now you will have 2 lines to work with.
