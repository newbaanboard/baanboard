In ttadv2130m000 you will find a button "Next variant" and "Prev variant" (form 1), and there is a display field "Present Variants". If you see "Present Variants" <> "None" you can use the buttons to go to the variant you want to delete and then press delete, until you see "Present Variants" = "None"
