You can use your report script for that.

In your report script define the field say -

extern domain tcmcs.str100 my_field


detail.1:
before.layout:

my field = strip$(ldel.addr(1,1))&'' - '' & strip$(ldel.addr(1,2))&'' - '' &.......... | Define however you want


Now, use this my_field in your report layout.
