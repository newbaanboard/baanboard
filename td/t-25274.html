After your check in the check.input: if length of input > 30 then
message("not allowed to enter description longer than 30 pos")
stp.reset.value() |this resets the value of the field to the previous value
choice.again() | this stops execution of the program, passes control back to user.

You can also use set.input.error()

Hope this helps..
