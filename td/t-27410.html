2 Empty Rows before the detail may also be caused, by the window size of the detail. Check in "Maintain Report Layouts" the number of Lns.
If its 3, mark the Layout, and Edit. In The Editor move your Layout to the first line, then go in the Menu of the Editor to Mask, Resize Window. Resize the Window to one line.
Recompile the Report and try again.
