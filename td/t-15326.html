What's the DNC session? Is that a standard session? Do you have source code to debug this with? A little more info would help us help.

As a complete shot in the dark, the array errors are usually the data being put into the array, or the subscript id it's trying to fill. For example, your error may be saying that they are trying to put data into an array at subscript '-2', which is illegal. It may be fine on the report as called from one session, but the other session may pass bad data to it.
