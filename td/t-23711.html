You can amend the company by adding the _COMPNR variable to the WHERE clause of your queries.

E.g:

select tccom999.*
from tccom999
where tccom999._compnr = 200
and tccom999._index1 = 'tum-tee-dum'
for update
selectdo
bla bla bla
endselect
