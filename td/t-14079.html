Hi,

although we have HP-UX instead of your Solaris, I guess that you also have a sendmail.cf file on your machine (/etc, /etc/mail or so), and that sendmail itself might be the transport program that is called by mailx itself.

So, you could have a look at 'man sendmail', and you should define your mail server as "smart relay host"; all mail that cannot be delivered locally (user a on machine mail server sends a mail to user b on that machine) is given to that machine -- just one level up -- and maybe that machine gives it to "the internet" and so on.
