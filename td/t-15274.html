You can use it in your own way. I personally use them for:
a) to group all components created for one project/application.
b) when I change/remove/dupplicate components for testing/debugging which have to be reverted afterwards, then I add comments indicating that in the search arguments
c) if you have several Baan developpers at your company, you may find it interesting to use a search argument field to enter the user-name of the person in charge of the component

Etc etc
