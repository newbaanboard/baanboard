morpheus
26th February 2003, 14:19
Thomas,
Sorry, I should have also mentioned about resizing the window!!
Anyway,
1. Go to the editor.
2. Select "Resize window" under "Form" option.
3. Use arrow keys to increase/decrease the layout size.
4. Press "space bar" once you are finished with the resizing.
5. Insert lines.
