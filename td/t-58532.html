Single Dimentional Array:
Data_Type array_name(x)
example: long qty.arr(2)
you can store the values in...
qty.arr(1) = your value1
qty.arr(2) = your value2

Double Dimentional Array:
Data_Type array_name(x,y)
example: long qty.arr(2,3)
you can store the values in...
qty.arr(1,1) = your value1
qty.arr(1,2) = your value2
qty.arr(1,3) = your value3
qty.arr(2,1) = your value4
qty.arr(2,2) = your value5
qty.arr(2,3) = your value6


Please note all the elements of an array will have the same data type.
You can declare long, double, and string arrays

Just define the array of an appropriate length say..
long qty.arr(100)
then use any conditional statement & pass a variable in array instead of a number..
Example:
LONG qty.arr(100), i
FOR i = 1 TO 100
IF qty.arr(i) <= 0 THEN
CONTINUE | Next iteration
ENDIF
ENDFOR

You can put a record count in your script, to count the number of records & use record_count instead of "100" in above example with for loop.
The above is just an example. you can use your own logic.
