You can also use print overlays on a color laser. You can design a logo or form (I often do this when printing legal disclaimers on different forms) in word or another program and print it to a file. There is a program for doing a PCL overlay from this printed file. The file is loaded into the printers memory, then through Baa/LN you use an escape sequence to print the overlay in memory, and then your report is printed on top of it. It all come out together.
