It is the no. of bytes that are consumed.
So , in case of string the physical length & this length would match.
But in case of double it will be 8
Date - 4 ,
long - 4 ,
Integer - 2 ,
Enums - 1,
Multibyte String - 32 & so on.

If you want to know the physical length it can be seen in Maintain Domains according to the domain.
