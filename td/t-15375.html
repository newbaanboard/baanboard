18th May 2004, 17:13
Well for example on an HPLaserjet 6MP - go to Printers/Properties/General Tab/Printing Preferences button/Effects tab & there you will see a section for watermarks. Also do the same for Advanced tab/Printing Defaults/Effects tab.

Name your printer in Windows something like "STATEMNT" for example. Setup a device with the same name in Baan. Then when you print to that device it will pick up the watermarks setting.

However, this watermark will remain the same until you change the setting in the driver.
