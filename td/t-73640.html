Just create a report script. If you know the conditions for summing lines #2 and #6, then you can just do something in the script in before.layout event for the details layout. Then maybe print it in after.report layout or something like that. Sample below you can tweak for your purposes.


declaration:
extern domain tcamnt cumm.totl

before.program:
cumm.totl = 0

details.1:
before.layout:
if somecondition then
cumm.totl = cumm.totl + tisfc010.rutm
endif
