Try below:

where tdsls961.clot not like ".*[aA].*"
or
where tdsls961.clot like "[^aA]*"


Reference: Wildcard characeters (http://www.baanboard.com/programmers_manual_baanerp_help_functions_expressions_runtime_expr_compile)
