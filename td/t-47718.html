Hello,

you have to know that the Baan EDI module can`t create messages in the EDIFACT format, because Baan has it own internal standard called BEMIS (BAAN Electronic Message Interchange System). This Baan messages mut be translated to an EDI Standard fromat (e.g. EDIFACT or VDA) by an external EDI translator.
