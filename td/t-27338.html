Hi,

In Baan V, you should use disable.field() & enable.field() functions. But once it is disabled in the before.field section, you won't be able to get to the before.zoom section.

My advice is to let the user type what he wants, then you can check what he typed in the after.input section. If it wasn't "legal" you can delete it.

I hope it helps,

- George
