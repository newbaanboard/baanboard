You have to convert the utc values to date values:

utc.to.date(tdpur406.ddte, year1, month1, day1, dummy, dummy, dummy)
utc.to.date(tdpur401.ddta, year2, month2, day2, dummy, dummy, dummy)

Now calculate the days:

date.to.num(year1, month1, day1) - date.to.num(year2, month2, day2)
