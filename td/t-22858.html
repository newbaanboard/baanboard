I only know how to do this in Baan IV but perhaps something simular is possible in Baan V:

In "maintain reports" go to "layouts"
zoom to "special" -menu
choose "Copy from other layout" ttadv3535s000
choose language and report you want to copy layouts from
select layouts you want to copy
choose continue process

And voila, the layouts from another language (and/or other report) are copied to your current report.

Perhaps this helps ?

Danny
