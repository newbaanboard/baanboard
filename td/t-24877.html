Yes, not very clear. Maybe when you mean, when selecting on a date field, and you want on that date or previous? Only 1 record..


select tablefields
from table
where table.item = :my.item |* Hopefully on an index...
and table.date <= :my.date |* anything with your date or lower
order by table.date DESC |* Puts the criteria in descending order, so
|* The latest date comes first
as set with 1 rows |* Assures it will get only 1 record, the one
|* closest to your date
