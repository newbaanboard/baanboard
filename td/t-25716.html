Make sure each of the items are set-up correctly at the bottom. I make the menu I am editing at the bottom be yellow and bold for the current menu. The others should be white and bold. Make sure all of them are correct in all three menus. You may also need to run ttadv5214m000 (Create RTDD for forms and menus). And as a reminder make sure you log off and back into the system when changing menus.
