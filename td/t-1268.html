Locate "Bwprint Document" or something similar in the "File Types". Do "Edit". In the actions, you should see "Open" and this should be the default action (it will be in bold if it is). If you don't then do a "New" and fill:
Action: New
Application used to perform action: <path>\bwprint.exe "%1"

That should do the trick.
