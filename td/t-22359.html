That was the window registry that i had to change but i had to change it to to 30d40 (hex value) from 0. This resolved the problem. Here is my current registry settings:

"ForegroundLockTimeout"=dword:30d40

[HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Control Panel\Desktop]
"ForegroundLockTimeout"=dword:30d40
