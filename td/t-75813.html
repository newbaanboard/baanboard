tcibd200.ocys uses domain tcosys with constants:
tcosys.sic (1) = "SIC"
tcosys.mps (2) = "Planned"
tcosys.fas (3) = "FAS"
tcosys.mnl (4) = "Manual"

So like @mark_h stated: tcibd200.ocys = ltoe(2) or tcibd200.ocys = tcosys.mps should work.
