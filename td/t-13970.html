I think the Problem is:

--------------------------------------------------------
To print on a Win98 Client the PrinterDefinition in ttaad3500m000:

Example:
Device: P51_W98
Device Queue: printserver.printer

---------------------------------------------------------
To print on Win2000/XP Client the Printerdefinition in ttaad3500m000:

Example:
Device: P51_XP
Device Queue: \\printserver\printer

---------------------------------------------------------
You see the differenz in Device Queue of ttaad3500m000

The Win2000/XP Client do not know what is "printserver.printer" he needs "\\printserver\printer"

hope it helps
