Change the field ttadv360.desc "The description of the menu. The description can be used as a special field in the menu."

Also in the menu during edit: press <Esc>-F which takes you to session "Maintain Menu Data" (ttadv3164s000). In this session you were allowed to do changes in the box/color options that (indirectly) influences the boxes at the bottom of the ASCII screen in which the subsequent menus are shown.
