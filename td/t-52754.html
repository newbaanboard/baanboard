Hi,

You have to go to Maintain Forms (ttadv3100m000) > Spec. Options and fill the fields, then go back to Maintain Forms and click on Buttons, and fill the same number you did in spec. options.
The default position is on the right side of the form.

If you want to put the button somewhere else check this links:
