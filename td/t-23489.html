I suggest using the GUI to your advantage. If there is a row that you want most of the information from a previous row to be using on a row being inserted, select that row before doing the insert. When Baan displays a 0 or blank to the screen, the GUI will display the previous occurrance values as default values.

Otherwise, whenever the screen is full of information, it will blank the screen and place you on the first row.

Depending on your skill level, you may try to do a split window like sales order lines in Baan IV where you display multiple lines in the session on the top of the window, and have an input session at the bottom. Depending on your need it may be more trouble that it is worth.
