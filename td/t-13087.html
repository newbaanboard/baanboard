assuming that i typed in 15 lines of text for my item , then how it is stored ??I don't know about Oracle, but in SQL Server the texts are split into 240-character blocks and stored separately in tttxt010 under the same text-number (ie your textn=6589) and language-key (ie 2=English), but different sequence-numbers (1, 2, 3...).

As Mark hinted, if you simply include your text-field in the report, the Baan Text-Manager does the job in the background of "reconstituting" the text and figuring line-breaks etc. for the report.

The text should be in tttxt010 of which ever company you are using. You can also use tttxt1100m000(Maintain Texts).

Yes - if you wanted only the first two lines you could use text.to.buf to put it into an array variable. Then just print the first two lines.
