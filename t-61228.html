When the "Perform Inventory Valuation" (whina1210m000) session is run, the option "Compare Inventory Value With - FTP" can be selected. The FTP will be retrieved from the (receipt) price of the "Item - Standard Valuation Prices" (ticpr3540m000) session.

Underlying table is ticpr340.
