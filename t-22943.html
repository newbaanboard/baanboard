Multi-Edit (www.multiedit.com) was my editor of preference for a long time.

I have developped a set of macros for this editor that will do wonderful things. It should do all that the scite editor does, as well as auto indent and reformat declaritives. (Makes the declaration section very nice)

It has some rules for formatting case while, if, repeat, select... endselect etc syntaxes, and knows how to do keyword matching, including the jumping over of nested expressions (while matching if`s jumps over nested if's).

I recommend the editor, and if you have it, or want it, I will gladly at no cost, send you the baan stuff (including an install guide).

I am not associated in any way, except as a customer with the purveyors of multi-edit editor. But it is, for me, a wonderful tool. I would not want to live without it.
