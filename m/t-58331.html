Found the solution to it, and in matter of fact it wasn't really a problem and the entries weren't missing.

My problem was on a 64bit machine, and in thoses cases MMC defaults to the 64bit view, where the Baan Application DCOM config is not visible, well, because it is 32 bit.

Launching MMC with the -32 flag (start > run > mmc -32) and adding the Component Services snap-in afterwards, I was able to see the "missing" entries and define their permissions as expected.

more information:
https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/aa815172(v=vs.85).aspx
