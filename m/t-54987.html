Hello,

create two Drivers in $BSE/lib/printinf with different INIT PCL sequence.
One for BIN 1 and One for BIN 2

Assign this driver to your BaaN device eg:

XP6250-B1 for BIN 1
XP6250-B2 for BIN 2
