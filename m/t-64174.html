We have a mix of users that use the Baan text editor OR notepad. By far most users use the Baan text editor. Due to the nature of their job and interaction with Baan, the existing features work best for them as they have 'standard' text which is used on quotes/orders/invoices which they copy from an original text number.

However, for some of our staff, we have set up the use of customized text fields so that they can copy large blocks of text out of a work instructions/quality documentation and paste them directly into Baan without retyping.

Mark brings up a very good point regarding the control characters, but I honestly have never come across that issue in our environment. We are a Microsoft Windows based organization.
