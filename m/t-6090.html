Hi Andy,

The very latest GA version is iBaan ERP 5.2a (internally called "Reger"). However, only two customers are currently running this version. Most customers choosing for Baan 5 choose to go live on iBaan ERP 5.0c (internally called Corelli). This is because several enhancements (integrations, translations) are not available on 5.2a but are on 5.0c.
