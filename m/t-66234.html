Hi I remember having kind of similar problem and worked around by changing compatibility of C:\Program Files (x86)\Infor\BW\Baan IV\bin\bwPrint.exe to "Windows XP(Service Pack 3)". You can do this by right clicking on the bwprint.exe file and chosing properties>Compatibility. Hope you find this useful.
