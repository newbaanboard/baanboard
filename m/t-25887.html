Hi Berthe,

I guess its too late for a reply but maybe this will help someone else.
You need to make entries in "Maintain Paper Types" and "Maintain Fonts by Paper Types" sessions to be able to use Landscape mode of printing. Also, you need to use this new paper type when configuring the device for Crystal. You can configure a new device for crystal and set the paper type for landscape. Once done, you are good to go.
