Dear all,

Since I worked in a company that implements both Baan and Business Objects (on completely different customer sets), I can't help but digress the discussion from ERP to Business Intelligence (BI) side:
Wouldn't it be better to build a data warehouse on top of your currently running BaanIV and then run Business Objects (BO) on top of the data warehouse? Running BO directly on BaanIV, although it may work perfectly, will just make BO into a very expensive reporting tool, while you know that BO can be used for much more than that
