Dear ramankaplish,

the procedure is as follows:

1. Add a new Job e.g. JOB1
2. Go to the session you want to add to the job
3. Go to the form of the session -> EXTRA -> Make Job and connect the session with the JOB1
(the form feature "Make Job" must be activated).
4. Answer the job specific questions in the automaticly opened dialogue.

This means you link the sessions to the job FROM the Session e.g. tdsls4404m000. You can not add them directly in the Job Management.

Please find enclosed the quick guide for job management within Baan IV.
