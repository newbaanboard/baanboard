Theoretically, it might be possible, because that's what segmented domains are good for - at least in theory. In practice, you will run into troubles somewhere very soon, because the project segment is often checked in many circumstances and for sure some programmer might have forgotten somewhere that the project segment might have a size of zero. And another exciting new case for the fabulous BW message window....

Disable PCS in the "implemented software components", then the project segment will be greyed out always. And then tell your users to ignore that field. In the long run that will cause a less headaches for all people involved then messing around with the segements.
