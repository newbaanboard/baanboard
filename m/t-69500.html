Hello,
the table tt txt 010 stores the textlines .
field tttxt010.ctext is the textnumber
field tttxt010.clan stores the text language
field tttxt010.seqe is the text line number
field tttxt010.text stores the text (string 240)

Example:
the item text in tcibd001 (Item master) is stored as text number in field tcibd001.txta.
This text, for example, consists of 6 lines
The text exists in language "2" (English)
Therefore in tttxt010 are 6 records

textnumber
language
line = 1
1st textline

textnumber
language
line = 2
2nd textline

textnumber
language
line = 3
3rd textline
.....................................
