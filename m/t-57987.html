BOD for those that were not aware: Business Object like a PO, requisition or invoice.

Process: a request from a non-system of record (SOR) object to a SOR object to process a transaction. Example: a third party e-Procurement application could send a request to ERP to absorb a requisition for further processing. This would be a ProcessRequisition BOD.

Sync: the system of record publishes a BOD on the service bus for other non-SOR application for further processing. For instance a SyncPurchaseOrder BOD that can be absorbed by Business Intelligence to report on a MyDay page or to a 3rd party Payables system for a 3-way match.

It might be worth checking out the OAGi (http://www.oagi.org/dnn2/)site in case you would need more info around BODs and other OAGi documents.
