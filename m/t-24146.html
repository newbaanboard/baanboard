Hi,
I'm using Baan 5.0c now (before migrate from Baan 4). Several diff. between Baan 4 & 5 from :
Viewing Side:
Baan 5 more friendly just like Ms. Windows viewing and easy to convert to other Ms. Office appl. (excel, word, txt). For my user more easier than Baan 4.
Common data Side: terminology of Supplier & Customer in Baan 5 is Business Partner (can be Supplier or Customer only, can be both for one code).
Distribution Module side:
Baan 5.0c Invoicing can be base on packing Slip (applicable for our Co. due separately invoicing for one SO).
Baan 4 Invoicing based on Sls.Order (One SO, Several Packing Slip, One Invoice).
Invoicing has own module in Baan 5.0
