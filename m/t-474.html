Hi Erik,

If you look in
Finance/General Ledger/Intergration with other Modules/Miscellaneous
you will see
session Maintain Inventory and WIP Transaction Accounts(tdinv8150m000)

There must be at least 1 record in this session for each Transaction Origin/Financial Transaction combination which is in your error log. You can have multiple records because there may be time when you want a specific item or warehouse to go to different ledger account, but at least one should exist for integration to work.
