David,

We do not generate Material Safety Datasheets from Baan, but do store them in PDF format on the server and can access them as needed.

You can even create a session to allow your users to click a button and have the .pdf document display.

If you wanted Baan to generate MSD sheets, your developers should be able create a session to allow you to enter the required data and then print out the MSDS information which would also give you your results.
