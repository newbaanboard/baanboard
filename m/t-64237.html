I am not sure what you are looking for. But here's my answers from what I understand.


You can try using ZZZZZZZZZZZVD99 kind of format instead of %A005.

The Z's represent the numbers before decimal. The 9's represent the numbers after decimal.

Hence, if you want to display:-

15.99
You can have :- ZZVD99

15.99210
You can have :- ZZVD99999

1214151251.15125112
You can have :- ZZZZZZZZZZVD99999999

I hope this clears your question.
