We also use this to accept orders from a customer with high mix/low volume/frequent sales orders. We use an email parsing tool to monitor an email box. Scripts create the orders1, 2, 3 files from the email message and upload to Baan server. Baan Job periodically checks upload location for files, processes any if they exist.

Do make sure your system is properly patched because there may be problems that would make it impossible to troubleshoot.
