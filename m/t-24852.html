In Baan IV, the integration is defined into two main aspects - Transaction origin and the financial transaction. A few examples

for GRNs (Goods receipt) the origin is Purchase and the Fin transaction is Receipt.
for Purchase Variance the origin is Purchase and the fin transaction is Result.

A detailed help of all the possible Transaction origins and the related Financial transactions and the related double entries are provided as a hel under the following menu :

1. Finance --> GL --> Integration with other modules --> Miscellaneous --> Maintain inventory and WIP transaction accounts.
2. Open the session and go to Help and click on "Using session"
3. You will see a help screen and click on "Inventory and WIP transaction accounts"
4. A new help screen will open and you can see a link "Integration with other modules"
5. Click on that and press Relations and you can find all the related integration transaction details.

Hope this helps.
