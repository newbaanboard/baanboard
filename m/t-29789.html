There were definitely problems with old BWclients working properly with XP - they are fixed in newer versions, so you should upgrade.

Installing to C:\Program Files\Baan\Baan IV makes it easier to have access to Baan4 and Baan5 from the same client - but you can always change the default back to C:\Program Files\Baan if you are only ever using Baan4

The main problems I've had with newer BWclients is with bwprint sometimes not being there, or being an out-of-date version. You have to be careful where you get your BWclient from, some of the 'UNIX versions' don't have bwprint.exe on them!

Don't forget, a newer bwclient means you can play with worktop - which is well worth a try...
