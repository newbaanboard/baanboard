P.S: Just to make sure this isnt any password related issue, Go to Maintain Database Groups {ttdba0120m000} and enter the password again. You do this by first selecting the "baandb" group in tab Database Groups and then going to the tab Maintain Db Group and click on the password. It will open up a small window for DBA Name and Password. Enter the correct name and password, same as what you entered while you installed baan. After this the system will ask you for the New Password for the baandb group, again enter the password you gave during the installation of baan. Save this record and go back to the previous tab "Database Groups" and then click on Create And Dump button. Do this only if you are sure about what passwords you gave during installation of Baan.
