If you have a valid licence agreement and if you get validation keys for licences and install it to your Baan system succesfully - via ttiex3100m000 ttiex3101m000 etc your licences should be active untill "End date" parameter expires. It doesn't matter whether you receive technical support or not after it. Having valid /.brand dir (in Unix) allows you to use your Baan untill End date expires.

This concerns concurrent licences maybe named licences have some differences but i guess it is the same.
