Dear Brother..

To initialize the parameters in ERP LN you should maintain the following steps :

(1) After creating the new company you should specify the entity type for your company "tcemm1170m000" choosing financial or logistic ... etc.
(2) Maintain the segmented domains "ttgfd4412m000"
(3) Initialize parameters for your enterprise
"ttaad0100m000"
"ttaad6102s000"
"tcmcs0295m000"
"tccom5000m000"
"tcccp0100m000"

note:-
1- Before initializing the parameters for your enterprise you should fill your enterprise common data " Addresses, contacts, number groups .....etc"
2- Parameters may vary from an enterprise against another enterprise the above sessions are commonly used for all enterprises types.
