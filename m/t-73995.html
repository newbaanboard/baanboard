It is possible. See
- Infor Document Management Integration Guide for Infor LN
- Infor ES InContext Modeler Development Guide

You can change the mapping of the id2 in the table model and then refresh the session model(s). Note that if you have already documents linked to sn.no in IDM, the Related Information contextual app will not show those anymore after adding id2 to the context message. The same applies when you change the emp.no...
