Hi, a few quick answers:

Baan Object Data Management can link files/documents to Baan sessions using the paperclip functionality. Also you can use revision control for your documents. For example link a specific document with intsructions to a production order.
Or link instructions to your service orders for your field engineers.

With Baan Office integration you can export and import data from and to Baan from MS Office applications

Baan Currency Initial Initialization is from implemention of new currency systems, like implementation of the Euro in your company
