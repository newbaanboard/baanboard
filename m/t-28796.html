Hi Jeffers:
I suppose you have a parent item PI for which, you need to maintain unit effectivity. This item has two items:
- Child Item 1 (CI1) which is purchased and
- Child Item 2 (CI2) which is purchased, but not mandetory in the BOM and exeptions are allowed.
- Then you can PI with "Unit Effective" in the Item Master (tcibd0101s000) as "yes"
- For both the children define the "Unit Effective" in the Ietm Master (tcibd0101s000) as "no"
- Then define the Items-Effectivetity series (tcuef0101m000) for PI.
- After this, define the effectivity units for PI (tcuef0502m000) and in special define the requierment for which you want the exeptions to be.
- After this try to define BOM and then you will see the exceptions field to be accessible in which you can select for CI2

Hope this will solve your problems in defining expections.

Best Luck.
