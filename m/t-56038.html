Hi,

I assume - it's not a formatting issue, it's to do with how it imports the dates. It always seems to want to interpret imported dates as being in US format, except when your day is greater than 12 in which case it can't.

Example :

01/10/07
02/11/07
05/12/07
14/09/07
15/09/07

Dates are stored in Excel as a number of days past 01/01/1900 (which you can see by changing the format of a date to number).

example : 4 june 2009 is 39968

Hence - check the format for dd/mm/yy and also the export settings in b2win.

if the date format is in A1 try this =TEXT(A1,"mm/dd/yyyy")
