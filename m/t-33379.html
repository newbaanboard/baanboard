Hi André,

Fax is nothing but a digital picture. One can theoretically use OCR to convert the picture ito words but you can directly forget it concidering the normal fax quality and the fact that a faxed document can have any layout imaginable depending on your customers. Even processing an E-Mail is not going to be easy for similar reasons. That is exactly the reason why people came up with EDI.

PS: An E-Mail with a standardadized XML attachment (generated say wehen customer enters order details in a web site and presses submit button) would be a solution but which would involve some heavy duty programing.
