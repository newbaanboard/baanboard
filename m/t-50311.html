Hi Günther,

12:44:23 PM is 12:44:23

12:00 AM = 0:00
1:00 AM = 1:00
...
11:00 AM = 11:00
12:00 PM = 12:00
1:00 PM = 13:00
...
11:00 PM = 23:00
