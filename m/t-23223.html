First of all, how can you use barcode with BaaN? Do you have something to enable or I have to develope some plug-in to receive the data from scanner and relay it to BaaN for dataentry?

Typically a barcode scanner works as a keyboard (input device), so everything that you scan is inserted into the active text-field.

You will have to use 4GL functionality to code your way around avoiding additional input as much as possible.
