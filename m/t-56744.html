Hi Pet,

We recently had an audit on our system. I can supply with the info below.

1.) Yes, Smart Audit is still used.
2.) The programs are checked on compilation date, so if you recently modified a standard programs and you official have no source codes available you might have a problem. This means, you can remove sources codes but from the last compilation date can still be retrieved from the objects.
3. The users available in the Baan user table ( tools ) are scanned, afterwards the Smart Audit check during a period of six weeks which users are active. Times, number of logons, used sessions etc. are monitored.

After the audit the audit team might ask you for explanations for issues found on your system, f.i. unknow softwarecomponents or source codes found. Source codes can be distributed by Baan Support self when importing solutions, you mentioned this yourself already. The audit team supplies you with detailed informtion about the audit results after the audit.

Regards,

JP
