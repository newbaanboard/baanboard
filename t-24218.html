The wrapper programs (QKey, RMCgen and rush on these boards) would also work for your needs in 4a and 4b, no patching required. QKey and RMCgen are 3rd party products. My company puts out RMCgen so, take that as you may. I can't speak for the others, but using RMCgen you can generate a library from the session, and you can call that library with parameters for the screen inputs.

In earlier Baan versions where afs is not supported or feasible to patch to, the wrapper programs are a good substitute because they use the old load_dll functions that were working all the way back to Triton.
