We are proud to announce the release of B2Win 4.0, the latest version of our popular product that allows you to take output from Baan into Excel and Word in one click.

With the new version, we added new features like B2PDF, B2XML and B2HTML. In addition, there is better handling for date conversions in Excel.

One interesting feature we added is a Blue Status Bar. Many thanks to all the people at BaanBoard who have explained how this can be accomplished.
