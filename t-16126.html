The master data like "customers" are only copied to the archive company.
If there is no reference from the transaction data to customer number in the live company, then you could delete the customer.
BUT, if you archive again, the "old" customer in the archive will be overwritten with the "new" customer. This is critical, e.g. at Germany, it is by law to protocol the changes of the last 10 years in the master data.
Therefore you have to find a way for documentation of this changes.
