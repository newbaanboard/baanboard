Hi,
I found a way of doing this without custom dll's.Sub Main()
Set BaanObj = CreateObject("Baan4.Application")
BaanObj.ParseExecFunction "ottstpstandard", "zoom.to.menu(" & Chr(34) & "tiitm0101m000" & Chr(34) & ",2," & Chr(34) & Chr(34) & "," & Chr(34) & Chr(34) & ",0,0)"
BaanObj.Quit
Set BaanObj = Nothing
End SubAlso, did you know that you can get away from all of the Chr(34)'s?
VB6 will let to put quotes in quotes.BaanObj.ParseExecFunction "ottstpstandard", "zoom.to.menu(""tiitm0101m000"",2,"""","""",0,0)"
