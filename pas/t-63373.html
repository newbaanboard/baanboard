ION is an integration framework which runs with ERP LN (fp7 and higher versions). It generates alerts via Emails using standard Business Object Documents which is quite essential at times when several important transactions needs to be tracked and the alerts regarding the same is required by the senior management or the key users.

Various other facilities like triggering the workflows, LN message, document flows etc are some other concepts.
