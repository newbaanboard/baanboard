I can't say much about Citrix. The last time I worked with it was several years ago. Back then I found it annoying (slow, copying & pasting didn't work etc.) but that was one of the first versions and I guess that has improved by now.

But that is something most companies don't use and otherwise using VPN is without any problems and should work well for a remote office.
