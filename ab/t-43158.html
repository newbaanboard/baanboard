About EFS Technology
EFS Technology (Enterprise Form Solutions) was founded in 1988, EFS Technology developed the output & document management product AutoFORM LaserNet, a fully integrated and comprehensive range of output document and processing management systems for business, each document management module integrates to form a complete enterprise wide output solution.

EFS Technology market their products under the name AutoFORM LaserNet. AutoFORM LaserNet reflects the historical marketing names of the same product suite, traditionally known in Denmark as LaserNet and AutoFORM in the UK. With the expansion of the range into other parts of Europe, Asia and the USA. EFS Technology now use both brand names to depict the different output & document management modules.
