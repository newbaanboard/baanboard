1) New sponsor: Qtris

We are glad to announce that Qtris has chosen to sign up for a 12 months
sponsorship with Baanboard.com.

About Qtris:
On September 1st last Qtris (located near Amsterdam, The Netherlands), launched
its new website at http://www.qtris.com/ and also its BAAN2 Linux program.

In the world of today IT investments and expenses are under pressure and at Qtris
we believe that BAAN customers should spend their money wisely. That is why we
have come up with our BAAN2Linux program. We think our program provides a faster
and cheaper solution for SSA Global BAAN customers than the standard offerings
that have been around for such long time, e.g.: UNIX and Oracle or Informix,
Windows and Oracle or SQL Server etc.

Especially those BAAN IV_c4 and BAAN V_c customers that are replacing their
current hardware (including O/S and database) and/or upgrading to SSA Global
ERP LN6.1 will benefit the most. Our BAAN2Linux program will show that Linux is
a viable and stable platform and low-cost alternative.

In case you are interested in our BAAN2Linux program and would also like to
start spending your money wisely we invite you to visit our website or
contact us directly today.

2) Baanboard at BWU Belgium Event - 15/09/2005

Baanboard.com's 'boardmaster' Patrick Van der Veken will be presenting an
introduction to the history and workings of baanboard.com during the upcoming
BWU Belgium event. A short live demo is included in the presentation.

The event will take place on Thursday, 15th September 2005 at the Faculty Club,
Groot Begijnhof 14, 3000 Leuven, Belgium.

4) About Baanboard.com and this newsletter

Baanboard.com is a non-profit community driven website aiming to provide help
and information for and by Baan users and developers.

This newsletter is sent to all Baanboard.com members. There is no fixed
interval by which this newsletter and future editions will be published.
