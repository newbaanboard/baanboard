2) Did you know?

Did you know that you can upload multiple screenshots for each post with
automatic thumbnailing?

Each post can contain a maximum of 5 attachments. These can be regular documents
and/or picture files or a mix of both. You can attach pictures or screenshots
in a variety of formats but we *highly* recommend to use either the JPG or PNG
format. Both formats will preserve as much as possible of the quality of the
original image whilst being economical in the space they take up in our forum
database.
We do not allow any kind of (large) bitmap files to be uploaded (e.g. .BMP).
Please convert your BMP images to JPG or PNG first.

To attach images/documents to your post, go to:
Edit screen of your post > Manage Attachments > Browse (and select file(s)) >
Upload > Close Window > Save Changes
