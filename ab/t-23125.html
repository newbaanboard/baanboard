2) New forum for Thai Users

Since a couple of weeks Baanboard has been facilitating a separate forum for
Thai users allowing discussions to take place in the Thai native language.
With the aid of some configuration changes each Baanboard member will be able
to participate in Thai-language topics. Following changes are required to your
user settings:

a) add yourself to the public usergroup 'Thai language' from the list of group
memberships (UserCP > Group Memberships):
http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/profile.php?do=editusergroups
b) select 'Thai' as default forum language in your user options (UserCP > Edit
Options): http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/profile.php?do=editoptions

Please visit our Thai forum at:
http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/forumdisplay.php?f=74

3) Did you know?

Did you know that Baanboard.com forum allows you to 'rate' both forum threads
and forum members?

Threads can be rated by using the 'Rate Thread' shortcut that
you can find in the little menubar above the thread text when viewing a
thread. Threads ratings may go from 0 to 5 'stars' and help other members
to identify top quality discussions. Threads may also be sorted on their ratings
by clicking on the 'Rating' column header in the forum view.

Apart from rating threads you may also with to 'reward' a member for his or her
outstanding contribution. By doing so you will add to his 'repuation' which is
shown as little coloured gem (default is dark green) inside the user info
header of a user's post or in his/her public profile. A user may receive both
positive and/or negative comments but also has the option to disable the public
display of his/her current reputation. Visit your last received reputation
comments at:
http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/usercp.php?

4) Spam List

The popular email address 'boardmaster@baanboard.com' receives on average
several hundreds e-mail messages a day of which 97% is spam. Therefore we have
introduced a domain-based blocking filter on the main @baanboard.com e-mail
addresses that will hopefully make both our mailbox and blacklist more
manageable again.
A list of blocked e-mail domains will be published in the Wiki on a regular
basis at http://www.baanboard.com/twiki/bin/view/Main/BaanboardSpamList.
If your e-mail address falls under one of these domains then it is highly
unlikely that you will be able to contact us through this e-mail address.
In that case use another address if you require to contact us or else use the
private messaging feature on the forum.

See also: http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?t=23063
