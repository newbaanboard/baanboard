Hi all,

If you look in the SIG section of the board, you will notice 2 new fora: Enhancements (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=63) and Verticals (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=62).

Why Verticals?
Well simply because of the demand and the way Baan is moving its product line into that direction. So if you have any questions regarding Baan A&D, Automative, Electronics, Process etc then please post these in the Verticals forum.

Why Enhancements?
We choose to setup this forum to give the Baan community a chance to voice their opinion on the quality and features of the product. If you take into account that not only Baan employees are visiting Baanboard but also alot of independant solution providers and vendors, then there is a chance that you - as user requesting extra, better, enhanced functionality - may find a listening ear.

So enjoy the new fora. We hope it enhances your BB experience!

PS: Members interested in moderating these forums please contact the boardmasters.
