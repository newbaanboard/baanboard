HINT OF THE MONTH: Thread Rating
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Did you know that baanboard has a rating system for any thread on
the board? Rating a thread allows you to show your preference for
certain threads and/or point out high-quality threads to other
members. This will help your fellow members to quickly pick out
threads that are worthwhile to look at.
Threads can be rated with the 'Rate this thread' option at the
bottom of each thread page. The possible choices range from 'Worst' to 'Best' and are displayed in the form of 1 to 5 stars on the forum
display.
