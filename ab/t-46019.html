2) Upcoming site changes

I am contemplating replacing the rather ailing Wiki by a full blown CMS solution
in the near future. As things stand now, the likely candidate will be vbDrupal.
vbDrupal will do away with the need of having the register for both Forum and
Wiki separately and will offer some other nice features like blogs, links
directory etc. I am aiming to have the migration ready by the end of Q1, 2008.
Updates and more news on this will be posted on the forum.
