Subscribed forums can be viewed in your "User Control Panel - Home area"
(UserCP link at the top of the page).

The UserCP Home can be a useful starting place for Baanboard. It shows any new Private Messages you may have received, shows only your subscribed forums, plus any subscribed threads. So at a glance, you can see a lot of relevant information minimising surfing around.
