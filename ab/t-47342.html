Hi all,

Per recommendation of George7 I have added a LinkedIn profile field in the User Control Panel which gives each member the possibility to specify the link to his/her personal LinkedIn page.

You can find the new LinkedIn field by going: User CP (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/usercp.php) > Edit Profile > Additional Information

The LinkedIn information is also displayed on your public profile and searchable via the Members List (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/memberlist.php).
