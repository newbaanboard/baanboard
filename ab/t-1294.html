The main duties/characteristics of a moderator are:
check for posts that violate the posting rules (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/faq.php?faq=bb_faq#faq_bb_disclaimer_privacy) (delete) or are off-topic (move)
correct posts for syntax content
be knowledgeable about the topics the forum is covering
try to help solving issues where possible
generally keep his/her forum 'tidy'
If you feel that you can contribute as a moderator and thus would like to join the Baanboard 'staff' and that you can you visit the forum you wish to moderate on a regular basis (daily; 3 x a week), then please contact me:
