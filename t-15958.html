I think the answer is that sometimes you do have to repeat field inputs twice. Not all field events get executed. After putting all the fields the save/continue/insert/update cause the field events to happen - so sometimes not all events happen correctly. Yes - sometimes you may have to update fields twice to get a field populated. In a couple of cases I could not get fields updated and just went and updated the table directly.
