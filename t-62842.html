Hi hannah,

if I am right you are connecting CR directly to the database tables. Baan software runs in an international context. A user in USA can be seeing an working on the same sales order in USA at the same time as a colleague in China. They must both see a date such as planned delivery date in their own local time!

To solve this problem the date saved in Database is therefore in GMT this is converted to the date and time of a user according to his or her Time Zone setting. If you connect CR directly to the Baan database you allways see the GMT date and time which is 8 hours behind Malaysian local time. So a sales order date captured at 7 am on 04/02/2012 Kuala Lumpur time is saved in database as 11 pm on 03/02/2012 GMT! Strange but true!!

Crystal does have functions for adding times to dates like dateadd() (http://crystaltricks.com/wordpress/?p=153) which you can use to show the local time on your reports.
