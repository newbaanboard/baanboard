Sigit,

Before simply answering your simple question (...), we need to know how you mark/indicate an inactive item. There is no status field (by my knowledge) like there is for bp's, so you probably refer to some own marking/indicating system...?

At our company for instance they decided at the time to use the old fashined astrix (*) as a prefix to the item description. I.e: an inactive/expired item, gets '***' before it's description. In this situation you can easily exclude those items from being displayed in tcibd0501m000 by using query.extend.where() in programming script, or AFS or any wrapper tool...

Hope this helps...?
