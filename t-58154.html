We use Crystal reports XI and Business Objects (SAP) Enterprise XI. Although the Baan driver will no longer be enhanced, it was still available last I knew. Our database is Oracle 10g on a Solaris 10 system, and I have been able to use Oracle's PL/SQL programming language to retrieve just about any data needed for Crystal reports, making the Baan driver unnecessary.
