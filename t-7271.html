The four conditions listed by you are the functional properties to be exhibited by the new company which you want to include under the existing group company. i.e if the new company will also operate under the same home currency, country, dimension and periods then you can include the new company under the exisiting group company.

You just need to create the new company and then preferably (especially tccom000) have the table sharing as applicable to other companies in the group company. Once you attach the existing group company to the new company automatically the properties (periods, dimensions) will apply to the new company.

If your existing group company is not multi site, change the parameter to multisite.
