Solix Technologies delivers enterprise data archiving for Baan applications to manage data growth within the Baan applications. Large databases can decrease application performance, by archiving under-utilized data; performance can be enhanced or restored to optimal levels. Archiving can also reduce storage costs by moving data from Tier 1 storage to lower cost Tier 2 storage. Solix Baan Archiving does not require the creation of a second company. Company specific business rules can drive the archiving processes, rather than rigid, pre-coded archiving rules. Customizations are fully supported. Solix Master Data management repository maintains a single copy of metadata so maintaining referential integrity is much simpler than standard Baan archiving, especially if multiple companies are maintained within the same instance.
