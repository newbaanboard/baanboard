Dear all,

We are glad to announce the launching of Baan Color Changer 2.1

With this new version, you can assign different colors to companies holding the same company number.

If for example, you have company number 100 both on the production server and the test server, Baan Color Changer allows you to assign a different color for each company, and therefore allowing you to diffrenintiate between them, while the Blue Title Bar is similar for both companies (They become the red company and the yellow company...)

This version allows you to have a unique color for any Baan company within your organization and therefore preventing un-necessary mistakes.

A fully operational demo version can be downloaded from our web site at www.nazdaq-it.com
