Hello,

Dont be shocked by this info.

Its not based on transactional data as what is the logistic company that is determined.

In case of logistic transactions like sales order, purchase orders, etc, et. The company that is attached to that particular bus component in your directory service is the determinator.

So, if you can share your customer data across two logistic companies, then if you properly define your bus components in your directory service, then for same customers, you can have different sales order numbers in differnet logistic companies.
