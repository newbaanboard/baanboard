If you have started all your buscomponents from the same baan session/bshell, then you still run into problems as same bshell is responsible for communicating via all the buscomponents.

Can you try starting multiple bshells and then start the buscomponents by spreading them over these bshells. Hopefully this will make it more stable.

Also may be you should consider upgrading your OpenWorld version as one of the clients I worked with has OW2.3 and they have not complained so far about any problems and they do have lots of transactions. Just a suggestion ;)
