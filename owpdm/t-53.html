To your request about iBaan info, Baan is releasing on a regular basis a newsletter called "iBaan News". It include information about new developments and new iBaan compliant solutions. Here's an extract from the May 2001  Issue 1:

-----

The New Web Enabled iBaan DEM


DEM Process Models & Diagrams for Baan IV & iBaan ERP 5 can now be web-enabled thanks to a new agreement signed between Baan and DynaFlow Modeling Solutions. Under this agreement, Baan will offer DynaFlows EZ-Process e-Product Suite to extend the management of DEM-based Business Models, as part of Baan's supply chain-wide E-Business focus. This adds to the iBaan product suite of web enabled applications.

This alliance not only extends the benefits of DEM for Baan IV and iBaan ERP 5 customers, but also provides all Baan Consultants with the entire catalog of Baans DEM Reference Models which will be web enabled and thus available on the Baan Intranet. This will greatly facilitate the demo preparation of any DEM model by removing time-consuming installations and enable the entire project team members to review and validate the model content, regardless of their location and time zones.


Features

> Navigate through Process Models (Baan DEM IV
or iBaan DEM 5) using only a standard web-browser, regardless of location, operating system or ERP software.

> Present DEM business model(s) to your customers,
your project team or your Management
without any software installation. Simply point
your browser to the web-model location (URL).

> Perform collaborative model development by
sharing data online and update your intranet-based
Process Model more easily.

> Add several web & network links to your DEM
process and activities, enabling any document/
URL/content of any format to be attached to your
business processes.

> Access company wide documentation in a context
sensitive way, reducing significantly the
effort of end-users to find the right information.

> Invoke from any Process Activities various applications and scripts (FTP, mailto, dynamic web-pages, non-Baan applications, Web-Forms, etc.).

> Seamless integration with existing Document
Management Systems.

> Copy & paste and Email process diagrams within
seconds.

> Merge seamlessly iBaan DEM, SAP-Aris Toolset,
Visio,  diagrams into one unified framework.

> Through the Web-Feedback feature, any consumer (Intranet & Extranet users) is able to send
a comment or question to the process diagram
owner thereby starting a true collaboration
exchange (even by not having access to iBaan
ERP or iBaan DEM) 

> Zero client-based application or plug-in to install
or to maintain - saving implementation time
