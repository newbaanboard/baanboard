According to Baan OpenWorld is an EAI tool. The BOW Adapter can be seen as the replacement of BCBE however it already has better and more functionality as BCBE.

Baan OpenWorld consists of a lot more products than just the adapter:
- Connectors (for java, xml, sap, .net)
- Baan OpenWorld Broker
- Development environment (Studio)

The complete product can be seen as an EAI tool.
