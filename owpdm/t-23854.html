We are currently using OW2.5 with BaanIV and it has been working great. We are planning to upgrade from 2.5 to 2.6 soon due to text/error handling limitation of Connector for .NET 2.5.

I would assume that 2.6 is not a major release (compared to 2.5) and therefore it should work with little effort. But in your case, I believe you moved from 2.1 to 2.6 directly, which in my opinion is a big upgrade. OW 2.6 must have installed .NET framerwork too and there might be some issues with the version of MS JVM that you have on your machine. Did you also updated the MMC on your server (as per the requirement for BCLM/SLM)? Problems with your Enterprise Manager and DTS designer could very well be due to that.

All this might not help at all as it will be hard to clean your machine now but I can atleast share my experience of using OpenWorld 2.5 and other than its known limitations, it has been working great.
