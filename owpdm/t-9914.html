I think answer to all these questions are anybody's guess but here is what I think about them.

1. BES, I think, is the term loosely used to envelope all the applications offered by Baan.

2. When Baan changed their vision to become a 'Solution' provider rather than a 'software' seller, they started using iBaan with their product names. Hence, in my opinion, iBaan is not a product rather it is a new approach of selling the products in anintegrated form with other applications i.e. in form of a solution.

3. In my opinion, iBaan Value Apps means all the applications around ERP backbone, for example, all E-Enterprise products.
