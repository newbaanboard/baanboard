Hi Aamir,

BODs are using ION as middle-ware. If you install ION and then configure the routings in the ION Desk then you will get your subscriptions being generated.
So please read the general ION and the LN-ION documentation.

If you are looking for the subscribe and unsubscribe methods then you need to develop a BDE instead of a BOD.
