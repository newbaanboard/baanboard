In simple terms, the nature of integration between ERP and Webtop is different from that between ERP and CRM.

Webtop, in essence is like a front end application providing alternate user interface option for Baan ERP. So, webtop is always a client and Baan ERP the server, so, it is just sufficient to go ahead with Adapter for integration.

Whereas, both CRM and ERP are business apps with their own data (databases), logic, UI etc. In order for these apps to collaborate, the simple capabilities of Adapter are not sufficient and hence, once needs to make use of the sophisticated, great Broker tool.

Hope, this clarafies.
