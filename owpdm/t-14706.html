Hi all,

Webtop 3.0 is currently processed by the fulfillment department.
Availability of Webtop 3.0 is currently scheduled for April 15th. Those of you that have access to the Baan intranet can preview Webtop 3.0 via http://ibaanwebtop or http://srv-nld-maan/webtop_gy/defaultlogin.html. Webtop 3.0 has many improvements over its predecessor (Webtop 2.0), i.e. much improved performance, a new skin, improved usability and stability.

Installation must be done once (webserver, NOT the client) and when this is done, Webtop can be accessed by all users via the intranet (no internet support yet, although it is possible).

lvdberg
27th May 2004, 14:45
Indeed Webtop 3.0 only uses the Microsoft JVM for now (Microsoft will support its JVM until the end of 2007, at which time we will have removed the dependency of Webtop on the Microsoft JVM). You can remove the SUN VM for just the browser by opening the Control Panel on your computer, opening the Java Plug-in Control Panel, and on the Browser tab you have to deselect Microsoft Internet Explorer. If you use other applications with Internet Explorer that need the SUN VM, you indeed may have a problem.

As far as in house applications are concerned that have to work in Webtop, they require some restrictions in their code constructs. We can provide the service (billable) to make these applications work in Webtop.
