Hey Guys, My doubt got cleared by one of my senior at Infor. :)

Here is the solution:
I had to write it in the Approve DLL- tdpurdll4109.
As this was a specific case when Workflow is Implemented for PO(i.e. tdpur400 table is listed in Object Configuration Model under Common menu).
Since the status of PO will not change to "Approved" until the PO is Approved on the Workflow; as Approve button on PO will only submit the PO for Approval to Workflow. So couldn't write in DAL.
