Hello Xikkos

Baan V gives you the possibility to connet from outside via a socket connection to Baan. Therfore you must programm on your own.

Another way is to use the ole Connection/Server from Baan. Also you have to programm some code on Baan side for it.

Or you go directly via ODBC into the database.

We are working with BaanConnect Server (http://www.swiss1mobile.com) from the company swiss1mobile. This programm enables us to connect via a TCP/IP Socket to Baan and retrieve / update directly data from / within baan from any programm which is able to open a socket.

The programm is not free of charge however the price worth.
