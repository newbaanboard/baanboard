In order to view data in Baan Portal from Baan as far as I know there is no 'out of box' solution. The old iBaan OLAP client was completely integrated with iBaan Portal and if you had it, you would have easily accessed the data from BIS. Now I think Baan doesnot sell its OLAP client any more, there is no straight forward way of doing it. You can check with Baan if you can get a copy of it and it will be very straightforward.

One way of doing it could be of using Crystal Analysis + Crystal Enterprise solution. You make your OLAP views in Crystal Analysis and then publish it to Crystal Enterprise. That way your OLAP views will become 'web-enabled'. Then I think somehow you should be able to use E-Portfolio (a sample webbased application that comes with Crystal Enterprise) to provide access to users in the iViews.

There might be some other solutions out there.. but thats all I know :)

BTW, Baan BIS is now called Decision Manager.
