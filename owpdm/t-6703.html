Hi Alex,

Please refer to the "Admin Guide" for Decsion Manager 2.0 that comes along with the product CD. You need to export the data from BaanIVc or BaanERP 5.0c to the datawarehouse (SQL 7 or 2000) and then process the OLAP cubes (OLAP server or application server as the case may be).

Please be aware that the prefrred OLAP client for Baan BIS is Crystal Analysis Pro (version 8.0). Version 8.5 is likely yo be available shortly.
