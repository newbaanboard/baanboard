Hi,
Crystal Reports 8.0 doesn't support iplanet web and application servers.

Becaz,

1. iPlanet changed its architecture model after Crystal Reports 8.0 is released

2. Crystal Reports supports CGI ( Common Gateway Interface) based web servers like Apache, Lotus Domino etc., iPlanet doesn't support CGI.
