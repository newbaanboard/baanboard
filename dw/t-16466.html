Hi,

Several SSA Baan DEM users (Fujitsu, Siemens, Solar Turbines, Herman Miller, etc...), having the same need, are using EZ-Process to convert directly DEM diagrams into publishing format (to be used in webpages or Word/pdf documents). Baan certified, this application also enables entire DEM models to be published on your Intranet. Your multi-leveled DEM diagrams are then visible by all employees (Baan users or not) and are linked to their respective internal documents (ISO, training, policies, work instructions, etc), user-roles and SOX controls. Once converted by EZ-Process, a simple copy/paste can be done to use your DEM diagram(s) anywhere.
