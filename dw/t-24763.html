Hi,

same problem I had once also. Here the answer from Baan-Support about:

To use a different map to underlay in your enterprise structure model, you have to copy the bitmap in $BSE/eme/bmp/maps and on your client (i.e. XP) in C:\Documents and Settings\All Users\Application Data\Baan\eme\Bmp.

Too an old info from Ada about:
>The bmp-files need to be on the server and on your client to be able to
display them.
>Next to that you need to have in the EME the option File->User Settings
Download bitmaps checked.
>Ada


So check both out; it has worked on my site then well.
