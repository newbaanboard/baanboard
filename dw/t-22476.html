Pablo,

Import of Visio diagrams into the native version of Baan Business Organizer (now called DEM IV or ERP-5) is not possible. However, Visio diagrams can be imported and deployed side by side with DEM diagrams/models when using the web-enabled version of DEM, called EZ-Publisher.

Visit http://www.ez-process.net/ezpubdemo to see DEM IV, DEM ERP-5, Visio and Aris Toolset diagrams all published in one online model, and inter-related between them. Simply open the "Business Models" folder in the left tree and drill-down to "Sample Diagrams" to see the Visio diagrams. You can also visit www.BaanDEM.com for related DEM FAQ.

Hope this help you,
