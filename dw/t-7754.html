Hello,

When you want such a setup, then you need to work with different versions per company in which you make your employee-role links.

You can leave your BP all in the same version and derive the company specific versions from that one.

You need also to define different Project Models, per company 1.

This way it is possible to have a different user menu per company.

You can even have the same roles in the versions, but then you need different role-business process links in the company specific versions.

Hope this helps you.

Ada
