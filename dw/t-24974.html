HI,

You can get such a report with the help of follwing steps :

1. Print Where Used sessions
This can be found in 'Misc' menu of Master Data. This will print various business processes for a session

2. Find roles by business process

3. Find roles by user

Combining these three outputs you can derive 'User-Session accessibility' .
