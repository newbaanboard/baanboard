Workflow 7.4 is an another product, it's not an DEM extension.

it's a fujitsu product interstage 7.4 (A old version because the currently is the 10), it's packaging with infor Logo and plug-in to add connection to ERP ln and Baan IV session.
Interstage is a BPM solution.

It's possible the export DEM model (with XPL format) but in fact it's completely independent.

I think it's very good solution, i'm install this in few customer.
