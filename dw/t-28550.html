T$PRGM would be the field.

There are however standard print sessions available in DEM.
You have "business processes" tgbrg5400m000, which can print the activities and the linked sessions.
You have "project model" tgbrg4400m000, which prints in the context of the project model, which is the implementation context.

You have session tgbrg4450m000, which gives an overview per employee which sessions within which business processes he is authorized.
