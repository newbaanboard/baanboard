Hi Ganesh,
I am workign on BaaN IV so in that respect i am telling you how we can give the authoriztion on activity level for different roles.so following steps should be followed :
1.Go to the Maintain Business process by Project(tgbrg4160s000).Then select the Business process in which we want to play with the authorization.
2.Then go into the Editor for that particular BP.
3.Now suppose there there 3 session in it let say X,Y & Z and we want to remove the authorization from X for role let say P.
4.Then in the editor if we click on the "Man shaped icon" besides the X we will have a drop down of all the roles attached to it.
5.Now click on P then a session tgbrg3150s000(Maintain Roles by Activity by bus process by business model) will be opened where we can exclude this role from this session X for this BP.

So by above process we can remove any particulay role authorization on activity level this is when we have attached that particular role to the respective BP in the session "Maintain Role by BP and by Business Model"(tgbrg4141s000).
