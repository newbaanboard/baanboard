Hi,

in the session "Maintain calender codes (tgeis0105m000)" there is a field called "last calculation date". This is the first date for calculation in the session "Calculate PI (tgeis0214m000)".

But be careful, if you want to recalculate the "old" values, you have to insert a earlier date or set it to blank.

Good luck and best regards.
