More and more DEM customers are using the EZ-Process extentions to merge their ISO documentation to their DEM processes, and therefore be able to print them when needed.

One of modules of EZ-Process is call EZ-Publisher and enable any Baan DEM IV and/or ERP-5 diagrams to be made available to any Intranet employees (Baan users or not) in their standard browser. In addition to publish in the same models other formats of diagrams such as Visio/ARIS/etc..., it also enable various formats of content (doc, pdf, xls, htm, xml, scm, acad, etc...) to be attached directly to DEM processes and process activities. The resulting model can not only enable complete web-navigation from the "Parent" diagrams to the "Child" ones (unlimited drill-down levels), but it also provides the users the ability to request any ISO Work Instructions, training manuals, internal policies, etc to be displayed into their browser, without any time comsuming search. As this navigation always display the most updated process and documentation information, it is in my point of view better than printing the manuals.

When the printing is required, a complementary module, called EZ-Book, is used. Similar to the DKM application referred in this thread, it enable an "e-Book" to be generated specific to a user-role (Ex: Scheduler), a business segment (Ex: Production Planning), a specific model, etc... Any users (Baan and non-Baan), from their browser, can then request the generation of the customized "e-Book". This one, including diagram images and all related ISO/Business documentation, can then be printed easily. EZ-Book is certified on both Baan DEM IV and ERP-5.

A sample online demo of EZ-Book can be tried at www.ez-process.com (EZ-Book section).
