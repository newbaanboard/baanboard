Dear baaniac,

I understand your question, but to my knowledge this is not possible in the standard Baan environment.

This would require that it is possible to map a functional menu structure into a process-oriented flow. The business process logic is different from the functional logic, so automatic mapping would be out of the question anyway.

Still, I would encourage you to take time and investigate what you can win with DEM for your company. The maintenance burden that you've today can significantly be diminished by maintaining your authorization with DEM. I've seen examples of companies with 1000+ Baan users, where authorization and model administration is a part-time job.

Another approach could be to implement with DEM during a migration to another Baan version. Anyway, in case you need expert knowledge or assessment for such a move, you can contact me.
