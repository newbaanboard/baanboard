In DEM you can link roles to a BP or to an activity in the BP.
When you link a role to the BP, then the role gets authorization for all activities in the BP and child BP's.
You then can on activity exclude the role for certain activities when needed.
You can however also link a role directly to an activity.
Depending on how many activities you want to authorize you choose your modeling strategy.
When you have for example 10 activities in the BP and role X needs access to 2, then direct linking to activities is most efficient.
When you have for example 10 activities in the BP and role Y needs access to 8, then first linking to the BP and excluding the 2 activities can be more efficient.

Role linking is described in the DEM User manual chapter 7 and 8.
