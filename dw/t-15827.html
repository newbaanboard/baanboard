Hello,

In principle you should not copy any DEM table.
To get a 'copy' of your DEM environment on another system or in another company, you need to think of the following :
- export your used DEM versions
- export also your used DEM version, which includes the Operational ESM
- export your DEM master data (components)

On the new system
- set Central DEM company
- import OESM version
- import master data
- set DEM parameters with ref. to OESM and Run. Baan comp.
- import your version data.

Hope this helps you.
