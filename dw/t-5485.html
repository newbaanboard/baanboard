Hi,

see Solution 105949 for EME VErsion 2.27

This problem may be caused because of 3 reasons

1. Improper installation of EME: In this case you need to reinstall your EME.
2. Old version of EME used which has some problem on compatibilities: In this case you may install the latest EME.
3. Check with your BW version which may be very old on certain clients in which you have problems: Keep your BW version updated in this case.
This must solve your problem.
