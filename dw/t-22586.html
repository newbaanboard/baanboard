Depends whether you have bought "Industry Solution", IS CD or "Reference Models", RM. If the former, just follow the instructions on the CD, they also provide master data for a demo company and the live company. If the RM, unzip and upload the version dump, *.org file into you home directory (root C:/ if it is under Win) and enjoy sophisticated business process management. You will also need to validate the models before they expire with your nearest SSA office. If you encounter any difficulty with validation, please let me know, I might suggest a workabout.
