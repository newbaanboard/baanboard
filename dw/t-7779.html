Is it possible to set up several completely independent, single logistics, single finance companies but have them share one central DEM company? The sharing of the central DEM company can be taken care of by using Central Company to Store Enterprise Modeller Data, tgbrg0100s000. The companies also will vary slightly in their DEM requirements, but that's already taken care of using version derivation and multiple project models. Please see Central DEM co, different role assignment per co (http://www.baanboard.com/baanboard/showthread.php?s=&threadid=7754) thread for more information on our case.

The problem is, during sharing, the enterprise unit is shared as well since it's in a TG table, tgbrg030. This forces all companies to see only the enterprise unit of the central DEM company, which is linked to its financial company. Hence, all the other companies can't link to their own financial company. How can we solve this? Thank you very much
