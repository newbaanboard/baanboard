Hello,

This error means, that sometimes there is in the DB still a record in the table roles by activity for an activity, which is not present in the Business Process anymore.
You could run session Print Version Data and look for role entries with ***** behind them.
Those can give you a hint to search for the right record in the DB table tgbrg340.
