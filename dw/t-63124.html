One way of doing this I figured out is through the session tgbrg5223m000 i.e. Global Change of Submodel Application, and insert print and display authorizations for this session for all indirect access which is the case here as well.

There is a session, tgbrg5154m000, that maintains the relationship between component applications and sub-applications. If you add tdpur4122m000 as a sub-application of tdpur4100m000, you will then be able to control access through sub-application modelling in the normal way,
