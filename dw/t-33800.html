- BPML: Business Process Modeling Language (promoted by bpmi.org now merged with omg group..)
- BPEL: Business Process Execution Language (also known as BPEL 4WS... for Web Services..) norm resulting of joined work of IBM and MS..

These are XML representation of Business Process (with a graphic notation named BPMN) and of course, if we can import/export processes in DEM using BPEL, it would be great !!
