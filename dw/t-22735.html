Main Process is PP1000
Sub Processes are PP1006 and PP1007.

If you want Finance people having access to PP1000, but not to PP1006 and PP1007, then you can do this in two ways.
Link Finance Role to PP1000 and exclude role on activities which represent the sub processes PP1006 and PP1007.
Or Link Finance Role direct only to those activities in PP1000 for which you want to give authorization. So no link to the process PP1000 as a whole.
