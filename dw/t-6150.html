Jeff Ball
14th August 2002, 14:26
Its not quite what you were looking for, but if you go to the business processes session tgbrg5500m000, and hit the print icon, one of the options is a role to session matrix. Provided you select the right settings for the report, you can print out for selected roles a list of processes and sessions with an indication of the level of authorisation granted by the process activities.

The EZ-Process software also provides an option to review access at role level.
