In its mission to provide extended DEM and Workflow support to all Baan related organizations (customers & partners), the Baan World User Group (BWU) DEM/Workflow Sub-Committee members have recently launched a new website offering its visitors several resources to learn about DEM Modeling and Workflow Management, and to interact directly with the committee.

Visit WWW.BAANDEM.COM to:
- submit your DEM & WORKFLOW questions to the sub-committee members,
- Request a copy of the BWU DEM/Workflow Resources Library CD
- suggest/request that a specific DEM/Workflow topics be discussed in the monthly technical discussions,
- download valuable (and sometime hard to find) DEM/Workflow documentation
- etc ...

Don't forget to bookmark and visit often WWW.BAANDEM.COM as new documents and utilities will be added on a regular basis.

Enjoy,
