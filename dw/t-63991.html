We generate these two reports

1. Employee and roles
====================
tgbrg820 - Emp by roles by proj model
tgbrg810 - roles (join by role)
tgbrg840 - emp by proj model (join by user)

2. Detail emp by session - above tables plus
================================
tgbrg340 - role by BP B Model
tgbrg350 - role by activity by BP
tgbrg450 - activity authorization by role
tgbrg500 - busines processes
tgbrg520 - BP Activities
tgbrg830

Data is stored in the functional company. The 2nd one is tricky as there exists hierarchy in the data.
Hope you find this useful.
