Hi, Try checking table tgbrg100 for you DEM company in GTM. We had a similiar problem on some of our models after and export/import. We found that the licence check field was correct but the licence expiry date (form 3, ledt) had not been brought over/had been corrupted. We put a valid date in (taken form original export server) and found this worked. (Check the licence key to with the origial DEM company too)

Good Luck
