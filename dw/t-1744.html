Since this thread is relating to both the Baan IV demo workflow product and the EZ-Workflow application, I wish to make clear that these are two separate products. The Baan workflow demo, introduced by Baan in its version IVc, has been since discontinued and is no longer supported by Baan.

EZ-Workflow, part of the EZ-Process Product Suite, is available on the market and fully supported. In addition to be compliant to all releases of Baan, it can also be used to workflow enabled non-Baan areas/tasks of your organization.

Hoping this note enables the readers to distinguish each reply of this thread.
