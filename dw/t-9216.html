Han

Are you using Baan IV or Baan 5.0? If it is 5.0, 5.1 or 5.2, the authorisation level is configurable at the process activity level using the second form of the Properties, and if more control (eg configurable to roles and subsessions, a right-click on the activity enables roles to be set up with various access levels, sub-sessions and even sub-session/role combinations.
