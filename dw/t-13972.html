Launched in 2002, the www.BaanDEM.com / www.BaanWorkflow.com updated Resources Library now provides a comprehensive library of documentation and resources to address your Baan DEM and Workflow for Baan related questions. As a natural KnowledgeBase extention to BaanBoard, available resources include:

>> ability to download an extensive range of DEM & Workflow related documents, including user & technical manuals, training aids, presentation material, utilities, etc, (applicable for all Baan versions),

>> ability to browse through a FAQ section providing answers to the most common DEM/Workflow questions and issues, and providing resolution details (applicable for all Baan versions),

>> ability to read about the DEM Resolution & Optimization Program, to help your organization resolve quickly DEM authorizations and desktop generation issues, and maximise the usage of DEM & Workflow,

>> ability post your unresolved DEM & Workflow question to receive direct assistance from the Baan DEM/Workflow Competency Center,

>> etc...
