During generation it "walks" through the Business Processes linked to the Roles used by the user for which you are generating.

During that walk there is a syntax error detected in a Business Process,
so you get your error.
It can be, that if you look into the log-files (log.tgbrg), that you
would see, which BP it is.
It should also be visible on the screen of the User Generation session on
the bottom, at the moment of the error.

Then you need to check that BP in the DEM editor.
