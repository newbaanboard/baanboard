We have a couple of brands of barcode scanners connecting to BridgeLogix for our BaaN IV setup. Both our Janam and Intermec scanners are setup for VT100, but whenever you type a character in the Intermec scanner you get an additional foreign character in the front of each character.

For example when you type "test" the Janam scanner will show "test" but the Intermec show "ÕtÕeÕsÕt".
