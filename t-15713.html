This is the solution:

Log in as user "baan". (This is the equivalent in NT for root in Unix).
Start session ttadv0144m000: Change Password for General Table Maintenance.
The field "Current Password" is not checked now by the program, so it makes no difference what you fill in here.
Fill in the new password in field "New Password" and in the field "Re-enter Password" again.
Leave the session and the new password is valid now.
