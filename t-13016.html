I have no answer to your question, but I do have some remarks...

You are definitely using an old portingset, both on your live server as on your development server. I assume you also use old software in standard program.

If you check the text of solution 10923 (ttstpstandard et al), there have been some 40 defects solved in AFS. Most of them have been solved since january 2003, so recent fixes.

I think portingset is not the bottleneck, but it is the standard program. That in itself may depend on portingset updates, although there are no hard requirements.
